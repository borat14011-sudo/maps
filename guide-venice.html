What to do

Early morning 8:30-10:00am

Piazza San Marco

The expansive, flat paved main square is ideal for early morning stroller walks before the crowds arrive. The wide open space allows baby Mary Claire to look at the colorful pigeons while the morning air is still cool and refreshing.

Mid-day 12:00-2:00pm

Giardini della Biennale

This lush public park offers shaded gravel paths, mature trees, and a peaceful escape from the stone-paved center. It is the perfect spot for Mary Claire's mid-day nap in the stroller under a canopy of green leaves.

Morning 10:30am-12:00pm

Peggy Guggenheim Collection Gardens

The quiet, enclosed sculpture garden offers shady spots and stroller-friendly paved paths. It provides a highly relaxed art-viewing experience where parents can trade off watching the baby in a calm, modern setting.

Late afternoon 4:00-4:45pm

Private Gondola Ride from San Tomà

A private gondola ride starting from the quieter San Tomà station focuses on peaceful side canals rather than the busy Grand Canal. The gentle rocking motion on calm water is incredibly soothing for an infant.

Morning 9:00am-12:00pm

Lido di Venezia Beach

A short, scenic private water taxi ride brings you to the wide sandy shores of the Lido. The flat beachfront boardwalk is stroller-ready, and the gentle, shallow Adriatic waves are perfect for dipping tiny baby feet.

Morning 10:00am-1:00pm

Torcello Island

This ultra-quiet island in the northern lagoon features grassy paths and a peaceful canal-side walk. It is highly serene, completely free of crowds, and ideal for a long, undisturbed outdoor contact nap.

Afternoon 3:00-3:30pm

Fondaco dei Tedeschi Rooftop Terrace

This rooftop viewing platform offers stunning panoramic views of the Grand Canal and is fully accessible via a spacious elevator. Booking a private, timed slot ensures no crowds or noise, making it highly safe for a baby.

Morning 11:00-11:30am

Scala Contarini del Bovolo

Observe this stunning spiral staircase from the quiet, shaded courtyard directly below. It provides a peaceful architectural photo opportunity without the need to carry a heavy stroller up any steep stairs.

Afternoon 2:00-3:30pm

Isola di San Giorgio Maggiore

Take a brief water taxi ride to this quiet island opposite San Marco to explore its wide, flat open areas. The church interior is cool and stroller-accessible, offering a peaceful sanctuary away from the crowded mainland.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ristorante Quadri

This historic institution offers an elegant early outdoor terrace seating directly on Piazza San Marco. Parents can savor exquisite contemporary Venetian dishes while the baby is entertained by the live classical orchestra playing nearby.

Terrazza Danieli

Located atop the Hotel Danieli, this rooftop terrace offers breathtaking lagoon views and a spacious layout that easily accommodates strollers for early dinner seatings. The signature seafood risotto is exceptional.

Harry's Bar

This legendary Cipriani establishment is highly welcoming to well-dressed families during early lunch hours. The staff is famously accommodating to infants, and the signature Tagliolini gratinati is a must-try classic.

Lineadombra

Featuring a stunning wooden terrace floating over the Giudecca Canal, this restaurant offers plenty of space for strollers. The calm sea breeze, soothing water sounds, and fresh local seafood create a highly relaxed dining environment.

Antiche Carampane

A hidden gem tucked away in a quiet residential piazza, offering outstanding wild lagoon fish. Booking an early 7:00pm table in their intimate outdoor courtyard ensures a peaceful dinner before the venue fills up.
