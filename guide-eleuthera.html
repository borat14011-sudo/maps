What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

French Leave Beach

This breathtaking stretch of soft pink sand offers calm, shallow waters ideal for Mary Claire to dip her toes. The nearby casuarina trees provide excellent natural shade for post-swim naps.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Leon Levy Native Plant Preserve

Stroll along beautifully maintained, stroller-friendly wooden boardwalks winding through lush native coppice and medicinal gardens. The shaded canopy keeps the temperature cool and comfortable for baby.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Ten Bay Beach

A spectacularly calm, shallow cove on the Caribbean side of the island. Virtually wave-free at low tide, it acts as a warm, natural wading pool perfect for an infant.

Late Afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Governor's Harbour Historic Settlement

Walk the quiet, paved streets lined with charming 19th-century Victorian homes. The gentle harbor breeze and smooth sidewalks make for an effortless afternoon stroller walk.

Morning 9:30-10:30am / 1h

Ocean Hole

A massive, natural inland saltwater limestone sinkhole teeming with friendly fish. Mary Claire will be mesmerized by the swirling marine life from the safety of the shaded viewing platform.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Edwin's Turtle Lake Marine Reserve

A tranquil, sheltered saltwater lake where you can view green sea turtles in their natural habitat. The serene waters and quiet wooden docks provide a peaceful, low-stimulation environment for baby.

Full Day 9am-4pm / 7h

Harbour Island Day Trip

Take the short 10-minute water taxi from Gene's Bay to Dunmore Town. Rent a private golf cart with safety straps to explore the historic town and its world-famous pink sand beach.

Midday 11:30am-1pm / 1.5h

Queen's Bath

Explore these dramatic natural rock pools carved by the Atlantic. Visit strictly at low tide to sit in the warm, shallow, sun-heated tide pools while keeping baby securely in a front carrier.

Morning 9-9:30am / 30m

Glass Window Bridge

Witness the striking contrast between the deep blue Atlantic Ocean and the calm turquoise Caribbean Sea. Keep baby safe in the air-conditioned private vehicle while stopping for a quick, breathtaking photo-op.
