What to do

Early morning 8:30-10:30am / 2h

Knossos Palace

Explore the cradle of Minoan civilization; use a premium baby carrier as the ancient gravel paths and stone steps are not stroller-friendly. Go early to beat the crowds and heat.

Midday 11:30am-1:30pm / 2h

Heraklion Archaeological Museum

A world-class, fully air-conditioned museum that is completely stroller-accessible with elevators, making it the perfect escape from the midday sun for Mary Claire.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Cretaquarium Thalassokosmos

A modern, stroller-friendly aquarium featuring massive tanks; the gentle blue lighting and quiet marine atmosphere act as a wonderful sensory calm for infants.

Morning 9am-12pm / 3h

Spinalonga Island Private Boat Charter

Charter a private luxury rib boat from Elounda to tour the historic islet. Baby carrier required on the island, but the gentle sea breeze is highly soothing for a morning nap.

Late afternoon 4:30-7:30pm / 3h

AgrecoFarms

An authentic organic estate where parents can enjoy olive oil tastings while Mary Claire enjoys the shaded farm walks and gentle farm animals in a peaceful setting.

Sunset 6-7:30pm / 1.5h

Elounda Beach Promenade

A perfectly flat, paved coastal path ideal for a smooth sunset stroller walk with spectacular views of the calm Mirabello Bay and luxury yachts.

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Plaka Beach

Relax at a high-end beach club on this calm, pebbled beach; rent a luxury shaded cabana so Mary Claire can safely nap next to the gentle, lapping waves.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Historical Museum of Crete

A quiet, uncrowded sanctuary in Heraklion featuring elevator access and air conditioning, perfect for a leisurely stroller walk while exploring medieval Cretan history.

Morning 9-10:30am / 1.5h

Morosini Fountain at Lions Square

Stroll the pedestrianized heart of Heraklion; the square is entirely stroller-friendly, allowing easy stops at shaded outdoor cafes for cold Freddo espressos.

Set right on the water's edge in Elounda, reached via a quiet, stroller-accessible path; enjoy fresh grilled fish while baby watches the colorful fish below.

High-end seafood dining in Heraklion featuring a spacious, leafy outdoor terrace, early dining options, and an incredibly warm, baby-friendly staff.
