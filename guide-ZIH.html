Zihuatanejo

← back to travel board
Nestled in a protected bay, Zihuatanejo offers a peaceful, old-world escape where cobblestone streets meet tranquil, warm waters. It is the ultimate laid-back haven for families seeking gentle beaches, vibrant local culture, and authentic coastal magic. Here, the pace slows down, allowing toddlers and parents alike to fully embrace the easygoing rhythm of life by the sea.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Playa La Ropa Beach Day

This iconic crescent-shaped beach features soft, golden sand and exceptionally gentle waves, making it the premier spot for family swimming. Toddlers will love splashing safely in the shallow, calm shoreline while parents relax under shady palapas.

Morning / 4-5h

Playa Las Gatas Boat Adventure

Hop on a short, exciting water taxi from the town pier to this secluded beach protected by a legendary underwater stone barrier. Toddlers will be thrilled by the boat ride and the aquarium-like, wave-free snorkeling lagoon.

Morning / 2-3h

El Refugio de Potosí

This ecological reserve offers a lush jungle pathway where kids can spot spider monkeys, macaws, and a gigantic sperm whale skeleton. It is highly stroller-friendly, and toddlers will love the colorful butterfly pavilion.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Paseo del Pescador Stroll

Stroll along this scenic, car-free waterfront boardwalk where local fishermen bring in their daily catch and artists display colorful crafts. It is a smooth ride for strollers and offers plenty of ice cream stops to keep toddlers happy.

Morning / 1-2h

Mercado Municipal Exploration

Dive into a sensory wonderland of colorful tropical fruits, traditional wooden toys, and aromatic spices at this bustling indoor market. Keep toddlers engaged by pointing out the giant watermelons and vibrant piñatas hanging from the stalls.

Morning / 2-3h

Barra de Potosí Lagoon Tour

Take a flat-bottomed boat tour through these peaceful mangroves to spot herons, pelicans, and nesting birds in their natural habitat. The gentle, slow-moving boat ride is incredibly soothing and safe for even the youngest travelers.

Evening / 1-2h

Ayotlcalli Turtle Sanctuary

Participate in the magical conservation effort of releasing newly hatched baby sea turtles directly into the ocean sunset. Toddlers will be captivated watching the tiny hatchlings make their very first march down the beach.

Late Afternoon / 2-3h

Picante Catamaran Sunset Cruise

Board a spacious catamaran to glide across the calm bay waters and watch the sky turn brilliant shades of pink and orange. The stable deck offers plenty of room for toddlers to sit safely while enjoying the cool ocean breeze.

Evening / 1-2h

Plaza Principal Evening Play

Visit the vibrant main plaza in the evening to watch local families gather, kids play, and cultural dance performances take place on the stage. The wide-open, car-free space is ideal for toddlers to safely burn off their remaining bedtime energy.

Where to eat

  • Carmelitas Cafe — This beloved local gem serves legendary, authentic Guerrero-style breakfasts including homemade tortillas and mild, kid-friendly tamales in a warm patio setting.
  • La Perla Restaurant — Set directly on the sand of Playa La Ropa, this spot lets parents enjoy fresh seafood while kids play safely in the sand right next to the table.
  • Tacos Junior — A casual, bustling favorite offering delicious, freshly grilled al pastor tacos that are quick, inexpensive, and a massive hit with picky eaters.
  • Paletería La Michoacana — Cool down with an endless variety of fresh-fruit popsicles made with local ingredients like mango, coconut, and strawberry that toddlers adore.
👨‍👩‍👧 Stick to authorized taxis as local minibuses can be crowded and difficult to navigate with young kids, and always carry cash in pesos for beach vendors. Opt for a sturdy baby carrier over a stroller when exploring the charming, uneven cobblestone streets of El Centro.
