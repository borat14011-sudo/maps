Embrace the serene allure of Korčula, where medieval stone walls meet the crystal-clear, pine-fringed waters of the southern Adriatic. This historic island offers a slower, ultra-exclusive pace compared to neighboring Hvar, making it perfect for discerning families seeking relaxed coastal luxury. Will and Kathryn can easily navigate the flat, stroller-friendly seaside promenade stretching from the Old Town to the quiet shores of Žrnovska Banja. The shallow, sandy reaches of Lumbarda provide a gentle, safe haven for baby Mary Claire's first saltwater splashes under the Mediterranean sun.
🚕 Getting there: 2-hour private luxury Mercedes V-Class transfer from Dubrovnik Airport (DBV) to Orebić, followed by a scenic 15-minute private speed boat crossing directly to your resort dock, keeping baby Mary Claire comfortable and avoiding public ferry crowds.

What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Korčula Old Town

Stroll the pedestrian-only, fishbone-patterned medieval streets designed to shelter residents from strong winds. Use a baby carrier to navigate the occasional stone steps, and enjoy the shaded plazas and sea breezes.

Late morning 10am-12:30pm / 2.5h

Vela Pržina Beach

One of the rare sandy beaches in Croatia, located in Lumbarda. Ideal for baby Mary Claire to play safely in the warm, shallow water while Will and Kathryn relax on rented luxury sun loungers under a wide umbrella.

Afternoon 2-4:30pm / 2.5h

Badija Island

Take a quick, private taxi boat to this uninhabited forest island. Walk the flat, stroller-friendly pine path wrapping around the island and watch baby Mary Claire giggle at the friendly, free-roaming fallow deer that gather near the historic Franciscan Monastery.

Morning 10-10:45am / 45m

St. Mark's Cathedral

Admire this gorgeous Gothic-Renaissance cathedral made of local limestone. The interior is cool and quiet—perfect for a peaceful carrier-nap for Mary Claire while you appreciate the Tintoretto paintings.

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Bilin Žal Beach

Another pristine sandy beach in Lumbarda with crystal-clear, exceptionally calm waters. There is a stone pier for shade and a local konoba right on the sand for quick refreshments with the baby.

Afternoon 1-2:30pm / 1.5h

Bire Winery

Taste the famous, hyper-local Grk white wine on a shaded stone terrace in Lumbarda. It is highly accommodating to families, offering early afternoon tastings where Mary Claire can rest in her stroller beneath the grapevines.

Early morning 8:30-10am / 1.5h

Banje Beach

The oldest beach in Korčula, located just a short, completely flat walk from the Old Town. Perfect for a quick, fuss-free dip with the baby without having to pack up for a drive.

Midday 12-1pm / 1h

Korčula Town Museum

Housed in the beautiful Gabrielis Palace, this air-conditioned museum offers a quiet escape from the midday sun. The spacious ground floor is easily accessible and showcases local stone-carving and shipbuilding history.

Morning 10-11am / 1h

Semi-Submarine Korčula

Board the red semi-submarine departing from the main harbor. Mary Claire will be mesmerized by the underwater views of Adriatic fish through the large viewing windows in the air-conditioned cabin below deck.

🍽️ Where to eat

LD Restaurant

Michelin-starred dining on the historic town walls. Secure an early evening terrace reservation overlooking the Pelješac Channel; the staff is incredibly accommodating to luxury travelers with infants, gladly accommodating a sleek stroller at the table.

Adio Mare

Located right next to Marco Polo’s house, this legendary tavern serves spectacular fresh grilled fish and traditional Peka. Their spacious, open-air top terrace is lively enough to mask any infant noises while still maintaining a refined atmosphere.

Konoba Mate

A Michelin Bib Gourmand family-run tavern in the quiet inland village of Pupnat. The shaded, rustic courtyard is perfect for an early dinner, and the staff loves babies, happily providing highchairs and serving fresh, handmade Žrnovski makaruni pasta.

Filippi Restaurant

Elegant seaside dining on the Petar Kanavelić promenade. The outdoor terrace offers gentle sea breezes, smooth stroller access, and a relaxed yet high-end atmosphere for savoring local seafood.

Konoba Belin

Located in Žrnovo, this restaurant features a beautiful, leafy garden terrace that is exceptionally spacious and shaded. It is highly family-friendly, offering an authentic, peaceful dining experience away from the busier coastal crowds.
