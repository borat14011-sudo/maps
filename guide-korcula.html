Korčula

← back to travel board
Steeped in history and ringed by calm, azure waters, Korčula is a playground of car-free medieval alleys and pine-fringed beaches. Families will love exploring its miniature Dubrovnik-like old town and cycling through peaceful olive groves. It offers the perfect, slower-paced Dalmatian escape where toddlers can safely wander and build sandcastles.
🚕 Getting there: ~2.5 hours via private transfer from Dubrovnik Airport to Orebić, followed by a scenic 15-minute car ferry directly to the island.

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Korčula Old Town Gates & Revelin Tower

Climb up to the historic stone entrance of this walled town to feel like royalty. It is highly stroller-friendly around the wide harbor base, though the tower stairs are for baby carriers only.

Morning / 3-4h

Vela Pržina Beach in Lumbarda

A rare, genuinely sandy beach on the island with shallow, warm waters perfect for paddling. Toddlers will love digging in the soft sand while parents relax under rental umbrellas.

Afternoon / 45 mins

semiSUBMARINE Korčula

Peer through the glass underwater cabin of this bright red boat to spot fish and sea urchins. It is short enough to keep toddlers completely captivated without any risk of sea sickness.

Late Afternoon / 2h

Kočje Protected Landscape

A magical, moss-covered rock labyrinth and holm oak forest that feels straight out of a fairy tale. Toddlers will enjoy the easy, shaded walks, but leave the stroller behind for a carrier.

Morning / 1h

Korčula Town Open Market

A vibrant daily market filled with fresh local figs, peaches, and candied orange peels. It is a sensory delight for little ones who can sample sweet seasonal fruits on the spot.

Morning / 2-3h

Badija Island Deer Sanctuary

Take a quick 10-minute taxi boat to this uninhabited island where friendly, wild fallow deer eat carrots right from your hand. The flat, pine-shaded paths are ideal for stroller walks.

Morning / 1h

St. Mark’s Cathedral Square

The heart of the old town, featuring beautiful stone carvings of lions and sirens on its historic facade. The wide, car-free stone piazza is a safe, enclosed space for toddlers to toddle around freely.

Morning / 3h

Pupnatska Luka Beach

A breathtaking, deep cove with smooth white pebbles and crystal-clear, calm waters shaded by lush pine trees. The pebbles are great for toddler sorting games, and the bay is incredibly sheltered.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Honey Tasting at OPG Komparak

Visit this family-run farm just outside town to taste delicious local herbal honey and meet the friendly farm dogs. Toddlers will love seeing the colorful hives and tasting the sweet treats.

🍽️ Where to eat

Konoba Mate

Try their homemade goat cheese-stuffed pasta and unique herbal ice creams in a rustic, highly welcoming garden setting.

Adio Mare

Order the traditional, slow-cooked Korčulanska Žrnovski makaruni (handmade local pasta with beef sauce) that kids and adults alike adore.

Konoba Belin

Feast on traditional peka (slow-roasted meat under an iron dome) in a spacious, casual outdoor courtyard where kids can move around.

Makarun

Get the handmade local macaroni pasta with fresh, simple tomato sauce, served in a quiet, beautiful stone courtyard.

Lumbarda Resort Beach Bar

Grab a wood-fired Margherita pizza right on the beach, perfect for a fuss-free, sandy-toes family lunch.

👨‍👩‍👧 Bring a high-quality baby carrier instead of a bulky stroller, as the old town's charming streets are filled with steep stone steps. Stock up on water and beach snacks at the Tommy supermarket just outside the old town walls before heading to the more remote beaches.
