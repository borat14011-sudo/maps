Thassos

← back to travel board
Thassos is a lush, family-friendly paradise where pine forests meet the calm, shallow Aegean Sea. With its incredibly accessible beaches and slow-paced mountain villages, it offers a stress-free Mediterranean escape. Toddlers will love splashing in the gentle waves while parents soak in the rich history and warm Greek hospitality.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min taxi from Kavala Airport to Keramoti, then a scenic 35 min ferry to Limenas

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Golden Beach

Splash in the calm, knee-deep turquoise waters of this expansive sandy bay. Toddlers will love the soft, powder-like sand that is perfect for building sandcastles.

Morning / 2-3h

Aliki Beach and Ancient Ruins

Explore the ancient marble quarries and twin bays with crystal-clear waters. The flat archaeological path is easy for little legs, and the shaded pine trees offer great picnic spots.

Late Afternoon / 2h

Panagia Village Springs

Wander through a traditional mountain village filled with stone houses and rushing spring water channels. Toddlers will be delighted by the ducks swimming in the central fountains.

Morning / 1-2h

Ancient Agora of Thassos

Let the kids run free in this grassy, open-air archaeological site right in Thassos Town. It is stroller-friendly and completely free of traffic, making it a safe historical playground.

Early Morning / 2h

Marble Beach (Saliara)

Marvel at the striking contrast of dazzling white marble pebbles against bright turquoise water. Toddlers will enjoy scooping the smooth white stones, but close supervision is recommended.

Monday Morning / 2h

Prinos Monday Market

Immerse your senses in this bustling open-air market filled with colorful local fruits, olives, and clothes. It's a great spot to grab sweet fresh peaches and honey that toddlers adore.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Theologos Village

Step back in time in the island's historic stone-built capital, surrounded by lush olive groves. Stop by a local bakery for soft, honey-drenched loukoumades that toddlers will happily devour.

Full Day / 4-5h

La Scala Beach Club

Enjoy a highly organized beach day with comfortable daybeds, shallow waters, and lush green surroundings. The gentle entry to the sea and clean family changing facilities make it incredibly stress-free.

Evening / 2h

Potos Beach Promenade

Stroll along the pedestrian-friendly beachfront lined with cafes, gelaterias, and a small playground. It is the ultimate spot for a relaxed evening walk with a stroller.

🍽️ Where to eat

Simi Tavern

Order the fresh grilled sea bream and crisp zucchini chips while dining right beside the historic old harbor in Limenas.

Taverna Irene

Feast on the traditional moussaka and giant beans in tomato sauce in a relaxed beachfront setting in Potos.

Taverna Andreas

Try the legendary slow-spit-roasted goat and homemade tzatziki in a shaded, family-run courtyard in Theologos.

Taverna Pigi

Savor the incredibly tender pork souvlaki and Greek salad right next to the cool, running mountain springs of Panagia.

San Antonio Beach Restaurant

Enjoy fresh seafood pasta while your kids safely play in the soft sand just steps from your table in Potos.

👨‍👩‍👧 Rent a car at the ferry port to easily navigate the island's scenic ring road with a car seat. Always carry cash for beach loungers and small village bakeries, as card machines can sometimes lose signal in remote areas.
