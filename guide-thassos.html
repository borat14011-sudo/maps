Thassos welcomes luxury travelers with its emerald pine forests spilling down to meet the calm, shallow Aegean waters. Unwind in your private luxury suite while the gentle sea breezes and scent of wild thyme create an idyllic Greek island paradise.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 2.5h

Golden Beach (Chrysi Ammoudia)

A vast stretch of fine golden sand with exceptionally shallow, warm, and wave-free waters. It is perfect for baby Mary Claire's first ocean dips, and features a flat, paved seaside path ideal for smooth stroller walks under the morning sun.

Late Afternoon 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM / 1.5h

Ancient Agora of Thassos

Located in Limenas, this historic archaeological site offers flat, shaded grassy pathways under ancient olive trees. It is a peaceful and quiet spot to stroll with an infant carrier while admiring the marble ruins.

Morning 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM / 3h

La Scala Beach Club

A high-end beach club offering ultra-plush, shaded private cabanas and exceptionally calm waters. Families can relax with premium beachside service, clean luxury changing facilities, and a quiet, ambient atmosphere during the morning hours.

Evening 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM / 1.5h

Limenas Waterfront Promenade

A beautifully paved, completely stroller-accessible harbor walk lined with boutique shops and cafes. Ideal for an easy evening stroll to watch the sunset over the yachts with baby Mary Claire resting in her stroller.

Morning 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 3h

Aliki Beach and Archaeological Site

A breathtaking double bay with ancient marble quarries submerged in crystal-clear water. Stick to the shaded tavern-lined sandy cove for baby splash time, or use an infant carrier to explore the pine-shaded ruins above the cliffs.

Morning/Afternoon 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM / 4h

Saliara (Marble) Beach via Private Yacht

Famous for its snow-white marble pebbles and bright turquoise waters. Skip the bumpy dirt roads with a baby and instead book a private skippered catamaran charter to drop anchor in the calm, dazzling bay for a shaded swim.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 5:30 PM / 2.5h

Theologos Mountain Village

A preserved historical village tucked into the mountains. Use an ergonomic baby carrier to navigate the charming stone-paved alleys, enjoy the cooler mountain breeze, and admire the traditional Macedonian architecture.

Morning 9:30 AM - 12:30 PM / 3h

Makryammos Beach Resort & Deer Park

A private, pine-fringed sandy bay with incredibly calm waters and a small, family-friendly deer park. It offers a pristine, controlled environment with excellent facilities, shade, and manicured lawns for baby to crawl on.

Midday 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM / 1h

Sotirelis Olive Oil Museum

Located in the traditional village of Panagia, this cool, indoor working watermill museum offers a fascinating, quick sensory tour. It is quiet, air-conditioned, and highly accessible, making it an easy midday stop with a baby.

🍽️ Where to eat

Simi Restaurant

A historic seafood institution on the old harbor of Limenas serving fresh catch-of-the-day and lobster pasta. The spacious outdoor terrace is completely stroller-accessible, offers high chairs, and hosts an upscale, family-welcoming early dinner seating.

Taverna Krambousa

Set right on the edge of Golden Beach, this highly rated taverna serves traditional Greek dishes like slow-cooked lamb and fresh sea bass. The soft sandy setting, high chairs, and exceptionally warm staff make dining with an infant relaxed and stress-free.

Taverna Andreas

Located in the mountain village of Theologos, famous for its succulent, spit-roasted kid goat and local wine. The spacious, shaded garden courtyard is quiet, breezy, and highly accommodating for families who arrive for an early dinner.

Gorgona Taverna

A beautiful beachfront taverna in Skala Rachoni shaded by massive plane trees. Dine with your toes in the sand on grilled octopus while baby Mary Claire relaxes in the natural shade of the trees next to your table.

Sti Soula

A beloved, upscale traditional taverna in Limenaria offering exceptional Mediterranean cuisine and locally sourced ingredients. Features a spacious, quiet courtyard terrace that easily accommodates premium strollers and offers high-end high chairs.
