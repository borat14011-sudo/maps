Golden Beach

Known locally as Chrysi Ammoudia, this beach features ultra-shallow, crystal-clear water and soft sand, making it perfect for baby's first safe splash under a premium rented cabana.

Ancient Agora of Thasos

Panagia Village

La Scala Beach

Folklore Museum of Theologos

Moni Archangelou
