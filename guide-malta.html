What to do

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Valletta Waterfront

Walk along this historic, flat promenade featuring restored 18th-century warehouses. It is completely stroller-friendly with plenty of shade under the canopies and a gentle sea breeze that is perfect for baby Mary Claire's afternoon nap.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Upper Barrakka Gardens

These panoramic gardens offer stunning views of the Grand Harbour. The pathways are wide and paved for easy stroller rolling, and there are plenty of shaded benches under the arches to keep Mary Claire out of the midday sun.

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Mdina Old City

Known as the Silent City, this ancient walled town is entirely pedestrianized. The main paths are smooth enough for a premium stroller, and the exceptionally quiet streets ensure a peaceful environment for baby.

Morning 8:30-11am / 2.5h

Mellieha Bay (Ghadira Beach)

Malta's largest sandy beach features exceptionally shallow, calm water for yards out, making it safe for dipping Mary Claire's toes. Private lidos offer umbrella rentals to guarantee constant shade.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

San Anton Gardens

This elegant botanical garden in Attard features peaceful paved pathways, beautiful stone fountains, and roaming peacocks. It provides a serene, shaded oasis that is perfect for a quiet morning stroll.

Early afternoon 1-3pm / 2h

The Malta National Aquarium

This modern facility is fully air-conditioned and entirely wheelchair and stroller-accessible. The colorful marine life provides wonderful visual stimulation for Mary Claire in a calm, controlled environment.

Late afternoon 5-7pm / 2h

Spinola Bay Promenade

A flat, paved waterfront walkway connecting St. Julian's and Sliema. It offers beautiful harbor views, zero stairs, and plenty of breezy spots to stop and rest with the stroller.

Afternoon 2-3:30pm / 1.5h

Palazzo Falson Historic House Museum

This historic house in Mdina features a quiet, beautifully preserved central courtyard. It offers a calm, slow-paced atmosphere, though parents should use a baby carrier to access the upper floors.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Three Cities Private Harbour Cruise

Charter a private, traditional dghajsa wooden boat fitted with a protective sun canopy for a smooth tour of the Grand Harbour. The gentle motion of the calm water is incredibly soothing for an infant.

🍽️ Where to eat

Noni

Enjoy sophisticated Michelin-starred local cuisine. Booking the earliest 6:00 PM seating ensures a quiet dining room, and the staff is incredibly accommodating with space for a stroller near your table.

Tarragon

Indulge in award-winning seafood on a spacious outdoor terrace overlooking the sea. The gentle ambient noise of the waves easily masks any baby sounds, and highchairs are readily available.

The de Mondion

This fine-dining venue in Mdina offers breathtaking views from its outdoor terrace. The staff is highly attentive to families, providing early dinner slots and easily accommodating infant needs.

Zeri's Restaurant

Located at Portomaso Marina, this upscale Mediterranean spot offers a quiet marina-side terrace. It is very stroller-friendly with zero steps and features a relaxed yet luxurious atmosphere.

Caviar & Bull

This high-end modern restaurant offers striking views of Spinola Bay. The spacious outdoor terrace easily accommodates strollers, and the accommodating staff provides highchairs and prompt service.
