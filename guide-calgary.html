What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Wilder Institute/Calgary Zoo

Walk through the Canadian Wilds to see grizzly bears and cougars, then visit the Penguin Plunge. Toddlers will love the giant destination playground and the easy-to-navigate paved stroller paths.

Afternoon / 2-3h

TELUS Spark Science Centre

Ignite curiosity with interactive exhibits, a creative indoor playground, and a dedicated Creative Kids Museum. It's a perfect rainy-day refuge with soft play areas for toddlers to climb safely.

Morning / 4h

Heritage Park Historical Village

Step back in time with a vintage steam train ride, a horse-drawn wagon, and an antique midway. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the gently spinning carousel and the farm animals.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Prince's Island Park

This lush urban oasis in the Bow River offers scenic walking trails, a huge playground, and plenty of ducks to watch. Toddlers will love running on the wide grassy lawns and watching the river rafters.

Morning / 1-2h

Calgary Central Library

Marvel at the stunning architecture before heading to the vibrant children's section, which features a giant indoor play structure. It is free to enter, incredibly stroller-friendly, and has cozy reading nooks.

Morning / 1-2h

Calgary Farmers' Market West

Browse local treats, fresh fruits, and artisan crafts under one roof. The indoor kids' play area lets toddlers burn off energy while parents enjoy a local coffee and fresh pastries.

Morning / 1h

Calgary Tower

Experience a 360-degree view of the city and the distant Rocky Mountains from 191 meters in the air. Toddlers will be thrilled standing on the glass floor looking straight down.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Bowness Park

Rent a pedal boat on the lagoon in the summer or enjoy the miniature train ride through the trees. The shallow wading pool is perfect for toddlers looking to splash safely on a hot day.

Afternoon / 2h

Studio Bell (National Music Centre)

Explore five floors of Canadian music history with interactive instrument installations where kids can bang on drums and sing. Toddlers will love the sensory soundboards and light-up floor triggers.

🍽️ Where to eat

Peter's Drive-In

Get a classic paper bag of thick-cut fries and one of their legendary custom-mixed milkshakes.

River Café

Enjoy seasonal Canadian cuisine like wild salmon while kids enjoy gourmet, healthy kid-bites in a cozy, rustic cabin setting on Prince's Island Park.

Cluck N Cleaver

Grab some of the city's best crispy, southern-style fried chicken and biscuit sandwiches.

Village Ice Cream

Treat the family to handmade, locally sourced ice cream with flavors like Melted Chocolate and Salted Caramel.

Sidewalk Citizen Bakery

Pick up fresh sourdough croissants, shakshuka, and soft pastries that toddlers will happily munch on in the East Village.

👨‍👩‍👧 Utilize the CTrain free-fare zone downtown for easy, stroller-friendly hops between major attractions. Pack layers for the family since mountain weather can bring cool breezes and sudden temperature shifts even in mid-summer.
