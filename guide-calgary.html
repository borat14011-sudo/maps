From hands-on science exhibits to encounters with majestic wildlife, the city offers a welcoming, active escape for travelers of all ages. Experience the warm hospitality of this prairie gem where urban sophistication meets rugged outdoor adventure.
🚕 Getting there: ~20 min taxi or rideshare to downtown hotels, or hop on the convenient Route 300 BRT express bus.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Wilder Institute/Calgary Zoo

Explore diverse habitats from the Canadian Wilds to the Prehistoric Park, featuring life-sized animatronic dinosaurs. Toddlers will love the stroller-friendly paths, the indoor rainforest, and the dedicated play areas.

Afternoon / 3h

TELUS Spark Science Centre

A hands-on science playground with interactive exhibits, a giant dome theatre, and a creative outdoor park. The Creative Kids Museum inside is designed specifically for children under five to climb, splash, and build.

Morning / 1-2h

Calgary Tower

Get a bird's-eye view of the city and the Rocky Mountains from the 191-meter-high observation deck with a glass floor. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the tiny cars below, and stroller access is seamless.

Full Day / 4-5h

Heritage Park Historical Village

Canada's largest living history museum features a steam train, an antique midway, and friendly historical characters. Toddlers will delight in riding the gentle streetcar and visiting the farm animals.

Morning / 2h

Calgary Farmers' Market West

A bustling indoor market filled with local treats, fresh produce, and international food stalls. There is a fantastic indoor play area for toddlers to burn off energy while parents sip local coffee.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Bowness Park

A beautiful riverside park featuring a shallow wading pool in summer and a lagoon for boating or ice skating. Toddlers can enjoy the miniature train ride and the modern, shaded playground.

Afternoon / 2h

Prince's Island Park

An urban oasis on the Bow River with wide paved paths, picnic spots, and frequent family festivals. Toddlers will love watching the ducks and playing at the large creative playground on the island.

Morning / 2h

Studio Bell, home of the National Music Centre

Discover Canada's musical history through five floors of interactive exhibitions where you can play instruments and sing. The Soundscape installations are highly sensory and engaging for toddlers who love music.

Morning / 2h

The Hangar Flight Museum

Get up close to historic airplanes and helicopters in a real WWII hangar. It is a fantastic, uncrowded spot where toddlers can run under massive wings and try out simple hands-on controls.

🍽️ Where to eat

Peter's Drive-In

Order a classic, thick marshmallow-chocolate milkshake and a bag of hot crinkle-cut fries at this iconic, family-friendly drive-in.

Alforno Bakery & Cafe

Grab a fresh-baked pastry, handmade sourdough, and a creamy babyccino for the little ones in their bright, casual space.

Cluck N Cleaver

Try the crispy, southern-style Southern Fried Chicken or rotisserie chicken with a side of fresh gravy and biscuits.

Una Pizza + Wine

Order the famous thin-crust '4-Maggi' cheese pizza that kids adore alongside a fresh kale salad for the adults.

Myhre's Deli

Indulge in a classic, stacked Montreal smoked meat sandwich on rye, paired with a side of traditional squeaky-cheese poutine.
