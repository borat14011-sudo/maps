Calgary

← back to travel board
Calgary blends clean, modern corporate efficiency with rugged Canadian Rockies access, making it an incredibly low-stress destination for a family traveling with a toddler. The downtown core is highly walkable, exceptionally clean, and boasts extensive paved pathway systems along the Bow River that are perfect for high-end strollers. You'll find a sophisticated culinary scene that warmly welcomes early-dining families, alongside world-class green spaces that keep energetic parents and infants stimulated. It's the ultimate premium northern escape without the chaotic congestion of larger metropolitan hubs.
🚕 Getting there: ~20-25 minute taxi or private SUV transfer from Calgary International Airport (YYC) to downtown hotels.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Wilder Institute/Calgary Zoo

This world-class conservation hub features an impressive Penguin Plunge exhibit and a dedicated Canadian Wilds section where your toddler can spot grizzly bears and moose. The entire facility is impeccably paved for stroller pushing, and there are numerous clean, spacious family washrooms equipped with changing tables throughout the park.

Morning / 1-2h

Calgary Central Library

An architectural marvel in the East Village, this library houses a spectacular, dedicated early learning center on the ground floor complete with soft play structures and sensory developmental toys. The wide ramps and massive elevators make navigating the stunning wooden interior with a stroller entirely seamless.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Heritage Park Historical Village

Step back in time at Canada’s largest living history museum, featuring wide-open dirt roads that easily accommodate heavy-duty strollers and plenty of grassy areas for baby to crawl on. Don't miss a gentle ride on the antique steam train, which has accessible cars designed to fit strollers and diaper bags easily.

Morning / 1-2h

Prince's Island Park

Set on an island in the Bow River, this lush green space features beautifully paved, flat walking paths and a massive, modern playground with a soft rubber surface safe for toddlers. It's the perfect spot for an outdoor stroller nap while parents enjoy views of the city skyline and rushing water.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Bowness Park

Located in the city's northwest, this historic park offers scenic paved lagoonside paths, a shallow wading pool for splash-happy toddlers, and a miniature ride-on train that children love. There's also a scenic, glass-walled cafe right on the water where parents can grab a high-quality espresso.

Morning / 2h

Calgary Farmers' Market West

This bright, modern indoor market in the Greenwich community offers wide aisles for stress-free stroller navigation and an indoor kids' play area. Parents can sample artisan local cheeses and fresh pastries while your toddler enjoys the sensory sights of colorful, fresh Alberta produce.

Morning / 2-3h

TELUS Spark Science Centre

Head straight to the 'Creative Kids Museum' section, which is specifically designed for children under five with water-play tables, sensory crawl spaces, and soft climbing zones. It is fully indoor, temperature-controlled, and highly stroller-accessible, making it an ideal refuge on a rainy or chilly morning.

Morning / 2-3h

Glenbow Ranch Provincial Park

Just on the northwestern edge of the city, this park offers incredibly scenic, fully paved pathways winding through rolling foothills with majestic views of the Rocky Mountains. It provides a genuine Alberta nature hike experience that is 100% compatible with a standard stroller.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Studio Bell, home of the National Music Centre

This visually stunning, multi-story musical museum features interactive sound installations and sensory exhibitions that captivate infants and toddlers alike. The massive, whisper-quiet elevators and wide-open gallery floors make it exceptionally easy to navigate with a stroller.

🍽️ Where to eat

River Café

Order the pan-roasted local trout or seasonal game at this Michelin-recommended, rustic-chic lodge situated right inside Prince's Island Park. It's highly accommodating to families during lunch and weekend brunch, and the surrounding park allows parents to easily step outside for a quick walk if baby gets fussy.

Ten Foot Henry

This chic, vegetable-forward eatery features beautiful, plant-filled interiors and incredibly fresh, family-style sharing plates like the iconic roasted tomatoes with feta and sourdough. The lively, bustling room naturally masks toddler noise, and the spacious booths comfortably accommodate high chairs.

Una Pizza + Wine

Go for an early dinner at 4:30 PM to grab their signature thin-crust prosciutto pizza and kale salad before the hip evening rush. The loud, energetic music and fast service are perfect for keeping a toddler entertained while ensuring a quick exit if needed.

Sidewalk Citizen Bakery

Located in the historic Simmons Building along the Riverwalk, this artisan bakery is famous for its sourdough shakshuka and fresh pastries. The industrial-chic, open-concept space is loud and spacious, making it highly welcoming for parents pushing strollers directly to the counter.

Lina's Italian Market & Cafe

A spacious, vibrant Italian market on Centre Street where you can order authentic, freshly made paninis, wood-fired pizzas, and espresso. The casual, self-serve seating area is exceptionally kid-friendly, with plenty of room to park a stroller next to your table.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Sheraton Suites Calgary Eau Claire

Perfectly situated next to Prince's Island Park, this hotel offers spacious one-bedroom suites with separate living areas so you can put your toddler to bed without sitting in the dark. It also features a fantastic indoor pool with a waterslide and provides high-quality cribs upon request.

The Westin Calgary

Located in the heart of downtown, this hotel offers their signature Heavenly Cribs and features direct access to the indoor +15 skywalk system, allowing you to walk your stroller in climate-controlled comfort. The indoor heated pool on the top floor is great for a quiet family swim.

Hotel Arts Kensington

A boutique luxury option located in the quiet, highly walkable neighborhood of Kensington, just steps from Bow River paths. They offer exceptionally spacious rooms, highly personalized service, and can easily arrange premium cribs and childproofing amenities prior to arrival.

👨‍👩‍👧 Take advantage of Calgary's '+15 Skyway network', an extensive system of indoor pedestrian skybridges that lets you wheel a stroller throughout the downtown core completely sheltered from weather. Calgary sits at an elevation of over 3,400 feet and has an exceptionally dry climate, so pack heavy-duty infant moisturizer and ensure the whole family doubles their water intake. Consider renting a premium all-terrain stroller from local services like 'Baby's Away Calgary' to effortlessly navigate both urban paved paths and gravel foothill trails.
