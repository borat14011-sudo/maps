Tamarindo blends high-end Guanacaste sophistication with a laid-back Pacific rhythm perfect for a discerning young family. The town features a rare stroller-friendly paved beachfront promenade alongside gentle, rolling surf. At the secluded south end of Playa Tamarindo, calm estuary waters offer a peaceful, wave-free paddling environment for baby Mary Claire. Private oceanfront estates and world-class boutique resorts ensure your family has immediate sanctuary just steps from the golden sand.
🚕 Getting there: The journey from Liberia International Airport (LIR) to the Tamarindo hotel strip takes exactly 1 hour and 15 minutes. For a seamless luxury experience with an infant, pre-book a private premium SUV transfer equipped with a sanitised, rear-facing infant car seat and ample space for your stroller and luggage.

What to do

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

Playa Tamarindo South End

The southern end near Capitan Suizo offers the calmest, shallowest swimming waters in the area, sheltered from strong swells. Perfect for dipping Mary Claire's toes in the water, with ample natural shade from overhanging palm trees.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Tamarindo Estuary Boat Tour

A private, shaded motorboat safari through the calm mangrove canals of Las Baulas National Park. It is completely noiseless and smooth, allowing Mary Claire to nap comfortably while you spot howler monkeys and pink spoonbills.

Mid-day 11am-2pm / 3h

Playa Avellanas

A short 25-minute private drive south leads to this pristine, shaded beach. Relax under the ancient beachside palms with a fresh coconut while baby enjoys the cool breeze away from the town buzz.

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

La Senda Labyrinth

The world's largest labyrinth, nestled in a shaded tropical dry forest. The smooth, flat dirt pathways are easy to navigate with a high-end baby carrier, offering a serene, meditative morning walk for the whole family.

Saturday morning 8-10am / 2h

Tamarindo Saturday Feria

An upscale weekly farmers market featuring organic local fruits, artisan cheeses, and live acoustic music. The open-air setup is highly stroller-accessible and shaded, perfect for an easy-going morning stroll with the baby.

Morning 9am-1pm / 4h

Marlin Del Rey Catamaran Private Charter

Charter a private catamaran for a gentle morning sail. The luxury vessel provides an air-conditioned cabin for Mary Claire to escape the heat, high-end catering for Will and Kathryn, and calm, glassy water cruising.

Afternoon low tide / 1.5h

Playa Langosta Tide Pools

At low tide, this quiet upscale neighborhood beach reveals warm, shallow, crystal-clear tide pools. These natural, wave-free baby baths are ideal for a peaceful splash session away from the crowds.

Late afternoon 4-7pm / 3h

Black Stallion Eco Park

Enjoy a private family ranch experience on a 33-acre eco-reserve. Relax in their shaded, open-air ranch lounge and enjoy a gourmet private BBQ while listening to the gentle sounds of nature.

Late afternoon 4:30-6pm / 1.5h

Playa Grande Sunset Walk

Take a brief, private water taxi across the river mouth to Playa Grande. This expansive, protected white-sand beach is incredibly quiet and peaceful, offering a calm environment for a sunset carrier walk.

🍽️ Where to eat

Pangas Beach Club

Upscale oceanfront dining featuring fresh local seafood and steaks. Book a 5:30 PM beachfront table to enjoy the sunset with your toes in the sand; the natural beach setting is spacious and highly accommodating for strollers.

Dragonfly Bar & Grill

An upscale, romantic open-air garden restaurant. Highly praised for its accommodating staff, quiet garden corners, early seating options, and excellent gourmet Costa Rican-fusion cuisine.

Seasons by Shlomy

Fine Mediterranean dining situated poolside in a peaceful garden setting. The gentle ambient noise of the pool creates a soothing atmosphere for infants, and the staff happily accommodates early family seatings.

La Baula Pizzeria

Gourmet wood-fired pizzeria set in a massive, beautifully lit tropical garden. The open-air, spacious layout is perfect for families, offering plenty of room for strollers and a very forgiving noise level.

El Mercadito de Tamarindo

A trendy, high-end open-air food hall with a relaxed, lively atmosphere. Perfect for a casual lunch where baby's occasional fussiness is easily masked by the upscale, energetic ambient music.
