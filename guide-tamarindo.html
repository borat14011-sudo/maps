What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Playa Tamarindo

Enjoy the gentle surf, soft golden sand, and calm warm waters of the main beach strip. The southern end near the river mouth offers the calmest, shallowest water for toddlers to safely splash.

Morning / 2h

Tamarindo Estuary Boat Tour

Float through the mangroves of Las Baulas National Park to spot howler monkeys, crocodiles, and exotic birds. The shaded, flat-bottomed boats keep toddlers cool and completely captivated by the close-up wildlife.

Evening / 2h

Tamarindo Thursday Night Market

Experience a vibrant evening filled with live music, local crafts, clowns, and delicious street food. Arrive early at 6:00 PM to easily navigate the stalls before the main crowds arrive.

Low Tide / 1-2h

Playa Langosta Tide Pools

Explore crystal-clear, natural rock pools teeming with tiny fish and hermit crabs just south of town. Be sure to dress your toddler in sturdy water shoes to protect their feet from the sharp volcanic rock.

Morning / 3-4h

Playa Conchal Day Trip

Visit a breathtaking beach made entirely of millions of tiny, crushed white shells rather than sand. Toddlers will love the unique sensory play of scooping shells, but bring thick-soled sandals for walking.

Afternoon / 3h

Black Stallion Eco-Park

Venture into the dry forest for an easy, family-friendly ranch day featuring gentle horses and a rustic barbecue. Toddlers can safely pet the farm animals and enjoy the wide-open, grassy play spaces.

Morning / 1-2h

Tamarindo Saturday Feria

Stroll through the local farmers market to sample sweet passionfruit, fresh coconut water, and artisanal baked goods. It is a sensory delight for toddlers, who can munch on fresh bananas while you shop.

Morning / 2h

La Senda Labyrinth

Wander through the world's largest organic labyrinth nestled deep in the tropical dry forest. The flat, winding pathways are completely enclosed by nature, allowing toddlers to safely explore at their own pace.

Morning / 2-3h

Playa Avellanas and Lola's

Spend a relaxed morning at this scenic beach and meet Lola, the famous, friendly resident pig who loves to lounge in the sand. Toddlers will be absolutely enchanted seeing a giant pig enjoying the ocean breeze.

🍽️ Where to eat

El Chiringuito

Order the fresh local fish tacos and let your toddler play safely in the sand right next to your beachfront table.

La Baula Pizzeria

Get the classic Margherita thin-crust pizza in a beautiful open-air garden featuring a dedicated wooden playground for kids.

Nogui's

Savor a slice of their world-famous coconut cream pie while sitting steps away from the crashing waves.

Pangas Beach Club

Feast on fresh snapper served on a hot volcanic stone while the kids play under the shade of massive Guanacaste trees.

Green Papaya Taco Bar

Grab the delicious mahi-mahi tacos and sit on the fun, built-in swing seats that toddlers and parents both love.

👨‍👩‍👧 Opt for a sturdy baby carrier over a traditional stroller, as Tamarindo's sandy paths and uneven sidewalks can be difficult to navigate. Always keep a close eye out for falling coconuts when setting up your beach blanket under the palm trees for shade.
