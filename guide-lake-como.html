Lake Como

← back to travel board
Nestled against the dramatic Italian Alps, Lake Como offers a postcard-perfect playground where cobblestone villages meet shimmering blue waters. Traveling via Milan makes this alpine paradise incredibly accessible, allowing families to transition from city bustle to mountain serenity in under two hours. With its gentle boat rides, historic villas, and winding lakeside paths, it is a magical destination that captivates parents and toddlers alike.
🚕 Getting there: ~1 hour 15 min drive by private transfer or rental car from Milan Malpensa Airport (MXP) directly to Como town, or a scenic 1-hour direct train from Milan Central Station.

What to do

Morning / 2 hours

Villa del Balbianello

Explore terraced gardens and Star Wars filming locations with stunning lake backdrops. Toddlers will love running along the wide gravel paths, but leave the stroller behind for a carrier due to the steps.

Mid-day / 30-45 mins

Lake Como Public Ferry

Feel the wind in your hair on a classic ferry ride connecting the lake's most beautiful towns. The open-air deck provides endless entertainment for toddlers who love watching the splashing waves.

Morning / 1-2 hours

Como-Brunate Funicular

Ride a historic railway up the steep mountainside to the charming village of Brunate for breathtaking panoramic views. Toddlers will be glued to the windows as the train climbs upward.

Morning / 2 hours

Villa Carlotta Gardens

Wander through lush botanical gardens filled with giant rhododendrons, bamboo groves, and citrus tunnels. It is exceptionally stroller-friendly with wide, flat pathways perfect for active toddlers.

Afternoon / 3 hours

Lido di Mandello del Lario

Relax at this grassy lakeside beach with a playground, shallow water, and plenty of shade. It is a peaceful, less-crowded alternative where toddlers can safely splash in the gentle lake water.

Morning / 1 hour

Como Mercato Annonario

Experience a bustling covered local market filled with fresh cheeses, seasonal fruits, and warm pastries. Toddlers can munch on fresh berries while parents browse the authentic Italian ingredients.

Afternoon / 2 hours

Greenway del Lago di Como

Walk a gentle, scenic pathway that winds through ancient villages, olive groves, and lakefront gardens. Choose the flat Lenno-to-Tremezzo section for an easy, stroller-friendly family stroll.

Afternoon / 2 hours

Castello di Vezio

Climb up to this ancient castle ruin to see spectacular views and mysterious plaster ghost sculptures. Toddlers will be fascinated by the resident birds of prey during the afternoon falconry demonstrations.

Afternoon / 1-2 hours

Giardini del Tempio Voltiano

Let the kids burn off energy at this lovely lakeside park featuring a dedicated playground, carousel, and scenic paths. It is the perfect spot for toddlers to run free right next to the water while you enjoy a takeaway coffee.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ristorante Bilacus

Sit in the beautiful, quiet garden terrace and order the handmade ravioli with fresh lake fish.

Ristorante Pizzeria Fontana

Get the classic wood-fired Margherita pizza in an incredibly welcoming, highly kid-friendly environment.

Trattoria del Glicine

Dine under a romantic canopy of wisteria and order the melt-in-your-mouth homemade gnocchi.

Ristorante La Punta

Located at the very tip of Bellagio, order the local specialty of risotto with pan-fried perch fillet.

Gelateria del Borgo

Treat the family to authentic artisan gelato; try the pistachio and wild dark chocolate flavors.

👨‍👩‍👧 Opt for a sturdy baby carrier over a stroller, as the historic lakeside towns are filled with steep cobblestone steps and narrow walkways. Always buy ferry tickets online in advance to avoid long terminal queues with impatient toddlers during peak hours.
