What to do

Morning 9:30am - 11:30am (2h)

Como Lakeshore Promenade & Museo della Seta

Walk the wide, paved, stroller-friendly lakeside path in Como town before heading indoors to the Museo della Seta, where the rich history of Italian silk-making is displayed in spacious, quiet galleries perfect for a sleeping baby.

Midday 12:00pm - 1:30pm (1.5h)

Como-Brunate Funicular

Ride the historic cable railway up to Brunate for jaw-dropping Alpine views; the carriage easily accommodates folded strollers, and the crisp mountain air provides a refreshing respite for Mary Claire.

Late Morning 10:00am - 12:00pm (2h)

Villa Melzi d'Eril Gardens

Meander through spectacular, statue-studded botanical gardens featuring wide gravel pathways that are highly manageable with an all-terrain stroller, offering plenty of shade under giant plane trees for baby's naptime.

Afternoon 2:00pm - 4:00pm (2h)

Villa del Balbianello

Access this world-famous villa via a calm, private water taxi from Lenno, allowing Will and Kathryn to admire the terraced gardens while Mary Claire enjoys the gentle, soothing vibrations of the lake.

Afternoon 3:00pm - 4:00pm (1h)

Duomo di Como

Step inside the breathtaking Gothic-Renaissance cathedral; its cool, cavernous interior is ideal for escaping the midday heat, and the quiet, reverent atmosphere acts as a natural lullaby.

Morning 9:00am - 11:00am (2h)

Castello di Vezio

Take a scenic baby carrier hike up to this medieval outpost for unparalleled panoramas of the lake's intersection; skip the stroller here due to stony paths, but enjoy the peaceful olive groves at the top.

Morning 8:30am - 11:30am (3h)

Abbazia di San Pietro al Monte

A gorgeous Romanesque abbey nestled in the hills above Civate; use a premium hiking baby carrier for this uphill trek, rewarding the family with pure serenity and lush, quiet picnic spots.

Afternoon 1:00pm - 1:45pm (45m)

Scenic Bellagio Ferry Ride

Board the large, stable public ferry for a breezy transit across the lake's golden triangle; the spacious indoor and outdoor decks allow you to park the stroller easily while enjoying the mountain views.

Late Afternoon 4:30pm - 6:00pm (1.5h)

Menaggio Lakeshore Promenade & Piazza Garibaldi

Push the stroller along one of the widest, most beautifully landscaped flower-lined boardwalks on the lake, ending in the pedestrian-friendly Piazza Garibaldi for an effortless evening gelato.

🍽️ Where to eat

Crotto dei Pescatori

Dine on thin-crust pizzas on a lakeside veranda that makes you feel like you are floating over the water; the gentle lake breeze and early dinner seating options are perfect for Mary Claire.

La Tana dei Pescatori

Tucked away near the historic center, this hidden gem serves fresh seafood in a quiet, cozy atmosphere where early evening slot reservations make dining with an infant relaxed.

Vecchia Magnolia

One of the finest dining spots in Menaggio, offering an intimate outdoor terrace with plenty of space to park a stroller next to your table while enjoying handmade pasta.

Ristorante Bilacus

Feast on elevated regional dishes on a beautiful, quiet garden terrace; the warm staff is exceptionally accommodating to families, happily setting up a highchair for Mary Claire.

Il Gatto Nero

Will and Kathryn can enjoy spectacular cliffside views and fine Italian wine at an early 7:00 PM seating, where the soft ambient music and upscale yet welcoming service keep baby relaxed.
