Lake Como

← back to travel board
Lake Como balances high-end Italian sophistication with surprisingly accessible, stroller-friendly lakefront promenades. Staying in flatter towns like Menaggio or Cernobbio keeps the logistics easy while offering stunning mountain backdrops. You can easily balance Michelin-caliber dining with mid-afternoon ferry rides and quiet garden walks. It is the ultimate playground for a finance-minded dad who demands seamless execution and zero travel friction.
🚕 Getting there: A 50-minute private Mercedes V-Class transfer from Milan Malpensa (MXP) straight to your hotel in Como or Cernobbio via the A8/A9 autostrada.

What to do

Morning / 2h

Villa Carlotta

Stroll through 17 acres of terraced botanic gardens filled with giant rhododendrons and citrus trees. Use the designated stroller-friendly gravel path route to avoid the steep stone stairs while enjoying the lake breeze.

Morning / 1.5h

Funicolare Como-Brunate

Ride the historic 1894 funicular railway up to the hilltop village of Brunate for sweeping views of the Swiss Alps. Fold your stroller easily before boarding and enjoy the smooth, quiet 7-minute ascent.

Afternoon / 2h

Giardini di Villa Melzi

Walk along the flat, shaded gravel pathways lined with ancient trees and lakeside statues. It is the most stroller-friendly garden on the lake, offering plenty of quiet benches for bottle feeding.

Morning / 1-2h

Como Lake Playground (Giardini a Lago)

Let your toddler run free at this spacious, fenced public playground featuring swings, slides, and grassy lawns right on the lake. Grab an espresso from the nearby kiosks while she burns off some energy.

Morning / 1.5h

Mercato Coperto di Como

Explore the bustling indoor municipal market to sample local bresaola, fresh berries, and warm focaccia. The wide, flat aisles are easy to navigate with a stroller during the quieter mid-morning hours.

Afternoon / 1h

Mid-Lake Car Ferry Route

Skip the bouncy private speedboats and roll your stroller directly onto the large, stable public car ferry for a scenic crossing. The indoor seating area has large windows, perfect for keeping a toddler calm and out of the wind.

Afternoon / 1.5h

Greenway del Lago di Como (Lenno to Campo)

Walk the flat, paved portion of this famous trail that winds past historic villas and quiet stone hamlets. This specific stretch avoids steep climbs, making it ideal for a leisurely afternoon stroller push.

Afternoon / 3h

Lido di Menaggio

Enjoy a relaxing afternoon at this family-friendly beach club featuring a dedicated toddler wading pool and a grassy lawn. It offers clean changing rooms, private cabanas, and easy stroller access right to the water's edge.

Morning / 1.5h

Orrido di Bellano

Walk along the secure, modern footbridges suspended over a dramatic natural gorge with roaring waterfalls. Ditch the stroller for a baby carrier here so you can navigate the narrow, high-railed steel walkways with ease.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ristorante Bilacus

Order the hand-pinched ravioli with sage butter while sitting in their secluded, high-walled garden terrace. The outdoor seating is spacious enough to park a stroller next to your table without blocking waiters.

La Baia di Moltrasio

Try the artisanal wood-fired pizzas and the classic perch risotto in a casual, highly welcoming atmosphere. Located directly opposite the Moltrasio ferry dock, it is ideal for an early 6:00 PM dinner before the bedtime routine.

Trattoria del Glicine

Savor the slow-cooked beef cheek and local wines under a romantic canopy of purple wisteria. The staff is incredibly accommodating to young families, gladly pureeing simple vegetables for your toddler.

Ristorante La Punta

Order the signature salt-crusted lake whitefish while sitting at the absolute tip of the Bellagio peninsula. The outdoor terrace is massive, meaning baby babble is easily drowned out by the gentle sound of the lapping lake waves.

Caffè Bellini

Stop by for a casual lunch of fresh Caprese salad and artisanal club sandwiches. It features a flat entry, highly spacious indoor seating, and very clean restrooms equipped with baby changing facilities.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Grand Hotel Tremezzo

An iconic luxury palace offering a floating pool, private sandy beach, and lush rear gardens perfect for morning stroller walks. They provide wooden cribs, bottle warmers, and top-tier in-room babysitting services.

Villa d'Este

A legendary, ultra-secure 25-acre estate featuring immaculate flat gravel paths, a heated pool over the lake, and world-class service. It offers spacious family suites, high chairs in all fine-dining venues, and peerless privacy.

Grand Hotel Victoria Concept & Spa

A beautifully renovated luxury hotel located in the flat, highly walkable town of Menaggio. It features a gorgeous indoor family pool, spacious modern rooms with luxury cribs, and is steps away from a quiet pedestrian promenade.

👨‍👩‍👧 Choose the mid-lake car ferries over small water taxis as they are incredibly stable, spacious, and allow you to roll your stroller directly on board without folding it. Stick to flat base towns like Menaggio, Cernobbio, or Como for your accommodation, as Bellagio and Varenna are filled with steep, cobblestone stairs that are brutal on strollers. Always pack a high-quality baby carrier for visiting historical villas and gorges where strollers are prohibited due to narrow, historic stone paths.
