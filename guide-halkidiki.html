Halkidiki's three distinct peninsulas offer a pristine coastline of shallow, crystal-clear waters flanked by aromatic pine forests. For Houston couple Will and Kathryn, the calm, wave-free waters of the Vourvourou lagoon in Sithonia provide the ultimate safe haven for baby Mary Claire's first ocean dips. Stroller-friendly paved marinas and flat beachfront promenades ensure seamless transitions from upscale lounging to seaside walks. This stunning destination blends rich Macedonian culture with refined, stress-free hospitality tailored perfectly for discerning parents and their infant.
🚕 Getting there: The transfer from Thessaloniki Airport (SKG) to the main luxury hotel strip in Kassandra or Sithonia takes approximately 60 to 75 minutes. The most seamless and comfortable option for a family traveling with an infant is a pre-booked private Mercedes V-Class transfer equipped with a sanitised, rear-facing Cybex infant car seat and ample trunk space for a large multi-terrain stroller.

What to do

Late afternoon 5-7pm

Sani Marina

A highly sophisticated, circular marina lined with luxury yachts, high-end boutiques, and smooth, flat boardwalks. It is exceptionally stroller-friendly and perfect for a breezy afternoon stroll with baby Mary Claire.

Morning 9-11am

Afitos Village

A charming, historic cliffside village overlooking the Toroneos Gulf. While the cobblestone streets require a sturdy stroller or baby carrier, the shaded stone plazas and quiet morning atmosphere offer a peaceful cultural retreat.

Early morning 8-10am

Karidi Beach

A famous beach in Vourvourou renowned for its shallow, warm, and wave-free waters. Shaded naturally by overhanging pine trees, it is the safest spot in Sithonia for an infant's first swim.

Morning 9-11am

Prosphoriou Tower

The imposing Byzantine tower dominating the waterfront of Ouranoupolis. The surrounding paved promenade is entirely flat and stroller-accessible, allowing parents to admire the historic gateway to Mount Athos without negotiating stairs.

Half-day trip 9am-1pm

Ammouliani Island

A tiny, tranquil island accessible by a short ferry ride or private boat charter from Tripiti. Renting a skippered rib boat with a canopy provides shaded, private access to calm sandy coves like Alikes Beach.

Morning 10am-12pm

Arnaia Historic Village

A picturesque mountain settlement known for its traditional Macedonian architecture and cooler climate. The stroller-friendly central square and quiet, shaded alleys offer a refreshing escape from the coastal midday heat.

Evening 6-8pm

Parthenonas Village

A beautifully restored historic stone village nestled on the slopes of Mount Itamos. The peaceful, vehicle-free paved paths are ideal for a scenic walk using a baby carrier while enjoying panoramic sunset views over the gulf.

Early morning 8-10am

Possidi Cape

A dramatic, sandy spit of land extending deep into the Aegean Sea. Due to the deep sand, navigate this natural wonder using a baby carrier in the early morning to enjoy peaceful breezes and uninterrupted coastal views.

Morning 10am-12pm

Aristotle's Park

An educational, interactive theme park located in the historic village of Stagira. The lush, pine-shaded paths are highly stroller-accessible, offering a peaceful, cool forest walk accompanied by the gentle ambient sounds of interactive instruments.

🍽️ Where to eat

Tomata Restaurant

Sophisticated Mediterranean fine dining located in Sani Marina, helmed by Chef Alexis Gastronomes. The spacious outdoor terrace easily accommodates high-end strollers, and the early 6:30 PM dinner seating ensures a quiet experience before baby's bedtime.

Boukadoura

A celebrated traditional tavern situated on a grassy cliff at Elia Beach. The outdoor garden tables allow Mary Claire to play on the soft grass while Will and Kathryn enjoy freshly caught seafood and local olive oil pairings.

Kritikos Gallery & Restaurants

A premier seafood institution in Ouranoupolis offering a refined waterfront dining experience. The highly accommodating staff can easily prepare pureed organic vegetables for Mary Claire, and the spacious seaside deck easily fits strollers.

Marina Seafood

An award-winning seafood restaurant overlooking the canal of Nea Potidea. It features a wide, flat outdoor deck that is highly stroller-friendly, providing a serene setting for an early family dinner with calm sea breezes.

Sousourada

A gourmet tavern in Afytos known for its creative take on local Greek dishes. The quiet, verdant garden terrace is situated away from the bustling pedestrian traffic, offering highchairs and a relaxed, welcoming environment for families.

Eagles Palace

$200-400/night

A prestigious family-friendly luxury resort near Ouranoupolis. Featuring a private, pristine sandy beach with dedicated stroller pathways, the hotel provides top-tier baby amenities including bottle warmers, high-quality wooden cribs, and spacious bungalows with private grassy gardens.
