What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Praia da Concha

This is Itacaré's most family-friendly beach, featuring exceptionally calm, wave-free waters and plenty of beachfront kiosks. Toddlers will love splashing in the gentle shallows while parents rent stand-up paddleboards.

Morning to Afternoon / 4h

Praia de Jeribucaçu and River

This stunning spot is where a fresh, calm river meets the ocean, offering the best of both worlds with warm, still water pools to wade in. Toddlers can safely splash in the river bend entirely away from the stronger ocean waves.

Late Afternoon / Sunset

Passarela da Vila Stroll

The charming, cobblestoned pedestrian street comes alive in the evening with artisan shops, street food, and acoustic music. It is flat enough for strollers and perfect for grabbing an early evening gelato.

Morning / 3h

Rio de Contas Canoe Trip

Glide along the calm, mangrove-lined river on a traditional wooden boat to explore hidden, quiet waterways. Toddlers will enjoy the smooth, breeze-filled boat ride without any rough ocean waves.

Morning (during low tide) / 2h

Praia do Resende Tide Pools

A gorgeous beach lined with coconut palms and a grassy lawn perfect for shaded picnics. At low tide, small rock pools form, creating natural, toddler-sized aquariums filled with tiny, colorful fish.

Evening / 1h

Orla Capoeira Circle

Watch the traditional Afro-Brazilian martial art dance performed by locals along the waterfront promenade. The energetic drums, singing, and acrobatic movements are highly engaging and hypnotic for toddlers.

Saturday Morning / 1-2h

Feira Livre Local Market

A vibrant open-air market bursting with colorful tropical fruits, local crafts, and friendly vendors. Toddlers can safely try sweet baby bananas and fresh, warm tapioca crepes.

Morning / 30 mins

Mirante do Serra Grande Lookout

Located just outside town, this stunning cliffside lookout offers panoramic views of the endless coastline. It has an easy, paved viewing platform where toddlers can sip fresh coconut water while you enjoy the view.

Afternoon / 2h

Praia da Tiririca Surf Watching

The epicenter of Itacaré's surf scene, featuring a grassy bank and a small playground right by the sand. While the waves are too strong for toddlers to swim, they will love playing in the playground and watching the surfers.

🍽️ Where to eat

Manga Rosa

Get the traditional Bahian Moqueca de Peixe, a fragrant fish stew simmered in coconut milk and dende oil that can easily be shared with kids.

Tio Gu Creperie

Order the sweet banana and Nutella crepe or a savory cheese crepe, served in a lively, kid-friendly outdoor seating area.

Cabana Corais

Grab a beachfront table and order fresh coconut water and crispy tapioca dice (dados de tapioca) while your toddler plays safely in the soft sand right next to your table.

Restaurante Jiquiriçá

Try their highly rated grilled fish fillet with sweet passion fruit sauce, which offers a mild, tropical flavor that appeals to little ones.

Gelato Gula

Treat the family to artisanal gelato made with local Amazonian and Bahian fruits like cupuaçu, graviola, and mango.

👨‍👩‍👧 Opt for a baby carrier instead of a stroller for beach trails, but keep a lightweight stroller handy for the flat Passarela da Vila in the evenings. Always pack high-SPF sunscreen and natural insect repellent, as the surrounding Atlantic forest means mosquitoes can emerge at dusk.
