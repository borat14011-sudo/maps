Normandy (Deauville/Trouville & Bayeux)

Normandy's glittering Côte Fleurie blends grand Parisian seaside heritage with the pastoral charm of historic Bayeux. Here, the legendary Les Planches boardwalk in Deauville offers a pristine, stroller-ready promenade right along the sweeping sandy beach, perfect for breezy morning walks with baby Mary Claire. The region's gentle pace allows for stress-free exploration of medieval wonders and chic marinas, free from the overwhelming crowds of southern resorts. From private beach cabins to quiet garden estates, this coastal haven provides a seamless, sophisticated retreat for discerning families.
🚕 Getting there: A 2-hour and 15-minute private Mercedes V-Class transfer from Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) directly to your Deauville hotel, fully equipped with a premium rear-facing infant car seat and ample space for a luxury stroller.

What to do

Early morning 9-11am / 2h

Les Planches de Deauville

Stroll along this iconic 1923 wooden boardwalk. It is completely flat and stroller-accessible, featuring beautiful beach cabins named after famous actors, offering a breezy, low-stimulation morning walk for baby.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Bayeux Tapestry Museum (Musée de la Tapisserie de Bayeux)

Marvel at the world-famous medieval embroidery. The museum is stroller-friendly with elevators, and they provide audioguides so you can walk at your own pace while Mary Claire naps in her carrier.

Afternoon 2-3:30pm / 1.5h

Cathédrale Notre-Dame de Bayeux

A towering masterpiece of Norman-Gothic architecture. The paved paths outside are smooth, and the cavernous, cool interior provides a quiet, calming atmosphere that naturally soothes a resting infant.

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Plage de Trouville

A wide, sandy beach known for its family-friendly vibe. Rent a classic striped beach tent for shade, allowing Mary Claire to play safely in the soft sand away from the direct sun.

Afternoon 3-4:30pm / 1.5h

Le Jardin de l'Évêché (Bayeux)

A beautifully manicured public park in Bayeux featuring a monumental weeping beech tree. Its smooth, paved walkways are perfect for pushing a stroller under the cooling canopy of ancient trees.

Late morning 10:30am-12:30pm / 2h

Parc des Graves (Villerville)

A scenic clifftop park just minutes from Deauville, offering panoramic ocean views and gentle grassy lawns. It is a quiet escape for a family picnic with plenty of shade and clean air for baby.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Marché de Deauville

The colorful open-air market at Place du Marché. Arrive early to easily maneuver your stroller through stalls of local cheeses and fresh cider, enjoying the vibrant sights and aromas.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Villa Strassburger

A magnificent 1907 regional mansion set in a lush park. While the interior has stairs, the sprawling, manicured estate gardens are highly stroller-accessible and offer a quiet, picturesque setting for family photos.

Late afternoon 5-6:30pm / 1.5h

Port de Plaisance de Deauville

The chic marina area, perfect for viewing luxury yachts. The wide, flat docks are completely paved and stroller-safe, making for a serene late-afternoon walk watching the boats glide by.

🍽️ Where to eat

Le Central (Trouville-sur-Mer)

A legendary brasserie famous for its fresh seafood platters and sole meunière. It offers a lively atmosphere that easily masks any baby noises, with outdoor terrace seating and highchairs available.

La Rapière (Bayeux)

Refined Norman cuisine tucked away in a historic 16th-century alley. Request an early evening seating in their cozy dining room, where the staff is exceptionally welcoming to families with infants.

Le 1912 (Deauville)

Michelin-starred dining overlooking the estuary. Dress up for an early dinner slot; the serene atmosphere and spacious table layouts easily accommodate a sleeping baby in a high-end carrier.

La Flambée (Deauville)

A warm, rustic restaurant known for wood-fired pizzas and grilled meats. Highly family-friendly with a spacious layout, highchairs, and an accommodating staff that serves early evening meals.

Le Drakkar (Deauville)

A stylish, bustling brasserie popular with locals. It features a spacious outdoor heated terrace, perfect for dining with a stroller, and a classic menu of steak frites and fresh oysters.
