Krakow

← back to travel board
Krakow wraps families in a storybook embrace, where horse-drawn carriages clatter across Europe's largest medieval square and a legendary dragon breathes real fire. Beyond the cobblestones, lush green parks circle the old town, offering a peaceful, stroller-friendly escape under a canopy of ancient trees. It is a walkable, budget-friendly wonderland where historic charm effortlessly meets modern family comfort.
🚕 Getting there: ~25-30 min taxi or private transfer directly to Old Town hotels, or a quick 20-min train ride from Krakow Airport to the Main Station.

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Wawel Dragon's Den and Castle Courtyard

Kids will love waiting for the famous bronze dragon statue to breathe real fire every few minutes, followed by an easy stroll through the majestic castle courtyard. Toddler note: The dragon is exciting but can startle sensitive toddlers with its sudden roar.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Planty Park Loop

This lush green belt circles the entire Old Town, offering shady walking paths, playgrounds, and ice cream stalls away from city traffic. Toddler note: Extremely stroller-friendly with plenty of benches for quick snack breaks.

Morning / 1.5h

Rynek Underground Museum

Travel back to the Middle Ages in this high-tech underground museum beneath the main square, featuring interactive touchscreens and holograms. Toddler note: The dim lighting and sounds are exciting, but keep toddlers close as it can get crowded.

Morning / 3-4h

Krakow Zoological Gardens

Nestled inside the beautiful Wolski Forest, this cozy zoo is home to pygmy hippos, penguins, and a petting zoo perfect for little hands. Toddler note: Rent a wooden pull-cart at the entrance to keep tired toddlers rolling happily.

Midday / 30m

St. Mary's Basilica Trumpet Call

Gather in the Main Square to watch the trumpeter wave from the highest tower after playing the traditional hourly tune. Toddler note: Keep the little ones entertained in the open square with giant bubbles sold by street performers while waiting.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Krakus Mound (Kopiec Krakusa)

Climb this prehistoric grassy mound for panoramic views of the city and plenty of space for toddlers to safely run and roll down the gentle slopes. Toddler note: Skip the stroller and opt for a baby carrier, as the path to the top is unpaved and grassy.

Morning / 1h

Stary Kleparz Food Market

Krakow's oldest active marketplace is a sensory delight of colorful fresh berries, local cheeses, and warm pastries. Toddler note: Grab a bag of fresh obwarzanki (Krakow bagels) to keep little fingers busy snacking.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Stanislaw Lem Garden of Experiences

An outdoor sensory science park where kids can touch, spin, and play with giant interactive physics installations in a green park. Toddler note: Features a wonderful sensory maze and sand play areas that toddlers will absolutely adore.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Vistula River Boulevards & Boat Cruise

Walk or bike along the paved river paths to spot swimming swans, or board a gentle 1-hour sightseeing boat. Toddler note: The flat, car-free concrete paths are perfect for toddler scooters or stroller naps.

🍽️ Where to eat

Pierogarnia Mr Vincent

Try the sweet strawberry or blueberry pierogi topped with sweet cream, which double as a perfect kid-approved lunch.

Pod Aniołami

Feast on traditional roasted meats and potato dumplings in a magical, candle-lit medieval cellar that feels like a castle.

Cafe Szafe

Enjoy a warm babyccino or hot chocolate in this whimsical, colorful cafe filled with wardrobes, books, and quirky toys.

Cudowne Lata

Grab a cozy table to enjoy comforting, home-style Polish chicken noodle soup (rosół) and potato pancakes.

Chimney Cake Bakery

Treat the family to warm, spiral-shaped Hungarian chimney cakes coated in cinnamon sugar and filled with ice cream.

👨‍👩‍👧 Most of Krakow's Old Town is paved with uneven cobblestones, so opt for a sturdy stroller with good suspension or a baby carrier. Keep a handful of 2-złoty coins ready, as many public restrooms and park facilities require small change for entry.
