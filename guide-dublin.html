What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

St. Stephen's Green

A magnificent Victorian park in the heart of Dublin featuring wide, paved stroller-accessible pathways, scenic duck ponds, and shaded lawns perfect for Mary Claire's mid-day nap.

Morning 10am-1pm / 3h

Dublin Zoo in Phoenix Park

One of the world's oldest and most prestigious zoos, offering completely flat, paved walkways, expansive animal habitats, and plenty of quiet, shaded family rest areas.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

National Gallery of Ireland

A world-class art museum featuring spacious, quiet, climate-controlled galleries, stroller-accessible elevators, and dedicated nursing rooms perfect for a peaceful afternoon.

Early morning 9-10:30am / 1.5h

Trinity College Library and Book of Kells Experience

Marvel at the historic Long Room via a fully accessible, stroller-friendly ramp entrance; the low-lit, quiet atmosphere is excellent for keeping an infant calm.

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Merrion Square Park

A refined Georgian square boasting beautiful floral gardens, wide gravel paths, and a colorful, secure playground suitable for gentle, supervised infant play.

Morning 10am-12:30pm / 2.5h

Airfield Estate

A beautiful 38-acre working urban farm and gardens in Dundrum, highly accessible via the Luas tram, featuring smooth sensory paths, gentle farm animals, and baby-changing facilities.

Afternoon 1-3pm / 2h

National Botanic Gardens

An exquisite, serene escape in Glasnevin offering massive restored historic glasshouses, completely flat paved loops, and a calm, quiet atmosphere for stroller walks.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

EPIC The Irish Emigration Museum

An award-winning, fully interactive museum housed in a modern, spacious building with wide corridors, completely stroller-accessible layouts, and peaceful family restrooms.

Morning 9:30am-12:30pm / 3h

Malahide Castle Demesne and Gardens

Take a short coastal drive to enjoy the sweeping, manicured lawns, flat wooded walking trails, and a magical, quiet walled garden ideal for a family picnic.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Woollen Mills

Savor their famous Irish lamb stew or fresh seafood on the spacious, heated outdoor terrace overlooking the River Liffey; they offer plenty of highchairs and welcoming early-evening dining.

Fallon & Byrne (The Dining Room)

An upscale culinary destination serving exceptional dry-aged ribeye; the elegant dining room is highly spacious, accommodating stroller placement easily during their quieter early dinner service.

The Winding Stair

Indulge in classic Irish artisan cheeses and slow-cooked pork belly in a historic bookstore setting; book an early 5:00 PM seating for a peaceful, highchair-equipped experience.

Saba (South William Street)

Enjoy award-winning Thai and Vietnamese cuisine in a vibrant, spacious dining room where the ambient hum easily masks any baby noises; excellent highchairs and wide booths are available.

Avoca Suffolk Street Café

Perfect for a high-end lunch featuring fresh seasonal salads and house-baked pastries; the café is fully accessible via a spacious elevator, with plenty of highchairs and attentive staff.
