Funchal

← back to travel board
Funchal climbs a dramatic volcanic amphitheater, offering families a sun-drenched European escape with a lush, tropical soul. Here, cobblestone streets wind past vibrant fruit markets, while modern cable cars sweep over terraced hills down to the sparkling Atlantic. It is a playground of botanical wonders and ocean breezes, perfectly scaled for little feet and curious minds.
🚕 Getting there: ~20-25 minute taxi ride or Aerobus transfer from Madeira Airport (FNC) to central Funchal hotels.

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Monte Cable Car (Teleférico do Funchal)

Glide above the red-tiled roofs of Funchal up to the lush hills of Monte. Toddlers will love looking out the large panoramic windows, and the spacious cabins easily accommodate folded strollers.

Morning / 2-3h

Monte Palace Tropical Garden

A stunning hillside garden featuring koi ponds, oriental bridges, and colorful exotic plants. It is a sensory wonderland for toddlers, though parents should prepare to push the stroller up some steep paved slopes.

Afternoon / 1.5h

Madeira Story Centre

This interactive museum in the Old Town brings Madeira's history to life with sights, sounds, and hands-on exhibits. It is highly engaging for young kids and fully stroller-accessible with elevators.

Morning / 1h

Mercado dos Lavradores (Farmers' Market)

A colorful, bustling market filled with exotic fruits like passionfruit-bananas and beautiful flower stalls. Toddlers will love tasting the sweet fruit samples, but visit early in the morning to beat the crowds.

Morning / 1h

Botanical Garden Cable Car (Teleférico do Jardim Botânico)

Take this second, quieter cable car line across a dramatic wild valley directly into the Botanical Garden. It is a thrilling ride that spares little legs the steep hillside climbs.

Morning / 2h

Madeira Botanical Garden (Jardim Botânico da Madeira)

Home to thousands of exotic plants and a dedicated bird park with colorful parrots. It offers plenty of open, terraced space for toddlers to explore, though some paths are gravel.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Santa Catarina Park

A sprawling green park overlooking the harbor with a fantastic fenced playground and a duck pond. It is the perfect spot for toddlers to burn off energy while parents enjoy the coastal views.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Lido Promenade and Pools (Complexo Balnear do Lido)

A scenic coastal path leading to a seawater pool complex with a dedicated, shallow toddler splash pool. The flat, paved promenade is ideal for easy stroller walks with sea breezes.

Morning / 2.5h

Dolphin and Whale Watching Boat Tour

Board a spacious catamaran from Funchal Marina to spot wild dolphins and whales swimming in the Atlantic. Many boats have covered indoor seating and bathrooms, making it comfortable for families with toddlers.

🍽️ Where to eat

O Tasco

Order the traditional hot 'bolo do caco' (garlic butter flatbread) and fresh grilled limpets in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere.

Restaurante Santa Maria

Try the kid-friendly black scabbard fish with sweet fried banana in this spacious, art-filled dining room located in the Old Town.

Il Vivaldi

Treat the family to handmade pasta and fresh seafood on a spacious outdoor terrace overlooking the harbor.

Kano

Get the traditional espetada (garlic-rubbed beef skewers cooked over laurel wood) which is served hanging from table stands.

Confeitaria Felisberta

Stop by this beloved local bakery for freshly baked pastel de nata and traditional Madeira honey cake that toddlers will love.

👨‍👩‍👧 Funchal's iconic mosaic sidewalks can be incredibly slippery when wet and bumpy for small wheels, so opt for a sturdy, all-terrain stroller or a baby carrier. When dining out, remember that many casual spots serve food all day, making it easy to feed toddlers before the typical European dinner rush.
