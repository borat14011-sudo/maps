What to do

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Lake Hallstatt Boat Cruise

Enjoy a gentle, whisper-quiet cruise across the fjord-like lake, offering stunning panoramic views of the historic town while baby Mary Claire relaxes in the enclosed, climate-controlled luxury cabin. The vessels are completely level, making stroller boarding smooth and stress-free.

Early morning 9-11am / 2h

Hallstatt Skywalk Welterbeblick

Ride the smooth Salzbergbahn funicular up to this dramatic viewing platform, which is fully stroller-accessible and offers breathtaking alpine vistas, crisp mountain air, and quiet morning walking paths.

Early morning 8-9am / 1h

Classic Hallstatt Viewpoint

Stroll along the pedestrian-only Gosaumühlstraße to capture the iconic postcard shot of Hallstatt early in the morning before crowds arrive, ensuring a peaceful walk for a sleeping infant in her carrier.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Lake Gosau Stroller Loop

Walk the flat, paved, stroller-friendly loop around this pristine alpine lake, offering dramatic views of the Dachstein Glacier and plenty of shaded benches for relaxed infant feedings.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Kaiservilla and Kaiserpark in Bad Ischl

Explore the manicured gravel pathways of the former summer residence of Emperor Franz Joseph, where wide, shade-drenched avenues are perfect for long stroller walks amidst imperial gardens.

Midday 12-2pm / 2h

Wolfgangsee Ferry Ride

Board the elegant paddle steamer on nearby Lake Wolfgangsee, featuring spacious, flat decks that easily accommodate luxury strollers while providing a soothing, cool breeze.

Afternoon 3-4:30pm / 1.5h

Bad Ischl Esplanade

Wander along the elegant Traun river promenade lined with grand historic villas, offering a flat, paved, and exceptionally quiet route ideal for an afternoon infant nap.

Morning 9-11:30am / 2.5h

Dachstein Krippenstein Cable Car

Ascend to alpine heights in a spacious, modern cable car cabin that easily fits a stroller, allowing the family to enjoy crisp mountain air and panoramic viewing decks without strenuous hiking.

Afternoon 1-3pm / 2h

Lake Altaussee Promenade

Walk the level, scenic path hugging the shores of Lake Altaussee, renowned for its crystal-clear waters, peaceful atmosphere, and lack of crowds, making it ideal for a relaxed family afternoon.

Café Konditorei Zauner

Gasthof Zauner Seewirt
