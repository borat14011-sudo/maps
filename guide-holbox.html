Car-free island paradise made for slow family adventures

Holbox

← back to travel board
Isla Holbox is a dreamlike car-free sanctuary where gentle golf carts navigate soft sandy streets and calm, waist-deep turquoise waters stretch as far as the eye can see. This tranquil Mexican Caribbean island offers an incredibly relaxed pace perfect for young families seeking nature without the mainland crowds. Toddlers will delight in hunting for pastel seashells, spotting pink flamingos, and splashing in wave-free shores.
🚕 Getting there: ~2 hour private shuttle from Cancun Airport to Chiquila port, followed by a scenic 25-minute ferry ride and a 10-minute golf cart taxi to your hotel

What to do

Late Afternoon / 2-3h

Punta Cocos Sunset and Bio-luminescence

This quiet beach offers shallow, wave-free waters for toddler daytime wading, turning into a magical glowing playground for bioluminescence viewing after dark.

Morning / 3h

Yalahau Cenote Boat Trip

A refreshing freshwater spring surrounded by lush mangroves that features a shallow swimming area and a wooden lookout tower. Toddlers will love the gentle boat ride over from the main island.

Early Morning / 2-3h

Punta Mosquito Sandbar Walk

Walk along a magical, ankle-deep sandbar in the middle of the ocean where wild pink flamingos gather. Keep toddler walks short or bring a carrier as there is absolutely no shade.

Afternoon / 2h

Golf Cart Island Exploration

Rent a private golf cart to navigate the bumpy, unpaved sandy streets, which is an exciting and breezy ride in itself for little kids. Strap toddlers securely in your lap or a carrier during the ride.

Morning / 1-2h

Holbox Street Art Tour

Wander around the colorful downtown area to spot massive, vibrant murals painted on nearly every building facade. Toddlers will enjoy pointing out the colorful depictions of animals and local faces.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Playa Holbox Hammocks

Relax in the iconic colorful water hammocks while your toddler plays safely in the incredibly calm, warm shoreline. It is the ultimate spot for low-energy parent relaxation.

Morning / 2h

Isla Pasion (Passion Island)

A tiny, peaceful islet reachable by a short boat tour, offering calm knee-deep wading areas and views of nesting wild birds. It is ideal for toddlers who love gentle boat rides and watching seagulls.

Afternoon / 1h

Refugio Holbox Animal Sanctuary

A heartwarming volunteer-run shelter where families can visit rescued dogs, cats, raccoons, and birds. Toddlers can gently pet the friendly resident animals and learn about island wildlife conservation.

Evening / 1-2h

Plaza Principal Playground

The local playground in the town center comes alive in the evening with local families, toy vendors, and street food. It is a fantastic, safe spot for toddlers to burn off energy and socialize.

🍽️ Where to eat

Roots Pizza

Get the famous wood-fired lobster pizza while sitting in a bohemian, sand-floored garden courtyard that keeps kids happily playing in the sand.

El Guacamole

Order the freshly prepared table-side guacamole and the mild, grilled fish tacos which are healthy and easy to share with toddlers.

Painapol

Get the beautifully presented, nutrient-packed fresh fruit smoothie bowls served in coconut shells that toddlers will love eating with a spoon.

Luuma

Enjoy gourmet Mexican tapas in an upscale, open-air sandy garden with plenty of space for toddlers to explore under the twinkling string lights.

Alma Bar

Head to this rooftop spot for mocktails and pool splashing, where you can order delicious, kid-approved quesadillas with panoramic beach views.

👨‍👩‍👧 Bring plenty of cash and reef-safe mosquito repellent, as island ATMs frequently run out of money and mosquitoes thrive near the mangrove wetlands. Opt for a sturdy baby carrier instead of a stroller, as the unpaved sandy streets are bumpy and prone to large puddles after rain.
