What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM / 1.5h

Templo de Santo Domingo de Guzmán

This iconic 16th-century baroque church features a spacious, flat stone plaza perfect for stroller walks. Arrive in the quiet morning to admire the gilded interior while baby enjoys the cool, shaded stone sanctuary.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 1.5h

Jardín Etnobotánico de Oaxaca

A stunning botanical garden showcasing native cacti and flora. Book a private family tour to navigate the flat gravel paths at your own pace; the serene, architectural green spaces are incredibly photogenic and peaceful for a sleeping baby in a carrier.

Late Afternoon 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM / 1.5h

Andador Turístico (Macedonio Alcalá)

This pedestrian-only cobblestone street is paved with smoother, flat green quarry stone flags, making it the best thoroughfare in the city for a luxury stroller walk past colorful galleries and historic mansions.

Midday 11:30 AM - 1:30 PM / 2h

Museo de las Culturas de Oaxaca

Housed in a former monastery next to Santo Domingo, this museum features wide, ramped stone corridors and thick walls that keep the interior naturally cool—an ideal escape from the midday sun for Mary Claire.

Early Morning 8:30 AM - 10:00 AM / 1.5h

Barrio de Jalatlaco

A quiet, artistic neighborhood with brightly colored walls, murals, and cobblestone streets. It is far less crowded than the main tourist center, offering a peaceful, slow-paced morning walk with plenty of shade.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 1.5h

Mercado Orgánico El Pochote

A small, tranquil organic market under shaded trees. It is much quieter and cleaner than the massive central markets, making it a stress-free spot to enjoy fresh fruit juices while baby relaxes in a highchair or carrier.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 2h

Árbol del Tule (Santa María del Tule)

A short private drive leads to this park hosting the world's stoutest tree trunk. The surrounding manicured gardens feature flat, paved, stroller-friendly paths and quiet grassy lawns perfect for a family portrait.

Morning 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 3h

Teotitlán del Valle Artisan Visit

Take a private SUV day trip to a master weaver's family home like Centro de Arte de Teotitlán. The spacious, open-air courtyards are highly welcoming to children, allowing baby to watch the colorful natural dye process in a calm environment.

Late Afternoon 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM / 1.5h

Zócalo (Plaza de la Constitución)

The vibrant central plaza is lined with massive laurel trees that provide deep shade. Take a seat on a stone bench to watch balloon vendors and enjoy the marimba musicians; it is highly sensory but open and airy.

🍽️ Where to eat

Criollo

Created by Chef Enrique Olvera, this culinary hotspot operates out of a beautifully renovated historic home. The stunning, sandy open-air courtyard garden is spacious enough for a stroller and provides a tranquil, relaxed atmosphere for an early family dinner.

Casa Oaxaca El Restaurante

Famed for its tableside salsa service and views of Santo Domingo. Reserve a ground-floor courtyard table rather than the rooftop for easy stroller access, and request an early 6:00 PM seating for top-tier service and a highly welcoming attitude toward Mary Claire.

Las Quince Letras

An upscale institution celebrating traditional Oaxacan mole. The interior courtyard is quiet, spacious, and features comfortable highchairs and highly attentive staff who are famously accommodating to families traveling with babies.

Origen

Chef Rodolfo Castellanos offers refined modern Oaxacan cuisine in a stylish, serene second-floor dining room. The staff goes above and beyond to accommodate infant needs, offering quiet corner tables and custom purees upon request.

Alfonsina

Located just outside the city center, this exclusive dining experience set in a tranquil, open-air residential courtyard offers an exceptionally relaxed, intimate setting where baby can happily babble without disturbing typical fine-dining crowds.

A highly rated luxury boutique hotel offering grand, spacious suites with private terraces. They provide top-of-the-line cribs, incredibly comfortable bedding, and stellar soundproofing, situated just steps from Santo Domingo for effortless midday nap returns.
