Hand-in-hand, your family can explore traffic-free streets, cargo-bike through lush parks, and discover a city built specifically for little adventurers. It is a clean, safe, and utterly magical destination where toddler-friendly hygge is a way of life.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min metro ride or a 20 min taxi from Copenhagen Airport (CPH) directly to the city center.

What to do

Afternoon to Evening / 3-4h

Tivoli Gardens

The world's oldest amusement park offers whimsical rides, historic gardens, and magical lights at night. Toddler note: Little ones will love the vintage car ride and the Petzi's World playground.

Morning / 1-2h

The King's Garden & Rosenborg Castle

Run around the sprawling lawns of Denmark's oldest royal garden before viewing a real renaissance castle. Toddler note: There is an excellent, artistic wooden playground right inside the gardens.

Late Morning / 1h

Nyhavn Canal Tour

Glide past colorful 17th-century townhouses and historic wooden ships on a classic canal boat tour. Toddler note: Choose an open-top boat on a sunny day to keep squirmy toddlers easily entertained by passing ducks.

Midday / 1-2h

Torvehallerne Food Market

A bustling glass-market offering everything from fresh Danish pastries to open-faced sandwiches. Toddler note: Grab an outdoor bench where kids can eat casually without worrying about indoor restaurant etiquette.

Morning / 3h

Copenhagen Zoo

Meet polar bears, pandas, and elephants in one of Europe's oldest and most modern zoos. Toddler note: The dedicated children's zoo features a petting area with friendly African dwarf goats.

Morning / 2-3h

The Blue Planet (Den Blå Planet)

Northern Europe's largest aquarium is a stunning, whirlpool-shaped building right by the sea. Toddler note: The hands-on touch pools and outdoor water playground are absolute hits for toddlers.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Superkilen Park

An urban park in the diverse Nørrebro neighborhood featuring vibrant red, black, and green zones with global design elements. Toddler note: The undulating slides and climbing hills are perfect for burning off toddler energy.

Morning / 2h

Fælledparken Traffic Playground

A fantastic public park featuring a famous traffic playground where kids can learn road rules on miniature streets. Toddler note: You can rent tiny pedal cars or bring a stroller to navigate the miniature stoplights.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Bakken Amusement Park (Dyrehaven)

Located in a historic deer park just north of the city, this is the world's oldest operating amusement park with free entry. Toddler note: Combine rides with a picnic under ancient oak trees where wild deer roam freely.

🍽️ Where to eat

Restaurant Schønnemann

Indulge in traditional, high-quality Danish smørrebrød (open-faced sandwiches) in a historic, welcoming basement setting.

Juno The Bakery

Treat the family to their legendary cardamom buns and seasonal pastries that are well worth the morning queue.

Gasoline Grill

Grab some of the world's best smash burgers served out of a retro, functioning gas station for an easy, delicious kid-friendly lunch.

Pizzeria MaMeMi

Enjoy thin-crust, Roman-style gourmet pizzas crafted with organic ingredients in a casual, highly family-welcoming atmosphere.

Hahnemanns Køkken

Visit this cozy, organic eatery in Østerbro for delicious rye bread sandwiches, fresh salads, and a spacious outdoor seating area perfect for kids.
