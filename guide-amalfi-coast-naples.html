What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Villa Comunale Park

This tranquil, shaded public park in Sorrento offers breathtaking cliffside views of Mount Vesuvius and the Bay of Naples. The paved paths are entirely stroller-accessible, and the gentle sea breezes make it a soothing spot for Mary Claire to enjoy a mid-morning nap under the shade of mature palm trees.

Morning 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Private Gozzo Boat Charter by Exclusive Cruises

Charter a traditional wooden gozzo boat fully equipped with a shaded sun canopy, cushioned lounge deck, and air-conditioned cabin. This allows Will and Kathryn to glide along the Amalfi shoreline while Mary Claire rests comfortably out of the sun, lulled by the gentle rhythm of the Mediterranean waters.

Afternoon 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM

Cloister of San Francesco

This 14th-century monastery in Sorrento features a remarkably quiet, atmospheric courtyard filled with beautiful greenery and climbing vines. It is completely stroller-accessible, offering a serene, cool, and silent retreat where you can escape the midday sun and enjoy a peaceful moment together.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:30 PM

Naples National Archaeological Museum

Explore one of the world's finest collections of Greco-Roman antiquities inside grand, climate-controlled galleries. The museum features smooth, flat marble floors and spacious elevator access, making it highly stroller-friendly and an excellent air-conditioned afternoon activity for the whole family.

Late Afternoon 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Lungomare Caracciolo

This expansive, pedestrian-only waterfront promenade in Naples is completely flat and paved, making it ideal for an effortless stroller walk. Will and Kathryn can enjoy panoramic views of Castel dell'Ovo and the sea while Mary Claire is entertained by the gentle coastal sights and refreshing maritime breeze.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Royal Palace of Naples

Stroll through the majestic apartments and grand courtyards of the Palazzo Reale, which is fully equipped with elevators for strollers. The spacious, quiet corridors and fascinating historical displays provide a highly cultured, low-stress indoor experience with plenty of room to move.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Villa Rufolo Gardens

Located in the quiet mountaintop town of Ravello, these historic gardens feature stunning multi-level terraces overlooking the sea. While there are some steps, the main garden paths are wide and stroller-friendly, boasting lush floral displays and plenty of shaded benches perfect for a quiet feeding session.

Evening 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM

Piazza Tasso

The vibrant heart of Sorrento is a flat, pedestrian-friendly square lined with historic cafes and beautiful architecture. It is the perfect spot for a relaxed evening stroll where the local family-friendly atmosphere means Mary Claire will be warmly welcomed by the locals.

Morning 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Bagni Salvatore Beach Club

Access this historic Sorrento beach club via a convenient cliffside elevator, completely bypassing the steep stairs. Will and Kathryn can relax under a shaded umbrella on the private platform while Mary Claire dips her toes into the calm, shallow volcanic tide pools safely away from the waves.

🍽️ Where to eat

Terrazza Bosquet

Located inside the Grand Hotel Excelsior Vittoria, this Michelin-starred restaurant features a spacious outdoor terrace overlooking the sea. They offer excellent highchairs, spacious seating arrangements, and a welcoming staff who accommodate early evening dining requests for families.

Ristorante Bagni Delfino

Set on a wooden pier in Marina Grande, this historic seafood restaurant offers magnificent panoramic views and incredibly fresh catch-of-the-day dishes. The spacious outdoor seating, soothing sound of lapping waves, and exceptionally warm Italian hospitality make it highly accommodating for infants.

Don Alfonso 1890

This world-renowned Relais & Châteaux restaurant in Sant'Agata offers a refined, farm-to-table organic tasting menu. The lush, spacious garden terrace provides a calm, quiet atmosphere and plenty of space to wheel in a stroller for an elegant early-dinner seating.

Adamo ed Eva Restaurant

Perched high in Positano inside Hotel Pasitea, this restaurant offers stunning vertical views with the convenience of elevator access. The outdoor terrace is spacious enough for a stroller, and the early dinner hours feature a relaxed, quiet ambiance perfect for a sleeping baby.

Ristorante Lorelei

This exquisite fine-dining restaurant in Sorrento offers a stunning cliffside terrace and gourmet Mediterranean cuisine. The spacious layout easily accommodates a highchair, and the gentle coastal breeze creates a tranquil environment where Mary Claire can rest while you dine.
