What to do

Early morning 8:30-10am

Santuario di Santa Maria dell'Isola

This iconic cliffside church is spectacular, but has steep steps; use a premium Artipoppe carrier instead of a stroller to explore the lush, shady gardens at the top.

Late afternoon 5-7pm

Corso Vittorio Emanuele

A completely flat, paved boulevard ideal for a smooth stroller ride while shopping high-end Italian linen or enjoying a quiet evening passeggiata with the family.

Morning 9-11:30am

Spiaggia di Grotticelle

A stunning bay with crystal-clear, calm waters and private lido beach club setups with premium umbrellas to shield baby Mary Claire from the midday sun.

Early evening 6:30-7:30pm

Belvedere Piazza Cannone

A wide, paved viewing platform offering panoramic coastal vistas with zero stairs, making it perfectly stroller-accessible for family sunset photos.

Morning 10-11:30am

Piazza Ercole

The vibrant heart of Tropea's historic center, wide open and pedestrian-only, ideal for letting Mary Claire look around from the stroller while you enjoy espresso.

Afternoon 3-4:30pm

Faro di Capo Vaticano

A scenic lighthouse with flat, paved coastal pathways offering dramatic cliffside views and cooling sea breezes without any strenuous climbing.

Day trip 10am-2pm

Chianalea di Scilla

Known as the Venice of the South, this nearby fishing village features flat, paved boardwalks directly over the water, perfect for a peaceful coastal stroll.

Afternoon 2-4pm

Piazza della Repubblica in Pizzo

A spacious, car-free square famous for Tartufo gelato, easily accessible for strollers with plenty of shade under elegant restaurant umbrellas.

Morning 9-11am

Spiaggia della Rotonda

Located right below the Tropea cliff, this beach has private beach club options with direct stroller-ramp access and ultra-gentle, shallow surf.

🍽️ Where to eat

Alice Restaurant

Upscale seafood in a spacious, quiet garden setting that easily accommodates luxury strollers and offers a peaceful ambiance for early dining. Signature dish: Handmade sea bass ravioli.

Ristorante La Lamia

Refined Calabrian dining on a breezy outdoor terrace with spacious seating, welcoming staff, and highchairs available on request. Signature dish: Fileja pasta with Tropea sweet onions.

Ristorante Stromboli

Beachfront luxury dining at Capo Vaticano featuring a relaxed terrace where ocean waves mask baby sounds, offering early 7:00 PM seating. Signature dish: Grilled local swordfish with citrus glaze.

Osteria del Pescatore

A highly-rated seafood gem with a spacious outdoor pedestrian patio, making it easy to park the stroller beside the table. Signature dish: Paccheri with fresh blue lobster.

Ristorante Peccato di Gola

Elegant local spot with a quiet, shaded outdoor veranda, extremely accommodating to families with infants and offering highchairs. Signature dish: Tuna tataki with caramelized red onion marmalade.
