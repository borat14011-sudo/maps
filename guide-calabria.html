🚕 Getting there: ~50 min taxi or rental car drive from Lamezia Terme International Airport (SUF) directly to Tropea beach hotels.

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Santuario di Santa Maria dell'Isola

Climb the iconic monastery on the rock for breathtaking panoramic views of the Coast of the Gods. Toddlers will love the Mediterranean garden at the top, but use a baby carrier as the stone stairs are too steep for strollers.

Morning / 3-4h

Spiaggia della Rotonda

Relax on this pristine beach directly beneath Tropea’s dramatic cliffside town. The shallow, crystal-clear water and soft sand make it perfect for toddlers to splash safely and dig for shells.

Late Afternoon / 2h

Tropea Historic Center Stroll

Wander through the car-free alleys of the old town, discovering charming piazzas and dramatic sea overlooks. It is stroller-friendly in most parts, offering toddlers a safe space to run around away from traffic.

Morning / 3-4h

Capo Vaticano Beach (Grotticelle)

Take a short drive to these famous cove beaches surrounded by granite cliffs and lush vegetation. The calm tide pools are wonderful for tiny toddlers to wade in while observing small fish.

Morning / 1-2h

Tropea Onion & Local Market Experience

Explore the vibrant local markets filled with braids of famous, sweet red onions and local fruits. Toddlers will love the bright colors and tasting the naturally sweet, mild raw onions.

Afternoon / 3h

Pizzo Calabro Tartufo Tasting

Take an easy day trip to nearby Pizzo Calabro, a beautiful fishing village with a flat, kid-friendly main piazza. Reward your little ones with the town's famous Tartufo, a decadent, melting chocolate-hazelnut gelato bomb.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Villa Comunale Park

Take a peaceful break in this shaded public park perched right on the cliff's edge. It features a simple playground, green grass, and benches where parents can enjoy the sea breeze while toddlers play.

Morning / 2-3h

Coast of the Gods Family Boat Tour

Embark on a gentle, sheltered boat tour along the coastline to spot caves and swim in turquoise lagoons. Choose a morning departure with canopy shade so toddlers can comfortably watch the fish swim under the clear water.

Morning / 2h

Zungri Stone City Caves

Explore this ancient rock-cut settlement just outside of Tropea, featuring real cave dwellings carved into the mountain. Toddlers will find these 'fairy-tale caves' magical to explore, though a baby carrier is highly recommended.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ristorante Pizzeria Vecchio Forno

Get the classic Margherita pizza baked in a traditional wood-fired oven, which is always a massive hit with toddlers.

Osteria del Pescatore

Order the fresh local tuna cooked with sweet Tropea red onions for a sweet, savory, and incredibly tender bite.

Gelateria Tonino

Try the famous sweet onion gelato for yourself, and grab a classic, creamy fior di latte cone for the kids.

La Lamia

Order the homemade fileja pasta, a traditional rolled Calabrian noodle that is easy for toddlers to hold and eat.

Alice Restaurant

Get the crisp, lightly battered fritto misto (fried seafood) basket, which offers a fun, finger-food experience for the whole family.

👨‍👩‍👧 Bring a sturdy baby carrier because Tropea's historic steps and cliffside descents are not stroller-friendly. Always keep some small cash on hand for beach umbrella rentals and quick gelato stops.
