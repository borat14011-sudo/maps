Black Sea Coast

← back to travel board
Where the Danube meets the tide, Romania's Black Sea Coast pairs endless shallow-water beaches with vibrant, ancient history. From the lively plazas of Constanța to the wild sands of Corbu, it is an easygoing haven for families seeking relaxation and discovery. Toddlers will love the soft, fine sand, while parents appreciate the warm, shallow waters and welcoming local culture.
🚕 Getting there: ~30 min taxi or shuttle from Mihail Kogălniceanu International Airport to Constanța and Mamaia beach hotels

What to do

Late Afternoon / 1h

Constanța Casino Promenade

Admire this stunning Art Nouveau masterpiece right on the breezy clifftop promenade. It is stroller-friendly and perfect for a scenic walk where toddlers can safely watch the waves crash against the sea wall.

Morning / 2h

Dolphinarium Constanța

Enjoy the lively dolphin and sea lion shows, coupled with a small kid-friendly micro-reserve zoo next door. It's a huge hit for animal-loving toddlers, though booking tickets online in advance is highly recommended.

Morning / 3-4h

Mamaia Beach

Dig in the ultra-fine, golden sand of this famous barrier beach boasting exceptionally wide shores and very shallow, gentle waters. It is absolutely perfect for toddler paddling and safe sandcastle building.

Afternoon / 30m

Mamaia Telegondola

Glide high above the resort in this aerial tramway for thrilling panoramic views of the sea and Lake Siutghiol. The fully enclosed cabins are highly secure and exciting for toddlers.

Evening / 2h

Constanța Historic Old Town

Walk around Ovidiu Square, a pedestrianized zone rich with history, Roman mosaics, and lively street performers. Toddlers can safely run around the open plaza while parents enjoy a coffee.

Midday / 1.5h

Museum of National History and Archeology

Explore ancient Greek and Roman relics inside a beautiful building in Ovidiu Square. The cool, quiet galleries are great for a midday sensory break, and toddlers will love the large, colorful mosaic exhibits.

Morning / 3-4h

Plaja Corbu

Discover a pristine, wild beach within the Danube Delta Biosphere Reserve where nature remains untouched. It's ideal for a quiet, shell-hunting family day away from the resort crowds.

Morning / 3-4h

Aqua Magic Mamaia

Splash in the dedicated toddler zones featuring gentle slides, water playgrounds, and shallow pools. It's a brilliant, energetic alternative to the beach on hot summer days.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Tomis Harbor Marina

Stroll through the bustling harbor to see yachts and fishing boats while exploring the local stalls. Strollers navigate easily along the wide wooden docks while toddlers watch the bobbing boats.

🍽️ Where to eat

Bacaro Port

Order the fresh grilled Black Sea turbot and the kid-friendly fried calamari right by the yacht-filled marina.

La Scoica Land

Try the traditional Romanian fish soup while the kids enjoy the dedicated indoor and outdoor play areas on Lake Siutghiol.

Reyna

Get the grilled sea bass with garlic sauce and enjoy spectacular panoramic sea views from the terrace.

Pescăria lui Matei

Feast on their legendary crispy fried anchovies and traditional fish polenta in a lively, rustic seaside setting.

Popasul Pescarilor

Order the slow-cooked fish brine while the kids play on the private sandy beach right in front of your table.

👨‍👩‍👧 Always look for beaches marked 'Blue Flag' in Mamaia or Năvodari for the cleanest sand and calmest toddler-friendly waters. Renting a car is highly recommended if you plan to explore wilder northern beaches like Corbu, as public transit with beach gear can be challenging.
