From scenic gondola rides to gentle creek-side parks, this historic mountain town blends outdoor adventure with cozy, family-friendly charm. It is a spectacular natural playground for the entire family in any season.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min taxi or free hotel shuttle from Aspen/Pitkin County Airport (ASE) directly to downtown hotels

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Maroon Bells Scenic Loop

Take a shuttle to see the most photographed peaks in North America, featuring an easy, stroller-friendly paved walk along Maroon Lake. Toddlers will love tossing pebbles into the clear water while parents soak in the towering mountain views.

Late Morning / 2h

Silver Queen Gondola Ride

Ride this scenic enclosed gondola up to the summit of Aspen Mountain, where kids can play on the grassy lawns. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the smooth, high-flying journey and the giant sandbox at the top.

Saturday Morning / 1-2h

Aspen Saturday Market

Stroll through this vibrant downtown market filled with Colorado-grown fruits, artisan crafts, and live music. It's a great spot for toddlers to enjoy fresh pastries and watch balloon artists in a lively, vehicle-free setting.

Afternoon / 1-2h

John Denver Sanctuary

This peaceful park features engraved boulders, wetlands, and a gently winding creek perfect for splashing. Toddlers can safely wander the flat grassy lawns and watch ducks swim in the quiet ponds.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Aspen Recreation Center

Escape the afternoon weather at this incredible facility featuring an indoor pool with a lazy river, water slides, and a zero-entry toddler play area. It’s the ultimate energy-burner for little ones on a chilly day.

Morning / 1-2h

Snowmass Ice Age Discovery Center

Learn about the incredible mammoth and mastodon fossils found nearby through highly interactive, kid-friendly exhibits. Toddlers will love touching real cast replicas of ancient bones.

Morning / 1-2h

Rio Grande Trail Walk

This flat, paved trail hugs the Roaring Fork River, making it perfect for strollers and active toddlers. Keep an eye out for local wildlife and peaceful river beaches along the way.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Herron Park and Playground

Tucked along the river, this park features modern climbing structures, infant swings, and a gentle stream where toddlers can safely splash on warm days. It is a local favorite for a relaxed afternoon break.

Afternoon / 2h

Breathtaker Alpine Coaster

Wind through the forest on an elevated coaster track where parents control the speed. Toddlers over 36 inches can ride as passengers, offering a thrilling but safe mountain rush.

🍽️ Where to eat

CP Burger

Head here for classic beef burgers, sweet potato fries, and gourmet milkshakes next to a mini-golf course in summer or an ice rink in winter.

Woody Creek Tavern

Grab a table at this legendary, eccentric spot just outside town for their famous fresh lime margaritas and a basket of cheesy quesadillas for the kids.

Hickory House Ribs

Order the baby back ribs platter with a side of mac and cheese in a rustic, log-cabin setting that warmly welcomes noisy families.

The White House Tavern

Cozy up in this historic miner's cabin to share their legendary crispy chicken sandwich and a side of heavily salted french fries.

Paradise Bakery & Cafe

Treat the family to freshly baked chocolate chip cookies and locally made gelato at this beloved corner bakery with outdoor seating.

👨‍👩‍👧 Keep your little ones hydrated and apply sunscreen frequently to prevent altitude sickness and sunburn in Aspen's thin mountain air. Take advantage of the free, stroller-friendly Downtown Aspen shuttle buses to easily navigate town without having to park.
