What to do

Late morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Silver Queen Gondola

Enjoy a relaxing, fully-enclosed cabin ride to the summit of Aspen Mountain where you can walk along the flat, grassy lawn with Mary Claire while taking in the high-altitude views in a quiet setting.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Hunter Creek Trail

This beautiful trail features a gentle, heavily shaded lower section running alongside a rushing creek, making it ideal for a morning carrier walk with Mary Claire away from the crowds.

Afternoon 2-3:30pm / 1.5h

John Denver Sanctuary

A peaceful downtown park with perfectly manicured lawns, soothing water features, and smooth paved paths that are highly stroller-accessible and perfect for afternoon tummy time on a blanket.

Saturday morning 9-11am / 2h

Aspen Saturday Market

Stroll through this vibrant, open-air downtown market showcasing Colorado-made crafts and fresh fruit; go early in the morning to easily navigate your stroller before the mid-day crowd arrives.

Morning 9:30-11am / 1.5h

Rio Grande Trail

A completely paved, flat, multi-use trail running along the Roaring Fork River, providing a smooth stroller ride and the gentle, calming sounds of rushing water to help Mary Claire nap.

Early morning 8-10:30am / 2.5h

Maroon Bells Scenic Area

Take the accessible paved path from the shuttle drop-off point to the breathtaking Maroon Lake shoreline, providing magnificent, crowd-free views early in the day when the mountain air is calm.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Ashcroft Ghost Town

Explore this well-preserved 1880s silver mining camp along Castle Creek Road via a flat, easy gravel path that is highly manageable with a rugged stroller or a baby carrier.

Afternoon 3-4:30pm / 1.5h

Wagner Park

A sprawling, centrally-located park in downtown Aspen with wide open lawns and plenty of mature shade trees, offering a perfect spot to let Mary Claire play and relax between shopping stops.

Midday 12-1:30pm / 1.5h

Sundeck

Located at the top of the Silver Queen Gondola, this casual mountaintop lodge offers a spacious outdoor patio, clean restrooms with changing tables, and a comfortable, noise-tolerant environment to feed baby.
