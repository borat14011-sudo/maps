Asheville

← back to travel board
Nestled in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains, Asheville blends breathtaking outdoor adventures with a vibrant, welcoming arts scene. Families will love exploring lush mountain trails, whimsical gardens, and interactive museums designed to spark young imaginations. It is a cozy, creative haven where mountain air and kid-friendly fun go hand in hand.
🚕 Getting there: ~20 min taxi or rideshare drive from Asheville Regional Airport (AVL) to downtown hotels

What to do

Morning / 4-5h

Biltmore Estate

Explore America's largest home, featuring stunning gardens and the Antler Hill Village farmyard where kids can meet friendly animals. Toddlers will love running around the wide-open lawns and playing at the wooden playground.

Morning / 2-3h

Western North Carolina Nature Center

Walk along paved, stroller-friendly paths to see native Southern Appalachian wildlife like black bears, cougars, and playful river otters. It is the perfect size for toddlers, offering several sensory play areas along the route.

Afternoon / 2h

The North Carolina Arboretum

Wander through 434 acres of beautiful gardens, featuring an incredible outdoor G-scale model train exhibit that kids will adore. The flat, paved paths make it highly accessible for strollers and tiny steps.

Morning / 2h

Asheville Museum of Science

Let your little ones dig for fossils, climb through a canopy treehouse, and interact with hands-on water tables. It is a perfect rainy-day retreat with a dedicated Toddler Nest play area.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Splasheville at Pack Square Park

Cool off in the heart of downtown at this interactive splash pad with dancing water jets. Toddlers will love chasing the water while parents relax on the surrounding grassy lawn.

Morning / 3h

Cradle of Forestry in America

Take a paved, stroller-friendly stroll through a lush forest to explore historic cabins, a vintage steam locomotive, and interactive indoor exhibits. It is an easy, educational outdoor experience perfect for little legs.

Afternoon / 2h

Carrier Park

Run wild at Asheville's premier park, which features a massive wooden playground castle, a paved track perfect for strollers, and plenty of open green space. Toddlers will love the dedicated swings and safe climbing structures.

Afternoon / 1h

French Broad Chocolate Lounge

Treat the family to world-class liquid truffles, artisanal chocolates, and house-made ice cream in a vibrant downtown space. They offer plenty of kid-approved cookies and milk options for younger palates.

Morning / 2h

Craggy Pinnacle Trail

Experience stunning panoramic Blue Ridge views on this short, kid-friendly 1.4-mile roundtrip hike through rhododendron tunnels. Toddlers will need a carrier for the uneven stone steps, but the rewarding views are worth it.

🍽️ Where to eat

Tupelo Honey Southern Kitchen & Bar

Order the famous scratch-made cathead biscuits served with honey and blueberry jam alongside a plate of kid-friendly mac and cheese.

White Duck Taco Shop

Grab some casual, creative tacos like the pulled pork or black bean while the kids enjoy simple cheese quesadillas by the river at the River Arts District location.

Sunny Point Cafe

Feast on world-famous carrot hotcakes or stuffed French toast in a lively garden setting with a dedicated, kid-friendly outdoor waiting area.

Luella's Bar-B-Que

Enjoy tender pulled pork, mac and cheese, and sweet potato fries in a relaxed, highly accommodating family atmosphere.

Double D's Double Decker Bus

Sip on a milkshake or latte while sitting on the upper deck of this iconic red British double-decker bus parked right downtown.
