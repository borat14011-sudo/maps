St. Thomas blends upscale Caribbean sophistication with pristine, white-sand shorelines perfect for discerning families. The island's historic harbor of Charlotte Amalie offers tax-free boutique shopping alongside beautifully preserved Danish colonial architecture. For little Mary Claire, the exceptionally calm, wave-free waters of world-renowned Magens Bay provide the ultimate natural wading pool. From private yacht charters to shaded beachfront dining, this tropical paradise ensures a seamless, luxurious escape for parents and baby alike.
🚕 Getting there: Cyril E. King Airport (STT) to the main resort strips takes 15 to 30 minutes; booking a private luxury SUV transfer with a pre-installed rear-facing infant car seat through Caribbean Logistics ensures a stress-free journey with plenty of room for your folding stroller.

What to do

Early morning 8-11am / 3h

Magens Bay Beach

This world-famous beach features an incredibly calm, wave-free lagoon that mimics a giant baby pool, surrounded by lush coconut groves that provide essential shade for Mary Claire's naps. It is highly stroller-accessible with flat, paved paths leading directly from the parking area to the sand.

Morning 9am-12pm / 3h

Coral World Ocean Park

A beautifully maintained marine park next to Coki Beach featuring stroller-friendly paved pathways, shaded underwater observatories, and gentle exhibits where Mary Claire can marvel at colorful tropical fish without any loud, sudden noises.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Phantaseas Tropical Botanical Garden

A tranquil, lush oasis filled with orchids and butterflies, offering cool canopy shade and comfortable gravel walking paths perfect for a quiet morning baby-wear hike away from the intense coastal sun.

Late afternoon 4-4:30pm / 30m

Drake's Seat

An iconic, easily accessible roadside lookout offering breathtaking panoramic views of Magens Bay and the British Virgin Islands. It requires absolutely no walking, allowing Will and Kathryn to snap stunning family photos while Mary Claire rests comfortably in the air-conditioned car.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Charlotte Amalie Historic District

Stroll along the historic, paved waterfront promenade and explore upscale shops nestled in shaded Danish colonial alleyways, offering a refined blend of history and luxury retail that is highly stroller-friendly.

Afternoon 2-5pm / 3h

Secret Harbour Beach

A highly sheltered, low-traffic cove with tranquil waters and palm trees extending right to the shore, offering a peaceful, private feel perfect for gentle infant wading and relaxing under a premium rental umbrella.

Morning 9am-1pm / 4h

Water Island Ferry from Crown Bay

Take a smooth, 10-minute pontoon ferry ride to Water Island, where you can rent a private golf cart to explore the exceptionally quiet Honeymoon Beach, famous for its calm waters and lack of crowds.

Late afternoon 4-5:30pm / 1.5h

Skyride to Paradise Point

Board a smooth, modern aerial tramway featuring spacious, enclosed cabins that easily accommodate a folded stroller, carrying you up to a stunning overlook with spectacular harbor views and gentle afternoon breezes.

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

Coki Point Beach

Visit this vibrant beach in the early morning before the crowds arrive to enjoy crystal-clear, shallow waters where baby Mary Claire can safely splash while schools of friendly, colorful fish swim right up to the shoreline.

🍽️ Where to eat

Sunset Grille

Located at Secret Harbour, this upscale, open-air restaurant offers a signature butter-poached Caribbean lobster alongside refreshing ocean breezes, an early dinner seating starting at 5:00 PM, and highchairs with plenty of space between tables for stroller parking.

Mafolie Restaurant

Perched high above the harbor, this legendary establishment serves exceptional callaloo soup and fresh catch of the day on a spacious, breezy terrace where the ambient hum of the wind and soft music easily masks any infant fussiness.

The Old Stone Farmhouse

An elegant, fine-dining destination set in a historic 200-year-old sugar plantation courtyard, serving prime dry-aged steaks; their early evening seatings provide a serene, upscale atmosphere with accommodating staff who happily welcome well-behaved infants.

Gladys' Cafe

A beloved Charlotte Amalie institution serving authentic Caribbean jerk chicken and conch fritters in a cool, air-conditioned stone building, featuring highchairs, a warm family-friendly staff, and a lively midday atmosphere that is perfect for a baby-friendly lunch.

Mim's Seaside Bistro

A lovely, relaxed waterfront dining spot famous for its coconut curry lobster, offering outdoor seating right on the water's edge where the soothing sound of rolling waves keeps Mary Claire calm and entertained throughout the meal.
