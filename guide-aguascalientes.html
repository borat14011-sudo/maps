What to do

Early morning 8:30-10:00am / 1.5h

Jardín de San Marcos

This historic, balustraded park features wide, paved walkways shaded by massive, centuries-old ash trees. It is the perfect spot for a tranquil morning stroll, offering plenty of quiet benches where Kathryn can comfortably feed Mary Claire away from the city bustle.

Morning 10:15-11:15am / 1h

Templo de San Antonio

Admire the stunning, multi-styled architectural masterpiece of this historic temple. The surrounding plaza is wide and flat, making it exceptionally easy to navigate with a stroller, while the quiet, cool interior offers a peaceful, low-sensory environment for baby Mary Claire's morning nap.

Morning 9:00-11:00am / 2h

Baños Termales de Ojocaliente

These historic mineral springs offer private family rental pools where Will and Kathryn can unwind in warm, therapeutic waters. Booking a private bath chamber ensures a quiet, exclusive space where baby Mary Claire can rest comfortably under supervision in a warm, relaxing environment.

Morning 11:30am-12:30pm / 1h

Plaza Patria

The expansive main plaza of the city offers smooth, flat paving stones that are a dream for strollers. Will and Kathryn can admire the neoclassical government palace and central fountain during the quiet morning hours before the midday heat.

Afternoon 3:00-4:30pm / 1.5h

Parque Tres Centurias

A beautifully restored railway heritage park featuring dancing fountains, manicured lawns, and wide concrete paths ideal for strollers. The spacious outdoor setting allows Mary Claire to enjoy the visual stimulation of the gardens while keeping noise levels completely manageable.

Late morning 11:30am-12:30pm / 1h

Museo José Guadalupe Posada

Dedicated to the famous local printmaker, this quiet museum is housed in a beautiful, air-conditioned historic building. It features spacious galleries that easily accommodate strollers and provides a calm, culturally enriching escape with low noise levels.

Afternoon 4:00-6:00pm / 2h

Isla San Marcos

This expansive green leisure park features a massive artificial lake and wide, flat walking paths perfect for a long stroller walk. It offers a very quiet, breezy atmosphere on weekdays, making it an ideal spot for an outdoor family picnic under the shade of mature trees.

Morning 10:00am-12:00pm / 2h

Museo Descubre

An interactive science museum featuring beautifully landscaped outdoor areas and fully air-conditioned indoor spaces. The museum features gentle sensory exhibits and wide, stroller-friendly corridors, offering a comfortable, climate-controlled environment for the whole family.

Afternoon 2:30-3:30pm / 1h

Centro Cultural Los Arquitos

Originally a 19th-century bathhouse, this beautifully restored cultural center features tranquil stone courtyards and shaded corridors. The quiet, artistic atmosphere and stroller-accessible layout make it a delightful, low-stress spot for a peaceful afternoon stroll.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Estación

Housed in a beautifully restored historic railway building, this elegant restaurant offers high-end Mexican cuisine and outstanding service. The spacious layout easily accommodates strollers, and the peaceful outdoor terrace is perfect for an early dinner with Mary Claire.

Las Costillas de Sancho

Famous for its premium, tender ribs and upscale family-friendly atmosphere, this local favorite offers highchairs and spacious seating. Opt for an early evening seating on their ventilated terrace to enjoy a relaxed, premium dining experience with plenty of space for the baby.

Augusto's

An upscale international restaurant known for its refined steak and seafood dishes served in an elegant, quiet garden setting. The gentle ambient noise and spacious outdoor tables provide an incredibly comfortable atmosphere for dining with an infant.

Mochomos Aguascalientes

A chic, high-end Sonoran steakhouse featuring creative modern Mexican cuisine and an upscale ambiance. Booking an early dinner reservation allows you to enjoy their exquisite terrace, which offers ample space for a stroller and attentive service.

La Saturnina

Set in a gorgeous, upscale colonial courtyard, this celebrated restaurant is famous for its traditional Aguascalientes breakfast specialties. The spacious courtyard layout, gentle morning light, and friendly staff make it highly welcoming for families with infants.
