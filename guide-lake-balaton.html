Lake Balaton

← back to travel board
Lake Balaton offers a sophisticated, ultra-relaxed alternative to crowded Mediterranean beaches, making it the perfect escape for a busy Houston family. The northern shore features elegant tree-lined promenades, shallow calm waters, and historic estates that are highly stroller-friendly. It is a place where you can savor dry, volcanic Furmint wines on sunny terraces while your toddler plays safely on manicured lakeside lawns.
🚕 Getting there: ~1 hour 45 minutes private Mercedes V-Class transfer from Budapest Liszt Ferenc Airport (BUD) directly to your resort in Balatonfüred.

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Tagore Promenade (Tagore Sétány)

Walk along this historic, wide, plane-tree-shaded path directly on the water in Balatonfüred. It is completely flat, paved, and ideal for a smooth stroller ride while enjoying views of the yachts.

Morning / 2h

Tihany Abbey (Tihanyi Bencés Apátság)

Explore this iconic 11th-century hilltop monastery offering breathtaking panoramic views over the turquoise lake. The main paths leading up from the parking area are paved, allowing you to easily push a stroller to the viewpoint.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Kisfaludy Beach (Kisfaludy Strand)

This premium, family-focused beach in Balatonfüred offers pristine lawns, heated baby pools, and a sandy, shallow lake entry. It features excellent family changing rooms and shaded playground areas perfectly suited for an infant.

Late Afternoon / 2-3h

Folly Arboretum and Winery (Folly Arborétum és Borászat)

A stunning botanical garden on a hillside where parents can taste estate-grown white wines while walking under ancient cedar trees. The lower paths are manageable with a stroller, and there is an exceptional wooden playground for when the toddler needs to stretch.

Morning / 2-3h

Festetics Palace (Festetics-kastély)

This magnificent baroque palace in Keszthely features incredibly manicured, flat French gardens that are perfect for a peaceful stroller walk. The carriage museum on-site is spacious and easy to navigate with a baby in tow.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Lavender House Visitor Center (Levendula Ház)

An interactive visitor center on the shore of Tihany's Inner Lake that explores the area's volcanic origins and lavender heritage. It features flat, indoor wooden boardwalks and a lovely garden that is easy to navigate with a toddler.

Morning / 3-4h

Veszprém Zoo (Veszprémi Állatkert)

Located just a short drive north of the lake, this modern zoo is nestled in a beautiful, shady valley with wide, stroller-friendly gravel paths. It features a fantastic kid-friendly petting zone and a spacious African savanna enclosure.

Morning / 2h

Bella Zoo Park (Bella Állatpark)

A beautifully kept petting zoo in Siófok where toddlers can see alpacas, camels, and double-humped llamas up close. The paths are wide, flat, and dirt-packed, making stroller navigation straightforward and stress-free.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Annagora Aquapark

A massive water park in Balatonfüred featuring dedicated heated baby pools, gentle water sprayers, and wave pools. It is exceptionally clean with plenty of rental sunbeds and shaded grassy areas for comfortable infant naps.

🍽️ Where to eat

Bistro Sparhelt

Order the elevated local catfish paprikash with homemade pasta at this top-tier Balatonfüred bistro. They open early for lunch, offer plenty of space for strollers next to the tables, and feature a calm, welcoming atmosphere.

Apátsági Rege Cukrászda

Order their signature lavender-infused cream cake while sitting on the spectacular outdoor terrace overlooking the lake. The spacious terrace easily accommodates strollers and provides a breezy, sensory-friendly spot for a napping baby.

Szent Donát Borkúria

Savor the dry Furmint wines paired with pasture-raised chicken on a terrace overlooking the Csopak vineyards. The restaurant has a massive, gently sloping grassy lawn where your toddler can safely stretch her legs between courses.

Kistücsök Étterem

Taste the legendary slow-cooked pork belly at this culinary landmark on the southern shore. They are highly accommodating to families, offering early dinner seating, immaculate high chairs, and spacious dining rooms.

Karolina Kávéház és Koktélbár

Enjoy a slice of traditional Dobos torte at this elegant, nostalgic café located right on the Balatonfüred harbor. The outdoor patio tables are spaced widely apart, giving you ample room to park a stroller comfortably by your side.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Kolping Hotel Spa & Family Resort

This is Hungary's premier family-focused resort, featuring 'Bobo Land'—a massive indoor/outdoor play kingdom with professional nursery care, baby-friendly pools, and fully equipped family suites with cribs, changing tables, and bottle warmers.

Anna Grand Hotel

A beautifully restored historic hotel in the heart of Balatonfüred, right next to the Tagore Promenade. It features exceptionally large family suites, high-end cribs, and a tranquil indoor pool area ideal for a relaxing swim with an infant.

Lotus Therme Hotel & Spa

A luxury five-star resort in Hévíz offering pristine thermal pools, expansive shaded parklands for quiet stroller walks, and top-tier service that easily handles special family requests like in-room baby baths and cribs.
