Anguilla offers an ultra-exclusive Caribbean escape defined by its pristine white sands and exceptionally calm, reef-protected waters. The island's sophisticated, low-key atmosphere is perfect for Will and Kathryn to unwind without the hectic crowds of larger tourist hubs. Meads Bay provides a stunning, beachfront expanse where baby Mary Claire can safely experience her very first dip in the sea. This luxury beachfront haven seamlessly pairs high-end amenities with a gentle, infant-friendly pace of life.

🍽️ Where to eat

Blanchards Restaurant

Savor the famous Caribbean jerk chicken and key lime pie on a breezy, beautifully lit outdoor garden terrace. They gladly provide highchairs and accommodate early 5:30 PM seatings.

Straw Hat Restaurant

Enjoy fresh red snapper directly on the boardwalk of Meads Bay. This open-air venue is extremely welcoming to babies, featuring stroller parking right next to your table and gentle ocean breezes.

Jacala Beachfront Restaurant

Indulge in exquisite French-Caribbean fusion like grilled lobster. The quiet, upscale beachside deck is best enjoyed during an early lunch when the noise level is low and baby can nap in her stroller.

Veya Restaurant

Experience fusion cuisine in a lush, treehouse-like setting. Book the earliest reservation slot at 6 PM to enjoy the quiet ambient sounds before the live music volume increases.

Picante

A vibrant, upscale Mexican tavern offering fresh fish tacos. The spacious open-air garden layout is incredibly family-friendly, easily accommodating highchairs and active toddlers.
