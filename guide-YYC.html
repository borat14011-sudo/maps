Calgary

← back to travel board
Nestled where the rolling foothills meet the majestic Canadian Rockies, Calgary blends rugged outdoor adventure with a vibrant, welcoming urban pulse. Families will love exploring its sprawling green spaces, interactive museums, and dynamic culinary scene, all framed by crisp blue skies. It is the perfect western playground for curious minds and energetic little legs.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Wilder Institute/Calgary Zoo

Walk through global habitats to see pandas, penguins, and Canadian wildlife up close. The Prehistoric Park with animatronic dinosaurs is a massive hit for toddlers who love to roar.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Heritage Park Historical Village

Step back in time on a vintage steam train and explore Canada's largest living history museum. Toddlers will love the gentle antique midway rides and the friendly farm animals.

Morning / 2-3h

TELUS Spark Science Centre

Ignite curiosity with hands-on science exhibits, a creative giant indoor playground, and an immersive dome theatre. The dedicated Creative Kids Museum is specifically designed for safe, sensory play for under-fives.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Prince's Island Park

Walk along the scenic Bow River to this beautiful island oasis featuring wide paved paths and a fantastic creative playground. Toddlers can safely watch the ducks splash in the lagoon while you enjoy a relaxing stroll.

Morning / 1-2h

Calgary Public Library

This architectural masterpiece features a spectacular wood-clad lobby and a massive, dedicated early learning center. Toddlers can climb, slide, and explore sensory play structures in a cozy, welcoming indoor environment.

Morning / 1-2h

Calgary Farmers' Market West

Experience a bustling indoor market packed with fresh local foods, handmade treats, and artisanal goods. It features a great indoor play area where toddlers can burn off energy while parents grab coffee.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Bowness Park

A beloved local park offering boat rentals in the summer and ice skating in the winter. The shallow wading pool and miniature train ride are perfect for keeping toddlers active and entertained.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Calgary Tower

Soak in 360-degree views of the city and the distant Rocky Mountains from 191 meters in the air. Toddlers will get a thrill standing on the reinforced glass floor looking straight down at the streets below.

Morning / 1-2h

Gasoline Alley Museum

Located at Heritage Park's entrance, this indoor museum showcases a stunning, colorful collection of vintage cars and retro gas pumps. The interactive play zone with mini-tricycles and vintage games is perfect for active toddlers.

Where to eat

  • Cluck N Cleaver — Created by local chefs, this counter-serve spot serves up crispy, southern-style fried chicken and easy-to-eat chicken bites that are perfect for kids.
  • Village Ice Cream — A local institution offering handcrafted, delicious flavors like Salted Caramel and Melted Chocolate that will delight both parents and toddlers.
  • Peters' Drive-In — An iconic Calgary staple since 1962, famous for its thick, customizable milkshakes and classic burgers served fast in a family-friendly drive-in format.
  • Blue Star Diner — A welcoming Bridgeland neighborhood diner focusing on locally sourced ingredients, featuring an excellent kids' menu with fluffy pancakes and grilled cheese.
👨‍👩‍👧 Utilize the C-Train transit line downtown, which is completely free to ride between City Hall and Downtown West/Kerby stations. Calgary's weather can shift rapidly, so dress your family in easily removable layers and always carry a windproof jacket.
