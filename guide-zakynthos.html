What to do

Early morning 8:30-10:30am

Tsilivi Beach

This Blue Flag beach boasts exceptionally calm, warm, and shallow waters stretching far out, making it the safest spot on the island for dipping infant toes. Rent a premium shaded cabana to keep Mary Claire cool during the midday heat.

Late afternoon 5:30-7:00pm

Strata Marina Promenade

A beautifully paved, wide harborfront promenade in Zakynthos Town that is entirely stroller-friendly. It is perfect for a breezy sunset walk while Mary Claire naps in her carrier or bassinet.

Morning 9:30-11:00am

Solomos Square

A massive, car-free pedestrian square in the heart of the capital flanked by stunning neoclassical architecture. Excellent for letting a crawling baby stretch her legs on a clean blanket in the shade of the cultural center.

Early morning 8:00-10:00am

Gerakas Beach

Part of the National Marine Park, this pristine sandy beach is the nesting ground for Caretta caretta turtles. The sand is exceptionally soft, and the entry is very shallow, though you should bring your own UV pop-up tent as umbrellas are regulated to protect nests.

Morning 9:00-11:00am

Askos Stone Park

A shaded forest park home to friendly, free-roaming deer, goats, and tortoises. The stone paths are best negotiated with a sturdy baby carrier rather than a stroller, offering a quiet, nature-filled sensory experience for a curious infant.

Afternoon 3:00-4:00pm

Agios Dionysios Church Plaza

The spacious plaza surrounding this grand, historic church is flat and completely paved. Step inside the cool, quiet, and cavernous interior for a peaceful respite from the Mediterranean sun during non-service hours.

Late afternoon 4:30-6:00pm

Bochali Castle Viewpoint

Perched high above Zakynthos Town, this scenic hill offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Ionian Sea. Shaded by towering pine trees, the paved terrace path is easily stroller-accessible and features several quiet cafes for a breezy iced coffee.

Morning 9:00-11:30am

Banana Beach

A wide, expansive golden sand beach with excellent wind protection. High-end beach clubs offer extra-large luxury daybeds with deep canvas umbrellas, ensuring Mary Claire remains fully shielded from the sun while parents enjoy beachside service.

Morning 10:00-11:30am

Skinari Cape Windmills

This scenic northern cape features majestic, traditional white windmills overlooking the sea. The cafe deck provides a wonderfully breezy, shaded vantage point to enjoy the coastal views safely tucked away from the steep cliff-edge steps.

🍽️ Where to eat

Yard of Taste

An upscale oasis in Zakynthos Town serving modern Greek gastronomy in a beautiful, quiet courtyard garden. Book an early 7:00pm seating to dine peacefully before the dinner rush; highchairs are readily available.

Anadalis

Located right on the water in Argassi, this restaurant features refined Mediterranean-terroir dishes. The outdoor grassy lawn and spacious seaside terrace easily accommodate a stroller right at your table.

To Kantouni

A charming, traditional taverna tucked into a quiet pedestrian alley in Zakynthos Town. Known for fresh grilled sea bass, the gentle outdoor environment is highly welcoming to young families.

Lofos Restaurant

Perched on a hillside in Meso Gerakari, this spot offers spectacular sunset views and traditional local rabbit stew. The spacious outdoor seating area is highly accommodating for families, with staff who happily welcome babies.

Varkarola Tavern

A refined local favorite in Zakynthos Town famous for traditional cantades acoustic music. Snag an outdoor table for an early dinner of slow-cooked lamb while the gentle background music provides a soothing ambiance for baby.
