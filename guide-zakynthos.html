Zakynthos

← back to travel board
Zakynthos blends dramatic limestone cliffs with shallow, warm Ionian waters that are incredibly forgiving for a toddler's first swim. The island offers a relaxed Mediterranean pace, allowing you to bypass the chaotic party scenes for upscale, family-friendly pockets like Tragaki and Vassilikos. Here, you'll find accessible luxury, private villa resorts, and incredibly welcoming locals who treat babies like royalty. It is the ideal balance of rugged Greek beauty and seamless logistics for an active young family.
🚕 Getting there: ~20-minute private Mercedes V-Class transfer from Zakynthos International Airport (ZTH) straight to your resort in Tragaki, bypassing the standard taxi queues.

What to do

Morning / 3h

Gerakas Beach

This protected turtle nesting site features soft golden sand and extremely shallow, calm waters ideal for a toddler to safely splash. Strollers must be left at the wooden ramp entrance, but the short walk to the shoreline is easy even while carrying a baby and beach gear.

Morning / 2h

Askos Stone Park

A shaded, stone-paved forest park where your toddler can see deer, goats, and tortoises in a quiet, natural habitat. The stone paths are bumpy but wide enough for a sturdy multi-terrain stroller, and there are plenty of wooden benches under the olive trees for rest breaks.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Venetian Castle of Bochali

Perched high above Zakynthos Town, this pine-forested fortress ruin offers sweeping harbor views and shady, peaceful walking paths. The pine needle paths are stroller-accessible with a bit of pushing, and the adjacent Bochali village has flat, paved cafes.

Morning / 2-3h

Porto Limnionas Cove

A stunning, fjord-like cove with deep turquoise waters and a highly rated, shaded tavern perched on the cliffside. The rocky path down to the water is not stroller-friendly, but the flat, grassy tavern terrace offers breezy views perfect for nursing or a contact nap.

Morning / 3-4h

Tsilivi Waterpark

A highly managed, safe family venue featuring a dedicated, shallow 'Aqua Jungle' splash zone designed specifically for infants and toddlers. It is highly stroller-friendly with flat paved walkways, plenty of sun loungers, and clean changing facilities.

Evening / 1h

Alykes Salt Pans

A unique, flat wetland area behind Alykes beach where you can take an easy, breezy stroller walk to spot migratory birds. The terrain is completely flat and gravel-paved, making it an excellent, low-stress spot for a golden-hour family walk.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Banana Baya Beach Club

A premium beach venue with expansive wooden boardwalks and manicured grassy lawns right up to the sand, making stroller navigation seamless. Relax on high-end daybeds with full shade umbrellas while your toddler plays safely on the soft, stone-free sand.

Morning / 1-2h

Therianos Family Organic Farm

A welcoming, sensory-rich organic farm where kids can see olive oil pressing and touch fresh herbs under giant olive trees. The flat, dirt farm paths are easy to navigate with a baby carrier or stroller, and the owners treat young children like family.

Evening / 1-2h

Solomos Square

A massive, completely flat, vehicle-free marble square on the waterfront that is perfect for letting a toddler run wild or pushing a stroller at sunset. It is surrounded by high-quality gelato shops and shaded benches, offering a lively but safe local Greek vibe.

🍽️ Where to eat

Lofos Restaurant

Order the slow-cooked lamb kleftiko. It features a massive, fenced grassy lawn and wide-spaced outdoor tables, allowing you to dine in peace while the baby enjoys the cool mountain breeze.

Portokali Restaurant

Order the handmade ravioli with local goat cheese. The restaurant is set in a whimsical, colorful fairy-tale garden with soft grass, offering plenty of space to park a stroller next to your table away from any street noise.

Nobelos Bio Seaside Restaurant

Order the organic grilled sea bream with wild greens. Located right on a quiet sandy cove, it has a relaxed organic luxury vibe with plenty of natural shade from pine trees, perfect for a sleeping baby in a stroller.

Yard of Taste

Order the beef fillet with local truffle and Zakynthian gravy. The quiet, inner courtyard keeps your toddler insulated from city bustle, and the staff is famously accommodating with early dining hours and highchairs.

Varkarola Tavern

Order the traditional Zakynthian rabbit stew. The outdoor street-side seating is lively and loud enough to easily drown out any toddler tantrums, and the early evening service is highly accommodating.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Lesante Cape Resort & Villas

Offers massive family suites and private villas with temperature-controlled plunge pools, plus a dedicated kids club and baby-sitting services. The resort is impeccably paved for high-end strollers and provides high-quality cribs on demand.

Porto Zante Villas & Spa

The pinnacle of family luxury, offering private beachfront villas with heated pools, custom baby equipment menus, and a dedicated kids club. It is fully gated and private, meaning total security and bespoke, stress-free infant care.

Domes Aulūs Zante

A luxury resort set on a pine-forest beach with a dedicated 'KiEPOS' kids club and shallow family pools. It features spacious family bungalows with separate living areas, making nap-time coordination effortless.

👨‍👩‍👧 Rent a mid-size SUV rather than a compact car to easily fit a heavy-duty stroller and handle the steep, winding mountain roads leading to northern sights like Askos. Stick to the southern and eastern beaches like Gerakas or Alykes, which feature soft sand and shallow, wave-free waters compared to the deep, rocky west coast. Pack a sturdy, structured baby carrier alongside your stroller, as historic sites like Bochali Castle and coastal taverns feature uneven cobblestones and stone steps.
