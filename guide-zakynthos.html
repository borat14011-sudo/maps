Zakynthos

← back to travel board
With its dramatic limestone cliffs, neon-blue waters, and shallow sandy shores, Zakynthos is an Ionian paradise tailor-made for families. Beyond the legendary Shipwreck Cove, you will discover quiet olive groves, friendly farm animals, and nesting sea turtles. It is a magical island where slow-paced Greek hospitality makes traveling with little ones feel like a breeze.
🚕 Getting there: ~15-20 min taxi or pre-arranged private shuttle to the main family beach resorts of Tsilivi or Kalamaki

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Navagio Beach Viewpoint

Marvel at the world-famous shipwreck from the safety of the cliffside viewing platform. Toddler note: Keep toddlers securely in a carrier as the drop-offs beyond the designated path are very steep.

Morning / 3-4h

Gerakas Beach

Build sandcastles on a golden beach famous as a nesting ground for Loggerhead sea turtles. Toddler note: The shallow, calm waters are perfect for splashing, but note there are no water sports or loud music allowed.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Zakynthos Turtle Rescue Center

Learn about local marine conservation efforts and see rescued turtles recovering in rehabilitation tanks. Toddler note: The indoor displays are colorful, low-set, and highly engaging for curious little eyes.

Full Day / 4-6h

Zante Water Village

Cool off at one of Greece's largest water parks, featuring dedicated toddler spray zones and a relaxing lazy river. Toddler note: The Jungle area is specifically designed with miniature slides and shallow splash pools.

Morning / 2-3h

Askos Stone Park

Walk through a beautiful forest reserve where kids can feed friendly deer, goats, and tortoises. Toddler note: The stone pathways can be bumpy, so a sturdy all-terrain stroller or baby carrier is highly recommended.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Tsilivi Beach

Spend a relaxed day on this blue-flag sandy beach boasting exceptionally warm and shallow waters. Toddler note: Rent a front-row sunbed with an umbrella so your little one can nap comfortably right next to the shoreline.

Morning / 2-3h

Glass-Bottom Boat Tour to Marathonisi

Take a gentle boat ride to spot wild sea turtles and peer through the glass floor at colorful marine life. Toddler note: Covered boat canopies protect toddlers from the midday sun, and infant life jackets are provided.

Afternoon / 1h

Aristeon Ecological Olive Press Museum

Discover how olive oil is made and sample fresh local bread dipped in extra virgin olive oil. Toddler note: Kids will love running around the shaded outdoor display of ancient stone-pressing machinery.

Evening / 1-2h

Solomos Square

Let the kids run free in this massive, pedestrian-only harbor square surrounded by grand Venetian-style architecture. Toddler note: Grab a local gelato nearby and watch the fishing boats bobbing in the harbor as the sun goes down.

🍽️ Where to eat

Lofos Restaurant

Order the traditional Greek moussaka while soaking in breathtaking panoramic sunset views next to a fantastic kid-friendly grassy play area.

Taverna Akrogiali

Enjoy fresh grilled sea bass and Greek salad right on the sand while your kids play safely in the gentle, shallow surf just feet away.

Windmill Restaurant

Feast on slow-cooked lamb kleftiko under a canopy of trees next to a historic stone windmill with plenty of open space for toddlers to explore.

Vardians

Try the handmade mini chicken gyros and Greek fries at this highly welcoming, family-run taverna famous for its warm hospitality toward toddlers.

Stani Taverna

Taste the local Zakynthian rabbit stew or kid-approved souvlaki skewers in a charming, quiet courtyard draped in vibrant bougainvillea.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack a lightweight stroller with good suspension to navigate uneven cobblestone paths, but keep a baby carrier handy for steep beach descents. Always bring a pop-up beach tent for shade, as many protected turtle-nesting beaches have strict limits on where you can place umbrellas.
