St. Maarten

← back to travel board
Divided between chic French sophistication and vibrant Dutch convenience, St. Martin offers a frictionless tropical escape perfect for a young family. With calm, reef-protected beaches like Le Galion and world-class dining in Grand Case, you can easily balance toddler nap schedules with high-end experiences. The island's compact size means minimal car seat time, while private villa-style resorts provide the space and amenities a finance dad demands for a seamless, stress-free getaway.
🚕 Getting there: ~15-20 min drive via a pre-arranged rental car or private SUV taxi from Princess Juliana International Airport (SXM) to Simpson Bay, or ~35 min to Grand Case.

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Parrot Ville Bird Sanctuary

This walk-in aviary lets you hold cups of seed to feed hundreds of colorful, friendly parrots. The flat gravel paths are easy to navigate with a stroller, though a baby carrier keeps your hands free for the birds.

Morning / 2-3h

Le Galion Beach

Widely known as 'Baby Beach' because a protective outer reef keeps the water completely flat, shallow, and wave-free. Park an all-terrain stroller under the sea grape trees and let your toddler safely splash in the calm tide.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Philipsburg Boardwalk

A wide, flat, completely paved concrete boardwalk running along Great Bay Beach that is 100% stroller-friendly. It is lined with open-air cafes, shade structures, and clean facilities, making for an effortless oceanfront stroll.

Morning / 3-4h

Pinel Island Ferry

Take a ultra-brief, ultra-calm 5-minute ferry ride from Cul-de-Sac to a tiny, uninhabited island paradise. Ditch the stroller in your car trunk and use a baby carrier to explore the shallow, warm waters and spot wild iguanas.

Morning / 1-2h

Seaside Nature Park

A peaceful 30-acre seaside park featuring a clean petting zoo with ducks, miniature goats, and tortoises for hands-on sensory play. The dirt trails are bumpy but manageable with a sturdy jogging stroller.

Morning / 1-2h

Marigot Market

An open-air waterfront market on the French side filled with vibrant tropical fruits, fresh coconut water, and colorful local crafts. The paved flat surfaces make stroller access a breeze, especially if you arrive early to beat the crowds.

Morning / 2-3h

Mullet Bay Beach

One of the island's most scenic beaches, framed by shade-giving sea grape trees and incredibly soft white sand. Bring a baby carrier to easily transport your gear from the nearby dirt parking lot to a shaded spot under the trees.

Morning / 2-3h

Loterie Farm

A lush, upscale nature sanctuary nestled in the hills with a stunning, spring-fed swimming pool. While the mountain hiking trails are too steep for strollers, the beautiful paved pool garden area is a tranquil oasis for a morning splash.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Fort Louis

A historic 18th-century fort offering sweeping, panoramic views of Marigot Bay and Anguilla. This is strictly a baby-carrier excursion due to the steep stone steps leading up to the ruins, but the breeze and views are unmatched.

🍽️ Where to eat

Talk of the Town

Order the signature slow-smoked Creole ribs with a side of Johnny cakes. The lively, open-air roadside barbecue 'lolo' vibe is loud enough to completely drown out any toddler tantrums.

Rainbow Café

Savor the fresh tuna tartare and passionfruit cocktails while sitting directly on the beach. Reserve a beachfront daybed for lunch so your toddler can play in the sand right next to your table.

Karakter

Try the fresh mahi-mahi wrap and Dutch bitterballen at this converted school-bus beach bar. The ultimate feet-in-the-sand dining spot, it offers plenty of space to park a stroller right next to your shaded beach table.

Zee Best Bakery

Indulge in flaky, fresh-baked almond croissants and custom-made savory omelets. Highly popular with early-rising families, this casual spot is perfect for matching a toddler's early wake-up schedule.

Spiga

Feast on exquisite house-made ravioli with white truffle oil in a refined, air-conditioned wooden cottage. Book an early dinner slot right at opening; the staff is incredibly welcoming to young families and easily accommodates high chairs.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Grand Case Beach Club

Offers spacious, apartment-style rooms with full kitchens to easily prep baby bottles, alongside two private, wave-free beaches. They provide high-quality cribs and maintain a tranquil, family-first atmosphere.

La Samanna, A Belmond Hotel

The ultimate five-star luxury retreat featuring multi-bedroom clifftop villas, private nanny services, and a pristine, quiet pool. Ideal for a premium, highly secure, and deeply relaxing infant-friendly sanctuary.

Simpson Bay Resort, Marina & Spa

Features expansive multi-room villas, a dedicated shallow toddler wading pool, and an incredibly convenient location. It sits just minutes away from major supermarkets and pharmacies for stress-free baby supply runs.

👨‍👩‍👧 Rent a mid-size SUV from a reputable local agency to easily navigate the island's steep hills and store your stroller.
