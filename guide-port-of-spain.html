Port of Spain offers an exhilarating blend of cosmopolitan Caribbean energy, rich oil-wealth luxury, and stunning natural backdrops. For upscale families traveling with a little one, the city provides premium havens of tranquility amidst the cultural bustle. The stroller-ready, paved expanse of the Waterfront Esplanade allows for breezy, scenic sunset walks right outside your hotel door. It is a sophisticated destination where tropical elegance meets modern convenience, ensuring a seamless luxury vacation for baby Mary Claire.
🚕 Getting there: The drive from Piarco International Airport (POS) to the Port of Spain waterfront takes approximately 35 minutes; the absolute best luxury family transport is a private, pre-arranged luxury SUV transfer booked through your hotel concierge, complete with a sanitized, rear-facing infant car seat.

What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Royal Botanical Gardens

Established in 1818, these manicured grounds offer towering canopy shade trees. The paved pathways are highly stroller-accessible, making it effortless to push Mary Claire while enjoying the cool, exotic flora.

Late Afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Queen's Park Savannah

This massive 260-acre park is ringed by the magnificent 'Magnificent Seven' mansions. Bring a carrier or stroller for a sunset stroll around the paved perimeter, letting Mary Claire enjoy the gentle evening breeze.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Emperor Valley Zoo

Located right next to the botanical gardens, this quiet zoo offers shady pathways and gentle walks. It is perfectly scaled for a baby's attention span, with colorful birds and quiet enclosures that won't startle her.

Morning 8-10am / 2h

Chaguaramas Boardwalk

A beautifully developed seaside boardwalk just west of the city. The completely flat, wide wooden planks are ideal for high-end strollers, offering pristine ocean views and refreshing sea breezes.

Late Afternoon 4-6:30pm / 2.5h

Caroni Bird Sanctuary

Witness thousands of brilliant Scarlet Ibis returning to roost at sunset. Booking a private, flat-bottomed boat charter ensures you can control the noise level and pace for Mary Claire's comfort.

Midday 11am-1pm / 2h

Castle Killarney

An architectural marvel modeled after Balmoral Castle. The surrounding manicured lawns are perfect for a quiet family picnic, offering a calm, uncrowded space for a crawling baby.

Midday 1-3pm / 2h

National Museum and Art Gallery

An air-conditioned cultural sanctuary in the heart of the city. It features quiet, spacious galleries with wide doorways that easily accommodate strollers during the midday heat.

Morning 9am-1pm / 4h

Maracas Beach

A scenic 40-minute drive through the rainforest brings you to this iconic white-sand beach. Rent a luxury shaded cabana near the calmest shallows so Mary Claire can safely splash in the warm Caribbean water.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Yerette - Home of the Hummingbird

Located in the lush Maracas Valley, this private sanctuary hosts thousands of hummingbirds. The calm, mesmerizing atmosphere is incredibly soothing for babies, though carrier-only is recommended due to steps.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Waterfront Restaurant

Savor premium international cuisine with stunning views of the Gulf of Paria. The spacious outdoor terrace is perfect for managing baby noise, and they happily provide premium highchairs.

Aioli

This upscale Mediterranean restaurant in Ellerslie Plaza offers a sophisticated, quiet dining room. Opt for an early 6:00 PM seating when the atmosphere is calm and the staff can lavish attention on Mary Claire.

Prime Restaurant

A premier steakhouse located in the BHP Billiton building. Its large, plush leather booths provide a comfortable, semi-private cocoon to keep Mary Claire relaxed while you enjoy world-class cuts.

Il Colosseo

An elegant Italian restaurant featuring a beautiful, romantic garden courtyard. The outdoor garden seating offers a natural, soothing ambiance that easily masks any baby fussiness.

Chaud Cafe & Wine Bar

A chic bistro located at One Woodbrook Place. It offers a sophisticated but relaxed daytime atmosphere, perfect for a high-end lunch with a sleeping baby in a stroller.
