Dominica invites families into a lush wonderland of cascading waterfalls, warm geothermal pools, and emerald-green rainforests. Known as the Nature Island, it offers a refreshing escape where kids can spot wild parrots and splash in gentle rivers. This pristine paradise balances thrilling outdoor treks with calm, family-friendly pockets of volcanic magic.
🚕 Getting there: ~60 to 75-minute taxi ride across the scenic forest reserve to Roseau, or ~45 minutes to Portsmouth

What to do

Morning / 1.5h

Emerald Pool

An easy, paved path leads to a magical, grotto-like waterfall basin. Toddlers will love splashing in the shallow, clear edges of the emerald water.

Morning / 2h

Indian River Boat Tour

Glide along a calm, swamp-forest river in a hand-rowed wooden boat under a canopy of Bwa Mang trees. It is a peaceful, shade-filled ride that will mesmerize young children without any scary rapids.

Saturday Morning / 1-2h

Roseau Market

Immerse your senses in a colorful labyrinth of tropical fruits, sweet coconut water, and local spices. Toddlers will enjoy tasting fresh bananas and sweet mangoes straight from the friendly vendors.

Afternoon / 2h

Cabrits National Park and Fort Shirley

Explore a historic, partially restored 18th-century fortress featuring wide grassy lawns for toddlers to run around safely. The panoramic views of Prince Rupert Bay are breathtaking and highly accessible.

Morning / 2h

Kalinago Barana Autê

Experience the rich heritage of Dominica's indigenous people at this cultural village featuring traditional thatch-roofed huts. Kids will love the live craft demonstrations and easy coastal walking trails.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Mero Beach

Relax on this warm, calm black-sand beach which features very gentle waves perfect for toddlers to paddle in safely. Nearby amenities and beachside restaurants make it highly convenient for families.

Morning / 2h

Papillote Tropical Gardens

Wander through lush, safe garden paths filled with orchids, hot springs, and giant ferns. Toddlers will be delighted by the fluttering butterflies and colorful frogs hiding in the foliage.

Morning / 1h

Trafalgar Falls

View these iconic twin waterfalls from a very short, flat, family-friendly boardwalk viewing platform. It offers all the visual drama of a rainforest trek without the difficult hiking for little feet.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Soufriere Outdoor Sulphur Baths

Soak in the naturally warm, shallow mineral pools surrounded by lush rainforest gardens. The cooler pools are incredibly relaxing and perfectly safe for a gentle toddler splash.

🍽️ Where to eat

Keepin' It Real

Grab a beachside table at Toucari Bay and order the fresh, butter-basted grilled lobster or mild fish fingers for the kids.

Ruins Rock Cafe

Enjoy their famous fruit juices, flavorful jerk chicken, and mild, kid-friendly potato wedges in a quirky, historical stone setting in Roseau.

Poz Restaurant & Bar at Calibishie Cove

Relax in the tropical garden setting and try the crispy coconut shrimp or their homemade local passion fruit juices.

Pearl's Cuisine

Order the traditional, comforting callaloo soup or a plate of seasoned creole chicken with sweet fried plantains in Roseau.

Madiba Beach Cafe

Sit right on the sand in Portsmouth and savor a plate of grilled snapper with kid-friendly local sides like sweet potato.
