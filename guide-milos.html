What to do

Early morning 8:30–11am

Sarakiniko Beach

Iconic white volcanic rock formations with natural pools. Stroller-accessible boardwalk leads to calm shallow water perfect for baby paddling. Shade scarce – bring umbrella.

Morning departure 10am–3pm (half-day)

Kleftiko Boat Tour

Sea caves and crystalline waters accessible only by boat. Choose a private catamaran with infant life vest and shaded cabin. Gentle cruise avoids loud music – baby-friendly.

Late afternoon 5–7pm

Plaka Village

Charming hilltop capital with cobblestone alleys and sunset views. Stroller can manage main paths; babywearing recommended for steep steps. Shaded cafes with highchairs.

Late morning 10am–12pm

Klima Fishing Village

Colorful boat garages (syrmata) along a sheltered inlet. Flat promenade perfect for stroller stroll. Quiet ambiance with gentle lapping waves – ideal for infant nap.

1 hour (check hours)

Catacombs of Milos

Early Christian underground tombs with guided tours. Cool interior offers relief from heat. Stroller not allowed inside – baby carrier recommended. Short visit, skip if noise-sensitive.

Late morning 9–11am

Papafragas Caves

Sea cave complex with a natural bridge and turquoise pool. Accessible via short path – stroller possible on flat sections. Calm water for safe dipping. Shade available.

Morning 9:30–12pm

Firopotamos Beach

Small sandy cove with calm, shallow water. Sheltered from wind with a few tamarisk trees for shade. Ideal for infants – gentle entry and soft sand. Beach bar has highchair.

Late afternoon 4–5:30pm

Ancient Theatre of Milos

Hellenistic theatre with panoramic views over the bay. Flat stone seating – stroller accessible only at lower levels. Quiet site; baby can crawl on grass nearby.

Flexible – early evening 5–7pm

Pollonia Beach

Lively fishing village with a sandy shore and calm waters. Stroller-friendly boardwalk, shaded playground nearby, and beachfront tavernas with highchairs. Gentle swimming for baby.

🍽️ Where to eat

Medusa Taverna

Iconic seafood spot in Pollonia overlooking the marina. Signature grilled octopus and lobster pasta. Highchairs available, outdoor terrace with shade, early seating at 6pm accommodates babies. Quiet family-friendly vibe.

Armakas

Plaka’s romantic restaurant with panoramic sunset views. Specializes in Greek fusion – try the lamb chops. Highchair and early reservations recommended. Terrace has breeze for hot evenings.

Sirocco Restaurant

Beachfront in Adamas with a varied menu and kids' corner. Signature tomato keftedes (fritters). Outdoor tables with shade, highchair, and space for stroller. Noise level moderate – good for early dinner.

O! Hamos

Traditional taverna in Plaka with stone courtyard. Grandma’s moussaka and stuffed vegetables. Baby-friendly courtyard with highchairs, early dinner at 7pm avoids crowds. No loud music.

Utopia Restaurant

Cliffside in Plaka with sensational sunset views. Seared sea bass and risotto. Terrace has glass railings – safe for stroller. Highchair and kids menu available. Reserve early for front row.

Boutique resort near Pollonia with extensive gardens and a large pool. Family bungalows with separate living area. Crib, blackout curtains, and baby monitor available. Beach shuttle for easy access. Excellent for extended stays.
