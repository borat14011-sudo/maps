Milos

Milos offers a serene, moon-like landscape with turquoise coves and white volcanic rock, perfect for a refined family retreat. The island's calm, shallow waters at places like Papafragas provide safe splashing for your little one. Stroller-friendly promenades in Plaka let you explore cobblestone-free terraces with sunset views. For baby Mary Claire, beachfront access at Firopotamos means sand and sea steps away from your sun lounger.
🚕 Getting there: Milos Airport (MLO) to the main hotel strip (e.g., Pollonia or Plaka) takes 15–20 minutes by private transfer with a baby seat and stroller storage; request a luxury van from Milos Transfers for seamless arrival.

What to do

Late morning 10am-12pm (avoid midday heat)

Sarakiniko Beach

Unique white lunar rock formations with natural tidal pools; calm, shallow water perfect for baby paddling. Stroller access via a short path, but carry over uneven sections.

Morning 9am-1pm (half-day tour)

Kleftiko Sea Caves

Boat tour around dramatic sea caves and crystal-clear water; infant-friendly with life vests provided and shaded cabin options. Book a private catamaran for flexibility.

Late afternoon 4-6pm (sunset strolling)

Plaka Village

Charming hilltop village with winding lanes, bougainvillea, and a castle ruin; stroller-friendly main streets and shaded squares for breaks. Visit the Folk Museum for cool indoor time.

Early morning 9-10:30am

Papafragas Caves

Natural swimming channel between rock walls with a small sandy beach; shallow entry and calm water ideal for infants. Accessible via a short staircase (carry stroller).

Morning 9-11am

Klima Fishing Village

Colorful two-storey boat garages (sirmata) along the waterfront; flat promenade perfect for strolling with a stroller. Quiet in the morning, with tiny pebble beach for gentle water play.

Mid-morning 10am-12pm

Firopotamos Beach

Sheltered sandy cove with shallow turquoise water and a tiny white church; easy beach access (no stairs) and gentle waves. Shade from umbrellas and a nearby taverna for baby snacks.

Afternoon 1-2:30pm (escape heat)

Milos Mining Museum

Indoor museum showcasing Milos's geological history with interactive exhibits; air-conditioned and stroller-accessible, with a quiet corner for nursing. Perfect for a mid-day break.

Early morning 9-11am

Tsigrado Beach

Secluded sandy cove with a dramatic stone staircase (not stroller-friendly, but worth it wearing baby in carrier); calm, clear water for a refreshing dip. Infant note: go early to avoid crowd noise.

Late afternoon 4:30-6pm

Adamas Port

Harbor town with a wide, stroller-friendly promenade, playground, and several cafés with outdoor seating. Watch fishing boats and feed the ducks; excellent for a low-key afternoon.

🍽️ Where to eat

O! Hamos! Taverna

Authentic Greek cuisine in Plaka with a family-friendly courtyard; signature slow-cooked lamb and fresh salads. Highchair available, early seating at 7pm, and low noise level.

Avli Restaurant

Elegant terrace overlooking the Aegean in Plaka; known for grilled octopus and lobster pasta. Offers a kids' menu, changing table, and calm early dinner vibes.

Sirocco Restaurant

Beachfront dining in Pollonia with stunning sunset views; specializes in fresh seafood and Greek mezze. Stroller-friendly path, outdoor terrace with shade, and highchairs on request.

Barbarossa Restaurant

Historic establishment in Adamas serving traditional moussaka and grilled fish; indoor/outdoor seating with a quiet air-conditioned room for infants. No minimum age, highchair available.

Medousa Restaurant

Family-run taverna in Pollonia with a sandy-floor beachfront deck; famous for its seafood spaghetti and fresh catch. Shaded tables, baby-friendly atmosphere, and early bird specials at 6pm.
