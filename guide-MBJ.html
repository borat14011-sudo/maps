Sun-soaked family adventures in Jamaica’s tropical playground.

Montego Bay

← back to travel board
Montego Bay welcomes families with its warm turquoise waters, gentle island rhythms, and lush tropical landscapes. From building sandcastles on calm white-sand beaches to exploring gentle rivers, this Jamaican gem offers a stress-free Caribbean escape. It is a place where kid-friendly adventure and laid-back relaxation blend seamlessly under the warm island sun.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Doctor's Cave Beach

Famous for its translucent, calm waters and white sand, this beach is incredibly safe for swimming. Toddlers will love splashing in the virtually waveless shallows while parents rent umbrellas and chairs.

Morning / 2h

Martha Brae River Rafting

Glide down a tranquil river on a handcrafted bamboo raft steered by an experienced guide. It is a peaceful sensory experience for toddlers, who can sit safely between parents on the padded seat.

Morning / 2h

Ras Natango Gallery and Garden

This hilltop eco-retreat features vibrant tropical gardens filled with native plants and tame hummingbirds you can hand-feed. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the colorful birds fluttering right in front of them.

Late Morning / 1-2h

Harbour Street Craft Market

A bustling market filled with colorful wood carvings, woven baskets, and Jamaican souvenirs. It is a great spot to introduce kids to local culture, though keeping toddlers in a carrier is recommended due to the narrow aisles.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Rocklands Bird Sanctuary

Just outside the city, this peaceful sanctuary lets you hold tiny bottles of sugar water to feed wild hummingbirds. Toddlers and older children alike will love the gentle, close-up encounter with nature.

Afternoon / 2h

Dump Up Beach and Events Park

A spacious public park and beach with calm waters and plenty of flat grassy areas to run around. It is a fantastic free option where local and visiting toddlers can play safely away from large crowds.

Morning / 2h

Montego Bay Marine Park Glass Bottom Boat Tour

Peer through the glass bottom of a boat to see vibrant coral reefs and colorful tropical fish without getting wet. It is the perfect reef experience for toddlers who are not old enough to snorkel yet.

Afternoon / 3h

Walter Fletcher Beach

This lively beach park features bouncy water trampolines, go-karts, and a calm shore perfect for young swimmers. Toddlers can safely build sandcastles in the shade of sea grape trees while older siblings enjoy the water sports.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Dead End Beach

Located right at the end of the airport runway, this local beach lets you watch massive airplanes fly incredibly low overhead. Toddlers will squeal with delight at the loud, exciting sights while playing in the shallow surf.

Where to eat

  • Scotchies — Famous for its mouthwatering jerk chicken cooked over sweetwood, this rustic, open-air spot has spacious garden seating where kids can wiggle around freely.
  • The Pelican Grill — A beloved, air-conditioned family diner serving kid-friendly American classics alongside traditional Jamaican dishes like mild ackee and saltfish.
  • Juici Patties — Grab some mild beef, chicken, or cheese patties at this local staple for a quick, cheap, and delicious hand-held meal that toddlers love to nibble on.
  • Doctor's Cave Bath & Grill — Enjoy easy beachfront dining featuring fresh fruit smoothies, french fries, and mild grilled chicken served right to your lounge chair.
