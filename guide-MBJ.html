Sun-soaked shores and carefree island adventures for all ages

Montego Bay

← back to travel board
Montego Bay welcomes families with its warm Caribbean waters, vibrant reggae rhythms, and lush tropical landscapes. Beyond the resort walls, you'll discover gentle lagoons, friendly wildlife, and rich cultural flavors that captivate travelers of every generation. It’s a stress-free tropical paradise where slow island time perfectly matches a toddler’s pace.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Doctor's Cave Beach

Swim in the calm, translucent waters of this legendary white-sand beach, complete with convenient amenities. Toddlers will love splashing in the virtually waveless, shallow shoreline.

Late Morning / 2h

Rafting on the Martha Brae River

Glide gently down a scenic river on a 30-foot handmade bamboo raft steered by an expert local guide. Toddlers can sit safely between parents' laps and enjoy looking at the overhanging tropical canopy.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Rocklands Bird Sanctuary

Get up close with exotic, colorful birds that will feed right out of your hands in this peaceful forest haven. Gentle toddlers can sit quietly to watch the tiny hummingbirds buzz around, though active ones may need to be held to stay still.

Morning / 1.5h

Rose Hall Great House

Explore the sprawling, historic grounds and stunning gardens of this famous 18th-century Georgian mansion. Toddlers can freely toddle across the wide manicured lawns while parents take in the coastal views.

Midday / 1-2h

Montego Bay Cultural Centre

Discover Jamaican art, history, and craft stalls inside this colorful, air-conditioned colonial building in Sam Sharpe Square. It is an easy, stroller-friendly escape from the midday heat for little ones.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Harmony Beach Park

Run around this pristine, modern public park featuring a state-of-the-art playground, paved walking paths, and a protected beach. Toddlers can safely play in the dedicated splash pad area and soft sandy shore.

Morning / 2h

Green Grotto Caves

Walk through mystical limestone caves filled with dramatic stalactites, stalagmites, and a crystal-clear subterranean lake. Sturdy toddlers will enjoy the adventure, but bring a baby carrier as strollers cannot navigate the cave steps.

Morning / 1-2h

Harbour Street Craft Market

Browse a bustling, colorful maze of stalls filled with handmade wooden carvings, woven baskets, and bright tie-dye clothing. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the vibrant colors, though it is best to keep them close in a carrier due to tight spaces.

Evening / 1.5h

Glistening Waters Luminous Lagoon

Experience the magic of a glowing blue bay at night, where harmless microscopic organisms light up when disturbed. Toddlers will be enchanted watching the water glow from the safety of the boat deck.

Where to eat

  • Scotchies — Feast on authentic, slow-roasted jerk chicken and pork cooked over pimento wood in a casual, open-air garden setting that is highly welcoming to energetic toddlers.
  • The Pelican Grill — Enjoy family-friendly, classic Jamaican comfort food like oxtail and fresh snapper in a comfortable, retro diner-style setting with high chairs available.
  • Island Grill — Grab a quick, healthy, and highly kid-approved meal of jerk chicken, rice and peas, and sweet plantains at this beloved local fast-casual chain.
  • Pier One — Dine on fresh seafood and tropical drinks on an open-air deck directly over the water, where toddlers can watch boats sail by as they munch on sweet festival bread.
👨‍👩‍👧 To navigate Montego Bay comfortably with a toddler, opt for a sturdy baby carrier over a stroller, as many sidewalks and natural attractions have uneven terrain. Always pack plenty of high-SPF reef-safe sunscreen and mosquito repellent, and stick to bottled water for mixing formula or keeping little ones hydrated.
