Nestled along a dramatic, protected bay, Zihuatanejo offers a slow-paced, sophisticated alternative to bustling mega-resorts. The golden sands of Playa La Ropa provide exceptionally calm, shallow waters perfect for baby Mary Claire's first gentle ocean dips. Parents Will and Kathryn can easily navigate the flat, scenic Paseo del Pescador boardwalk with a luxury stroller while taking in the fresh sea breezes. This tropical enclave seamlessly blends authentic coastal Mexican charm with upscale, infant-friendly boutique comforts.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 2.5h

Playa La Ropa

This mile-long stretch of soft sand features exceptionally calm waves, making it the safest spot for Will and Kathryn to dip Mary Claire's toes in the ocean. Palm trees offer natural shade, and beach clubs provide plush loungers.

Early morning 8:00 AM - 9:30 AM / 1.5h

Paseo del Pescador

A beautifully paved, flat waterfront boardwalk that is highly stroller-friendly. It is perfect for a breezy morning walk to watch the local fishing boats and enjoy the coastal views without navigating cobblestones.

Mid-day 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM / 4h

Playa Las Gatas

Accessible via a brief, scenic 10-minute water taxi ride from the municipal pier, this beach features a historic stone reef barrier that keeps the water as calm and shallow as a swimming pool. Perfect for an infant-friendly beach day.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 2h

El Refugio de Potosi

A lush, shaded ecological reserve just a short drive away, offering flat walking paths. Will and Kathryn can carry Mary Claire in a baby carrier under the cool jungle canopy while observing colorful butterflies and rescued macaws.

Afternoon 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM / 3h

Playa Quieta

Located a short drive north in Ixtapa, this aptly named beach is shielded by Isla Ixtapa, resulting in virtually wave-free, lake-like conditions. It is ideal for quiet family relaxation away from any crowds.

Late afternoon 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM / 1.5h

Plaza Principal

Zihuatanejo's central town square is completely flat, paved, and pedestrian-only. It is a wonderful spot for a late afternoon stroll with the stroller to experience local culture, shaded by large gazebo structures.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 1.5h

Mercado de Artesanías

A covered, open-air market featuring vibrant local crafts, hand-woven baby blankets, and clothing. The stalls are shaded and spacious enough to navigate with a compact travel stroller during quieter morning hours.

Early morning 7:30 AM - 8:30 AM / 1h

Playa Principal Fishermen Market

Stroll along the beach edge in the early morning to see local fishermen bringing in their fresh daily catch directly onto the sand. It is a colorful visual experience that is lively but quiet enough for a baby in a carrier.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 2h

Barra de Potosi Lagoon Boat Tour

A gentle, slow-moving private panga boat ride through the calm mangrove lagoon. The boats are fully shaded, providing a cool and tranquil environment for Mary Claire to nap while her parents spot herons and egrets.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Perla

A legendary beachfront spot right on Playa La Ropa. They provide highchairs and have a spacious, open-air layout where the sound of the ocean easily drowns out any baby whimpers while you enjoy fresh grilled red snapper.

El Manglar

Set along a quiet mangrove estuary, this lush oasis offers excellent shade and a peaceful atmosphere. Parents can dine on delicious coconut shrimp while Mary Claire enjoys watching the resident ducks and turtles from the safety of an outdoor highchair.

Bandidos

A vibrant downtown favorite with a spacious, breezy indoor-outdoor layout that easily accommodates strollers. The lively atmosphere is incredibly welcoming to families, and they offer early dinner seatings with high-quality Mexican classics.

Angustina

An upscale mezcaleria and kitchen offering sophisticated contemporary Mexican cuisine in a relaxed courtyard setting. Booking an early 6:00 PM reservation ensures a quiet, uncrowded dining experience with attentive service.

La Gula

A highly rated, intimate bistro serving refined international and Mexican dishes. The outdoor terrace seating is peaceful, upscale, and perfect for a relaxed family dinner before the baby's bedtime.
