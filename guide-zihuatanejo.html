Nestled along a tranquil bay, Zihuatanejo offers a sophisticated yet gentle retreat where high-end comfort meets authentic Mexican charm. The calm, wave-sheltered waters of Playa La Ropa provide the ultimate safe haven for baby Mary Claire's first dips in the ocean. Parents can unwind in beachfront cabanas under swaying palms while enjoying impeccable, personalized service. With its flat, stroller-friendly seaside paths and breezy open-air dining, this coastal sanctuary is effortlessly accommodating for an elegant family getaway.
🚕 Getting there: A 20-minute private luxury SUV transfer from Ixtapa-Zihuatanejo International Airport (ZIH) featuring a pre-installed rear-facing infant car seat is the ideal way to reach your Playa La Ropa resort.

What to do

Morning 8:30-10:30am / 2h

Playa La Ropa

The crown jewel of Zihua's beaches features exceptionally calm, warm waters and soft sand, making it perfect for baby's first splash. Excellent shade is provided by luxury resort palapas lining the shore.

Morning 9:00-11:30am / 2.5h

Playa Las Gatas

Take a quick, scenic water taxi from the town pier. Protect baby from the sun under the thatched palapas and wade together in the shallow, reef-protected pool that remains completely wave-free.

Late afternoon 5:30-6:30pm / 1h

Paseo del Pescador

This beautiful, flat waterfront boardwalk is completely stroller-accessible, offering gorgeous ocean breezes, local art, and views of fishing boats without the hassle of bumpy cobblestones.

Morning 9:00am-1:00pm / 4h

Isla Ixtapa Island

A smooth panga boat ride leads to this tranquil island sanctuary. Choose Playa Varadero for its exceptionally quiet, shallow waters and plenty of shaded dining spots for a comfortable family base camp.

Midday 11:30am-12:30pm / 1h

Museo Arqueológico de la Costa Grande

This small, paved museum offers a quiet and cool cultural break. The flat exhibition paths are easy to navigate with a stroller while baby enjoys a calm, air-conditioned nap.

Early morning 8:00-9:30am / 1.5h

Playa Larga

A striking, pristine stretch of sand perfect for an early morning stroll. While the waves are too strong for baby swimming, the flat shore is ideal for a peaceful walk in a baby carrier.

Morning 8:30am-12:00pm / 3.5h

Xihuacan Archaeological Site

A short, private air-conditioned drive leads to this ancient ceremonial center. The flat dirt paths are highly stroller-friendly and the peaceful, uncrowded atmosphere is excellent for a slow-paced excursion.

Morning 9:00am-1:00pm / 4h

Playa Manzanillo

Book a private, luxury catamaran charter to this secluded, pristine bay. It offers incredibly peaceful conditions and pristine waters where the family can swim and relax away from the crowds.

Early morning 9:00-10:00am / 1h

Mercado Municipal Zihuatanejo

Explore the vibrant colors of local life. Go early in the morning when the temperature is cool; use a baby carrier to navigate the bustling aisles easily and pick up fresh tropical fruits.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Casa Vieja

Indulge in authentic Guerrero culture with their famous green pozole. The spacious, open-air second-floor terrace is incredibly welcoming, and an early 2:00 PM Thursday seating aligns perfectly with baby's schedule.

La Gaviota Restaurant

A high-end beachfront favorite on Playa La Ropa specializing in fresh-caught mahi-mahi. The toes-in-the-sand setup allows parents to dine elegantly while baby safely explores the soft sand next to the table.

Tentaciones Restaurant

An exquisite fine-dining venue perched above the bay. Secure an early reservation at sunset; the spacious, breezy outdoor terrace easily accommodates a sleeping baby in a stroller amidst a romantic backdrop.

El Suspiro Restaurante

Enjoy refined cliffside seafood dining with gentle ocean breezes that keep baby perfectly cool. The highly attentive staff is exceptionally accommodating to families, providing spacious seaside tables.

Rufo's Grill

A beloved, upscale-yet-relaxed open-air grill known for superb steaks and seafood. The friendly, noisy atmosphere easily masks any baby fussiness, and highchairs are readily available for dinner.
