Istria welcomes your family with its sun-drenched olive groves, medieval hilltop towns, and the sparkling, calm waters of the Adriatic Sea. In Rovinj, the pastel-colored Venetian facades rise directly from the water, creating a postcard-perfect backdrop for your luxury getaway. The stroller-friendly Lungomare Plaza boardwalk provides a seamless, smooth path for Mary Claire's stroller, leading directly to peaceful, pine-shaded beaches. Here, you can transition effortlessly from private yacht charters to exceptional seafood dining, all while enjoying Croatia's gentlest microclimate.
🚕 Getting there: A 40-minute drive from Pula Airport (PUY) to Rovinj is best executed via a pre-arranged Mercedes V-Class private transfer, fully equipped with a rear-facing Cybex infant car seat and ample luggage space for your luxury stroller.

What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Lim Fjord Private Boat Charter

Glide through the calm, emerald waters of this dramatic sunken valley on a private speedboat with a protective sun canopy, avoiding any open-sea swells that could disrupt Mary Claire's nap.

Morning 8-10am / 2h

Punta Corrente Forest Park

Stroll along wide, flat, gravel-paved pathways winding through centuries-old pine and cypress trees, offering total shade and pristine air for a peaceful morning stroller walk.

Late morning 10am-2pm / 4h

Brijuni National Park Veliki Brijun Island

Take the gentle 15-minute ferry from Fažana to this car-free island sanctuary, where you can rent a private family golf cart equipped with seat belts to explore shaded archaeological ruins and see exotic animals.

Afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Lungomare Plaza Promenade

A beautifully paved, stroller-friendly seaside walk offering gorgeous views of the old town, boasting high-end boutiques and perfect spots to rest under pine trees while Mary Claire gazes at the gentle waves.

Evening 6-7:30pm / 1.5h

Church of St. Euphemia

While the historic interior is quiet and majestic, the spacious paved plateau outside offers breathtaking panoramic sunset views over the Adriatic with plenty of room to rock an infant in a baby carrier.

Morning 9-12pm / 3h

Grožnjan Hilltop Village

Explore this car-free medieval hilltop town of artists, opting for an ergonomic baby carrier instead of a stroller to easily navigate the historic cobblestone streets while enjoying classical music drafts from open windows.

Morning 9-10:30am / 1.5h

Valdibora Farmer's Market

Browse vibrant stalls of fresh figs, lavender, and local honey just past the historic Balbi's Arch, enjoying flat ground and lively morning sensory stimulation for Mary Claire.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Lone Bay Beach

A pristine, wind-sheltered pebble beach bordering the forest park, offering crystal-clear, wave-free waters perfect for dipping baby Mary Claire's toes under the natural shade of pine trees.

Afternoon 3-4:30pm / 1.5h

Chiavalon Organic Olive Oil Estate

Experience an award-winning organic olive oil tasting in a sleek, modern, climate-controlled facility with plenty of space for a stroller and an incredibly welcoming family-first staff.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Puntulina

Signature fresh sea bass in Istrian malvazija sauce; the spacious outdoor terrace accommodates a compact stroller, and booking an early 6 PM seating offers a gentle sea breeze and comforting ambient ocean white noise.

Restaurant Santa Croce

Grilled Adriatic calamari; its spacious, quiet garden courtyard is highly welcoming to families, providing ample room for highchairs, quick service, and a relaxed atmosphere that easily absorbs baby giggles.

Taverna De Amicis

Black truffle handmade pljukanci pasta; this cozy spot features an airy outdoor terrace with highchairs available on request and a warm, hospitable staff who adore infants, making an early dinner effortless.

Kantinon Tavern

Slow-cooked beef cheek with local polenta; a rustic-chic harborside tavern offering spacious indoor booths, highchairs, a lively but not too loud atmosphere, and an accommodating staff that serves early dinners.

Restaurant Blu

Fresh lobster spaghetti; sits directly on a quiet beachfront with a flat, stroller-accessible outdoor terrace where Mary Claire can watch the gentle waves under a shaded pergola during a late lunch.
