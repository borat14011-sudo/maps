What to do

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

Playa Langosta

Boasting some of the calmest, shallowest waters in Cancún, this beach is essentially a giant natural wading pool perfect for dipping Mary Claire's toes. Plentiful palapas offer excellent sun protection.

Midday 11am-1pm / 2h

Interactive Aquarium Cancun

Located inside the open-air La Isla Shopping Village, this fully stroller-accessible, air-conditioned aquarium provides a vibrant sensory experience for an infant with its colorful fish and gentle touch tanks.

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

El Meco Archaeological Site

A highly overlooked, intimate Mayan ruin site located just north of downtown. It features lovely shade from mature trees and flat grassy paths, making it a peaceful, crowd-free alternative to Tulum for a baby-carrier stroll.

Late afternoon 4-5:30pm / 1.5h

Playa Delfines Lookout

Enjoy the sweeping views from the stroller-accessible wooden boardwalk and take photos by the colorful 'Cancun' sign before the midday heat. The lookout platform allows you to experience the dramatic ocean vistas without struggling through deep sand.

Early evening 5-7pm / 2h

Parque Las Palapas

Experience local culture at this central plaza in downtown Cancún. An early evening visit allows Will and Kathryn to stroll the paved plaza, watch local families, and enjoy a gentle breeze before the late-night crowds arrive.

Morning 9am-12pm / 3h

Nizuc Resort Private Beach

Book a day pass to access this ultra-exclusive, reef-sheltered cove. The water is virtually waveless, and the beachside service will set up premium umbrellas and plush loungers to keep Mary Claire cool and comfortable.

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

La Isla Shopping Village

A beautiful, canal-side outdoor shopping plaza with wide, flat paved paths that are highly stroller-friendly. Features excellent family restrooms, nursing rooms, and shaded rest areas.

Sunset 5:30-7pm / 1.5h

Marina Town Center Boardwalk

Located at Puerto Cancún, this upscale marina offers a wide, quiet, harbor-front boardwalk ideal for a peaceful sunset stroller walk away from the bustling Hotel Zone traffic.

Morning 8:30-10:30am / 2h

Playa Tortugas

Another excellent north-facing beach featuring shallow, lagoon-like waters and very minimal undercurrent. Go early in the morning to secure a shaded beach chair near the calmest swimming areas.

🍽️ Where to eat

Lorenzillo's

An iconic overwater lagoon restaurant famed for its live Caribbean lobster. The expansive outdoor wooden deck offers a refreshing breeze and plenty of space for a stroller, with an early 5:30 PM opening perfect for beating the dinner rush.

Puerto Madero

An elegant Argentine steakhouse serving high-end ribeyes and fresh seafood. The indoor dining room features exceptionally spacious table layouts and cold air conditioning, making it highly accommodating for a sleeping baby in a stroller.

Harry's Grill & Prime Steakhouse

This high-end steakhouse offers a gorgeous, tranquil outdoor terrace right on the Nichupté Lagoon. Booking an early 6:00 PM seating allows you to enjoy the legendary sunset and upscale service before the livelier, louder crowd arrives.

Rosa Negra Cancún

While known for a high-energy party vibe later at night, their early evening seating (starting at 5:30 PM) on the spectacular open-air lagoon terrace is peaceful, spacious, and highly welcoming to families.

Chambao Cancún

An ultra-luxury grill offering prime cuts in a stunning, open-air palapa-style design next to the water. The early evening atmosphere is relaxed, with large, plush booths that provide ample space to comfortably settle an infant.
