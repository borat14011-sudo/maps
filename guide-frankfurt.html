What to do

Morning 10am-12pm

Städel Museum

Will and Kathryn can admire European masterworks while utilizing the museum's spacious, smooth-floored galleries that make stroller navigation exceptionally easy, plus there are quiet baby-changing facilities on site.

Midday 12pm-1pm

Kaiserdom

This historic imperial cathedral offers a cool, quiet interior ideal for a peaceful contact nap for Mary Claire, with wide stone aisles that easily accommodate a luxury stroller.

Morning 9-10:30am

Römerberg

The picturesque heart of the Old Town features paved pathways where the family can admire rebuilt medieval houses, though baby earmuffs are recommended if the nearby cathedral bells ring.

Afternoon 2-4pm

Palmengarten

This majestic botanical garden offers lush, climate-controlled greenhouses and wide gravel paths perfect for a shaded afternoon stroll with Mary Claire among exotic flora.

Morning 10am-12pm

Senckenberg Natural History Museum

Fascinating dinosaur skeletons will captivate the parents, while the wide, air-conditioned exhibition halls offer a comfortable, sensory-friendly environment for a baby.

Morning 10-11:30am

Kleinmarkthalle

Will and Kathryn can sample gourmet local delicacies and fresh fruits at this vibrant indoor market hall, which is best visited during quieter mid-morning hours to avoid heavy crowds with a stroller.

Morning 10-11:30am

Museum Judengasse

This poignant and beautifully designed museum features quiet, modern exhibition spaces that are fully stroller accessible, offering a reflective and peaceful morning activity.

Late afternoon 4-5pm

Alte Oper

While attending an evening show might be tough with an infant, strolling around the grand plaza with its soothing fountains provides a delightful, open-air photo opportunity for the family.

Evening 6-7pm

Mainkai River Promenade

The paved, car-free riverbank promenade offers sweeping views of the dramatic Frankfurt skyline and is ideal for a sunset stroller walk while Mary Claire dozes in the breeze.

Sample local apple wine and hearty frankfurters on the spacious, lively outdoor terrace, where the natural ambient noise easily drowns out any infant fussiness.

Opéra Restaurant
