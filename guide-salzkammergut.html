Salzkammergut

← back to travel board
Nestled between dramatic limestone peaks and crystal-clear waters, Salzkammergut offers an idyllic alpine playground for families. From the nostalgic steam cog railway of St. Wolfgang to the turquoise swimming bays of Lake Attersee, every corner feels like a living postcard. It is a slow-paced haven where toddlers can splash in pristine lakes while parents soak in the dramatic mountain vistas.
🚕 Getting there: ~50 min drive by rental car or pre-booked taxi from Salzburg Airport (SZG) directly to the lakeside hotels in St. Wolfgang.

What to do

Morning / 3h

SchafbergBahn Cog Railway

Chug up the mountain on Austria's steepest steam cog railway for jaw-dropping views of the surrounding lakes. Toddlers will love the sensory thrill of the vintage red train, but pack a jacket as it gets windy at the summit.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Wolfgangsee Lake Cruise

Hop aboard a nostalgic paddle steamer to glide across the brilliant turquoise waters of Lake Wolfgangsee. It is a relaxing, stroller-friendly way to travel between villages, with plenty of room for little ones to roam the deck.

Morning or Afternoon / 3-4h

Abarena All-Weather Play Park

A massive indoor and outdoor play paradise featuring giant slides, water playgrounds, trampolines, and a model train city. It is the ultimate energy-burner for toddlers, regardless of the weather.

Friday Morning / 1-2h

St. Wolfgang Weekly Market

Stroll through the local market stalls filled with fresh alpine cheeses, gingerbread, and handmade wooden toys. It's a low-key sensory experience where toddlers can munch on soft pretzels while you shop.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Erlebnisbad Attersee

Splash in the heated pools or swim directly in the crystal-clear, Caribbean-blue waters of Lake Attersee. The shallow toddlers' wading pool and grassy picnic areas make it incredibly safe and comfortable for young families.

Morning / 2-3h

Zwölferhorn Cable Car

Ride the modern red cabins up to the Zwölferhorn mountain for an easy, panoramic loop trail. The path is stroller-accessible, allowing toddlers to wander safely among alpine wildflowers.

Late Afternoon / 1.5h

Gustav Klimt Theme Trail

Walk along the scenic shores of Lake Attersee where the famous artist painted, marked by educational plaques and art installations. The flat, paved path is perfect for double strollers and offers beautiful lake entry points for stone-throwing toddlers.

Morning / 2h

St. Wolfgang Old Town Exploration

Wander through the car-free, cobblestone streets lined with colorful frescoed buildings and visit the lakeside pilgrimage church. Toddlers will love spotting the wooden animal carvings and feeding ducks by the lakeside promenade.

Afternoon / 1.5h

Lake Attersee Artist Cruise

Take a scenic boat ride around Austria's largest lake, famous for its intense turquoise hue that inspired famous artists. It's a peaceful experience with climate-controlled cabins, perfect for a mid-day toddler nap.

🍽️ Where to eat

Romantik Restaurant Kaiserterrasse

Order the classic Wiener Schnitzel or fresh lake trout while dining on the floating lakeside terrace of the famous White Horse Inn.

Kaffeewerkstatt

Indulge in a slice of homemade Austrian apple strudel and excellent specialty coffee in a cozy, exceptionally family-friendly atmosphere.

Dorfalpe

Feast on a hearty plate of Kaiserschmarrn (shredded caramelized pancakes) in a rustic, log-cabin setting with plenty of space for kids.

Restaurant Langostinos

Savor freshly caught Attersee char (fish) and delicious seafood pasta right on the northern shore of the lake.

Das Attersee

Enjoy a relaxed family lunch of local venison ragout or handmade dumplings on the sun-drenched deck overlooking the water.

👨‍👩‍👧 Purchase the Salzkammergut Sommercard for significant discounts on the SchafbergBahn, cable cars, and boat cruises. Always carry cash for mountain huts, and pack an all-terrain stroller or baby carrier, as many scenic alpine paths are gravel.
