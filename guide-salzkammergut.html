Salzkammergut

← back to travel board
Salzkammergut offers a pristine, crowd-free alternative to busy European coastlines, blending crystal-clear lakes with dramatic alpine backdrops. For a family with an infant, the region provides exceptionally clean mountain air, smooth paved lakeside promenades, and highly efficient infrastructure. St. Wolfgang and Lake Attersee offer the perfect balance of upscale Austrian hospitality and absolute safety, making stroller logistics and baby nap times entirely stress-free.
🚕 Getting there: ~50-minute private taxi transfer from Salzburg Airport (SZG) directly to your lakeside hotel via the B158 highway.

What to do

Morning / 3h

SchafbergBahn Cog Railway

Ascend the mountain on Austria's steepest historic steam cog railway for sweeping, panoramic views of the lakes. Foldable strollers fit easily in the historic wooden carriages, but using a baby carrier is highly recommended for exploring the gravel paths at the summit.

Midday / 1.5h

WolfgangseeSchifffahrt Ferry

Cruise between St. Wolfgang and St. Gilgen on a historic paddle steamer with spacious, stroller-accessible indoor and outdoor decks. It is the most scenic, vibration-free way to lull your toddler to sleep while enjoying alpine lake breezes.

Afternoon / 2h

Wildpark Kleefeld

Walk through shaded forest paths to see red deer, mountain goats, and a dedicated petting zoo area perfect for toddler sensory play. The gravel paths are wide and well-suited for a sturdy, all-terrain stroller.

Morning / 1.5h

Bürglstein Promenade

Walk this flat, wooden stroller-friendly boardwalk suspended directly over the water, connecting Strobl and St. Wolfgang. It offers a peaceful, traffic-free walk with stunning lake reflections and zero elevation gain.

Morning / 2-3h

Zwölferhorn Cable Car

Ride the state-of-the-art, fully barrier-free gondolas to the top of Zwölferhorn for a panoramic view of the Wolfgangsee. The summit features a flat, stroller-friendly loop trail called the Pillsteinweg with jaw-dropping views and no steep drop-offs.

Afternoon / 3h

Strandbad Unterach

Relax at this family-friendly public beach on Lake Attersee, featuring a dedicated, shallow heated toddler pool and wide shaded lawns. It is the perfect spot for safe water play away from the deeper, colder lake waters.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Klimt Artist Trail

Stroll along the paved lakeside promenade in Schörfling, featuring beautiful art installations dedicated to Gustav Klimt. The path is entirely flat, stroller-friendly, and offers numerous benches for bottle feeding or quick breaks.

Morning / 3h

Abarena All-Weather Play Park

An absolute paradise for toddlers, this indoor and outdoor park features soft play areas, water playgrounds, and miniature ride-on trains. It is highly accommodating to infants with clean nursing rooms and stroller parking throughout.

Afternoon / 1.5h

Attersee-Schifffahrt Boat Tour

Enjoy a peaceful, whisper-quiet cruise on Lake Attersee's modern solar-powered vessel. The smooth, climate-controlled cabins provide a sanctuary for infant feeding while parents admire the turquoise, Caribbean-like waters.

🍽️ Where to eat

Gasthof Weißer Hirsch

Order the classic Wiener Schnitzel made with local veal, served with parsley potatoes. The lakeside terrace is spacious enough to park a stroller right at the table, and the gentle lake breeze masks any baby noise.

Dorfalpe

Try the fluffy, caramelized Kaiserschmarrn (shredded pancake) served with sweet plum compote. The rustic alpine atmosphere is loud and lively, meaning you will never have to worry about a crying baby disrupting other diners.

Restaurant Langostinos

Savor the fresh-caught Attersee Char (Seesaibling) grilled with garlic and fine herbs. The outdoor garden seating overlooks the water and has plenty of room for high chairs and stroller positioning.

Fischbrathütte in der Au

Get the traditional Steckerlfisch (smoked trout on a spit) wrapped in paper, eaten at casual wooden picnic tables. This ultra-casual, outdoor lakeside setup allows for quick, stress-free dining with zero wait times for hungry toddlers.

Kaffeewerkstatt

Order a premium flat white and a slice of homemade Austrian apple strudel. This modern, airy cafe has a relaxed vibe, clean diaper-changing facilities, and plenty of space for stroller maneuvering.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Romantik Hotel Im Weissen Rössl

This legendary lakefront hotel features a heated pool floating directly inside Lake Wolfgangsee, premium family suites, and top-tier crib setups. Its central location in St. Wolfgang means you can walk everywhere with a stroller.

Familienhotel Post

A dedicated family resort on Lake Attersee offering professional infant care, indoor soft play areas, baby monitors, and a shallow baby pool. Perfect for parents looking for a premium, low-friction stay with high-end amenities.

Hotel Stroblerhof

Boasts a massive private park, a heated outdoor pool, and an incredibly peaceful atmosphere just steps from the lake. The spacious family rooms easily accommodate baby cribs, and the flat grounds make stroller navigation effortless.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack a high-quality all-terrain stroller like a Bugaboo or Thule, as many scenic alpine walking paths use packed gravel rather than smooth asphalt. Keep plenty of 1 and 2 Euro coins on hand, as many public lakeside changing stations and baby-friendly restrooms require coin access. Note that local supermarkets (like Billa and Spar) close early on Saturdays and are entirely closed on Sundays, so stock up on infant formula, diapers, and snacks beforehand.
