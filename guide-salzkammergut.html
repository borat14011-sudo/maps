What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 2.5h

SchafbergBahn Cog Railway

Board Austria's steepest steam cog railway for a nostalgic, gentle journey up the mountain; baby Mary Claire will be mesmerized by the rhythmic click-clack sound, and parents can easily fold a compact stroller to store in the historic red carriages.

Midday 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM / 1.5h

Wolfgangsee Schifffahrt Ferry

Enjoy a peaceful cruise across Lake Wolfgangsee on a historic paddle steamer; the spacious outdoor decks are perfect for wheeling a stroller while enjoying the fresh alpine breeze, and the indoor first-class saloon offers a quiet environment for an infant nap.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM / 1h

St. Wolfgang Pilgrimage Church (Wallfahrtskirche)

Admire the stunning Michael Pacher winged altar in this historic lakeside church; the entrance is completely flat and stroller-accessible, offering a cool, quiet, and shaded sanctuary ideal for a peaceful sensory break for Mary Claire.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 2h

Gustav Klimt Center at Lake Attersee

Explore the life and inspiration of the famous painter along the flat, stroller-friendly Klimt Promenade in Schörfling; the adjacent museum is air-conditioned and quiet, providing a relaxing, culturally enriching stroll for Will and Kathryn.

Morning 9:30 AM - 12:00 PM / 2.5h

Zwölferhorn Cable Car (Zwölferhorn Seilbahn)

Ride the modern, spacious cable car cabins which easily accommodate a stroller without folding; the peak features a level, barrier-free panoramic loop trail (the Pillsteinweg) that is perfect for pushing Mary Claire while taking in 360-degree views of the lakes.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:30 PM / 2.5h

Kaiservilla Park and Gardens

Stroll the meticulously manicured, shaded gravel pathways of Emperor Franz Joseph’s summer residence in nearby Bad Ischl; the sprawling park is exceptionally quiet, offering a peaceful backdrop for baby to sleep in her stroller under the shade of ancient trees.

Late Morning 11:00 AM - 2:00 PM / 3h

Postalm Alpine Plateau

Drive up the scenic toll road to Europe's second-largest high plateau, where flat, paved alpine walking paths wind through gentle pastures; the clean mountain air and ringing cowbells provide a charming, sensory-friendly outdoor experience for the whole family.

Afternoon 2:30 PM - 4:00 PM / 1.5h

Attersee-Schifffahrt Cruise

Relax on a climate-controlled luxury cruise vessel across Lake Attersee, famous for its turquoise water; the boat features wide aisles for strollers, modern baby-changing facilities, and an onboard cafe where Kathryn and Will can sip Gruner Veltliner during Mary Claire's afternoon nap.

Late Afternoon 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM / 1.5h

Promenade Schloss Kammer

Enjoy a leisurely, flat walk along the shores of northern Lake Attersee directly beneath the iconic Schloss Kammer castle; the wide boardwalk is perfectly paved for strollers, boasts plenty of lakeside benches for feeding, and offers beautiful, wind-sheltered photo opportunities.

🍽️ Where to eat

Restaurant Im Weissen Rössl

Indulge in upscale Austrian cuisine on the legendary, wind-protected lakeside terrace; the staff are exceptionally welcoming to families, providing premium wooden highchairs, early 6:00 PM dinner reservations, and a quiet ambiance where Mary Claire can observe the gentle lake waves.

Litzlberger Keller

Feast on fresh Attersee wild-caught char in a shaded historic chestnut garden overlooking the lake; the spacious outdoor seating easily accommodates a sleeping stroller next to the table, and the relaxed lakeside vibe naturally absorbs occasional baby babble.

Paul der Wirt

A modern, family-friendly Austrian restaurant in St. Gilgen boasting spectacular elevated views of Lake Wolfgangsee; they offer a spacious terrace, an exceptionally clean baby-changing station, and early seating options to align with Mary Claire’s bedtime routine.

Restaurant Langostinos

Savor high-end Mediterranean seafood on a peaceful deck directly over Lake Attersee; the staff are warm, attentive, and quick to accommodate requests for hot water to warm bottles or pureed infant meals.

Konditorei Zauner

A historic Imperial pastry shop in Bad Ischl featuring a riverfront terrace; the bustling yet elegant atmosphere is perfect for Will and Kathryn to enjoy a legendary Zaunerstollen cake while Mary Claire is happily distracted by the passing strollers and gentle river currents.

Hotel Grafengut

$200-350/night

A highly rated, peaceful boutique property located directly on the shores of Lake Attersee with a sprawling private park. It features large, modern family apartments, a shaded lakeside lawn perfect for Mary Claire’s tummy time, and exceptionally quiet rooms that guarantee a restful night's sleep.
