What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Spiaggia Grande

The heartbeat of Positano, this iconic beach offers reserved VIP loungers under shaded orange umbrellas, a flat boardwalk for a stroller, and calm, shallow shoreline waters perfect for dipping Mary Claire's toes.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Villa Rufolo

Located in Ravello, these historic cliffside gardens offer paved, stroller-friendly pathways, soaring pine trees that provide excellent canopy shade for an infant, and breathtaking panoramic views of the Gulf of Salerno.

Early afternoon 1-2pm / 1h

Cloister of Paradise at Duomo di Amalfi

A serene, Moorish-style cloister with elegant white columns and a lush central garden that offers a quiet, shaded sanctuary away from the mid-day sun, perfect for a peaceful contact nap in a carrier.

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Giardini di Augusto

These beautiful terraced gardens in Capri are easily reached via a short, smooth funicular ride from the harbor, offering wide paved paths, stroller-accessible viewing platforms, and abundant shade.

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Marina Grande in Sorrento

A charming, historic fishing village with a wide, completely flat wooden boardwalk that is exceptionally stroller-friendly, offering calm waters and a relaxed pace away from the steep cliffs.

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Piazza Tasso

Sorrento's vibrant central square features a spacious, flat pedestrian layout ideal for a late afternoon stroller walk, surrounded by high-end gelaterias and shaded cafe terraces.

Morning 8:30-11:30am / 3h

Valle delle Ferriere Nature Reserve

A lush, forested valley near Amalfi featuring a gentle, shaded dirt path along the Canneto river, providing cool microclimates and soothing waterfall sounds ideal for an infant carrier hike.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Sentiero degli Dei Bomerano Trailhead

The starting flat portion of the Path of the Gods in Bomerano offers wide, smooth gravel paths and sweeping mountain views, manageable with an ergonomic baby carrier before the trail becomes too rugged.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Spiaggia di Fornillo

A quieter alternative to Positano's main beach, accessed via a scenic, flat coastal walk from the harbor, offering secluded stone coves with ample umbrella shade and a peaceful family atmosphere.

🍽️ Where to eat

Chez Black

This legendary beachfront institution offers spacious outdoor terrace seating, highchairs, and an incredibly warm staff who adore infants, making it easy to enjoy their signature sea urchin pasta.

Ristorante Max

Tucked inside a quiet, shaded art gallery courtyard in Positano, this upscale restaurant provides a serene environment, early dinner seating, and exquisite handmade pastas perfect for a relaxed family meal.

La Sponda

Located inside Le Sirenuse, this Michelin-starred venue accommodates refined families during their early evening opening, where acoustic musicians and a soft candlelit ambiance keep baby Mary Claire calm.

Ristorante Marina Grande

An upscale, modern beachfront restaurant in Amalfi featuring a completely flat, stroller-accessible outdoor deck, highchairs, and sensational local seafood with gentle sea breezes.

Da Adolfo

Reached via a quick, private boat shuttle from Positano pier, this casual beachfront luxury spot offers shaded sunbeds, a relaxed outdoor terrace, and excellent grilled mozzarella on lemon leaves.
