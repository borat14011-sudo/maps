What to do

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Dolmabahçe Palace Gardens

Stroll the pristine, flat manicured lawns overlooking the Bosphorus, perfect for a luxury stroller. The peaceful palace gardens offer a quiet escape from the city noise and plenty of shade under massive plane trees for Mary Claire's mid-morning nap.

Midday 12:00-1:30pm / 1.5h

Basilica Cistern

A cool, dimly lit subterranean wonderland that is wonderfully quiet, helping Mary Claire doze off to the sound of dripping water. The newly renovated wooden boardwalks are completely stroller-friendly with ramp access throughout.

Late afternoon 4:00-6:00pm / 2h

Bebek Park

A wealthy neighborhood park on the Bosphorus with a fantastic playground, manicured lawns, and paved stroller paths. Will and Kathryn can enjoy third-wave coffee while Mary Claire watches the yachts glide past.

Sunset 5:00-7:00pm / 2h

Private Bosphorus Yacht Cruise

Charter a private luxury yacht from Zoe Yacht for a gentle, private cruise on the water. The soft swaying of the calm water is incredibly soothing for an infant, and the indoor cabin offers air-conditioned comfort for feeding.

Early morning 8:30-10:00am / 1.5h

Sultanahmet Square

A wide, flat pedestrian-only plaza perfect for pushing a stroller under the shade of ancient obelisks. Best visited early in the morning before crowds build and temperatures rise.

Afternoon 2:00-4:00pm / 2h

Galataport Istanbul

A modern, ultra-sleek waterfront promenade that is completely flat, car-free, and stroller-accessible. It features premium nursing rooms and beautiful family lounges overlooking the harbor.

Morning 10:00am-12:00pm / 2h

Topkapi Palace First Courtyard

The outer courtyard features wide gravel and paved paths lined with massive trees providing deep shade. It is a quiet, airy space to stroll with an infant away from Sultanahmet's bustle.

Morning 9:00-11:30am / 2.5h

Emirgan Park

A sprawling hillside park famous for its gardens, ponds, and paved pathways. Rent a golf cart or push the stroller through shady groves of pine trees, stopping at historic wooden pavilions for Turkish tea.

Afternoon 2:00-4:30pm / 2.5h

Rahmi M. Koç Museum

A world-class transport and technology museum located on the Golden Horn with wide, flat exhibition halls that are stroller-friendly. It is exceptionally spacious, cool, and features a quiet waterside cafe for a relaxed baby feeding.

🍽️ Where to eat

Sunset Grill & Bar

Perched in Ulus Park with panoramic Bosphorus views, this upscale venue offers an early 6:00 PM dinner seating. Their signature Duck Roll and fresh sushi can be enjoyed on a spacious outdoor terrace that easily accommodates a stroller.

Karaköy Güllüoğlu

Savor the legendary Pistachio Baklava served with rich clotted cream. The spacious, modern outdoor seating area at their flagship Galataport-adjacent location is highly accessible, noisy enough to mask baby babbles, and has highchairs.

Pandeli

Located inside the Spice Bazaar, this historic, Michelin-recommended blue-tiled sanctuary serves impeccable slow-cooked lamb dome pilaf. Book a late lunch and request a corner table to park the stroller; the ambient hum of diners creates a welcoming white noise.

Feriye

An elegant waterfront restaurant serving modern Turkish cuisine like slow-roasted beef rib. Its massive, flat seaside terrace offers plenty of space for strollers, highchairs on request, and a breezy, relaxed atmosphere perfect for lunch.

Mangerie

Located in the upscale Bebek neighborhood, this terrace restaurant serves a world-class Turkish breakfast spread. The atmosphere is bustling and lively, which easily drowns out infant noise, though arriving early via their elevator is recommended.

Sura Hagia Sophia Hotel

$200-300/night

A highly rated luxury hotel in the heart of Sultanahmet boasting a serene outdoor pool and lush botanical gardens. Spacious family suites easily fit a complimentary baby cot, while the excellent soundproofing guarantees quiet afternoon naps.

Ibrahim Pasha Hotel

$200-280/night

This exquisite boutique hotel in Sultanahmet offers a cozy, residential feel with large deluxe rooms that accommodate premium cribs. While it lacks a pool, the hotel features a gorgeous, heated rooftop terrace for quiet evening drinks while Mary Claire sleeps downstairs, and the staff are famously accommodating to infant requests.
