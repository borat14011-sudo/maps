Zadar wraps you in a sensory embrace of ancient Roman history and Adriatic sophistication, where the gentle lap of the sea meets contemporary luxury. Strolling along the wide, marble-paved Riva promenade with baby Mary Claire offers unparalleled seaside views without the stress of navigating heavy steps. The sunset here is a legendary ritual, bathing the historic stone walls in warm gold as the Sea Organ plays its soothing, natural lullaby. For families seeking seamless comfort, the city's upscale coastal resorts provide private pool villas and sheltered beaches, making it an effortless sanctuary for sophisticated travelers.
🚕 Getting there: A seamless 20-to-30-minute private Mercedes V-Class transfer from Zadar Airport (ZAD) is the ideal choice for a stress-free transition, easily accommodating a premium stroller and pre-installed infant car seat.

What to do

Early morning 8-9:30am / 1.5h

Sea Organ

Listen to the sea sing through this architectural masterpiece. The wide, flat stone steps are perfectly stroller-accessible, allowing you to sit right by the water with baby Mary Claire during the quiet morning hours when the sound is soft and mesmerizing.

Sunset 7:30-8:30pm / 1h

Sun Salutation

This mesmerizing multi-layered glass installation harnessess solar power to create a playful light show. Visit just before sunset; the flat, expansive surface is ideal for holding Mary Claire while watching the colors shift as the light show begins.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Zadar Roman Forum

Explore the largest forum on the Adriatic, dating back to the 1st century BC. The surrounding grassy lawns offer a soft spot to lay a blanket for infant tummy time, while the wide stone paths are easy to navigate with a stroller.

Midday 12-1pm / 1h

St. Donatus Church

An iconic 9th-century Byzantine church with monumental acoustics. Step inside for cool air-conditioned relief from the midday heat; the cavernous interior has a natural calming effect that will easily soothe a restless baby.

Late afternoon 5-6:30pm / 1.5h

Zadar Riva

A magnificent, palm-fringed seaside promenade perfect for a scenic stroll. It features wide paved pathways completely free of vehicle traffic, making it the ultimate safe space to walk with your stroller while enjoying the ocean breeze.

Full day 8am-4pm / 8h

Plitvice Lakes National Park

A rewarding day trip to see Croatia's world-famous emerald lakes and Kozjak Lake. While the wooden boardwalks are not stroller-friendly, it is a spectacular experience when wearing Mary Claire in a premium hiking carrier under the shade of the forest canopy.

Morning half-day 8am-12pm / 4h

Velika Paklenica Canyon

Located in Paklenica National Park just 40km from Zadar, this beginner-friendly canyon trail features dramatic limestone cliffs and excellent shade. Perfect for Will and Kathryn to hike with baby Mary Claire comfortably secured in a front-carrier.

Afternoon 2-3:30pm / 1.5h

Museum of Ancient Glass

Housed in a 19th-century palace, this quiet and air-conditioned museum displays exquisite ancient Roman glass. The ramped entrances and spacious elevators make it entirely stroller-friendly, offering a quiet, sensory-friendly environment for the baby.

Morning 9-11:30am / 2.5h

Kolovare Beach

Zadar's premier beach, featuring paved coastal paths, pebble shores, and a lush pine forest providing natural shade. Rent a luxury sun lounger to relax with Mary Claire near the calm, shallow waters of the Adriatic.

🍽️ Where to eat

Restoran Bruschetta

Indulge in high-end Mediterranean flavors like fresh sea bass carpaccio. The spacious outdoor terrace easily accommodates luxury strollers, and the staff are notoriously welcoming to young families during early evening seatings.

Restoran 4 Kantuna

Famous for its premium truffle pasta and artisanal pizzas. Situated in a spacious corner of the Old Town, they offer highchairs on request and feature a lively but family-friendly atmosphere that easily masks any baby babble.

Restoran Foša

An upscale seafood restaurant situated in a historic harbor. Book a waterfront table on their stunning outdoor terrace to enjoy premium local wines and grilled fish while Mary Claire naps peacefully in her stroller beside you.

Restoran Kornat

Sophisticated fine dining highlighting traditional Dalmatian seafood with a modern twist. The restaurant's elegant, quiet indoor dining room and professional service ensure a smooth, comfortable experience during early dinner seatings.

Harbor Cookhouse & Club

A trendy waterfront spot with an industrial-chic vibe and a very spacious layout. Excellent for a casual luxury lunch; they have plenty of room for strollers, great air conditioning, and a wonderful terrace overlooking the marina.
