Puerto Escondido (Rinconada)

← back to travel board
Nestled on the cliffs above the calmest cove in Puerto Escondido, Rinconada offers a sleepy, walkable escape perfect for families. Here, days are measured by baby turtle releases, gentle toddler-friendly swims, and sunset strolls along the vibrant boulevard. It is the ultimate low-stress Mexican beach destination where convenience meets tropical paradise.
🚕 Getting there: ~10 min taxi from Puerto Escondido International Airport (PXM) straight to your Rinconada rental or hotel

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Playa Carrizalillo

A breathtaking turquoise bay nestled in a cove with the calmest swimming waters in the area. Note that the 160 stone steps down are steep, so leave the stroller behind and use a baby carrier.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Baby Turtle Release at Playa Bacocho

Join Vive Mar to help release newly hatched sea turtles into the ocean at sunset. Toddlers will be absolutely mesmerized watching the tiny hatchlings waddle across the sand.

Morning / 2-3h

Playa Puerto Angelito

A highly protected, wave-free harbor filled with local families and gentle shallow shores. It is the absolute best spot for toddlers to safely splash and play in the ocean.

Morning / 1-2h

Mercado Benito Juárez

A bustling local indoor market filled with colorful tropical fruits, fresh juices, and artisan crafts. The wide main aisles are easy to navigate with toddlers, who will love the sweet, fresh mango smoothies.

Morning / 2-3h

Laguna de Manialtepec

An incredible mangrove lagoon where you can take a gentle daytime boat tour to spot herons and iguanas. Opt for an early morning tour to keep toddlers cool and avoid mid-day heat.

Morning / 2h

Playa Coral

A secluded, low-key beach with soft powdery white sand perfect for building sandcastles. Access is through a gentle pathway, but bring your own umbrella as natural shade is limited.

Evening / 1h

Paseo Rinconada Walk

Stroll the wide, pedestrian-friendly sidewalks of Rinconada’s main strip lined with grassy medians and shops. It is a completely stress-free walk for toddlers to stretch their legs before dinner.

Morning / 2-3h

Dolphin Watching Boat Tour

Take a small boat charter from the main bay to watch hundreds of wild dolphins jump and spin. Ensure you request a boat with a canopy for shade and toddler-sized life jackets beforehand.

Morning / 2-3h

Playa Manzanillo

Separated from Puerto Angelito by a small rocky point, this beach offers incredibly calm, shallow waters and beachfront restaurants. Parents can sit with their feet in the sand while toddlers splash nearby.

🍽️ Where to eat

El Cafecito

Grab a table on the breezy terrace and order the legendary giant cinnamon rolls, fresh fruit plates, and fluffy banana pancakes.

El Sultán

Get the ultra-creamy hummus plate and freshly baked pita bread, which make for perfect, easy-to-eat toddler finger foods.

Espadín Restaurant

Perched on the cliffside, order the fresh local fish tacos and a coconut water while enjoying spectacular, unobstructed views of the cove below.

Pizzería La Luna Rosa

Get the authentic, thin-crust Margherita pizza baked in a wood-fired oven, a guaranteed crowd-pleaser for tired toddlers.

La Olita

Get the gourmet coconut shrimp tacos wrapped in fresh tortillas and pair them with their signature hibiscus agua fresca.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack a sturdy baby carrier because the scenic beach coves require navigating steep stone steps that are impossible for strollers. Always carry cash in Mexican Pesos, as many local Rinconada restaurants, beach palapas, and taxis do not accept credit cards.
