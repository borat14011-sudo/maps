Puerto Escondido (Rinconada)

Nestled cliffside above the gentlest bay in Puerto Escondido, Rinconada offers a walkable, pedestrian-friendly haven perfect for slow-paced family escapes. Here, toddlers can splash in calm turquoise waters while parents savor world-class sunsets and fresh Oaxacan flavors just steps from their villas.
🚕 Getting there: ~10 min taxi ride from Puerto Escondido International Airport (PXM) straight to Rinconada hotels

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Playa Carrizalillo

Puerto Escondido's most beautiful, sheltered cove features ultra-calm waters perfect for beginner swimmers. Note for toddlers: there are about 160 steep stone steps to climb down, so bring a baby carrier instead of a stroller.

Late Afternoon (5:00 PM) / 1-2h

Baby Turtle Release at Playa Bacocho

Join the local conservation group Vive Mar to release newborn sea turtles into the ocean at sunset. Toddlers will love watching the tiny hatchlings scurry across the sand into the waves.

Morning / 2-3h

Playa Puerto Angelito

A highly protected, wave-free harbor filled with local families and gentle shallow waters. It is completely flat with no stairs, making it incredibly easy to navigate with toddlers and beach gear.

Morning / 2-3h

Laguna de Manialtepec Boat Tour

Take a peaceful daytime boat ride through lush mangrove forests to spot iguanas, herons, and rare birds. It is a calm, non-bumpy ride that keeps toddlers entertained with wildlife sightings.

Morning / 1-2h

Mercado Benito Juárez

A vibrant, bustling indoor market filled with colorful tropical fruits, local crafts, and delicious food stalls. Toddlers will love tasting fresh bananas and mangoes while parents shop for souvenirs.

Early Morning / 2-3h

Dolphin and Whale Watching Boat Tour

Board a small boat from the main bay to spot massive pods of wild dolphins jumping alongside the hull. The excitement of seeing dolphins up close is magical for kids of all ages.

Morning (during low tide) / 2h

Playa Coral Tide Pools

A quieter beach tucked away near Rinconada that features shallow rock pools during low tide. These natural pools act like baby bathtubs where toddlers can safely splash and spot tiny crabs.

Evening / 1-2h

Rinconada Strip Evening Stroll

Walk the wide, stroller-friendly sidewalks of Benito Juárez boulevard in the evening. It is lined with grassy areas, ice cream shops, and family-friendly restaurants where kids can run safely.

Evening / 1-2h

El Adoquín Pedestrian Zone

This historic beachfront street closes to vehicle traffic at night, turning into a lively pedestrian mall. It is perfect for an easy evening walk with toddlers to see street performers and local toy vendors.

🍽️ Where to eat

El Cafecito

Order the famous, fluffy mango pancakes and fresh-baked pastries in their breezy, family-friendly palapa garden.

Espadín Restaurant

Savor fresh local ceviche and fish tacos while sitting on the cliffside deck overlooking the stunning sunset of Carrizalillo bay.

Pizzería JP-H

Treat the kids to delicious, authentic wood-fired Margherita pizzas in a very casual, welcoming open-air garden setting.

Smoked Fish Taco El Tío

Grab their legendary smoked sailfish tacos loaded with fresh cabbage and mild, kid-friendly cream sauces.

Almoraduz Cocina de Autor

Enjoy a relaxed but high-end Oaxacan dinner featuring slow-cooked pork belly and tasty, fresh-fruit mocktails for the kids.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack a lightweight carrier as the stairs to the best cove beaches are too steep for strollers. Always keep cash (pesos) handy for beach vendors and taxi rides, as card readers are rarely used here.
