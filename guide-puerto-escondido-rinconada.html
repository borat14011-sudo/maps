Rinconada offers the perfect antidote to chaotic resort towns, blending a laid-back, highly walkable restaurant strip with some of Puerto Escondido's calmest swimming coves. For a young family with an infant, its cliffside geography means cooler ocean breezes, minimal traffic, and a highly manageable footprint where everything is under ten minutes away. You can easily balance morning beach sessions in sheltered bays with quiet afternoon strolls along the shady, brick-paved main boulevard. It is safe, remarkably relaxed, and offers just the right amount of understated comfort for a discerning family.
🚕 Getting there: ~10 min taxi from Puerto Escondido International Airport (PXM) directly to Rinconada.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Playa Carrizalillo

This breathtaking, sheltered cove features ultra-calm waters perfect for dipping a toddler's toes safely. Keep in mind there are 165 steep stone steps to descend, so leave the stroller behind and opt for a sturdy baby carrier.

Morning / 2-3h

Playa Manzanillo

Known for its gentle waves and shallow shorelines, this beach is incredibly safe for toddlers to splash around in. It is much easier to access than Carrizalillo, allowing you to bring a lightweight travel stroller close to the sand.

Morning / 1-2h

Mercado Regional Benito Juárez

Introduce your toddler to vibrant colors and fresh tropical fruits like mango and papaya at this bustling covered market. The aisles can be narrow and busy, so a baby carrier is highly recommended over a stroller for navigating the stalls.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Vive Mar Tortugueros (Playa Bacocho)

Participate in releasing newborn sea turtles into the ocean at sunset, an unforgettable and gentle wildlife encounter for young kids. The wide, flat sandy beach is easy to walk on, though you will want to carry your toddler as the shoreline waves here are quite strong.

Morning / 2-3h

Laguna de Manialtepec Boat Tour

Take a peaceful, shaded motorboat tour through the calm mangrove forest to spot herons, iguanas, and various water birds. The gentle motion of the boat is incredibly soothing for infants, and the canopy provides essential sun protection.

Evening / 1-2h

Calle Benito Juárez Boulevard

This quiet, tree-lined main street of Rinconada is paved and highly stroller-friendly, perfect for an early evening stroll. It's lined with family-friendly cafes and has almost no fast-moving traffic, making it a stress-free walk with a toddler.

Evening / 1-2h

El Adoquín Pedestrian Street

Walk along this vehicle-free coastal corridor in the evenings to enjoy local street performers, artisan stalls, and a lively atmosphere. The flat, paved terrain is exceptionally stroller-friendly and allows your toddler to walk safely without traffic worries.

Late Afternoon / 30m

Mirador Las Tortugas

A scenic clifftop lookout point in Rinconada offering stunning ocean views and a chance to spot whales during winter months. It features a flat, paved viewing platform that is easily accessible with a stroller for a quick, breezy photo op.

Afternoon / 2h

Playa Coral

Access this secluded, quiet beach through a flat path from the Villa Sol beach club for a peaceful afternoon on the soft sand. The waves are generally calmer here than at Bacocho, and the beach club access makes it easy to retreat to shade with a baby.

🍽️ Where to eat

El Cafecito Rinconada

Order the classic Chilaquiles Verdes or fresh mango pancakes at this local institution. The open-air, spacious palapa layout offers plenty of room to park a stroller, and the bustling morning noise easily drowns out toddler babble.

Restaurant Espadín

Enjoy fresh catch of the day or coconut shrimp while taking in spectacular sunset views over Carrizalillo cove. They open early for dinner at 5:00 PM, allowing you to dine during toddler-friendly hours while securing a spacious edge table with room for a high chair.

Pizzeria La Luna Rosa

Savor authentic, wood-fired thin-crust pizzas in a relaxed, garden-like outdoor setting. The casual, welcoming staff are incredibly accommodating to families, and the outdoor tables provide a low-stress environment for a dining toddler.

Dulce Tierra Rinconada

Grab artisan sourdough bread, croissants, and homemade gelato to keep both parents and toddlers happy. The outdoor seating on the sidewalk is perfect for quick, casual bites where a fussy baby won't disrupt anyone.

Mana Rinconada

This healthy, bohemian garden spot serves excellent shakshuka, smoothies, and hummus bowls in a highly relaxed environment. The grassy, shaded garden seating allows toddlers to sit on picnic rugs safely away from any street hazards.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Villas Carrizalillo

Offering spacious multi-bedroom villas equipped with full kitchens, this hotel is ideal for preparing infant meals and sterilizing bottles. The cliffside pool features breathtaking views and plenty of shaded spots, though note the stairs down to the beach will require a baby carrier.

Hotel Quinta Rinconada

A peaceful boutique hotel featuring a lovely, clean pool surrounded by tropical gardens just steps from the main strip. The staff is famously warm and accommodating, often going out of their way to provide quiet ground-floor rooms perfect for stroller access.

Hotel Villa Sol

This family-oriented hotel features a dedicated children's pool, lush gardens, and private shuttle access to its own beach club on Playa Coral. They provide cribs upon request and have spacious suites, making it exceptionally comfortable for families traveling with infants.

👨‍👩‍👧 For beach days, skip the heavy stroller and bring a high-quality baby carrier to safely navigate the steep cliffside stairs down to Playa Carrizalillo. Stock up on high-quality diapers, wipes, and infant-safe mineral sunscreen at the large Chedraui supermarket located just a 5-minute taxi ride away in Centro. Stick to bottled water for everything, including brushing your toddler's teeth and washing bottles, and utilize the local laundromats along the Rinconada strip for quick, cheap turnaround on baby laundry.
