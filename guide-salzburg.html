What to do

Early morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM / 1.5h

Mirabell Palace and Gardens

Stroll the impeccably manicured gravel pathways where Mary Claire can gaze at vibrant floral displays. The wide, flat terrain is exceptionally stroller-friendly, and the quiet Pegasus Fountain area offers plenty of shaded benches under grand trees for a peaceful mid-morning bottle feed.

Late morning 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM / 2h

Hohensalzburg Fortress via Festungsbahn

Ascend to Salzburg's iconic clifftop castle via the modern, fully glass-enclosed Festungsbahn funicular, which easily accommodates strollers. The fortress courtyards are spacious, offering panoramic Alpine vistas for Will and Kathryn while baby enjoys a smooth, scenic ride.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM / 2h

Salzburg Zoo Hellbrunn

Set against a dramatic steep rock face, this zoo features beautifully paved, flat paths that are perfect for strollers. The lush, shady tree canopy keeps the temperature cool, making it a soothing environment for Mary Claire to observe gentle alpine and exotic animals.

Late afternoon 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM / 1.5h

Schloss Hellbrunn Park

While the famous trick fountains are best skipped to keep baby dry, the sprawling palace gardens feature expansive lawns, tranquil ponds, and paved paths. It is an ideal, quiet spot for an afternoon picnic or a gentle contact nap in the carrier.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 2h

Haus der Natur

An outstanding natural history museum featuring a massive, peaceful aquarium section. The dim lighting and slow-moving, colorful marine life provide a highly sensory yet calming environment for an infant, and the entire facility is equipped with modern elevators and spacious family restrooms.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM / 1.5h

Getreidegasse Stroll

Salzburg's premier historic shopping street is lined with high-end boutiques and ornate guild signs. Plan your walk during the quiet morning hours to avoid heavy crowds, allowing your stroller to glide smoothly over the historic flagstones while baby enjoys the visual stimulation.

Afternoon 1:30 PM - 3:30 PM / 2h

DomQuartier Salzburg

Explore the opulent staterooms of the Prince-Archbishops at a leisurely pace. The museum is highly accessible via elevators, and its quiet, reverent atmosphere acts as a natural white-noise chamber, often lulling babies into a peaceful nap while parents admire baroque masterpieces.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 3:15 PM / 1.25h

Salzach River Cruise on the Amadeus Salzburg

Board the sleek panoramic boat for a gentle cruise down the Salzach River. The smooth, vibration-free water transit provides scenic views of the city skyline without any bumpy terrain, making it incredibly relaxing for parents and a perfect opportunity for a baby nap.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 1.5h

Hangar-7

A futuristic glass-and-steel dome housing Red Bull's collection of historic aircraft and Formula 1 cars. The entire pavilion features a perfectly flat, polished concrete floor that is dream-like for strollers, and the airy, bright space is rarely crowded during weekday mornings.

🍽️ Where to eat

St. Peter Stiftskulinarium

Dine on elegant Salzburger Nockerl in Europe's oldest restaurant. Request a table in the spacious, open-air inner courtyard, which easily accommodates a stroller and provides enough ambient acoustic ceiling to mask any unexpected infant coos.

M32

Perched high on the Mönchsberg and reached via a spacious, stroller-accessible elevator. Enjoy a classic Wiener Schnitzel on the expansive outdoor terrace, offering panoramic city views, comfortable highchairs, and a breezy atmosphere perfect for families.

Café Tomaselli

Austria's oldest active coffee house is perfect for a morning Einspänner and a slice of Esterházy torte. The bustling, friendly outdoor terrace on the Alter Markt is highly welcoming to families, allowing you to park your stroller right next to your table.

Restaurant Esszimmer

For an upscale dining experience, this Michelin-starred venue is surprisingly warm and accommodating. Book an early dinner seating at 6:00 PM; the professional staff is happy to accommodate a sleeping baby in a stroller at your table while you enjoy locally sourced Arctic Char.

Gasthof Goldgasse

A refined yet cozy tavern serving traditional Tafelspitz. Ask for a table in the charming pedestrian alleyway seating area during the warmer months, which offers a relaxed, casual vibe and plenty of room to keep Mary Claire comfortable in her carrier.
