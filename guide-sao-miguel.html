São Miguel

← back to travel board
São Miguel offers the dramatic, emerald landscapes of Hawaii but with premium European infrastructure, a short overnight flight from the East Coast, and a highly manageable timezone shift for a young family. The island is incredibly safe, the highways are immaculate and easy to navigate via rental car, and the locals are deeply welcoming to babies. Here, you can hike misty crater lakes, relax in warm thermal waters, and dine on fresh Atlantic seafood without the intense crowds of mainland Europe.
🚕 Getting there: ~10 min drive via pre-booked rental car to Ponta Delgada hotels, or ~45 min drive to Furnas valley resorts.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Parque Terra Nostra

Explore stunning 18th-century botanical gardens featuring towering ferns and warm, iron-rich geothermal pools. The main paths are flat and highly stroller-accessible, making it easy to admire the exotic flora with an infant.

Morning / 1h

Miradouro da Vista do Rei

Take in the legendary panoramic view of the blue and green twin lakes of Sete Cidades. There is a smooth paved viewing platform right next to the parking area, allowing for effortless stroller access.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Fábrica de Chá Gorreana

Stroll through Europe's oldest operational tea plantation, featuring rolling green hills overlooking the ocean. The indoor museum is fascinating, and the outdoor grassy paths are perfect for carrying your toddler in a baby carrier.

Afternoon / 2h

Reserva Florestal de Recreio do Pinhal da Paz

This manicured forest reserve features massive hydrangeas, walking paths, and a fantastic children's playground. It is incredibly peaceful, flat, and offers plenty of shade for a mid-day toddler nap in the stroller.

Morning / 1h

Mercado da Graça

Visit this lively farmer's market to sample sweet Azorean pineapples and local cheeses. The wide, flat indoor aisles are easy to navigate with a stroller, offering a sensory-rich morning experience.

Morning / 2h

Monumento Natural da Caldeira Velha

Walk through a prehistoric-feeling jungle to discover natural hot springs heated by a volcano. A paved wooden boardwalk runs throughout the park, making it easy to transport a baby to the viewing areas.

Afternoon / 1h

Ananases A. Arruda

Walk through historical glass greenhouses to see how the island’s famous pineapples are grown in organic soil. The paths between greenhouses are flat and quick to navigate, holding a toddler's interest before she gets restless.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Praia das Milícias

This volcanic black sand beach features a wide, flat concrete promenade perfect for scenic stroller walks. It has easy beachfront parking, a beachside cafe for quick snacks, and calm, shallow waters near the shore.

Evening / 1h

Portas da Cidade

The iconic 18th-century city gates sit on a grand, flat public square in the heart of the capital. It's an excellent, vehicle-free space to let your toddler stretch her legs during a relaxed evening walk.

🍽️ Where to eat

A Tasca

Order the famous sesame-crusted seared tuna and local cheese platters. Arriving right at their early opening time of 6:30 PM ensures you secure a table before the crowd and aligns perfectly with early toddler bedtimes.

Restaurante Associação Agrícola de São Miguel

Savor the absolute best, ultra-tender Azorean beef steaks on the island in a massive, airy dining room. The spacious layout easily accommodates strollers, and the lively ambient noise effortlessly drowns out any toddler fussiness.

Restaurante Tony's

Try the legendary 'Cozido das Furnas,' a rich meat and vegetable stew slow-cooked underground by volcanic steam. The service is incredibly fast and family-friendly, making it an easy, high-chair-equipped lunch stop.

Bar Caloura

Feast on the freshest catch-of-the-day fish on a stunning outdoor cliffside patio right over the ocean. The open-air setting is ideal for keeping a baby entertained, and there is plenty of room to park a stroller table-side.

Tasquinha Vieira

Enjoy refined, market-fresh modern Azorean dishes in a chic yet welcoming environment. Book an early outdoor table so you can enjoy high-end gastronomy while your baby rests comfortably in her carrier.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Octant Furnas

This premium design hotel features 24-hour thermal pools, pristine cribs, and large family suites, offering a tranquil base in the lush valley.

Grand Hotel Açores Atlântico

A classic 5-star luxury hotel overlooking the marina with highly attentive service, soundproof rooms, indoor heated pool, and seamless stroller access to the oceanfront promenade.

Santa Bárbara Eco-Beach Resort

Offers gorgeous, modern villas equipped with kitchenettes—crucial for washing baby bottles and warming milk—situated right on a stunning beach with a heated saltwater pool.
