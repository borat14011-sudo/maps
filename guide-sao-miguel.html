São Miguel

← back to travel board
With its emerald crater lakes, steaming thermal pools, and dramatic coastlines, São Miguel is a playground of natural wonders. Families can explore mystical forests, watch whales leap from the Atlantic, and taste food cooked by volcanic steam. It is a safe, lush paradise where slow-paced island life meets epic outdoor discovery.
🚕 Getting there: ~10 min taxi or rental car drive to Ponta Delgada hotels

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Sete Cidades (Vista do Rei Viewpoint)

Peer into the massive twin volcanic lakes, one blue and one green, which offer breathtaking views and easy paved walking paths. Toddlers will love pointing at the distant cows grazing on the crater walls.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Terra Nostra Park

Wander through stunning botanical gardens with giant ferns and ancient trees before dipping into the warm, iron-rich thermal pool. Toddlers can safely splash in the shallower, cooler side streams or feed the resident ducks.

Morning / 1-2h

Gorreana Tea Plantation

Explore Europe's oldest tea plantation, running wild through the manicured maze-like rows of green tea. It's a great, open space for toddlers to run around safely, followed by a free cup of warm tea inside.

Morning / 2h

Lagoa do Congro Hike

Hike down into a hidden volcanic crater to find this emerald-green lake surrounded by dense, fairy-tale vegetation. The trail can be slippery, so carry toddlers in a carrier rather than trying to navigate with a stroller.

Morning / 3h

Whale Watching from Ponta Delgada

Board a spacious catamaran to spot resident sperm whales and playful dolphins leaping alongside the boat. Toddlers are welcomed on the larger, stabler vessels, but bring warm layers and sea-sickness bands just in case.

Morning / 1h

Mercado da Graça

Experience a burst of local culture at this vibrant farmers' market packed with sweet Azorean pineapples, fresh cheeses, and colorful flowers. Toddlers will enjoy tasting the sweet pineapple chunks sold directly from the stalls.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Caldeira Velha

Bathe in warm, iron-rich thermal pools nestled inside a lush jungle beneath a cascading waterfall. The lower pool has a gentle, shallow entry point perfect for a toddler's warm splash.

Late Afternoon / 2-3h

Praia das Milícias

Build sandcastles on this expansive, soft black-sand beach with gentle waves and easy access. Toddlers will love the unique volcanic sand, and parents will appreciate the beachfront cafes and stroller-friendly boardwalk.

Afternoon / 1h

Arruda Pineapple Plantation

Walk through the unique glass greenhouses to see how these tiny, sweet pineapples are grown in volcanic soil. The gravel paths are easy for toddlers to navigate, and the pineapple ice cream at the end is a must.

🍽️ Where to eat

Tony's Restaurant

Order the famous Cozido das Furnas, a hearty meat and vegetable stew slow-cooked underground by volcanic steam.

A Tasca

Savor the fresh grilled limpets with garlic butter and the local tuna steak sesame-crusted to perfection.

Associação Agrícola de São Miguel

Indulge in the island's best, ultra-tender beef steaks served with local red peppers and a fried egg.

Queijaria Furnense

Try the artisanal cheeses cured in volcanic waters paired with local honey and sweet bolo lêvedo bread.

O Tasco

Feast on traditional Portuguese tapas like octopus salad and broken eggs with local chorizo in a cozy, rustic setting.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack a high-quality baby carrier instead of a stroller, as most scenic trails, crater views, and historic cobblestone streets are highly uneven. Always bring quick-drying towels and dark swimwear for the family, as the iron-rich thermal waters will permanently stain light fabrics.
