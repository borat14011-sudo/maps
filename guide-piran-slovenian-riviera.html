What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Tartinijev Trg (Tartini Square)

This majestic, marble-paved oval square is completely car-free, making it the perfect spot to push your luxury stroller while admiring the pastel Venetian facades. Early mornings are wonderfully peaceful here, offering plenty of shade under the grand stone arcades for baby Mary Claire to relax.

Late morning 11am-12:30pm / 1.5h

Cathedral of St George

Perched above the town, this historic church offers sweeping panoramic views of three countries: Slovenia, Italy, and Croatia. While the walk up is a bit steep for strollers, a sturdy baby carrier allows you to easily navigate the path and enjoy the cooling hilltop breezes with Mary Claire.

Midday 12:30-1:30pm / 1h

Bell Tower of St. George's Parish Church

Modeled after the Campanile of St. Mark in Venice, this iconic tower offers breathtaking views of the red-roofed town below. Parents can take turns climbing the wooden steps to the top while the other rests with Mary Claire in the quiet, shaded courtyard gardens at the base.

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Secovlje Salina Nature Park

A serene, expansive landscape of ancient salt pans where salt is still harvested by hand using 700-year-old methods. The flat, wooden boardwalks are highly stroller-accessible, allowing you to easily stroll with Mary Claire while taking in the fresh, mineral-rich sea air.

Morning 9-11:30am / 2.5h

Portoroz Beach

The premier sandy stretch on the Slovenian Riviera, offering luxurious private cabanas and exceptionally calm, shallow waters. It is an ideal spot for baby Mary Claire's first gentle dip in the Adriatic Sea, with convenient beachfront facilities and paved paths for easy stroller access.

Morning 10-10:30am / 30m

Venetian House (Benečanka)

The oldest preserved house on Tartini Square, featuring stunning Venetian Gothic architecture and a romantic balcony. Located right on the flat main square, it is effortless to view from your stroller walk, allowing you to capture gorgeous family photos without any difficult steps to navigate.

Afternoon 2-3:30pm / 1.5h

Mediadom Pyrhani

A modern, air-conditioned cultural museum that uses state-of-the-art 3D technology to showcase Piran's history. It is a fantastic, quiet indoor escape to keep baby Mary Claire cool during the peak afternoon heat, featuring smooth surfaces that are fully stroller-accessible.

Afternoon 4-5pm / 1h

Minorite Monastery of St. Francis

An oasis of absolute silence, this Franciscan monastery features a beautiful inner cloister known for its incredible acoustics. The flat, shaded courtyard is highly welcoming for families, providing a tranquil, contemplative space to soothe a restless baby away from the sun.

Late afternoon 5:30-6:30pm / 1h

Trg 1. Maja (1st of May Square)

A charming, smaller paved square decorated with stone allegorical statues representing Law and Justice. It is an ideal, quiet spot to relax with a gelato while Mary Claire rests in her stroller, surrounded by local cafes and beautiful Baroque architecture.

🍽️ Where to eat

Restaurant Riviera Adriatic

This elegant waterfront establishment serves immaculate sea-to-table Slovenian cuisine alongside spectacular sunset views. They offer spacious outdoor terrace seating that easily accommodates a luxury stroller, highchairs on request, and an exceptionally welcoming staff.

Gostilna Pri Mari

Renowned for its fresh Mediterranean seafood and warm, family-friendly hospitality. Booking an early dinner seating at 6:00 PM is highly recommended, allowing you to enjoy their signature truffle handmade pasta in a relaxed, low-noise environment with Mary Claire.

Fritolin Pri Cantini

A beloved local open-air tavern located on the picturesque Trg 1. Maja. Savor fresh, crispy fried calamari and local sea bass at their casual outdoor wooden tables, which provide a lively yet relaxed family atmosphere where infant noise is easily absorbed by the open air.

Pavel 2

Featuring a prime seaside terrace on the Piran promenade, this upscale restaurant specializes in premium carpaccio and fresh wild-caught fish. Their outdoor dining setup is exceptionally stroller-friendly, allowing you to enjoy a crisp glass of local Malvazija wine while baby naps.

Restauration Sophia

An exquisite Michelin-recommended fine dining restaurant dedicated to Sophia Loren, located within the Kempinski Palace. The restaurant offers a sophisticated yet accommodating atmosphere for elegant families during early evening seatings, complete with luxury highchairs and impeccable, personalized service.

Hotel Piran

$200-310/night

Positioned directly on the historic Piran waterfront, this boutique hotel offers spacious superior family rooms and premium cribs on request. Parents will love the rooftop terrace for sunset drinks, while the direct, step-free access to the paved seaside promenade makes stroller outings seamless.
