What to do

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

Bell Rock Pathway

An exceptionally wide and hard-packed dirt trail that is highly accessible for all-terrain strollers, allowing Will and Kathryn to experience Sedona's red rocks up close while baby Mary Claire enjoys a shaded, smooth ride.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Tlaquepaque Arts & Shopping Village

A gorgeous, Spanish-colonial style village shaded by giant sycamore trees. The paved courtyard paths are highly stroller-accessible, and the soothing sounds of decorative fountains will keep baby calm.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Red Rock State Park Sentinel Meadow Trail

A gentle, flat, and partially shaded trail featuring boardwalk sections over lush meadows. Perfect for a morning family walk where you can spot local wildlife without strenuous climbing.

Mid-morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Sedona Trolley Tour

A private or small-group open-air trolley ride that offers a breezy and relaxing way to see the city's highlights. Ideal for letting Mary Claire rest on a lap while parents enjoy the narrations and spectacular views.

Late afternoon 4-5pm / 1h

Amitabha Stupa and Peace Park

A serene 14-acre park at the foot of Thunder Mountain. The short, flat walking trails are quiet and peaceful, offering a perfect, low-stimulation environment for an outdoor infant nap.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Slide Rock State Park Apple Orchard Walk

Skip the slippery water slides with the baby and instead stroll through the historic, flat apple orchards. The grassy areas under the shade of the canyon walls offer a lovely spot for baby tummy time.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Dead Horse Ranch State Park Lagoons

Located just a short drive away, this park features incredibly flat, stroller-friendly loops around calm fishing lagoons. The abundant shade and peaceful water views make for a highly relaxing morning.

Afternoon 1-3pm / 2h

Oak Creek Canyon Scenic Drive

A breathtaking 14-mile drive along Route 89A. This is the ultimate luxury nap-time activity for Mary Claire, allowing Will and Kathryn to admire towering rock walls in air-conditioned comfort.

Early morning 9-10am / 1h

Chapel of the Holy Cross

An architectural marvel built directly into the red rocks. The venue features a fully paved, step-free ramp winding up to the chapel, making it easy to push a stroller to the top for panoramic valley views.
