Samaná Peninsula

The Samaná Peninsula offers a pristine, eco-luxury retreat where lush rainforests cascade down to dramatic, golden-sand coastlines. For Will, Kathryn, and baby Mary Claire, the serene waters of Las Terrenas provide a gentle, wave-free environment perfect for a toddler's first dip. The paved, stroller-friendly beachfront promenade of Playa Las Ballenas allows for effortless morning walks under the shade of swaying coconut palms. This unspoiled haven balances wild Caribbean beauty with the seamless high-end comforts essential for a luxury infant getaway.
🚕 Getting there: Santo Domingo Airport (SDQ) to Las Terrenas is a scenic 2-hour drive via the modern Juan Pablo II Highway; we recommend a private luxury SUV transfer with a pre-installed rear-facing infant car seat and ample trunk space for your stroller.
