Montego Bay welcomes sophisticated travelers with its vibrant turquoise waters, lush green hillsides, and world-class hospitality. Along the pristine white sands of Doctor's Cave Beach, families will find a serene oasis perfect for introducing a little one to the warm Caribbean Sea. The area's elite resorts specialize in seamless family travel, providing private beach cabanas and dedicated nanny services. Parents can easily navigate the smooth paved promenades of the top-tier properties with a luxury stroller while taking in the gentle ocean breezes.
🚕 Getting there: 15 minutes from Sangster International Airport (MBJ) to the Rose Hall hotel strip via a private luxury SUV transfer booked through Island Routes, featuring a pre-installed, rear-facing Clek infant car seat.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Doctor's Cave Beach

This legendary beach features crystal-clear, calm waters with a sandy, shallow entry perfect for baby Mary Claire's first dip. Rent a shaded private beach umbrella and use the stroller-accessible concrete ramp right to the sand edge.

Mid-morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Montego Bay Marine Park Glass Bottom Boat Tour

Explore the vibrant coral reefs from a fully shaded glass-bottom boat, keeping baby completely protected from the sun while enjoying the gentle sway of the calm lagoon.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM

Rocklands Bird Sanctuary

A tranquil sanctuary in the hills where colorful hummingbirds fly down to feed. It is a quiet, shaded environment where parents can sit peacefully while baby watches the gentle birds from her carrier.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM

Ahhh...Ras Natango Gallery and Garden

A beautifully manicured hillside tropical garden offering fresh breezes and stunning vistas. The pathways are shaded by lush canopies, offering a peaceful, low-noise environment to walk with a baby carrier.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Martha Brae River Rafting

Enjoy a highly serene, ultra-calm 30-foot bamboo raft ride down a lazy river. The entire route is shaded by a dense canopy of bamboo trees, keeping the baby cool and comfortable on your lap.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM

Rose Hall Great House Gardens

Stroll the beautifully manicured, expansive lawns and paved garden paths surrounding this legendary estate. The grounds are highly stroller-accessible and shaded by giant banyan trees, ideal for an easy afternoon walk.

Late afternoon 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Harmony Beach Park

This modern, state-of-the-art public park features wide, paved, stroller-friendly boardwalks along the ocean. It is exceptionally clean, well-patrolled, and boasts modern changing rooms with baby-friendly facilities.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Chukka Ocean Outpost at Sandy Bay

A scenic coastal outpost offering ocean-side walking paths and beautiful seaside lawns. Parents can relax in shaded gazebos while enjoying the ocean breeze with baby in a calm, controlled environment.

Midday 12:30 PM - 2:00 PM

Montego Bay Cultural Centre

Located in historic Sam Sharpe Square, this air-conditioned facility offers a cool refuge from the midday heat. The galleries are spacious, flat, and stroller-accessible, offering quiet, visually engaging exhibitions.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Sugar Mill Restaurant

Indulge in the signature Jerk chicken penne or charcoal-grilled lobster on the romantic, historic outdoor terrace. Highchairs are available, and booking an early 6:00 PM seating allows you to dine peacefully before the late-night crowd arrives.

Marguerite's Seafood Restaurant

Savor the signature pan-seared snapper overlooking the water on the open-air oceanfront deck. The gentle sound of the waves masks baby babbles, and they happily accommodate strollers at the seaside tables during early seatings.

Scotchies Rose Hall

Feast on authentic, wood-smoked jerk pork served with festival bread in a casual, open-air garden setting. The ambient chatter and rustic outdoor layout make it highly infant-friendly, with plenty of room to park a stroller.

The Houseboat Grill

Enjoy the signature coconut-crusted shrimp on a unique, permanently moored floating tavern. Request an early terrace table on the lower deck for easy stroller access and a calm, quiet atmosphere directly on the water.

Mystic India

Savor authentic chicken tikka masala inside a spacious, beautifully air-conditioned dining room. The cool indoor climate is a welcome relief for baby, and highchairs are readily available upon request.
