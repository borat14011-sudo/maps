Montego Bay

Montego Bay welcomes families with its warm turquoise waters, powdery white sands, and the infectious rhythm of the reggae beat. From gentle, shallow lagoons perfect for tiny splashers to lush river adventures, this Jamaican paradise strikes a flawless balance between relaxation and tropical exploration. It is a vibrant playground where multi-generational memories are painted in shades of sunset and emerald green.
🚕 Getting there: ~10-20 min pre-arranged private shuttle or official JUTA taxi from Sangster International Airport to most major beach resorts

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Doctor's Cave Beach

This legendary beach features calm, mineral-rich turquoise waters and soft white sand that are perfect for building sandcastles. Toddlers will love the extremely gentle surf and the convenient on-site amenities like umbrella rentals and clean changing rooms.

Morning / 2h

Rafting on the Martha Brae River

Drift gently down a scenic river on a 30-foot bamboo raft steered by an experienced local guide. It is incredibly soothing for toddlers who can sit safely between parents and watch for colorful birds and butterflies in the canopy.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Rocklands Bird Sanctuary

Hand-feed vibrant hummingbirds that fly right up to you in this lush, peaceful garden. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the tiny finches and ground doves eating seeds directly from their hands.

Morning / 2h

Green Grotto Caves

Explore a subterranean wonderland of stalactites, stalagmites, and a crystal-clear underground lake. The paved pathways and gentle lighting make it accessible, though toddlers should be carried in a carrier as there are some steps.

Full Day / 4-5h

Chukka Eco-Adventure Outpost at Good Hope

This historic estate offers a fantastic jungle playground, a refreshing pool with a splash area, and a horse companion experience. Toddlers will enjoy the horse encounters and splashing in the shallow, kid-friendly pool areas.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Aquasol Theme Park at Walter Fletcher Beach

A lively beachfront park offering water sports, a go-kart track, and giant inflatable water slides. Toddlers can safely splash in the shallow, wave-free shore or play in the shaded grassy picnic areas.

Morning / 1-2h

Montego Bay Marine Park

Take a glass-bottom boat tour over this protected marine sanctuary to view colorful coral reefs and tropical fish without getting wet. It is an ideal way for toddlers to experience the underwater world comfortably and safely from the dry boat.

Morning / 1-2h

Harbour Street Craft Market

Browse a colorful, bustling open-air market filled with handmade wooden toys, straw hats, and vibrant Jamaican crafts. Toddlers will be entertained by the sensory sights and friendly local vendors showing off carved animal figures.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Rose Hall Great House Gardens

Stroll through the beautifully manicured, sprawling tropical gardens of this legendary 18th-century plantation. Toddlers have plenty of safe, lush grassy lawns to run around on while parents admire the ocean views and historic architecture.

🍽️ Where to eat

Scotchies

Order the famous, authentic jerk chicken or pork smoked over pimento wood, served with sweet festival dumplings that toddlers absolutely love.

The Pelican Grill

Get the family-friendly, classic Jamaican oxtail stew or their legendary old-fashioned milkshakes in a comfortable, air-conditioned retro diner setting.

Marguerites

Enjoy fresh-caught grilled red snapper on the oceanfront terrace while watching the sunset directly over the Caribbean Sea.

The Houseboat Grill

Sit on the deck of this unique floating restaurant and order the coconut-crusted shrimp while kids delight in watching fish swim in the illuminated water below.

Pier One

Feast on mild, kid-friendly fish and chips or a delicious tropical fruit platter at this open-air, waterfront spot with panoramic harbor views.

👨‍👩‍👧 Always use official JUTA taxis or pre-booked resort shuttles for safe and hassle-free transport with little ones. Pack plenty of reef-safe sunscreen and insect repellent, as the tropical sun and evening mosquitoes can be intense for sensitive toddler skin.
