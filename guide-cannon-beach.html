Cannon Beach offers an exquisite blend of dramatic Oregon coastline and upscale village charm, perfect for an elegant family retreat with baby Mary Claire. The iconic Haystack Rock rises majestically from the shoreline, providing a breathtaking backdrop for morning beachfront strolls just steps from your luxury suite. With its highly walkable, stroller-ready downtown lined with refined boutiques and art galleries, navigating the town with an infant is effortlessly smooth. Parents can indulge in the ultimate Pacific Northwest escape while enjoying pristine, soft-sand beaches and fresh, world-class coastal cuisine.
🚕 Getting there: Portland International Airport (PDX) to the Cannon Beach hotel strip is a scenic 90-minute drive; the premier family option is a private luxury SUV transfer equipped with a pre-installed, rear-facing infant car seat.

What to do

Morning low tide / 2h

Haystack Rock

Marvel at this iconic 235-foot monolith during low tide. Carry Mary Claire in a front carrier to explore the vibrant tide pools filled with colorful starfish and anemones without struggling with stroller wheels in the sand.

Late morning 10am-12pm / 1.5h

Ecola State Park Overlook

Take in dramatic clifftop views of the historic Tillamook Rock Lighthouse and the sweeping coastline. The paved pathway to the main viewpoint is fully stroller-accessible, offering a smooth ride and cool sea breezes for baby.

Afternoon 1-3pm / 2h

Hug Point State Recreation Site

Explore seasonal waterfalls and carved sandstone caves at low tide. Use a baby carrier to safely walk along the flat, packed sandy beach where Mary Claire can watch the cascading water in a calm, shaded cove.

Mid-afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Tolovana Beach State Recreation Site

A quieter stretch of beach featuring a fantastic playground and a flat, stroller-friendly paved ramp leading right to the sand. Ideal for setting up a luxury beach tent for an undisturbed afternoon nap.

Morning 11am-12:30pm / 1.5h

Cannon Beach History Center & Museum

A quiet, indoor sanctuary perfect for a rainy morning or during Mary Claire's mid-day nap. The flat, stroller-friendly exhibits showcase local coastal heritage and indigenous history in a peaceful, low-sensory environment.

Mid-day 12-1pm / 1h

Icefire Glassworks

Watch live, mesmerizing glassblowing demonstrations from a safe, spacious gallery. The bright, swirling colors of the molten glass will captivate baby, and the airy studio is fully stroller-friendly.

Early morning 8:30-10am / 1.5h

Arcadia Beach State Recreation Site

An upscale, serene beach escape located just south of town. Excellent for a peaceful morning carrier walk to admire the dramatic rock formations away from the busier downtown crowds.

Late afternoon 4-5:30pm / 1.5h

Cannon Beach City Park

A beautifully manicured green space in the heart of town featuring a modern playground and quiet shaded areas. Perfect for laying out a luxury blanket for Mary Claire's tummy time under the coastal pines.

Early afternoon 1-2:30pm / 1.5h

Haystack Hill State Park

Take a peaceful, deeply shaded carrier hike through a lush coastal forest trail. The dense canopy provides excellent sun protection for Mary Claire on bright afternoons while parents enjoy the quiet wilderness.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Wayfarer Restaurant & Lounge

Savor local Dungeness crab cakes and premier oceanfront views. Excellent highchairs, a welcoming staff, and early 5:00 PM dinner reservations make this a seamless luxury dining experience with an infant.

Pelican Brewing Company

Indulge in award-winning craft beers and coastal pub fare. The spacious, bustling dining room and outdoor patio easily accommodate strollers, and the natural ambient noise easily drowns out any baby babbles.

Lazy Susan Cafe

An iconic, cozy cottage spot famous for fresh marionberry waffles and seafood omelets. Opt for early morning seating when it is quietest, and enjoy their highly accommodating, family-first hospitality.

Seasons Cafe

Enjoy gourmet artisan sandwiches and fresh salads on the wind-sheltered outdoor terrace. The relaxed, upscale casual vibe is perfect for a quick, stress-free lunch with Mary Claire resting in her stroller.

Ecola Seafoods Restaurant & Market

A local institution serving world-class clam chowder and wild-caught salmon. The casual, spacious counter-service format ensures fast service, meaning you can eat delicious seafood on your own baby-led schedule.
