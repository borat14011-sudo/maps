Skiathos welcomes discerning families with its unique blend of lush, emerald pine forests and calm, crystal-clear Aegean waters. The island offers an upscale yet laid-back Greek island experience, perfectly suited for parents seeking relaxation with an infant in tow. Will, Kathryn, and baby Mary Claire will appreciate the flat, stroller-friendly wooden boardwalks of Koukounaries Beach and the peaceful, pedestrianized promenade of Skiathos Town. From private beach clubs with five-star service to quiet, shallow lagoons, this destination ensures a seamless luxury holiday with a baby.
🚕 Getting there: The journey from Skiathos Alexandros Papadiamantis Airport (JSI) to the luxury hotel strip near Koukounaries takes approximately 20 minutes in a private Mercedes V-Class minivan equipped with a pre-installed, rear-facing Cybex infant car seat and ample luggage capacity for a Bugaboo stroller.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Koukounaries Beach

Fringed by a protected pine forest, this world-renowned beach offers incredibly calm, shallow waters perfect for dipping baby Mary Claire's toes. The shaded pine paths at Koukounaries provide a cool, stroller-accessible retreat from the midday Mediterranean sun.

Late Afternoon 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM

Bourtzi Fortress Peninsula

This historic, pine-covered islet separates the old and new harbors of Skiathos Town. Paved paths wind through the fortress ruins under dense pine shade, offering a tranquil, stroller-friendly walk with panoramic ocean views.

Morning 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Tsougria Island Private Rib Boat Charter

Charter a private luxury motorboat with a protective sun canopy to visit this uninhabited neighboring island. The shallow, sandy turquoise lagoon is exceptionally warm and protected, making it an ideal spot for a quiet family swim.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM

Evangelistria Monastery

Located in the quiet hills above Skiathos Town, this historic monastery offers cool mountain breezes and a peaceful stone courtyard shaded by vines. The flat courtyard is highly accessible for strollers, making for a serene and quiet morning escape.

Midday 11:30 AM - 2:30 PM

Agia Paraskevi Beach

A wide, sandy bay known for its calm sea conditions and luxury daybed rentals with full-service beachside dining. The flat sand makes it easy to position a stroller directly under a large canvas umbrella for a breezy baby nap.

Early Morning 8:30 AM - 10:00 AM

Papadiamantis Street Promenade

Stroll down the main pedestrian street of Skiathos Town during the quiet morning hours. The paved, vehicle-free street is perfect for a smooth stroller ride past upscale boutiques, local bakeries, and blooming bougainvillea.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Achladies Beach

Board a private water taxi from Skiathos harbor to this gentle, wind-sheltered cove. The shoreline shelves very slowly into the warm sea, allowing parents to easily wade in the shallows while holding baby Mary Claire.

Late Afternoon 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Strofilia Wetland Boardwalk

Located directly behind Koukounaries Beach, this natural lagoon features flat wooden boardwalks ideal for stroller walks. The area is highly shaded and quiet, offering a peaceful setting to soothe a baby to sleep while observing local swans and migratory birds.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 5:30 PM

Maratha Beach

A secluded, pine-backed sandy cove offering a quieter alternative to the larger resorts. Serviced by luxury loungers with soft ambient music, the beach is well-sheltered from waves, providing a serene environment for an infant's sensory play.

🍽️ Where to eat

Marmita

Set in a beautiful cobblestone garden courtyard in Skiathos Town, this upscale tavern serves refined Greek cuisine like slow-cooked lamb. The spacious outdoor layout easily accommodates strollers, and the early evening service is highly welcoming to families.

Sklithri

A romantic seafood tavern where wooden tables are placed directly on the beach. Parents can enjoy fresh grilled lobster and local white wine while the baby rests comfortably in a stroller parked on the flat sand adjacent to the table.

The Windmill Restaurant

Located in a historic 1880 windmill, this fine-dining spot offers breathtaking sea views. Reserve an early 6:30 PM table on the spacious open terrace to enjoy gourmet Mediterranean dishes before the late-night crowd arrives.

Olive Land House

Perched on a hill overlooking the Aegean, this elegant restaurant specializes in traditional dishes infused with local olive oil. The expansive terrace features wide pathways for strollers and a quiet, breezy atmosphere perfect for an early family dinner.

Scuna Restaurant

Situated directly on the Skiathos waterfront, Scuna offers fresh seafood and high-end Mediterranean plates. They provide premium highchairs, have a highly attentive staff, and feature a flat, stroller-friendly outdoor dining deck.
