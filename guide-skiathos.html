Skiathos

← back to travel board
Skiathos pairs cosmopolitan Aegean sophistication with exceptionally lush, pine-fringed beaches that offer natural shade for sensitive toddler skin. The island's compact size means minimal car time for a fussy infant, while the calm, shallow waters of the south coast act like giant natural paddling pools. From strolling the flat, marble-paved alleys of the Old Port to resting at high-end beach resorts, it's a seamless blend of premium comfort and authentic Greek island charm.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min private taxi from Skiathos Airport (JSI) to the main south coast family resorts near Koukounaries or Agia Paraskevi.

What to do

Morning / 3h

Koukounaries Beach

This world-famous beach features extremely shallow, calm waters and a natural pine forest offering excellent shade. Stroller-friendly wooden boardwalks run along the back of the sand for easy navigating.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Bourtzi Peninsula

A lush, pine-forested islet separating the old and new ports with flat paved paths perfect for strollers. The outdoor cafe on the fortress grounds offers a peaceful, shaded spot for a baby nap.

Morning / 1h

Strofilia Wetland

A protected lagoon located directly behind Koukounaries Beach where toddlers can spot migratory birds, ducks, and swans. Flat, easy dirt paths make for a smooth all-terrain stroller ride.

Morning / 1.5h

Evangelistria Monastery

A peaceful, historic monastery nestled in the hills with a spacious, shaded stone courtyard. It is incredibly quiet and perfect for carrying baby in a carrier to explore the gardens and small shop selling local honey.

Evening / 1-2h

Skiathos Old Port Promenade

A wide, flat, pedestrian-only harbor front ideal for a scenic sunset stroller walk. Watch the wooden wooden kaiki boats bob in the water while enjoying a premium local gelato.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Mandraki Forest Trail

An easy, shaded walk through a fragrant pine forest that leads to Mandraki Beach. The soft, needle-covered paths are highly sensory and manageable with a sturdy stroller.

Midday / 1h

Ergon Skiathos

A premium Greek deli and open-air market in Skiathos Town where you can stock up on high-end toddler snacks, fresh fruit, and local cheeses. High chairs are available in their leafy courtyard for a quick lunch.

Morning / 2-3h

Agia Paraskevi Beach

A wide, golden sandy beach with exceptionally calm, warm waters perfect for an infant's first dip in the sea. Direct flat access from the nearby road makes transferring baby gear completely effortless.

Morning / 2h

Skiathos Dog Shelter

Tucked in the scenic hills, this volunteer-run shelter welcomes families to walk gentle, rescue dogs along quiet mountain paths. A wonderful sensory experience for animal-loving toddlers best done using a baby carrier.

🍽️ Where to eat

Marmita

Order the slow-cooked lamb or handmade local pasta. The spacious, romantic garden courtyard has plenty of room between tables for strollers, and the early evening service is incredibly welcoming to families.

Sklithri Taverna

Order the freshly caught grilled red mullet or lobster pasta. Tables are set right on the sand, meaning your toddler can play happily at your feet while you enjoy a glass of Assyrtiko.

Platanos Taverna

Order the traditional spit-roasted pork or Greek salad with local feta. Perched high above the town, the massive outdoor terrace is spacious, breezy, and cool even in the midday heat.

Taverna Asprolithos

Order the traditional moussaka or beef stifado. This long-standing taverna opens early for dinner and features wide, comfortable outdoor seating with highly attentive staff who adore children.

Under The Pine Tree

Order the pork gyros wrap or fresh chicken skewers. This ultra-casual, shaded outdoor spot near Koukounaries is perfect for a quick, fuss-free meal where toddler noise is easily absorbed by the open air.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Skiathos Princess Hotel

Set right on the flat sands of Agia Paraskevi, this resort features spacious family suites, baby-sitting services, a dedicated baby pool, and sprawling grassy lawns perfect for crawling.

Elivi Skiathos

A luxury retreat offering buggy transport across the property (great for strollers) and direct access to four stunning beaches. Rooms are incredibly spacious with private pool options and top-tier cribs provided.

Kassandra Bay Resort & SPA

Offers a quiet, premium atmosphere with dedicated family suites, a shallow children's pool, and a gently sloping beach. The hotel is highly accessible with ramps throughout and exceptionally warm service for infants.

👨‍👩‍👧 Stick to the south coast beaches like Koukounaries and Agia Paraskevi, as they are sheltered from the northerly Meltemi winds and offer calm, shallow waters for babies. Renting a compact SUV with a pre-booked car seat is highly recommended for navigating the winding, hilly forest roads safely. While Skiathos Town is generally stroller-friendly along the port, use a high-quality baby carrier for the stepped, cobbled back alleys of the old town and the historic monasteries.
