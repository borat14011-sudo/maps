What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Clearwater Marine Aquarium

This renowned rescue facility features floor-to-ceiling underwater viewing windows to see dolphins and sea turtles up close. The entire facility is highly stroller-accessible with wide ramps and elevators.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Pier 60 Park and Playground

A fully fenced playground with shaded canopy structures and soft rubber flooring located right on the beach. It is perfect for letting a toddler burn off energy while parents enjoy the ocean breeze.

Afternoon / 2h

The Tropics Boat Tours

This double-decker catamaran offers dolphin-watching tours with a fully shaded lower deck that easily accommodates strollers. The calm waters of the Intracoastal make for a smooth, motion-sickness-free ride for the little one.

Morning / 2h

Sand Key Park

A quieter, wide-open alternative to the main beach with a paved path, salt-marsh boardwalks for stroller walks, and a dedicated playground. It is much less crowded, making it ideal for a peaceful outdoor baby nap.

Early Morning / 1h

Clearwater Beach Walk

A winding, 1.5-mile paved promenade lining the beach, perfectly flat for strollers and lined with palm trees for quick shade breaks. It is ideal for an early morning walk before the Florida heat sets in.

Morning / 2h

Coachman Park Playground and Splash Pad

Located just across the bridge, this newly redesigned waterfront park features a massive state-of-the-art playground and a safe, modern splash pad. It is easily accessible via a quick, fun pedestrian ferry ride.

Morning / 1-2h

Clearwater Ferry

A breezy, smooth water taxi ride connecting the beach marina to downtown Clearwater and Dunedin. It serves as a cheap, sensory-friendly boat ride that keeps infants entertained without the commitment of a major cruise.

Morning / 2h

Florida Botanical Gardens

Located just minutes inland in Largo, offering miles of paved, flat loops winding through tropical flora and wild butterflies. It provides a peaceful, shaded oasis perfect for a quiet stroller walk and bottle feedings.

Morning / 1-2h

Moccasin Lake Nature Park

A quiet, 51-acre oak-canopied park with stroller-friendly wooden boardwalks, turtles, and peaceful surroundings. It is a fantastic shaded escape from the beach sun where a toddler can look at local wildlife.

🍽️ Where to eat

Frenchy's Rockaway Grill

Order the famous signature Grouper Sandwich. The open-air beachside seating and bustling ambient noise naturally drown out any sudden toddler fussiness.

Palm Pavilion Beachside Grill & Bar

Try the Beachside Gator Bites and fresh seafood on the spacious outdoor deck. The deck is highly accessible and allows you to park a stroller right at your table with a sunset view.

Clear Sky Cafe

Go for the upscale Eggs Benedict and gourmet crepes. They open at 7:00 AM, making it the perfect high-quality breakfast spot for parents up early with a toddler.

Caretta on the Gulf

Enjoy organic dry-aged steaks and seasonal seafood at this AAA Four-Diamond restaurant inside the Sandpearl. It offers a sophisticated dining experience for parents while remaining exceptionally accommodating to families with early reservations.

The Bait House

Order the famous Drunken Shrimp at this casual, open-air dockside restaurant on the marina. Kids can watch the boats and pelicans glide by while parents eat fresh fish tacos.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Sandpearl Resort

This luxury resort features a zero-entry lagoon-style pool ideal for holding a splashing toddler, plus pristine private beach chairs and pack-and-plays available upon request.

Wyndham Grand Clearwater Beach

Located steps from Pier 60, offering spacious Gulf-view rooms, a massive pool deck, and large elevators that make navigating the property with a heavy stroller incredibly easy.

Sheraton Sand Key Resort

Offers a quieter escape away from the main strip's traffic, featuring a massive private beach, beach-accessible stroller paths, and an excellent on-site playground.
