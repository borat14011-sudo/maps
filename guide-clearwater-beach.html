Clearwater Beach

← back to travel board
Clearwater Beach invites families to sink their toes into sugar-white sands while basking in the gentle, warm waters of the Gulf of Mexico. This vibrant coastal paradise seamlessly blends thrilling marine encounters with relaxing, sunset-filled beach days. It is the ultimate stress-free playground where toddler steps and wild dolphin sightings go hand-in-hand.
🚕 Getting there: ~30 min taxi or rideshare from Tampa International Airport (TPA) to Clearwater Beach hotels.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Clearwater Marine Aquarium

Meet rescued marine life like dolphins, sea turtles, and otters in an educational, indoor setting. Toddlers will love the low-level viewing windows and interactive touch tanks.

Evening / 2h

Pier 60 Sunset Celebration

Experience a daily festival featuring local artisans, street performers, and breathtaking views of the setting sun. Toddlers can burn off energy at the massive, shaded playground right on the sand.

Afternoon / 2h

Captain Memo's Pirate Cruise

Sail the Gulf of Mexico on a real pirate ship complete with treasure hunts, face painting, and water gun fun. The high-energy crew keeps kids engaged while toddlers safely enjoy the music and shaded decks.

Morning / 3h

Sand Key Park

Escape to this peaceful park featuring wide, shell-strewn beaches and a salt marsh full of nesting birds. It is much quieter than the main beach strip, making it ideal for a peaceful toddler nap in the stroller.

Morning / 1.5h

Little Toot Dolphin Adventure

Board a tugboat that creates massive wakes for wild dolphins to jump and play in right behind the boat. The boat is built low to the water, giving toddlers an exciting, close-up view of the playful sea life.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Clearwater Beach Walk

Stroll along this winding, beautifully landscaped pedestrian path lined with palm trees, shops, and cafes. It is completely stroller-friendly and offers great views of the surf without navigating the sand.

Morning / 2h

Coachman Park and Splash Pad

Head just over the bridge to this newly renovated downtown park featuring a massive, modern playground and a refreshing splash pad. Toddlers can safely splash and slide while parents enjoy views of the marina.

Evening / 1-2h

Pier 60 Sunset Market

Browse local crafts, fresh fruit pops, and handmade goods right on the pier as the sun goes down. It is a lively, sensory-friendly evening stroll where toddlers can snack on local treats.

Morning / 3-4h

Caladesi Island State Park Ferry

Take a short, exciting ferry ride to an untouched barrier island with crystal-clear waters and soft sand. The shallow, calm shoreline is ideal for toddlers to splash safely away from the crowds.

🍽️ Where to eat

Frenchy's Rockaway Grill

Order the famous Original Grouper Sandwich and a slice of key lime pie while sitting right on the beach.

Clear Sky Cafe

Go for a hearty family breakfast of stuffed French toast or fresh crab cake benedict just steps from the sand.

Palm Pavilion Beachside Grill & Bar

Grab a table on the deck for sunset views and enjoy the coconut shrimp alongside an excellent kid's menu.

Cooters Restaurant & Bar

Enjoy a relaxed family dinner featuring fresh oysters, crab legs, and a great, highly accommodating kid's menu.

Heilman's Beachcomber

Indulge in their famous pan-fried chicken dinner, a comforting family tradition since 1948.
