Roatán

← back to travel board
Roatán is a lush Caribbean paradise where vibrant coral reefs meet soft, white-sand shores perfect for little feet. This laid-back island offers unforgettable wildlife encounters and gentle shallow waters that make family travel a breeze. From cuddly sloths to calm beach coves, it’s an effortless tropical playground for all generations.

What to do

Morning / 3h

Gumbalimba Park

Interact with friendly capuchin monkeys and free-roaming iguanas in a lush jungle setting. Toddlers will love the stroller-friendly paths and the colorful macaws that land on your shoulders.

Morning / 3-4h

West Bay Beach

Famed for its calm, crystal-clear water and powdery white sand that stays cool under the sun. It's the ultimate spot for toddlers to splash safely without strong waves.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Daniel Johnson's Monkey and Sloth Hangout

An absolute must-do where you can gently hold a rescued sloth and meet playful exotic birds. The staff is exceptionally patient with young children, ensuring safe and calm animal interactions.

Full Day / 6h

Little French Key

A private island paradise featuring calm lagoons, paddleboarding, and a small rescue zoo. The shallow, current-free waters are ideal for toddlers to float safely.

Late Morning / 1h

Glass Bottom Boat Reef Tour

Explore the world's second-largest barrier reef without getting wet aboard a semi-submarine. Perfect for toddlers who are too young to snorkel but want to see sea turtles and colorful fish.

Morning / 1-2h

Arch's Iguana and Marine Park

Walk among thousands of free-roaming green iguanas and feed them leaves by hand. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the giant, gentle reptiles and the nearby marine touch tank.

Morning / 1-2h

Coxen Hole Street Market

Experience local life amidst colorful stalls selling fresh tropical fruits, handmade crafts, and Honduran street food. Keep toddlers in a carrier here as the bustling streets can get crowded and uneven.

Early Morning / 2h

Carambola Botanical Gardens

Wander through jungle trails lined with giant ferns, orchids, and spice plants. The lower trails are flat and easy for little legs to explore while spotting wild iguanas.

Late Afternoon / 2-3h

Half Moon Bay Beach

A horseshoe-shaped bay in West End offering incredibly calm, shallow waters and shade under swaying palms. It's a quieter alternative to West Bay, perfect for toddler sandcastle building.

Where to eat

  • Baleadas at Baleadas Express — Savor Honduras' signature dish: a warm, thick flour tortilla folded over refried beans, crema, and cheese, which is a massive hit with picky-eating toddlers.
  • The Black Pearl Restaurant — Enjoy fresh seafood and international dishes in a spacious, ocean-view setting with plenty of room for toddlers to wiggle.
  • Creole's Rotisserie Chicken — A family-friendly West End staple serving tender, juicy rotisserie chicken, rice and beans, and sweet plantains that kids love.
  • Gelato Dino in West End — Treat the family to authentic, artisanal Italian gelato with tropical flavors like passionfruit and coconut right by the beach.
👨‍👩‍👧 Stick to bottled water for drinking and brushing teeth, and pack a high-quality, reef-safe sunscreen to protect delicate skin during long beach days. Opt for a sturdy baby carrier over a stroller, as many beach paths and local markets feature uneven, sandy, or unpaved terrain.
