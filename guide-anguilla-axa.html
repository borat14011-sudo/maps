Tranquil turquoise shores and barefoot luxury for families

Anguilla (AXA)

← back to travel board
Anguilla welcomes discerning families with its thirty-three powder-white beaches, offering an exclusive and serene Caribbean escape. The island's exceptionally calm waters and low-key atmosphere make it a sanctuary for parents traveling with a young infant. At Shoal Bay East, the pristine shoreline offers a remarkably wide, flat stretch of sand perfect for shade-protected play sessions. The gentle, reef-protected shallows provide a safe, wave-free environment where baby Mary Claire can safely splash under the warm Caribbean sun.
🚕 Getting there: A 15-minute private luxury SUV transfer from Clayton J. Lloyd International Airport (AXA) directly to the West End hotel strip provides a seamless experience with a pre-installed rear-facing infant car seat.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Shoal Bay East

This world-renowned beach offers powder-soft sand and plenty of mature palm trees for natural shade. The crystal-clear shallows are perfect for introducing baby Mary Claire to the ocean, and the flat terrain is easy to navigate with an all-terrain stroller.

Early morning 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Rendezvous Bay Beach

A massive crescent of calm water with stunning views of neighbouring St. Martin. The damp, hard-packed sand near the shoreline is ideal for a smooth morning stroller walk while baby naps under the canopy.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Meads Bay Beach

Lined with high-end resorts, this pristine beach has exceptionally calm waters on its eastern end. Parents can rent a luxury shaded cabana with plush loungers to keep the infant completely out of the direct midday sun.

Late afternoon 3:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Maundays Bay

A highly protected, reef-sheltered cove that experiences virtually no waves. This creates a giant, natural warm-water swimming pool that is highly recommended for gentle infant wading and sensory water play.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Little Bay

Book a private, shaded boat charter from Crocus Bay to this secluded, cliff-protected cove. The cliffs provide excellent natural afternoon shade, making it a quiet and visually spectacular spot for a calm family picnic.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Sandy Ground Beach

A sheltered, historic bay that serves as a sleepy yacht harbor during the day. It is an excellent spot for a quiet, low-sensory stroll along the flat shoreline before the evening crowds arrive.

Midday 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM

Heritage Collection Museum

This quiet, air-conditioned museum in the East End offers a cool refuge from the midday tropical heat. It features a fascinating look at Anguillan history, and the tranquil environment is ideal for a sleeping infant in a carrier.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Island Harbour

A charming, authentic fishing village on the northeast coast. Families can sit under the shade of a breezy gazebo to watch colorful local boats bob on the water, offering a peaceful and sensory-rich experience.

Afternoon 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM

Cove Bay

An expansive, uncrowded beach facing south with constant gentle trade winds. The light, refreshing breeze helps keep the infant cool while parents enjoy an easy, uninterrupted stroll along the wide shoreline.

🍽️ Where to eat

Blanchards Restaurant

An elegant culinary institution offering a premium rack of lamb. The lush garden setting is incredibly spacious, and the early dinner seating allows families to enjoy fine dining with highchairs and attentive service.

Straw Hat

Positioned right on the boardwalk of Meads Bay, this open-air luxury restaurant serves local grilled red snapper. The soft sea breeze and gentle ocean murmur easily mask any minor infant fussiness during dinner.

Jacala Beach Restaurant

A sophisticated French beachfront bistro where the owner warmly welcomes families. Their signature local crayfish pasta is outstanding, and the early evening service provides a quiet, unhurried atmosphere.

Veya

Often described as dining in a luxury treehouse, this restaurant serves a spectacular jerk-spiced tuna. The spacious wooden wrap-around deck allows parents to easily rock a stroller if baby needs to fall asleep during dinner.

Blanchards Beach Shack

The highly upscale but casual sister property to the main restaurant, serving delicious lobster rolls. With outdoor picnic tables set directly on the sand, it offers an incredibly relaxed, low-stress lunch option with an infant.
