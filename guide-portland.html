International Rose Test Garden

Portland Japanese Garden

Experience tranquil, manicured landscapes with calm waterfalls; while some upper paths have stairs, the main pavilion and flat trails are accessible, or perfect for a scenic baby carrier walk.

Lan Su Chinese Garden

Hoyt Arboretum

Powell's City of Books

Crystal Springs Rhododendron Garden

Portland Art Museum
