What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Jardín Botánico de Bogotá

A tranquil oasis featuring flat, paved, stroller-friendly pathways winding through high-altitude Andean flora, a stunning orchid conservatory, and quiet, shady grassy knolls perfect for baby Mary Claire's tummy time away from city sounds.

Late Morning 11:30 AM - 1:00 PM

Museo Botero

Housed in a beautiful colonial mansion with a quiet central courtyard, this museum features wide, ramp-accessible corridors where Mary Claire will be visually stimulated by Fernando Botero's famously oversized, whimsical paintings and sculptures.

Early Morning 8:00 AM - 9:30 AM

Parque El Virrey

An upscale, safe linear park in the Chicó neighborhood, offering beautifully manicured, fully paved jogging paths shaded by mature trees, making it the premier spot for a smooth, uninterrupted morning stroller ride.

Morning 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM

Cerro de Monserrate

Take the smooth, enclosed cable car (teleférico) rather than the funicular to the summit for breathtaking panoramic views of the city; visit early on a weekday to avoid crowds, and use a baby carrier as the mountaintop pathways have some steps.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Museo del Oro

The world's largest gold museum is fully air-conditioned and equipped with spacious elevators and wide, smooth galleries, making it incredibly stroller-accessible while providing a calm, quiet atmosphere during morning hours.

Sunday Morning 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Mercado de las Pulgas de Usaquén

An upscale Sunday market filled with high-end crafts and street performers; while some streets are cobblestone, the central plaza area is manageable with a sturdy stroller, offering vibrant sensory stimulation for a baby.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM

Biblioteca Virgilio Barco

A modern architectural masterpiece designed by Rogelio Salmona, featuring expansive, gently sloping concrete ramps and reflective water pools that are incredibly fun and easy to navigate with a premium stroller.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM

Parque de la 93

A highly secure, vibrant green park surrounded by elite cafes and boutiques, featuring excellent flat walking paths and clean, grassy areas to relax with a blanket and watch local families.

Afternoon 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Centro Comercial Andino

Bogotá's premier luxury shopping mall, offering pristine, air-conditioned walkways, high-end baby boutiques, and exceptionally clean, dedicated family nursing suites and changing facilities.

🍽️ Where to eat

Harry Sasson

Set in a stunningly restored neo-Gothic mansion with a modern, light-filled glass atrium, this legendary restaurant accommodates families beautifully during early lunch seatings with spacious tables and top-tier service.

Criterion

Run by the Rausch brothers, this French-influenced fine dining icon offers an elegant yet welcoming atmosphere with an early 7:00 PM dinner seating option where the highly attentive staff easily accommodates infant carriers.

Primi

An upscale, stylish Italian eatery in Zona G featuring a beautiful, heated outdoor terrace, incredibly family-friendly service, high-end highchairs, and artisanal pasta dishes that make for a relaxed family lunch.

La Brasserie

A classic Parisian-style bistro in the heart of Zona T, featuring a spacious, airy layout with tables comfortably spaced for strollers and an exceptionally polite, accommodating staff during weekend lunch.

Osaka Bogotá

A magnificent Nikkei restaurant in El Chicó offering world-class sushi and Peruvian-Japanese fusion in a sleek, sophisticated setting with early dinner seatings ideal for avoiding the late-night crowd with an infant.
