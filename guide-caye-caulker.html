Caye Caulker

← back to travel board
Caye Caulker offers an effortlessly chic 'Go Slow' escape where sandy streets replace cars and the turquoise Caribbean lapped by the Belize Barrier Reef sets a tranquil pace. For Will, Kathryn, and baby Mary Claire, the island's gentle spirit is best experienced from the quiet shores of the North Channel or a private poolside villa. The absence of motor vehicles creates a peaceful sanctuary, while the calm, shallow waters at The Split provide a perfect, wave-free wading pool for a one-year-old. Here, luxury is defined by intimate boutique resorts and uninterrupted family bonding under swaying coconut palms.
🚕 Getting there: Fly from Belize Goldson International (BZE) to Caye Caulker Airstrip via a 15-minute Tropic Air scheduled flight, followed by a 10-minute private electric golf cart resort transfer with baby and luggage.

What to do

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

The Split

This iconic channel features crystal-clear, wave-free waters perfect for dipping baby Mary Claire's toes. Visit in the early morning for peaceful shaded decks and quiet wading before the afternoon crowds arrive.

Late afternoon 4-5pm / 1h

Iguana Reef Stingray Beach

Every afternoon, friendly southern stingrays glide right up to the shallow shore. It is an incredibly calm, interactive experience where Kathryn can hold Mary Claire in the shallow water to watch them splash.

Morning 10-11am / 1h

Caye Caulker Seahorse Sanctuary

A quiet, sheltered dock area where tiny seahorses cling to submerged mangrove roots. It is peaceful, completely stroller-accessible along the wooden dock, and offers excellent shade for an infant.

Morning to Afternoon 9am-1pm / 4h

Caye Caulker Marine Reserve Private Charter

Book a private luxury catamaran charter to explore the reef at your own pace. The boat provides a shaded cabin for Mary Claire to nap while Will and Kathryn take turns snorkeling the pristine barrier reef.

Morning 9-11:30am / 2.5h

Swallow Caye Wildlife Sanctuary

A private, low-speed boat tour to this protected manatee reserve. The calm, slow-moving waters and quiet boat engines make it a soothing, shaded sightseeing excursion perfect for an infant.

Afternoon 3-3:30pm / 30m

Tarpon Feeding Dock

Watch giant, gentle tarpons jump out of the water to catch bait. It is a quick, thrilling sight that you can safely view from the secure wooden dock while holding baby Mary Claire.

Early morning 8-9:30am / 1.5h

Caye Caulker Forest Reserve

Explore the quiet, bird-filled littoral forest on the northern part of the island. The shaded canopy provides excellent heat relief, making it a lovely spot for a scenic baby carrier walk.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

North Caye Caulker Pontoon Cruise

Rent a private pontoon boat to cruise the ultra-quiet northern split. The water is glassy and calm, offering a gentle rocking motion that is perfect for inducing an afternoon nap for Mary Claire.

Late afternoon 4:30-5:30pm / 1h

Caye Caulker Children's Park

A charming, sandy community playground located right by the sea. It features gentle bucket swings under the shade of mature palm trees, ideal for a relaxed, low-key play session.

🍽️ Where to eat

Il Pellicano Cucina Italiana

Upscale Italian dining tucked into a beautiful, quiet garden courtyard. They offer early dinner seating, spacious tables that easily accommodate strollers, and delicious fresh pasta that Mary Claire can nibble on.

Chef Juan's Kitchen and Pastries

Famous for its incredible key lime pie and savory coconut curries. This cozy, highly-rated spot offers shaded outdoor patio seating and a relaxed, family-friendly atmosphere where baby noise is never an issue.

Namaste Cafe

A breezy, health-conscious rooftop cafe with a relaxed, yogic vibe. Grab fresh-pressed fruit juices and avocado toast in a quiet environment that is perfect for an easy breakfast with a baby.

Hibisca Restaurant

Creative international-Belizean fusion served on a beautiful, romantic garden deck. The staff is incredibly welcoming to families, and the soothing outdoor ambiance keeps infants calm and entertained.

The Lazy Lizard

Though famous as a bar, their early lunch service on the open-air deck is quiet and breezy. Enjoy fresh fish tacos under a shaded palapa right next to the calmest wading waters on the island.
