Stockholm

← back to travel board
Stockholm seamlessly blends pristine water views and historic architecture with the world’s most progressive family infrastructure, making it an absolute dream for parents with a toddler. The city’s flat topography, massive green spaces, and exceptionally clean air provide a stress-free backdrop for a luxury-loving family. Here, high-end culinary experiences and world-class museums aren't just tolerant of infants—they are actively designed to welcome them.
🚕 Getting there: ~20 minutes via the high-speed Arlanda Express train directly to Stockholm Central Station, where kids under 17 travel free and there is level boarding for strollers.

What to do

Morning / 3h

Junibacken

Step into a magical indoor storybook world dedicated to Swedish children's literature, complete with a whimsical story train ride. The facility features ample indoor stroller parking, dedicated nursing rooms, and custom low-level play areas perfect for active toddlers.

Morning / 2h

Vasa Museum

Marvel at the world's best-preserved 17th-century warship, salvaged almost intact after 333 years underwater. The museum features wide, smooth ramps and spacious elevators connecting all viewing decks, making it incredibly easy to navigate with a stroller.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Skansen Open-Air Museum

Explore the world’s oldest open-air museum, featuring historic Swedish farmsteads, native Nordic wildlife like moose and bears, and a dedicated children's zoo. Its paved, car-free pathways are perfect for stroller walks, and there are numerous peaceful spots to stop for baby feeding.

Afternoon / 2h

Rosendals Trädgård

Relax in this beautiful biodynamic orchard and garden cafe where you can enjoy wood-fired bakery treats under apple trees. It’s highly family-friendly, allowing your toddler to crawl safely on the grass while you sip artisanal coffee.

Midday / 1.5h

Östermalms Saluhall

Experience Stockholm’s premier food hall, filled with high-end stalls selling fresh seafood, Swedish cheeses, and traditional meats. The wide aisles make it accessible for strollers during the early lunch hours, offering a refined dining experience without the formality.

Morning / 1.5h

Humlegården Park

Visit this historic park in the upscale Östermalm district, featuring a massive, well-maintained playground (parklek) with baby-safe swings and toys. The park is surrounded by lush lawns perfect for a quiet stroller nap under mature trees.

Morning / 2h

The Royal Armoury (Livrustkammaren)

Located in the basement of the Royal Palace, this museum showcases royal carriages, glittering armor, and magnificent coronation robes. It features a fantastic play room designed specifically for toddlers to dress up and sit in a mock royal carriage, with full stroller access.

Afternoon / 3h

Fjäderholmarna

Take a scenic 25-minute ferry ride from downtown to Stockholm’s closest archipelago island for fresh sea breezes and smoked fish. The island's walking paths are smooth enough for robust strollers, offering a quick escape into nature with the baby.

Morning / 2-3h

Tekniska Museet

Discover the National Museum of Science and Technology, featuring the innovative 'MegaMind' exhibition designed for sensory play. The museum is highly accessible, offering excellent changing facilities, stroller ramps, and interactive displays that captivate even the youngest toddlers.

🍽️ Where to eat

Wärdshuset Ulla Winbladh

Savor classic Swedish meatballs and fresh herring in a historic, elegant villa on Djurgården. The spacious dining rooms easily accommodate strollers, and the staff are famously welcoming to families with young children.

Sturehof

Indulge in high-end seafood platters and Swedish classics at this legendary, bustling brasserie in Östermalm. The lively, energetic atmosphere naturally absorbs toddler noise, and they open early enough for a comfortable 5:00 PM family dinner.

Urban Deli Nytorget

Enjoy a vibrant, casual dining experience with a menu of fresh salads, seafood, and charcuterie. Located right next to a popular playground, its bustling, noisy vibe is perfect for parents who don't want to worry about a crying baby.

Kvarnen

Experience rich Swedish culinary history in this grand, high-ceilinged beer hall serving hearty traditional dishes like reindeer and meatballs. The spacious layout makes maneuvering a stroller easy, and the ambient noise level is highly forgiving of infant vocalizations.

Restaurang Tradition

Enjoy a refined, modern take on traditional Swedish home cooking in a minimalist, clean environment. Located on a quieter edge of Gamla Stan, it offers a calm midday respite with spacious seating and high-quality high chairs.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Grand Hôtel Stockholm

Indulge in 5-star luxury overlooking the Royal Palace, featuring world-class service, an indoor pool with designated family hours, and premium wooden cribs. Its central waterfront location means you can easily walk to major attractions without folding your stroller.

Hotel Skeppsholmen

Stay in a tranquil, historic oasis on a car-free island that is still just a short, scenic walk from the city center. The hotel features spacious ground-floor rooms, sprawling lawns for baby to play on, and a peaceful environment perfect for infant sleep schedules.

Downtown Camper by Scandic

Opt for a modern, active-lifestyle hotel featuring large family suites, a heated rooftop pool, and complimentary stroller loans. The relaxed, social lobby atmosphere is incredibly welcoming to young families, and cribs are gladly provided upon request.

👨‍👩‍👧 In Stockholm, riding city buses is completely free if you are pushing a baby stroller, allowing you to board directly through the wide middle doors without purchasing a ticket. The city is virtually cashless, so ensure your credit cards are loaded onto your phone for easy tap-to-pay at every park kiosk and restaurant. Excellent, clean public baby-changing rooms (skötrum) equipped with nursing chairs can be found in major department stores like NK and Åhléns City.
