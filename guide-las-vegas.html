What to do

Early morning 8-9:30am / 1.5h

Bellagio Conservatory & Botanical Gardens

A breathtaking, climate-controlled indoor garden with towering floral sculptures. Visit during the quiet early morning hours (8-10am) for a peaceful, stroller-accessible walk with Mary Claire before the crowds arrive.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Shark Reef Aquarium at Mandalay Bay

An immersive undersea journey featuring walk-through tunnel exhibits. The gentle lighting and mesmerizing, slow-moving sharks and sea turtles provide a highly soothing sensory experience for infants.

Late afternoon 4-5pm / 1h

High Roller Observation Wheel

A 550-foot-tall giant wheel offering panoramic views of the valley. Book a private, climate-controlled cabin for a smooth, whisper-quiet 30-minute rotation that easily accommodates strollers and provides a calm environment.

Morning 9-11:30am / 2.5h

Springs Preserve

A 180-acre cultural and natural preserve featuring paved, stroller-friendly walking trails and lush desert botanical gardens. Plenty of shaded rest areas and peaceful native bird-watching spots make this a perfect escape from the Strip.

Afternoon 1-3pm / 2h

Grand Canal Shoppes at The Venetian

An indoor recreation of Venice complete with cobblestone paths, canals, and singing gondoliers. The wide walkways are highly stroller-friendly, and the gentle acoustics of the operatic singers are delightful for babies.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Floyd Lamb Park at Tule Springs

A lush, historic 680-acre park featuring scenic ponds, shade-giving cottonwood trees, and free-roaming peacocks. It is the ultimate tranquil spot for a luxury family picnic away from the downtown bustle.

Midday 12-1:30pm / 1.5h

The Aquarium at Silverton

A massive 117,000-gallon saltwater aquarium home to thousands of tropical fish. It is significantly quieter than Strip attractions and offers a beautiful, peaceful visual distraction for a contact-napping infant.

Early evening 6-7pm / 1h

Lake of Dreams at Wynn

A magical, multi-sensory puppetry and light show set against a pine-lined mountain waterfall. Watch the early evening shows from the outdoor terrace of Parasol Golden Hour, which is spacious and welcoming to families.

Afternoon 3-4:30pm / 1.5h

Sunset Park

A expansive county park featuring a scenic lake, shade trees, and paved walking paths. Perfect for an afternoon stroll where Will and Kathryn can unwind while Mary Claire enjoys the gentle desert breeze in her stroller.

🍽️ Where to eat

Mon Ami Gabi

Delectable prime steak frites and classic French onion soup. The outdoor terrace directly faces the Bellagio Fountains, providing excellent white noise to soothe Mary Claire, with highchairs readily available.

Lakeside

Exquisite ocean-fresh fish served right at the edge of the Lake of Dreams. An early 5:30 PM seating on the outdoor patio offers a luxurious, sensory-rich experience that easily accommodates strollers.

Bouchon by Thomas Keller

Award-winning French bistro fare including decadent roasted chicken and fresh pastries. The secluded outdoor garden patio on the Venetian's pool level is incredibly peaceful, spacious, and perfect for an upscale family brunch.

Spago by Wolfgang Puck

Iconic California-Italian cuisine like handmade pasta and wood-fired pizzas. Request a lake-front table on the outdoor patio during the early 5 PM seating to enjoy the fountain views with ample space for a stroller.

Estiatorio Milos

Ultra-fresh, refined Mediterranean seafood and pristine Greek salads. The dining room is exceptionally spacious and airy, making an early family dinner feel both highly exclusive and stress-free.
