What to do

Morning / 1 hour

Bellagio Conservatory & Botanical Gardens

The ever-changing, massive floral installations offer sensory wonder for toddlers with vibrant colors, theatrical lighting, and gentle water features. The wide, flat pathways are completely stroller-friendly, though visiting before 9:00 AM avoids the heavy crowds.

Mid-morning / 1.5 hours

Shark Reef Aquarium at Mandalay Bay

Walk through an underwater tunnel surrounded by sharks, green sea turtles, and giant rays. It is fully carpeted and stroller-accessible, making it an easy, cool indoor escape during the midday heat.

Morning / 2-3 hours

Springs Preserve

This 180-acre cultural and natural wonder features easy walking trails, native botanical gardens, and a dedicated toddler play area. The entire property is exceptionally stroller-friendly with plenty of shade and quiet zones for a quick escape.

Sunset / 1 hour

The High Roller at The LINQ

A slow-moving, 30-minute ride in a spacious, air-conditioned glass cabin provides panoramic views of the city without any steep drops. Strollers are permitted inside the cabins, allowing your little one to look out safely or even nap in comfort.

Afternoon / 2 hours

Downtown Container Park

An open-air shopping and entertainment center made of shipping containers featuring an interactive treehouse play area for kids. It is fully fenced and stroller-friendly, making it a safe, contained spot for an afternoon stroll.

Morning / 2 hours

Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area Scenic Drive

A stunning 13-mile one-way scenic drive with dramatic red rock formations just 30 minutes from the Strip. It is perfect for a scenic car nap for the toddler while parents enjoy the dramatic views, with easy paved pull-outs for quick photos.

Afternoon / 2 hours

Lake Las Vegas Water Sports Electric Boat Rental

Rent a quiet, shaded electric Duffy boat for a peaceful cruise on the calm waters of Lake Las Vegas, far from the Strip's chaos. It is an ultra-relaxing way to let your toddler watch the ducks while you enjoy the desert mountain backdrop.

Morning / 2-3 hours

Discovery Children's Museum

This three-story museum features Toddler Town, a dedicated sensory play zone designed specifically for children under five. It is exceptionally clean, stroller-accessible, and offers a perfect indoor energy-burner on hotter days.

Late Afternoon / 1 hour

The Park Las Vegas

A pedestrian-friendly outdoor dining and entertainment district between New York-New York and Park MGM featuring shaded spots and cool water walls. It is a great, car-free zone to let a toddler stretch their legs and watch the interactive water features.

🍽️ Where to eat

Mon Ami Gabi at Paris Las Vegas

Order the classic Steak Frites and a side of fresh berries on the outdoor patio facing the Bellagio Fountains, which provides excellent distraction for a baby. The staff is highly accommodating with highchairs and stroller placement.

Eataly Las Vegas at Park MGM

Grab a slice of Roman-style pizza from La Pizza & La Pasta in a vibrant food hall setting. The natural ambient noise of the market easily drowns out any toddler fussiness, and there is ample room to park a stroller near your table.

Honey Salt

This off-Strip Summerlin gem serves upscale comfort food like the Biloxi Buttermilk Fried Chicken in a bright, family-welcoming atmosphere. It is spacious, avoids all casino smoke, and offers a high-quality kids menu.

Yardbird Table & Bar at The Venetian

Famous for their Lewellyn's Fine Fried Chicken and giant buttermilk biscuits, which are easy for toddlers to snack on. Book an early dinner slot at 5:00 PM when the restaurant is lively but not packed.

Border Grill at Mandalay Bay

Enjoy upscale modern Mexican fare like the chicken chimichangas on the outdoor patio overlooking the lazy river. The relaxed, breezy atmosphere is perfect for a lunch where a toddler can make noise without disturbing others.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Four Seasons Hotel Las Vegas

A completely non-gaming, non-smoking oasis situated at the top of Mandalay Bay, offering a tranquil environment with direct, private pool access. They provide complimentary cribs, baby-proofing kits, and high-end strollers upon request.

The Vdara Hotel & Spa

A non-gaming, all-suite, smoke-free resort right on the Strip that offers spacious rooms equipped with kitchenettes, which are crucial for warming bottles and storing toddler snacks. Its quiet atmosphere and lack of casino crowds make bedtime transitions incredibly smooth.

The Venetian Las Vegas

Every room here is a massive sunken suite, providing a natural separation between the sleeping area for a crib and the living space for parents. The resort's wide, indoor canal-side walkways are fully stroller-accessible, and the pool deck features great shallow splash areas.

👨‍👩‍👧 Utilize the indoor walkways, trams, and pedestrian bridges to keep your stroller out of the intense desert heat and away from street traffic. Always request a empty refrigerator for your hotel room upon booking, as many Strip resorts use sensor-based minibars that cannot be used for storing baby milk. Stick to early dining reservations around 5:00 PM when the city's top-tier restaurants are quieter and highly welcoming to families with young children.
