What to do

Evening / 30 mins

The Fountains of Bellagio

Watch the spectacular water show dance to music, which is completely free and mesmerizing for little ones. Toddlers will love clapping along to the music and watching the water shoot high into the sky.

Morning / 2-3h

Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area

Drive the scenic loop to see stunning red rock formations, with easy boardwalk trails perfect for small legs. A toddler carrier is highly recommended for the rocky, off-trail viewing areas.

Morning / 1-2h

Shark Reef Aquarium at Mandalay Bay

Walk through underwater tunnels surrounded by sharks, giant rays, and green sea turtles. It's fully stroller-accessible and features touch pools that toddlers can easily reach.

Afternoon / 1h

High Roller Observation Wheel

Take a slow-moving, 30-minute ride in a spacious, enclosed glass cabin for 360-degree views of the valley. The cabins are large enough for strollers and allow toddlers to safely look out the floor-to-ceiling windows.

Morning / 1h

Bellagio Conservatory & Botanical Gardens

Explore the whimsical, seasonally changing floral displays made of thousands of real plants and flowers. The paved pathways are perfect for strollers, though it can get crowded, so aim for early morning.

Afternoon / 2h

Downtown Container Park

An open-air shopping and dining complex built from shipping containers, featuring a massive treehouse play area with slides. Toddlers will be fascinated by the giant fire-breathing metal praying mantis at the entrance.

Morning / 2-3h

Discovery Children's Museum

Three floors of interactive exhibits, including a water play area, climbing structures, and a dedicated sensory space for toddlers. Bring a change of clothes for the water tables, which are a major hit with the under-three crowd.

Morning / 2-3h

Springs Preserve

This 180-acre cultural and natural site features botanical gardens, a butterfly habitat, and interactive museum exhibits. Toddlers will enjoy the train ride and the hands-on desert playground.

Afternoon / 2h

Illuminarium at Area15

An immersive entertainment complex that offers family-friendly projection-mapped experiences. Toddlers will love chasing the interactive lights and animations on the floors and walls in a safe, indoor space.

🍽️ Where to eat

Mon Ami Gabi

Grab an outdoor patio table to eat delicious steak frites and warm baguettes while watching the Bellagio Fountains across the street.

Black Tap Craft Burgers & Beer

Treat the family to massive, over-the-top CrazyShakes piled high with cake slices or cotton candy alongside great burgers.

Hash House A Go Go

Order the famous, massive sage fried chicken and waffles to share family-style in this high-energy, casual eatery.

Peppermill Restaurant and Fireside Lounge

Enjoy classic diner fare like giant pancakes in a retro, neon-lit booth that will keep toddlers visually captivated.

Nacho Daddy

Feast on gourmet loaded nachos, with plenty of customizable, kid-friendly options like the cheese-only lil nacho.

👨‍👩‍👧 Keep toddlers hydrated in the dry desert climate by always carrying refillable water bottles and applying high-SPF sunscreen even on cloudy days. Opt for the air-conditioned monorail or rideshares during midday heat rather than walking long stretches of the Strip with a stroller.
