The Lake Como Region captivates with its dramatic alpine backdrop, sparkling deep blue waters, and grand Art Nouveau villas lined with lush botanical gardens. Elegant steamboats glide between historic lakeside villages, offering a serene pace perfect for families seeking both relaxation and cultural richness. For parents traveling with baby Mary Claire, the paved, stroller-ready promenade of the Lungolago di Como provides a seamless, scenic pathway for leisurely afternoon walks under the shade of plane trees. From private wooden speedboats to tranquil lakeside terraces, the region effortlessly pairs high-end Italian luxury with convenient, infant-friendly amenities.
🚕 Getting there: Milan Malpensa Airport (MXP) to Como is a 45-minute drive. The most seamless, stress-free option for a family with a stroller and infant is a pre-booked private Mercedes V-Class transfer equipped with a premium Cybex rear-facing infant car seat.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 2.5h

Villa Carlotta

Explore over 20 acres of magnificent terraced gardens and masterworks of art. The flat gravel pathways and dedicated elevators inside the villa make it highly accessible for baby Mary Claire in a premium stroller.

Late Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 2h

Giardini di Villa Melzi

Stroll along the serene lakefront English-style gardens filled with exotic trees and historic sculptures. The wide, flat pathways offer plenty of natural shade for an infant carrier-friendly morning walk.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM / 1.5h

Lungolago di Como

A wonderfully wide and completely paved lakeside promenade perfect for a seamless, bump-free stroller ride. Enjoy the gentle lake breeze and beautiful views of life on the water.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM / 1.5h

Funicolare Como-Brunate

Take the historic funicular railway up to the hilltop town of Brunate for breathtaking panoramic views of the lake and Alps. The quiet, enclosed cabins are comfortable for infants, though you should fold your stroller before boarding.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM / 1.5h

Parco Civico Teresio Olivelli

A beautifully manicured public park in Tremezzo featuring majestic trees, a historic fountain, and quiet shaded lawns. It is the perfect spot for baby Mary Claire to have some peaceful tummy time on a blanket.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:30 PM / 2.5h

Villa del Balbianello

Famous for its terraced gardens and movie-set views, this iconic villa is best explored with a baby carrier. The scenic private water taxi ride to the property provides a thrilling yet gentle water experience for the family.

Midday 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM / 1h

Duomo di Como

This stunning Gothic-Renaissance cathedral offers a cool, quiet, and awe-inspiring interior refuge from the midday heat. The flat, polished stone floors are fully stroller-accessible, allowing parents to admire the tapestries in peace.

Afternoon 1:30 PM - 4:30 PM / 3h

Lido di Cernobbio

A chic lakeside lido offering a peaceful pool deck, comfortable shaded cabanas, and family-friendly swimming facilities. Ideal for relaxing by the water with infant-safe shade and convenient changing amenities nearby.

Late Afternoon 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM / 1.5h

Lungolago di Menaggio

A picturesque, flower-lined promenade with completely flat, stroller-friendly paved walkways. Featuring plenty of benches under leafy trees, it is a relaxing setting for an afternoon stroll with easy access to nearby cafes.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ristorante Bilacus

Enjoy refined, authentic Italian cuisine on a quiet, elevated stone terrace surrounded by lush flowers. The staff is highly accommodating to families, offering early dinner seatings and space for a compact stroller.

Ristorante La Punta

Savor fresh lake fish and homemade pasta at the very tip of Bellagio where the lake branches meet. The spacious outdoor lakeside terrace easily accommodates highchairs and provides a gentle breeze to keep baby comfortable.

Crotto dei Platani

A stunning lakeside restaurant built into the cliffside, serving modern interpretations of traditional Lombardian dishes. The soothing sound of the lapping water on the covered veranda naturally calms infants during your meal.

Il Gatto Nero

An iconic, upscale restaurant perched high above Cernobbio offering legendary views and exquisite Mediterranean dishes. Book the earliest dinner seating to enjoy a quiet, romantic atmosphere before the late-night crowd arrives.

Ristorante La Darsena

This elegant lakefront restaurant features floor-to-ceiling windows and a spacious terrace serving contemporary Italian cuisine. The highly professional staff warmly welcomes infants and provides highchairs upon request.
