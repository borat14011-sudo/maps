Jersey

← back to travel board
Jersey blends British charm with French flair, offering pristine sandy bays and rich history just a short hop from the UK mainland. This compact island is a paradise for families, where tidal adventures, exotic wildlife, and medieval castles await around every corner. Parents will love the relaxed pace, while little ones will thrive in the safe, clean, and incredibly welcoming coastal environment.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min taxi or public bus route 15 to St Helier, or ~10-20 min to west/east coast beach hotels

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Jersey Zoo

Founded by Gerald Durrell, this world-renowned conservation zoo lets kids get up close to endangered species in lush, spacious enclosures. Toddlers will love the gentle lemurs, free-roaming tamarins, and the excellent outdoor play areas.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Elizabeth Castle

Take an exciting amphibious Castle Ferry ride across the bay at high tide to explore this historic island fortress. Toddlers will love climbing the battlements and watching the live cannon demonstrations.

Morning / 2h

Mont Orgueil Castle

Towering over the picturesque harbor of Gorey, this medieval castle features winding staircases and hidden rooms to spark young imaginations. Toddlers can easily manage the lower courtyards and enjoy the dedicated children's activity rooms.

Afternoon / 3-4h

St Brelade's Bay Beach

This south-facing beach offers soft golden sand and shallow, calm waters perfect for safe paddling and building sandcastles. Toddler note: The beachside promenade is entirely flat, making it ideal for buggy walks and ice cream stops.

Morning / 1h

Jersey Central Market

Step inside this vibrant, Victorian indoor market to admire the colorful flower stalls, fresh local produce, and the grand central fountain. It's a sensory delight for toddlers, and a perfect spot to grab fresh Jersey berries for a healthy snack.

Morning / 3-4h

aMaizin! Adventure Park

The ultimate family fun park, featuring indoor play barns, outdoor pedal go-karts, tractor rides, and friendly barnyard animals. There is a dedicated, safe soft-play zone specifically designed for toddlers to burn off energy.

Low tide / 2h

Plémont Bay

At low tide, this dramatic northern bay reveals deep rock pools and sandy caves perfect for mini explorers. Be prepared for many steps down, but toddlers will be mesmerized by the natural waterfalls and tiny crabs.

Afternoon / 2h

Maritime Museum

Located on the historic quayside, this hands-on museum lets kids feel the force of a gale, design virtual boats, and touch real marine life. It is highly interactive and sensory-friendly, keeping toddlers engaged from start to finish.

Evening at low tide / 1.5h

La Corbière Lighthouse

Walk the dramatic causeway at low tide to reach this striking white lighthouse perched on a rocky islet. While the walk requires holding toddler hands tightly due to uneven wet ground, the rock pooling along the way is unmatched.

🍽️ Where to eat

El Tico Beach Cantina

Grab a table overlooking the surf at St Ouen's Bay and order their famous fish tacos or a hearty kid's beef burger.

The Crab Shack St Brelade

Enjoy fresh Jersey crab and seafood right on the beachfront while the kids enjoy their dedicated menu and crayons.

The Hungry Man

Head to this colorful, legendary harbor kiosk in Rozel for giant crab sandwiches and hot chocolates that the whole family will love.

The Salty Dog Bar & Bistro

Feast on Jersey beef burgers or coconut-crusted prawns in a relaxed, family-welcoming atmosphere right by St Aubin's harbor.

Ransoms Tearooms

Treat the kids to incredible towering cakes and home-cooked family favorites next to a lovely garden center.
