Jersey

← back to travel board
Dotted with dramatic tidal fortresses and pristine sandy bays, Jersey offers an upscale, slow-paced British-French escape perfect for a young family. For a finance dad, it is a high-functioning haven with top-tier seafood, immaculate roads, and safe, walkable towns. Here, you can push a stroller along rugged harbor walls in the morning and dine on fresh Jersey Royals and oysters by evening. It is the ultimate low-friction, high-reward destination where European charm meets easy logistics.
🚕 Getting there: ~15-20 minute taxi ride from Jersey Airport to the beach hotels in St. Brelade's Bay or waterfront hotels in St. Helier, with pre-booked options offering toddler car seats.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Jersey Zoo

Founded by Gerald Durrell, this world-renowned conservation sanctuary features wide, flat, stroller-friendly pathways shaded by beautiful trees. The onsite Café Firefly is perfect for a quick toddler snack break next to the play areas.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Elizabeth Castle

Take the puddle-jumping amphibious Castle Ferry vehicle across the bay to this historic tidal fortress, an absolute thrill for a toddler. Stick to the outdoor courtyards and lower pathways, as the battlements are too steep for strollers.

Morning / 2h

St Brelade's Bay Beach

This south-facing bay offers soft golden sand and an immaculate, flat promenade perfect for pushing a stroller right alongside the waves. Drop by the beachfront cafes for an ice cream while your toddler paddles in the calm, shallow tidal pools.

Morning / 2h

Jersey Museum & Art Gallery

Located right by the harbor, this highly accessible museum features a dedicated children's play area and lifts to all floors. It is the perfect rainy-day refuge to learn about the island’s history without struggling with a buggy.

Morning / 1-2h

St Helier Central Market

Walk through this beautiful Victorian indoor market to see the stunning central fountain and vibrant flower stalls. It is sensory heaven for a toddler and has spacious, flat aisles for easy stroller navigation.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Mont Orgueil Castle

Towering over Gorey harbor, this medieval icon offers stunning views of the French coast and exhibits hidden in secret rooms. Keep the toddler in a carrier here, as the winding stone steps are a challenge for strollers but highly rewarding.

Morning / 3-4h

aMaizin! Adventure Park

A premier family park featuring a petting barn, sensory play areas, and a massive indoor soft-play zone tailored for toddlers. The entire park is designed with wide gates, ramps, and excellent diaper-changing facilities throughout.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

La Corbière Lighthouse

Walk down the paved causeway at low tide to witness one of the world's most dramatic tidal lighthouses. The concrete path is completely stroller-accessible, but check the tide times carefully to ensure a safe return.

Afternoon / 2h

Maritime Museum and Occupation Tapestry Gallery

This highly interactive, hands-on museum allows toddlers to touch, pull levers, and feel the wind and tide exhibits. It is fully accessible with wide lifts, making it an easy, educational afternoon out with a stroller.

🍽️ Where to eat

El Tico Cantina

Order the local crab tacos or the massive surf-and-turf burger while enjoying sweeping views of St. Ouen's Bay. It is loud, spacious, incredibly welcoming to families, and has plenty of room to park a stroller next to your table.

The Crab Shack St. Brelade

Sip on a local Liberation ale and order the local Jersey hand-picked crab salad or the crispy calamari. The vibe is laid-back and beachy, meaning a fussing toddler won't turn any heads, and high chairs are readily available.

Salty Dog Bar & Bistro

Treat yourselves to the signature fragrant coconut curry with local seafood or the Jersey beef sliders. They open early for dinner at 6 PM, offering a high-end dining experience that is surprisingly accommodating to young families.

Green Island Restaurant

Feast on fresh Jersey oysters and grilled garlic butter lobster right on the slipway of Green Island. The outdoor terrace is perfect for a sleeping baby in a stroller, and the gentle ocean breeze keeps everyone relaxed.

The Boat House

Snag a table on the upper deck or the spacious ground floor to enjoy classic fish and chips with fresh local cod. The restaurant features a dedicated kid's menu, plenty of space for strollers, and a gorgeous view of St. Aubin's harbor.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

L'Horizon Beach Hotel & Spa

Situated directly on St. Brelade’s Bay, this luxury hotel offers spacious family rooms, indoor heated pools, and direct, step-free access to the sand. They gladly provide high-quality wooden cribs and have a kids' menu that appeals to toddlers.

Longueville Manor

A 5-star manor house that masterfully blends high-end luxury with family warmth, offering a heated outdoor pool, beautiful gardens to stroll, and tailored baby-listening services. They provide complimentary baby cribs and can arrange expert local babysitters for a date night.

Radisson Blu Waterfront Hotel

Boasting large, modern family rooms with panoramic marina views, this hotel is entirely stroller-accessible with lifts and wide corridors. Its central location makes it easy to walk straight onto the St. Helier promenade with your toddler.

👨‍👩‍👧 Rent a compact SUV at the airport and pre-book a high-quality car seat, as Jersey’s winding lanes are incredibly scenic but very narrow. Always download a local tide table app; the island’s massive tidal range means beaches and causeways change dramatically in minutes, which affects stroller walks. Bring an all-terrain stroller with solid wheels, as navigating cobblestone streets in St. Helier or the sandy slipways of the coastal bays can be bumpy for a sleeping baby.
