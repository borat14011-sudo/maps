What to do

Morning 9:30am - 12:30pm / 3h

Durrell Wildlife Park (Jersey Zoo)

This world-renowned conservation park features wide, flat, stroller-friendly pathways shaded by mature canopy trees. Mary Claire will love the gentle sights of the free-roaming tamarins, and the on-site Dodo Cafe offers spacious, quiet nursing spots.

Late Afternoon 3pm - 5pm / 2h

St Brelade's Bay Beach

A pristine sweep of soft golden sand with an entirely flat, paved sea-front promenade perfect for rolling your luxury stroller. The bay's unique south-facing orientation provides incredibly calm, shallow waters ideal for dipping baby's toes.

Morning 10am - 12pm / 2h

Samares Manor and Botanic Gardens

Stroll through peaceful herb gardens and willow labyrinth paths that are wide enough for double strollers. The tranquil atmosphere and gentle scent of blooming roses provide a soothing, low-stimulation environment perfect for an infant's afternoon nap.

Midday 12pm - 2pm / 2h

Maritime Museum and Occupation Tapestry Gallery

Located on the historic harbor in St Helier, this completely indoor, climate-controlled museum features interactive displays and full elevator access. It is an ideal, quiet refuge to escape the midday sun with Mary Claire in her stroller.

Afternoon 2pm - 3:30pm / 1.5h

Jersey Lavender Farm

Wander through beautiful, fragrant lavender fields along flat dirt paths that are highly accessible for rugged strollers. The soothing scent of lavender acts as a natural relaxant for babies, and the cozy tea room offers highchairs and fresh bakes.

Evening Sunset 6pm - 7pm / 1h

La Corbiere Lighthouse Viewpoint

Take a scenic stroll along the paved headland path to view the iconic tidal lighthouse. While the rocky causeway itself is not suitable for strollers, the elevated paved viewing platform offers dramatic, wind-sheltered photo opportunities.

Morning 9am - 11am / 2h

St Aubin's Harbour Walk

Enjoy a leisurely walk along the historic bulwarks of this picturesque fishing village. The flat harbor path is completely stroller-accessible, lined with luxury yachts, and features plenty of benches for comfortable infant feeding stops.

Afternoon 3pm - 4:30pm / 1.5h

Gorey Harbour and Mont Orgueil Castle Base

Stroll the beautiful, flat pier beneath the towering medieval castle walls, avoiding the steep castle stairs. The harbor area is exceptionally scenic and stroller-friendly, allowing you to enjoy the views without climbing the ramparts.

Morning 10am - 11:30am / 1.5h

Jersey Museum & Art Gallery

An elegant, modern museum in St Helier featuring spacious elevators, wide galleries, and dedicated baby-changing facilities. It offers a fascinating look into island history in a highly sophisticated, quiet, and temperature-controlled environment.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ocean Restaurant at The Atlantic Hotel

Michelin-level dining featuring fresh local lobster in a bright, white-walled room overlooking the sea. They happily accommodate families for early dinner sessions, providing high-end highchairs and a wonderfully tranquil atmosphere.

Oyster Box

Chic, beachside dining overlooking St Brelade's Bay serving world-class local oysters. The outdoor heated terrace is highly accommodating for strollers, and the gentle sound of the ocean easily masks any infant fussiness.

Sumas Restaurant

An upscale eatery in Gorey offering fresh scallops with stunning views of Mont Orgueil Castle. Their heated deck is spacious enough for strollers, and the staff is incredibly welcoming to well-dressed young families.

Mark Jordan at the Beach

A relaxed yet sophisticated Michelin-bib restaurant on the shoreline of St Peter. It features an informal, lively luxury vibe where baby noise is easily absorbed, offering highchairs and excellent early-evening seatings.

Crab Shack St Brelade

An upmarket, highly family-friendly seaside joint right on the promenade. Offering premium local crab in a vibrant, forgiving atmosphere, it is the perfect spot for a stress-free lunch with Mary Claire.
