What to do

Early morning 9-11am / 2h

Fegina Beach (Spiaggia di Fegina)

Monterosso's wide sandy beach is the flat, stroller-accessible crown jewel of the coast. Rent a private cabana at Bagni Degli Orsi; the calm shoreline is perfect for dipping Mary Claire's toes.

Late afternoon 4-5:30pm / 1.5h

Via dell'Amore

This newly reopened, completely flat paved path connecting Riomaggiore and Manarola is a dream for families. It is ideal for a smooth stroller walk with breathtaking cliffside views and cooling sea breezes.

Mid-morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Vernazza Harbor (Piazza Marconi)

The vibrant heart of Vernazza features a flat, paved waterfront square. It is perfect for park-bench bottle feedings under shaded awnings while watching colorful fishing boats sway in the harbor.

Sunset 6-7:30pm / 1.5h

Manarola Scenic Viewpoint

Stroll up the paved, gently sloping pathway toward the viewpoint. The smooth trail keeps the stroller steady while you capture postcard-worthy family photos with the pastel town behind you.

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Monterosso Old Town (Centro Storico)

Explore the car-free, flat medieval streets of Monterosso's historic center. Find quiet, shaded alleyways for Mary Claire to nap in the stroller while you shop for local luxury linens.

Morning 10am-1pm / 3h

Private Ligurian Boat Charter

Board a luxury private launch from Monterosso. Sail the calm waters of the Ligurian Sea; yachts offer shade canopies and indoor cabins for baby's naptime while parents enjoy local Vermentino.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Belvedere di Santa Maria (Corniglia)

Take the convenient national park shuttle bus up from Corniglia station to bypass the famous steps. Enjoy a flat walk to this terrace offering stunning, unobstructed views of all five villages.

Full day 10am-4pm / 6h

Portovenere Promenade

Take a short, private water taxi to this nearby harbor. The flat, wide maritime boardwalk is stroller-heaven with zero stairs and endless views of Palmaria Island.

Early evening 5-6:30pm / 1.5h

Torre Aurora (Monterosso)

The historic 16th-century tower dividing the new and old towns. Access the lower, flat panoramic terrace for a refreshing drink in a quiet, breezy setting away from afternoon crowds.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ristorante Miky

Signature luxury dining in Monterosso famous for its seafood cooked in a clay crust. Book an early 7:00 PM outdoor garden table; the staff is exceptionally accommodating with highchairs and space for a stroller.

Nessun Dorma

Indulge in artisanal pesto-making and local charcuterie platters overlooking Manarola. Use their app to skip the queue and secure a shaded terrace table; the lively music and open air easily mask any infant fussiness.

Cantina di Miky

A more relaxed, beachfront sister to Miky right on the Fegina promenade. Perfect for a long lunch of handmade trofie al pesto; outdoor tables sit directly on the flat boardwalk for easy stroller parking.

Ristorante Marina Piccola

Perched directly on Manarola's harbor. Request an outdoor table facing the sea for an early dinner of grilled sea bass; the ambient sound of crashing waves acts as great white noise for baby.

Ristorante Portivene

Located in nearby, highly accessible Portovenere. Dine on handmade seafood pasta in a quiet, air-conditioned interior or on the flat pedestrian alleyway with plenty of room for a highchair.
