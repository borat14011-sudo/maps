Santa Fe

← back to travel board
Santa Fe welcomes sophisticated families with its warm adobe architecture, vibrant art scenes, and crisp mountain air. Framed by the Sangre de Cristo Mountains, this historic city blends rich Native American heritage with contemporary upscale comfort. For Will, Kathryn, and baby Mary Claire, the flat, stroller-friendly paved paths around the historic Santa Fe Plaza offer an effortless way to explore high-end galleries and boutiques together. Experience a serene, culturally rich getaway where baby-friendly luxury meets deep-rooted Southwestern charm.
🚕 Getting there: Albuquerque International Sunport (ABQ) to Santa Fe Plaza is a scenic 60-minute drive. The best luxury family transport is a private SUV service booked through Santa Fe Valet, which provides a pre-installed rear-facing infant car seat for Mary Claire and generous cargo space for high-end strollers and luggage.
