Nestled where the dense emerald rainforest spills directly onto white-sand crescent beaches, Manuel Antonio offers an unparalleled tropical sanctuary for discerning families. The destination seamlessly pairs raw biodiversity with high-end convenience, allowing you to spot sloths and squirrel monkeys right from your private terrace. For those traveling with baby Mary Claire, the gentle, protected waters of Playa Manuel Antonio inside the national park provide a serene, wave-free haven for gentle wading. Every logistically smooth day concludes with stunning Pacific sunsets that paint the sky, enjoyed from exclusive hillside retreats designed for effortless relaxation.
🚕 Getting there: A 2.5-hour private transfer in a premium SUV with a pre-installed, rear-facing infant car seat is the premier way to travel from San José International Airport (SJO) directly to your resort.

What to do

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

Manuel Antonio National Park Boardwalk Trail

This fully flat, elevated wooden boardwalk winds through the lush jungle canopy, offering a completely stroller-accessible route to spot wild sloths, monkeys, and colorful toucans in deep shade.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Playa Espadilla Norte

The main public beach offers wide, soft sands perfect for laying out a blanket for baby sensory play, with plenty of beach umbrella vendors providing critical shade close to upscale amenities.

Late morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Playa Manuel Antonio

Located inside the national park, this stunning white-sand cove features incredibly calm, wave-free waters protected by a natural rock barrier, ideal for wading safely with an infant.

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Marina Pez Vela

A highly modern, flat, and paved oceanfront marina in nearby Quepos that is perfectly suited for strollers, offering gentle sea breezes, upscale boutique shopping, and gorgeous yacht views.

Morning 9-11:30am / 2.5h

Damas Island Mangrove Boat Tour

Float along calm, shaded estuary waters in a fully covered, comfortable motorboat to view white-faced monkeys and silky anteaters, ensuring baby stays cool and completely dry.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Kids Saving the Rainforest

A beautiful wildlife rehabilitation center featuring flat, shaded gravel paths where you can tour rescued forest animals in a quiet, tranquil environment free from loud tourist crowds.

Mid-day 11am-1pm / 2h

Playa Biesanz

A secluded, jungle-fringed cove with virtually no waves, making it feel like a giant natural swimming pool; access it easily with a baby carrier along the short, shaded forest trail.

Morning 9-10:30am / 1.5h

Finca Naturales Wildlife Refuge

An easy, highly shaded day walk through a private nature reserve that features a stunning butterfly garden, offering a quiet, sensory-rich experience that is very soothing for infants.

Morning 8am-12pm / 4h

Nauyaca Waterfalls 4x4 Tour

Experience these dramatic, misty waterfalls via a smooth 4x4 open-air truck tour that avoids the strenuous hike, letting you enjoy the cool mountain air and spray from a safe viewing deck.
