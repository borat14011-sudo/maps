The dramatic Oregon Coast welcomes your family with misty sea breezes, towering evergreens, and the majestic silhouette of Haystack Rock rising from the shore. This pristine coastline balances raw, wild beauty with the upscale comfort of a charming, walkable seaside village. Parents will love the flat, stroller-friendly hard-packed sand that makes beachside strolls with Mary Claire incredibly easy and scenic. After a day of exploring, retreat to premium oceanfront accommodations where the sound of the Pacific tide lulls your little one to sleep.
🚕 Getting there: The journey from Portland International Airport (PDX) to the Cannon Beach hotel strip takes exactly 1 hour and 30 minutes. We recommend booking a private luxury SUV transfer through Portland Town Car Service, which provides a clean, pre-installed rear-facing infant car seat and ample cargo space for your stroller and luggage.

What to do

Morning low tide 9-11am / 2h

Haystack Rock

Stroll along the shoreline to view this iconic 235-foot basalt monolith. The hard-packed sand at low tide is highly stroller-accessible, and the colorful tide pools will captivate baby Mary Claire without any loud sensory overload.

Late morning 11am-12:30pm / 1.5h

Ecola State Park Overlook

Drive up to the paved viewpoint for a breathtaking panoramic vista of the coastline and the historic Tillamook Rock Lighthouse. The main viewing platform is completely stroller-friendly and offers plenty of quiet benches.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Hug Point State Recreation Site

Explore easy-to-reach sea caves and a seasonal waterfall nestled against the sandstone cliffs. Use a front baby carrier for this location to navigate the soft dry sand before reaching the wet, flat shoreline.

Morning 11am-12pm / 1h

Cannon Beach History Center & Museum

A cozy, quiet indoor sanctuary featuring local heritage exhibits and a replica longhouse. It is fully stroller-accessible and serves as an excellent low-decibel escape if the coastal weather turns chilly or rainy.

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Tolovana Beach State Recreation Site

This park features a fantastic playground and exceptionally easy, flat access to the beach. The paved parking lot transitions smoothly to the sand, making it a stress-free spot for a gentle stroller walk.

Morning 9:30-11am / 1.5h

Oswald West State Park

Take a serene walk along the stroller-accessible paved path that winds under a lush canopy of giant old-growth rainforest trees. This sheltered trail keeps baby shaded from the wind and sun while offering pristine nature views.

Morning walk 10-10:30am / 30m

Coaster Theatre Playhouse

Stroll past this historic local landmark in the heart of downtown Cannon Beach. While evening shows are too loud for an infant, the beautifully manicured courtyard outside is a peaceful, quiet place to pause.

Afternoon 1-2pm / 1h

Icefire Glassworks

An upscale, spacious gallery where you can watch live glassblowing demonstrations from a safe, comfortable distance. The studio is well-ventilated, stroller-friendly, and offers a warm, mesmerizing visual experience for families.

Evening 6-7pm / 1h

Arcadia Beach State Recreation Site

A quieter alternative to the main beach, located just two miles south of town. The area is highly scenic and perfect for a peaceful sunset stroll while wearing baby Mary Claire in a premium carrier.

🍽️ Where to eat

Wayfarer Restaurant & Lounge

Savor local Dungeness crab cakes and wild Oregon salmon while enjoying prime oceanfront views of Haystack Rock. They provide wooden highchairs, welcome families warmly, and offer early dinner seating starting at 4:30 PM.

Pelican Brewing Company

An upscale-casual, spacious brewpub featuring gourmet flatbreads and fresh seafood. The lively, open dining room easily absorbs baby babbles, highchairs are readily available, and there is ample room to park a stroller.

Lazy Susan Cafe

A beloved local institution famous for its fresh marionberry scones and savory seafood omelets. Arrive early for breakfast to secure a cozy corner table that comfortably accommodates a highchair or infant car seat carrier.

The Irish Table

Enjoy sophisticated, elevated comfort food like shepherd's pie and pan-seared scallops in an intimate, welcoming setting. We recommend booking their earliest reservation at 5:00 PM when the dining room is at its quietest.

Bistro Restaurant and Bar

An upscale, intimate dining spot featuring Northwest-inspired European cuisine. The outdoor patio is heated and beautifully landscaped, making it a perfect spot to dine with a sleeping infant in a stroller.
