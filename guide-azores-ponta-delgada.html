What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM (1.5 hours)

Sete Cidades Vista do Rei Lookout

Marvel at the legendary blue and green twin lakes from the island's most iconic viewpoint. The main paved viewing platform is completely stroller-accessible, allowing you to capture breathtaking family photos without navigating steep, uneven dirt trails. Opt for a calm, clear morning to avoid high-altitude winds and mist.

Late Morning 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM (2 hours)

Terra Nostra Park

Stroll through one of Europe's most magnificent botanical gardens, featuring paved, shady paths winding beneath giant ferns and majestic trees. The dense canopy offers excellent sun protection for an infant, making it a peaceful oasis for a morning walk. While the iron-rich thermal pools are too hot for a baby, the lush garden air is incredibly soothing.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM (1.5 hours)

Augusto Arruda Pineapple Plantation

Explore the historic glass greenhouses where the island's famous sweet pineapples are grown using traditional smoke-curing methods. The flat, compacted gravel paths between the greenhouses are highly accessible for a stroller. Relax at the chic outdoor cafe afterwards to enjoy a fresh, non-alcoholic pineapple mocktail in a serene farm setting.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM (1.5 hours)

Jardim António Borges

Discover a romantic 19th-century municipal park in the heart of Ponta Delgada, featuring exotic plant species and dramatic stone caves. The wide, flat dirt paths are shaded by massive banyan trees, creating a quiet sanctuary for an afternoon nap in the stroller. It is highly accessible and far removed from the city traffic noise.

Early Morning 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM (1 hour)

Portas da Cidade Square

Walk through the historic city gates of Ponta Delgada, situated on a grand, flat public square paved in beautiful black-and-white basalt mosaics. The open pedestrian layout makes it easy to wheel a stroller while admiring the historic architecture. Stop by the adjacent marina front for a gentle ocean breeze.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM (1.5 hours)

Gorreana Tea Plantation

Visit Europe's oldest functioning tea plantation to witness traditional harvesting methods amidst rolling green hills overlooking the ocean. The main visitor facility and tasting room are stroller-friendly, allowing you to enjoy a cup of fresh organic tea in peace. Skip the steep estate hiking trails and stick to the paved terraces near the entrance.

Late Morning 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM (1.5 hours)

Lagoa das Furnas Caldeiras

Walk along the wooden lakeside boardwalks to watch geothermal steam rise from active volcanic vents, where locals cook traditional stews underground. The flat boardwalk system is incredibly stroller-friendly and keeps you at a safe, comfortable distance from the warm steam and sulfur scents. It is an educational and sensory-friendly outdoor experience.

Afternoon 2:30 PM - 4:00 PM (1.5 hours)

Pinhal da Paz Forestry Reserve

Escape to a beautifully manicured forest reserve featuring sprawling lawns, wild hydrangeas, and quiet walking paths. The park is exceptionally flat and offers numerous quiet, shady spots perfect for spreading out a blanket to let an infant crawl. It is highly regarded by local families for its serene, wind-sheltered microclimate.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM (1.5 hours)

Caldeira Velha Environmental Interpretation Centre

Wander along the elevated wooden boardwalks suspended above a lush, jungle-like ravine filled with prehistoric tree ferns and thermal waterfalls. The modern interpretation center is fully accessible and air-conditioned, providing a quiet indoor retreat if the outdoor humidity becomes too high. The tree canopy offers excellent shade throughout your walk.

🍽️ Where to eat

À Terra

Located inside the stylish Octant Ponta Delgada, this upscale restaurant specializes in rustic, wood-fired Azorean cuisine. They offer spacious table configurations that easily accommodate a stroller, exceptionally high-quality highchairs, and an early dinner service starting at 7:00 PM. Try the signature wood-fired octopus and slow-cooked local beef.

Anfiteatro Restaurant

A sophisticated waterfront dining venue showcasing contemporary Azorean gastronomy with gorgeous marina views. The spacious outdoor terrace is perfect for fine dining with a stroller, allowing you to enjoy fresh Atlantic limpets and seared tuna while the sea breeze keeps the infant cool. The service is highly attentive and very welcoming to families.

A Tasca

A highly regarded culinary institution in Ponta Delgada serving refined, traditional Azorean petiscos in a rustic tavern setting. To bypass the lively crowds and high noise levels, book the earliest dinner seating at 6:30 PM. Their famous sesame-crusted tuna and sweet pineapple cake are absolute must-tries.

Terra Nostra Restaurant

An elegant, white-linen dining room inside the historic Terra Nostra Garden Hotel, famous for its luxurious presentation of Cozido das Furnas. The spacious, quiet dining room offers a calm atmosphere that is excellent for keeping an infant relaxed. Highchairs are readily available, and the staff delivers impeccable, old-school European service.

Otaka

An exquisite fine-dining restaurant offering a high-end Japanese-Azorean fusion tasting menu centered around fresh, local Atlantic seafood. The sleek, modern dining room is quiet and intimate, making it best suited for an early evening seating right when they open. The staff is happy to accommodate a stroller at the spacious corner tables.

$220-380/night

It features a heated outdoor pool, an outdoor play area, and spacious family suites complete with kitchenettes and blackout shutters for seamless infant nap times. The serene, natural setting is incredibly peaceful and private.
