Vieques offers a rare, slow-tempo Caribbean paradise where wild horses roam free along pristine shores. Caracas Beach

Located within the National Wildlife Refuge, this stunning white-sand beach features modern, shaded gazebos. The water is gentle and crystal-clear, perfect for a peaceful family lounge session under a private UV tent.

Fortín Conde de Mirasol

The last Spanish fort built in the Americas offers paved ramps that are highly stroller-accessible. It provides stunning panoramic views of Isabel Segunda and a cool ocean breeze to keep baby comfortable.

🍽️ Where to eat

El Quenepo

An upscale, open-air fine dining institution right on the Malecon serving gourmet Puerto Rican-fusion dishes like whole pan-bronzed snapper. They offer early dinner seatings at 5:30 PM and are highly accommodating to families with infants.

Bili

Famous for its fresh local mofongo and snapper, this vibrant bistro features a spacious, breezy outdoor terrace. The ambient hum of the ocean breeze and light music easily masks any infant fussiness.

Tin Box

A unique, open-air treehouse-style restaurant serving slow-smoked BBQ and fresh local oysters. The casual atmosphere, spaced-out tables, and lush garden setting make it incredibly stress-free for dining with a baby.

Bananas VQS

A highly welcoming beachfront staple on the Malecon offering exceptional fish tacos and local beers. They provide sturdy highchairs and have a high-energy, open-air layout where Mary Claire will love watching the waves.

Coqui Fire Cafe

An intimate, cheerful spot in Isabel Segunda famous for its homemade hot sauces and delicious Mexican-Caribbean fare. Perfect for a quick, early lunch where the friendly staff eagerly welcomes young families.
