Bonaire

← back to travel board
Bonaire captivates families with its pristine, crystal-clear waters, resident pink flamingos, and incredibly laid-back Dutch Caribbean charm. Free from massive crowds, this eco-paradise offers accessible shallow reefs and gentle coastal breezes perfect for little explorers. It is a slow-paced haven where toddlers can safely splash while parents soak in the vibrant island colors.
🚕 Getting there: ~10 to 15 minute taxi ride or rental car drive from Flamingo International Airport (BON) to the main town of Kralendijk and nearby beach resorts.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Sorobon Beach at Lac Bay

This shallow, wind-protected bay features knee-deep crystal-clear water for hundreds of yards, making it the ultimate safe marine playground. Toddler note: The soft sand and calm, warm waters are perfect for tiny splashers and sandcastle builders.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Donkey Sanctuary Bonaire

Drive or walk through this heartwarming sanctuary home to hundreds of rescued, incredibly friendly wild donkeys. Toddler note: Keep car windows rolled up halfway if your toddler gets intimidated by eager donkeys poking their noses in for carrots.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Pekelmeer Flamingo Sanctuary

Marvel at the striking contrast of bright pink salt pans, towering white salt pyramids, and thousands of wild pink flamingos. Toddler note: Bring binoculars so your little ones can get a closer look at the distant birds from the roadside viewing areas.

Morning / 3-4h

Boca Slagbaai in Washington Slagbaai National Park

A historic yellow building frames a calm, sandy cove perfect for spotting green parrots and swimming in gentle waters. Toddler note: The drive inside the park is bumpy, so choose the shorter route to keep toddler car-sickness at bay.

Morning / 3h

Klein Bonaire Beach Trip

Take a short, exciting water taxi ride to this uninhabited coral island boasting pristine white sands and curious sea turtles. Toddler note: There is zero shade on the island, so bring a pop-up UV tent and plenty of sunscreen for your little one.

Morning or Evening / 1-2h

Wilhelmina Park Playground & Market

Stroll the colorful streets of Kralendijk and let kids burn energy at the grassy park playground, which often hosts local craft markets. Toddler note: The playground equipment is well-shaded by large trees, offering a cool respite from the midday sun.

Morning / 1.5h

Lac Bay Mangrove Solar Boat Tour

Glide silently through the protected mangrove forests on an eco-friendly solar boat to spot baby fish and unique birds. Toddler note: The solar boat is completely shaded and quiet, making it a peaceful, motor-free ride that won't scare sensitive toddlers.

Afternoon / 2h

Te Amo Beach

Located right near the airport, this sandy beach offers calm waters, excellent easy snorkeling, and front-row seats to watch planes land. Toddler note: Toddlers will love splashing in the shallows while pointing at the giant airplanes flying low overhead.

Morning / 1-2h

Bari Reef Shore Splash

Known for having the highest diversity of fish species in the Caribbean, this reef has very easy, shallow shore access. Toddler note: Sit on the shallow rocky ledge with your toddler to watch colorful yellow damselfish swim right up to your feet.

🍽️ Where to eat

Sebastian's Restaurant

Request an early table on the overwater pier during sunset to enjoy fresh grilled seafood while watching fish swim directly beneath your feet.

Kite City Food Truck

Grab a fresh, melt-in-your-mouth fish wrap or a simple burger to eat picnic-style right on the sand at Te Amo Beach.

Between 2 Buns

Stop by this cozy, air-conditioned local favorite for towering club sandwiches, fresh fruit smoothies, and decadent homemade cakes.

La Cantina Brewery & Restaurant

Dine in the spacious, breezy courtyard garden and order the artisanal fish tacos while kids enjoy the simple, fresh sides.

Gio's Gelateria & Caffè

Cool down after a warm day with a scoop of their legendary, creamy Italian gelato, featuring toddler-approved flavors like strawberry and mango.
