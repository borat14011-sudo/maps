What to do

3h (morning)

Palombaggia Beach

Crystal-clear, shallow water and fine sand perfect for baby splashing. Stroller-friendly boardwalk leads to shaded pine groves. Arrive early (9am) to claim a spot and avoid midday heat.

2h (early morning)

Bonifacio Citadel

Explore the clifftop fortress with a baby carrier (cobblestone streets not stroller-friendly). Stunning sea views and old-world charm; skip the windy upper paths with infant. Visit early morning for cooler temps and fewer crowds.

4h (9am-1pm)

Scandola Nature Reserve Boat Tour

Private half-day boat trip from Porto or Calvi – calm waters, shaded cabin, and baby-safe life vests provided. Watch for ospreys and dolphins. Ideal mid-morning departure with infant nap opportunity.

1.5h (late afternoon 5pm)

Calanques de Piana

Drive the scenic D81 road (stroller parking at viewpoint) – the red rock formations are a short walk from the car. Shaded picnic spots. Best at golden hour for photos, but avoid late afternoon heat.

2h (8am-10am)

Aiguilles de Bavella

A gentle hike along the flat section of the GR20 near the mountain pass – stroller-friendly for the first 500m. Breathtaking granite needles. Bring baby carrier for uneven patches. Early morning for cool air.

3h (morning)

Santa Giulia Beach

Sandy beach with a protected lagoon – calm, shallow water ideal for baby. Shaded areas under pine trees. Stroller-accessible path from parking. Family-friendly, with nearby cafes.

2h (10am-12pm)

Filitosa Prehistoric Site

Ancient menhirs and statues in a shaded forest – stroller accessible on gravel paths. Educational and uncrowded. Visit before noon to avoid heat; baby-wearing recommended for the steep sections.

1.5h (9am-10:30am)

St. Florent Harbor & Church

Stroll the stroller-friendly promenade past pastel houses and fishing boats. Visit the 16th-century Santa Maria Assunta church (quiet interior for feeding). Morning light is lovely; patisseries nearby for coffee.

3h (morning 8:30-11:30)

La Pelosa Beach (Propriano)

Family-friendly beach with fine sand and very shallow water – perfect for baby to play. Wooden walkway from parking to sand. Lifeguard in summer. Go early (8:30am) to get shade under umbrella.

🍽️ Where to eat

Le Grand Bleu, Calvi

Michelin-starred seafood with a terrace overlooking the marina. Highchair available, early seating (6:30pm) for families. Signature bouillabaisse – mild enough for baby? Quiet, elegant atmosphere.

U Farniente, Bonifacio

Authentic Corsican cuisine on a shaded terrace in the old town. Highchair and children’s menu (pasta, fish). Signature wild boar stew (civet de sanglier). Book early dinner to avoid late crowds.

La Table de la Ferme, Porto-Vecchio

Farm-to-table restaurant inside a luxury hotel (Les Bergeries). Terrace with sea views, highchairs, and early dinner slots. Signature lobster linguine. Calm environment with space for stroller parking.

Chez Pierre, Ajaccio

Classic bistro near the port with outdoor seating and a relaxed vibe. Highchair available; serves fresh fish and Corsican charcuterie. Try the ‘tarte aux figues’ – baby-friendly desserts. Noise moderate.

A Pignata, Saint-Florent

Warm family-run restaurant in the village square. Terrace with shade, highchair, and simple pasta dishes for little ones. Signature chevre Chaud salad. Early dinner recommended (7pm).

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Hotel Casadelmar, Porto-Vecchio

$280–400/night

5-star boutique with spacious family suites (some with private plunge pool). Crib on request, baby-proofed balcony, large pool with shallow entry. Blackout curtains, kids’ club for older siblings (not needed). Price range off-season or deluxe rooms.

Hotel U Capu Biancu, Sant'Amanza

$300–400/night

Luxury cliffside property with private beach cove (calm water). Family suites with separate living area, crib provided, two pools (one heated). Beachfront access with stroller-friendly boardwalk. Quiet, secluded.

La Signoria, Calvi

$250–400/night

Elegant 5-star hotel in a converted convent. Family suites with garden terrace, crib on request, large outdoor pool. Shaded grounds, 10-min drive to Calvi beach. Club lounge for light meals. Blackout curtains available.
