Experience bohemian grand-tour luxury while enjoying private boat charters and world-class infant hospitality in Europe's most picturesque capital.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Wallenstein Garden

This serene Baroque garden features manicured hedges, giant koi ponds, and roaming peacocks that will easily captivate baby Mary Claire. The gravel pathways are wide and flat enough for a high-end stroller, while the quiet atmosphere is perfect for an outdoor nap.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM

Kampa Island Park

A tranquil oasis separated from the busy Lesser Town by the Čertovka stream, offering wide grassy areas and paved walking paths. It is highly stroller-accessible and features plenty of shade trees where you can spread out a blanket for tummy time by the water.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Prague Castle Royal Garden

Avoid the crowded cathedral courtyards and head straight to this renaissance garden, which offers a smooth, paved promenade and stunning views of the city. It is significantly quieter than the main castle grounds, making it ideal for a peaceful stroller walk.

Late Afternoon 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Private Vltava River Cruise on a Jazz Boat or Venice Boat

Book a private mahogany water taxi with Prague Venice cruises to glide under the Charles Bridge without the crowds. The gentle rocking of the water is incredibly soothing for infants, and the covered cabin provides instant shade and wind protection.

Midday 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM

Stromovka Park

Prague's largest royal park offers paved, car-free avenues, peaceful lakes, and majestic old oaks. It is the ultimate destination for a long, uninterrupted stroller nap while parents enjoy a gourmet takeaway coffee from nearby boutique cafes.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM

Letná Park Paths

Take a gentle stroll along the flat, tree-lined paths of Letná hill to enjoy panoramic views of Prague's bridges. The park features wide promenades and quiet, shaded benches where you can nurse or feed baby Mary Claire away from the city noise.

Afternoon 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM

National Museum

For a rainy or hot afternoon, this grand neo-Renaissance building features spacious, air-conditioned galleries and excellent elevator access for strollers. The grand entrance hall is visually stimulating for a baby, while the quiet side galleries offer a peaceful escape.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Vrtba Garden

While this terraced Baroque garden has stairs that require a baby carrier rather than a stroller, the sheer beauty and absolute silence make it worth the effort. It is a hidden sanctuary with meticulously manicured hedges and a quiet, sheltered courtyard at the base.

Late Afternoon 3:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Vyšehrad Fortress Grounds

Located on a hill south of the city center, this historic fortress features flat, paved pathways, lush green lawns, and a highly relaxed atmosphere far from the tourist crowds. It is perfect for a peaceful family walk with spectacular, elevated river views.

🍽️ Where to eat

Café Savoy

A grand historic cafe with a magnificent neo-Renaissance ceiling, perfect for a luxury breakfast of gourmet pastries and French toast. The bustling, lively atmosphere easily masks baby vocalizations, and the staff is highly accommodating with highchairs and stroller placement.

CottoCrudo at Four Seasons

An upscale Italian restaurant offering a sophisticated menu alongside beautiful views of the Vltava River. They provide an exceptionally welcoming environment for families, featuring high-quality highchairs, a dedicated children's menu, and early dinner seating options.

Kampa Park Restaurant

A premier fine-dining venue located right on the water's edge. Reserve a table on the heated outdoor terrace where the gentle sound of the river provides natural white noise for a sleeping infant, and enjoy world-class seafood while baby rests in her stroller.

Café Imperial

An Art Deco masterpiece featuring spectacular ceramic wall mosaics and a vibrant, energetic ambiance. The bustling environment is ideal for parents who want a luxury dining experience without worrying about their infant making noise, and highchairs are readily available.

Terasa U Prince

This iconic rooftop terrace offers elevator access and unmatched views of the Old Town Square. Visit during the quiet mid-afternoon hours for an early family dinner or light bites, allowing you to enjoy the views in a relaxed, uncrowded setting with baby.

$200-400/night
