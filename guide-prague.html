What to do

Late Afternoon / 1.5-2h

Letná Park

Walk the paved paths for panoramic Vltava river views, then stop at the large playground near the historic Letná Beer Garden. It is highly stroller-accessible and offers plenty of shade under the massive chestnut trees for a midday nap.

Morning / 3-4h

Prague Zoo

Consistently ranked among the best in the world, this massive zoo features flat paths, baby changing stations, and dedicated splash areas. Rent a wooden pull-wagon at the main entrance to pull your toddler around easily and comfortably.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Kampa Park

A peaceful riverside green space right off the bustling Charles Bridge, offering wide grassy lawns perfect for a crawling baby and a fully fenced playground. Don't miss the famous giant, quirky bronze baby statues by artist David Černý situated nearby.

Morning / 1-2h

Strahov Monastery

Skip the crowded library interiors and push the stroller through the peaceful, cobblestone-free courtyard to the panoramic viewpoint. The paved walking path winding down from here towards Malá Strana is entirely downhill, making for an easy, scenic stroll.

Morning / 2h

Stromovka Park

Prague's largest royal park features perfectly paved, flat paths wrapping around calm duck ponds and multiple modern, wood-carved playgrounds. It is the ultimate spot in the city for a peaceful morning stroller run or walk away from the tourist crowds.

Morning / 2h

Prague Castle Courtyards

Navigate the wide, flat, paved exterior courtyards of the castle complex to admire the gothic architecture without climbing stairs. Avoid the steep walk up the hill by riding Tram 22 directly to the Pražský hrad stop, dropping you off on flat ground.

Saturday Morning / 1-2h

Naplavka Farmers Market

Stroll along the Vltava riverbank on a Saturday morning to grab fresh Czech pastries like koláče while feeding the swans. The wide, flat paved quay easily accommodates strollers, and the vibrant local atmosphere is incredibly welcoming.

Afternoon / 2h

Petřín Funicular and Hill

Ride the historic funicular railway up Petřín Hill, which is fully accessible for strollers and exciting for young toddlers. At the summit, wander through the fragrant, flat rose gardens and let your daughter touch the flowers.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Slovanský Island

This quiet, leafy island on the Vltava River features a fantastic fenced playground with rubber safety flooring and a miniature train ride. It is a perfect, secure pitstop for your toddler to burn off energy right in the heart of downtown.

🍽️ Where to eat

Lokál Dlouhááá

Order the fried cheese (Smažený sýr) and freshly tapped Pilsner Urquell beer. The bustling, high-ceilinged beer-hall atmosphere easily drowns out any toddler tantrums, making for a stress-free family dinner.

Café Savoy

Treat yourselves to the decadent Savoy Breakfast and a vetrník (caramel choux pastry) in this opulent, neo-Renaissance space. The staff is exceptionally welcoming to families, providing high chairs and clean, spacious baby-changing facilities.

Kuchyň

Enjoy traditional Czech braised beef with cranberries (Svíčková) in a spacious outdoor courtyard overlooking Prague's red roofs. The casual, open-air setting makes it easy to stroll with a fussy baby while waiting for your meal.

Eska

Try the potatoes cooked in ash with smoked fish alongside their famous freshly baked sourdough bread. Located in the flat, trendy Karlín neighborhood, this industrial-chic spot has wide aisles and plenty of space to park a stroller next to your table.

Marina Ristorante

Indulge in wood-fired Neapolitan pizzas and fresh pasta aboard this spacious, stationary ship floating on the river. The wide layout easily accommodates strollers, and the views of passing boats will keep your toddler thoroughly entertained.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Four Seasons Hotel Prague

Offers unparalleled luxury on the riverbank with complimentary high-quality wooden cribs, baby welcome amenities, and childproofed rooms on request. The indoor vitality pool and central, flat Old Town location make sightseeing with an infant completely seamless.

Augustine, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Prague

Housed in a 13th-century monastery, this peaceful retreat offers massive multi-room suites ideal for keeping a sleeping baby undisturbed. Located in the quiet Malá Strana district, its serene, private courtyard garden is perfect for calm morning strolls.

Mama Shelter Prague

A vibrant, stylish option that is incredibly family-friendly, offering large family rooms and loaner cribs. The spacious lobby features ample stroller parking, play areas, and a highly relaxed, noise-tolerant atmosphere that takes the stress out of traveling with a baby.
