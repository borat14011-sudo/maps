Perched dramatically over the Pacific, Lima blends pre-Columbian history with a breathtaking modern coastal energy. The misty sea cliffs yield to manicured green spaces and colonial-era haciendas radiating old-world charm. For families, the flat, beautifully paved Malecón de Miraflores provides miles of stroller-ready coastal paths high above the crashing waves. Here, Houston sophistication meets Peruvian warmth, ensuring a seamless, high-end escape even with a little one in tow.
🚕 Getting there: The drive from Jorge Chávez International Airport to the Miraflores hotel strip takes 45 to 60 minutes. The best luxury family option is a pre-booked private SUV transfer arranged directly through your hotel's concierge, guaranteeing a certified, rear-facing infant car seat to safely navigate Lima's bustling traffic.

What to do

Early morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Malecón de Miraflores

Stroll this stunning six-mile cliffside boardwalk. The paved pathways are perfect for your stroller, offering cool ocean breezes, manicured lawns at Parque del Amor, and plenty of quiet benches for infant feedings.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:30 PM

Museo Larco

Explore 5,000 years of pre-Columbian history housed in an 18th-century mansion. The vibrant, flower-filled gardens feature ramp access for strollers, and the on-site garden cafe is a serene spot for baby Mary Claire to nap.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Parque El Olivar

A peaceful forest of over 1,600 historic olive trees in San Isidro. It is incredibly quiet, shaded, and flat, making it the ultimate destination for a peaceful stroller walk away from the city traffic noise.

Early evening 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM

Circuito Mágico del Agua

A spectacular park of illuminated fountains. Go right at the 6:00 PM opening to catch the brilliant water displays before the larger, noisier crowds arrive, keeping the sensory experience gentle for baby Mary Claire.

Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Huaca Pucllana

Marvel at this great clay pyramid built by the Lima culture. Book the early morning guided tour to enjoy cooler temperatures; while some paths are gravelly, major viewpoints are easily accessed with a sturdy carrier or stroller.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM

Larcomar

This upscale, multi-level shopping center built directly into the cliffs of Miraflores is fully elevator-accessible. Take a breezy walk to browse local boutiques with stunning, unobstructed ocean views.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM

Plaza Mayor de Lima

Walk the historic heart of the city to admire the Cathedral and Government Palace. The plaza is flat and pedestrian-friendly; visit early to avoid the midday sun and peak commuter traffic.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Puente de los Suspiros

Walk through the bohemian Barranco district to this iconic wooden bridge. The morning hours are peaceful and quiet, offering lovely colonial architecture and shade from surrounding trees before the nightlife crowds wake up.

Mid-day 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM

Parque de la Exposición

A massive, historic park in downtown Lima featuring beautiful pavilions, a small lake, and wide, paved walking paths. It offers ample shade and open lawns, perfect for a quiet picnic blanket session with your baby.

🍽️ Where to eat

Astrid y Gastón

Set in a majestic 17th-century estate in San Isidro. Book a 12:30 PM lunch in the spacious, sunlit courtyard which easily accommodates strollers and absorbs infant coos, allowing you to enjoy world-class cebiche stress-free.

Maido

Experience world-renowned Nikkei cuisine. Reserve the very first lunch seating; the staff is exceptionally attentive to families, and the modern, spacious dining room allows a quiet corner for baby to rest while you savor the 50-hour short rib.

Huaca Pucllana Restaurant

Dine on upscale Peruvian classics right on the edge of the illuminated ruins. Book an early outdoor terrace table at 7:00 PM; the open-air ocean breeze and ambient ruin lighting provide a relaxing, spacious atmosphere for a stroller.

Isolina Taberna Peruana

Famous for generous portions of traditional Criollo dishes like Seco de Res. Arrive right at the 12:00 PM opening for a warm, bustling family atmosphere where baby's noises are easily drowned out by the lively, welcoming lunch crowd.

Mangos Restaurant

Located on the cliffside terrace at Larcomar. Offers a highly convenient, stunningly beautiful spot for high-end casual dining. They feature excellent highchairs, a very accommodating staff, and a gentle breeze that keeps baby cool.
