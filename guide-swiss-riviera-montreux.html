This sun-drenched crescent of Lake Geneva pairs Belle Époque elegance with legendary Swiss efficiency, making it an incredibly low-friction destination for a family traveling with an infant. Parents can sip crisp, exclusive Chasselas overlooking the terraced vineyards of Lavaux while pushing a premium stroller along wide, flat lakeside promenades lined with palms and exotic flowers. The region's impeccably clean, punctual trains and historic paddle steamers turn transit into a scenic joyride rather than a logistical chore. It's a playground where high-finance sophistication meets absolute ease for a young family.
🚕 Getting there: Fly into Geneva Airport (GVA) and take a direct, highly efficient 60-minute train ride (SBB) straight to Montreux Station, or opt for a private ~1-hour Mercedes V-Class transfer booked via Swiss Luxury Transfer to easily handle all your luggage and infant car seats.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Château de Chillon

Explore this legendary island fortress where the flat lakeside path from Montreux is perfect for stroller naps, though you should swap to a baby carrier once inside to navigate the historic stone steps. Parents will love the medieval architecture and sweeping lake views while the toddler is mesmerized by the lakeside swans.

Morning / 3-4h

Rochers-de-Naye Cogwheel Railway

Board this historic cogwheel train from Montreux station up to 2,042 meters, offering a thrilling but comfortable cabin ride that keeps toddlers engaged. At the summit, visit the 'Marmot Paradise' to see live alpine marmots, and enjoy panoramic views without any strenuous hiking.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Lavaux Express (Cully Loop)

Ride this charming, rubber-tired mini-train that winds through the UNESCO-listed terraced vineyards of Lavaux, keeping you off the steep slopes with a toddler. It's a relaxed way for parents to enjoy the dramatic vineyard views while the little one is entertained by the gentle open-air ride.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Chemin de la Promenade

This exceptionally flat, paved path runs along the water from Villeneuve to Vevey, lined with exotic plants, sculpture gardens, and clean playgrounds. It's the ultimate low-stress stroller runway where you can grab a gelato and let your toddler stretch her legs safely away from car traffic.

Afternoon / 1.5h

CGN Belle Époque Paddle Steamer Ride

Board a historic, beautifully restored paddle steamer for a breezy cruise across Lake Geneva's crystal waters. The first-class decks are spacious enough for a stroller, and the gentle engine vibration is practically guaranteed to lull an infant to sleep.

Morning / 2h

Alimentarium Food Museum

Located right on the Vevey lakefront, this interactive food museum features sensory exhibits and culinary play areas that captivate toddlers while parents enjoy the history of food. It's fully stroller-accessible with spacious elevators and a great lakeside garden for a quick outdoor break.

Morning / 2-3h

Chaplin's World

Set in Charlie Chaplin's former estate, this museum features a stunning, stroller-accessible 10-acre park where your toddler can toddle on lush lawns. The modern studio building is fully paved and air-conditioned, offering a highly visual, light-filled experience that is easy to navigate with a baby.

Morning / 1h

Montreux Marché Couvert

This historic, covered 19th-century market hall sits right on the water and serves as a vibrant, weather-proof hub to stroll through with an infant. Grab local cheese and fresh pastries to enjoy on the adjacent steps while watching the boats dock.

Afternoon / 2h

Domaine Croix Duplex

Sip crisp local Chasselas on a stunning outdoor terrace overlooking the lake while your baby relaxes in the stroller beside you. This family-run estate in Grandvaux is exceptionally welcoming to children and offers easy stroller access to their panoramic outdoor seating area.

🍽️ Where to eat

Le Deck at Le Baron Tavernier

Order the local perch fillets (Filets de Perche) paired with a glass of St-Saphorin wine while soaking in the most dramatic clifftop view of the lake. The spacious, open-air terrace easily accommodates strollers and masks baby babbles with soft lounge music and breeze.

Les Saisons at Grand Hôtel du Lac

Indulge in refined French-Swiss seasonal cuisine in an elegant setting that welcomes families with high chairs and spacious seating. Early dinner reservations are perfect here, allowing you to enjoy Michelin-caliber dining while your baby sleeps in her bassinet next to the table.

Le Coucou Restaurant

Order the traditional Swiss rosti with Gruyère cheese and a farm-fresh egg in this ultra-chic alpine chalet in Caux. The atmosphere is warm, rustic, and bustling, making it completely stress-free if your toddler decides to be vocal while you enjoy the mountain air.

Restaurant du Port

Feast on fresh-caught Lake Geneva fera fish in a butter-lemon sauce on their lovely pedestrian-only street terrace in Lutry. It's highly accommodating for families, with plenty of room to park a stroller right at the table and quick service to suit toddler attention spans.

Café Bellagio

Savor the slow-cooked beef tenderloin on the lakeside terrace where the gentle lap of the water provides natural white noise. The staff at this upscale spot is highly attentive to families, quickly arranging high chairs and offering kid-friendly menu adjustments.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Fairmont Le Montreux Palace

This legendary 5-star palace offers spacious suites, exceptional crib setups, and a beautiful indoor pool perfect for a splash with your toddler. Its prime location directly opposite the flat lakeside promenade makes spontaneous stroller walks effortless.

Grand Hôtel du Lac

A boutique 5-star oasis in Vevey featuring a tranquil private garden, an outdoor pool, and incredibly spacious family suites. They provide high-quality wooden cribs and personalized baby amenities, making it feel like a luxurious, high-end home away from home.

Royal Plaza Montreux

Situated directly on the water's edge, every room features a private balcony for parents to enjoy a glass of local wine after the baby falls asleep. The hotel boasts a heated indoor pool and seamless, elevator-to-promenade stroller access.

👨‍👩‍👧 Utilize the free Montreux Riviera Card provided by your hotel for free local bus and train travel, which lets you easily hop on and off transit without fumbling with ticket machines while managing a stroller. If you plan to explore the steep, cobblestoned vineyard villages of Lavaux like Saint-Saphorin, swap your stroller for a high-quality baby carrier to comfortably navigate the historic stairs and inclines. Stock up on baby essentials like organic purees and diapers at the Migros or Coop supermarkets located near the main train stations, as local pharmacies have limited weekend hours.
