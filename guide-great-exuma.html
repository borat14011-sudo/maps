What to do

Early morning 9-11am / 2h

Coco Plum Beach

This breathtaking beach is famous for its vast shallow sandbars at low tide, making it incredibly safe for wading with baby Mary Claire. The crystal-clear, knee-deep water stretches for yards, offering plenty of shade under palm trees and a peaceful spot to hunt for sand dollars.

Late morning 11am-1pm / 2h

Chat 'N' Chill

Take a quick, private water taxi from George Town to Stocking Island to experience this legendary beach spot. Arrive early to sit in the shade of the wild pines, dip Mary Claire's toes in the ultra-calm shallows, and watch the resident stingrays glide peacefully by the shore.

Morning 9-11:30am / 2.5h

Tropic of Cancer Beach

Also known as Pelican Beach, this is the longest continuous stretch of powder-soft sand on Little Exuma. The water is remarkably calm on windless days, and there is a small shaded pavilion near the entrance perfect for keeping baby out of the midday sun.

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Hoopers Bay

This sheltered, tranquil bay is famous for its resident green sea turtles that swim close to the shoreline. The water here is remarkably still, making it an ideal spot to float gently with an infant in a baby spring float while enjoying the beautiful views.

Morning charter 8:30am-12:30pm / 4h

Exuma Cays Private Charter

Book a customized, private half-day boat charter with a canopy for sun protection to explore the calm inner cays. This allows you to set your own pace for Mary Claire's nap schedule while enjoying the stunning, vibrant hues of the shallow flats.

Morning 8:30-10:30am / 2h

Jolly Hall Beach

A quiet, scenic cove lined with shady casuarina trees that provide excellent natural protection from the Bahamian sun. The water here is almost completely wave-free, making it an excellent spot for a peaceful morning walk with a baby carrier.

Morning boat tour 9am-12pm / 3h

Moriah Harbour Cay National Park

Explore the stunning, protected marine environment of this national park via a private, slow-moving pontoon charter. The pristine, shallow sand dunes offer a quiet, wind-sheltered landscape perfect for a peaceful family picnic on the sandbars.

Morning stroll 10-11:30am / 1.5h

George Town Straw Market

Enjoy a slow-paced morning stroll through this small, colorful local market in the heart of George Town. The flat terrain surrounding the market area is easily navigable with a sturdy stroller, allowing you to browse handmade Bahamas crafts in a relaxed setting.

Late afternoon 4-5:30pm / 1.5h

Little Exuma Bonefish Flats

Wade through these exceptionally shallow, warm saltwater flats located near the bridge connecting Great and Little Exuma. The ankle-deep water offers a safe, tranquil sensory experience for an infant experiencing the warm ocean for the first time.

🍽️ Where to eat

Shirley's Fish Fry

A celebrated local institution offering exceptional fresh grouper and snapper in a casual, open-air setting. The lively, bustling outdoor atmosphere easily masks any infant fussiness, making it a stress-free dining option for an early dinner.

The Palapa Grill

Located at Grand Isle, this upscale open-air restaurant offers a serene poolside ambiance with stunning ocean views. They provide highchairs, exceptionally friendly service for families, and delicious fresh lobster flatbreads during early evening seatings.

Peace & Plenty Restaurant

This historic indoor-outdoor dining room overlooks Elizabeth Harbour, offering a cool breeze and a relaxed luxury vibe. The staff is highly accommodating to families, and the early dinner hours are perfect for dining before Mary Claire's bedtime.

Rusty Anchor

Situated at February Point, this sophisticated yet highly family-friendly restaurant features a spacious outdoor terrace with sweeping harbor views. It offers a quiet, relaxed setting with highchairs available and excellent wood-fired pizzas.

Santanna's Bar and Grill

An iconic open-air oceanfront eatery in Little Exuma famous for its cracked lobster and laid-back bahamian charm. The casual outdoor picnic tables under wide wooden roofs provide plenty of shade for baby while you enjoy a scenic lunch.
