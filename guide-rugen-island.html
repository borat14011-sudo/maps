Rügen Island

← back to travel board
Rügen Island captivates families with its dramatic white chalk cliffs, endless sugar-sand beaches, and dense beech forests. It is a nostalgic seaside playground where historic steam trains chug through the countryside and kid-friendly adventure parks wait around every corner.
🚕 Getting there: ~1.5 to 2-hour drive via rental car or pre-booked private shuttle from Rostock-Laage Airport (RLG) across the scenic Rügen Bridge directly to the beach resorts.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Jasmund National Park & Königsstuhl

Marvel at the dramatic white chalk cliffs contrasting with the blue Baltic Sea. The modern visitor center offers interactive exhibits and stroller-friendly pathways directly to the main viewing platform.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Rasender Roland Steam Train

Ride this historic, slow-moving steam railway that connects the island's charming resort towns. Toddlers will love the rhythmic chugging sound and the open-air window views.

Morning / 2h

Naturerbe Zentrum Treetop Walk

Walk among the treetops on this giant wooden path that spirals up above the forest canopy. The gentle, stair-free incline is perfectly designed for strollers and energetic toddlers.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Seebrücke Sellin

Stroll along this iconic, fairy-tale-like pier stretching over the Baltic waves. A convenient elevator next to the main stairs makes descending to the beach with a stroller effortless.

Morning / 3-4h

Karls Erlebnis-Dorf Zirkow

An absolute toddler paradise, this massive strawberry-themed farm park features indoor play barns, animal petting areas, and family tractor rides. Don't miss the freshly made strawberry sweets at the rustic market.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Binz Beach and Promenade

Relax on soft, white sand while renting a traditional striped hooded beach chair to shelter the little ones from the wind. The shallow, calm shorelines are highly safe for paddling toddlers.

Morning / 2-3h

Dinosaurierland Rügen

Follow a shady forest trail to discover dozens of life-sized, moving dinosaur models. Young kids will enjoy the large adventure playground and the hands-on fossil-digging sandpit.

Morning / 2-3h

Kap Arkona Lighthouses

Explore the windy northern tip of the island featuring historic lighthouses and dramatic coastal views. Toddlers will love riding the colorful trackless tourist train from the main parking lot.

Morning / 1-2h

Rügen Market in Thiessow

Browse local crafts, fresh sea buckthorn juices, and artisanal foods at this lively open-air harbor market. It is an easy walk for little legs with plenty of delicious finger foods to try.

🍽️ Where to eat

Weltenbummler

Try the freshly caught Baltic plaice served with crispy pan-fried potatoes while the kids enjoy the dedicated children's menu.

Nautilus

Dine inside a wonderfully quirky restaurant designed to look like Jules Verne's submarine, and order the delicious Baltic cod fillet.

Kleine Melodie

Grab a table on the sun terrace overlooking the sea and order the traditional local fish soup followed by a slice of sea buckthorn cake.

Fährhaus Vaschvitz

Enjoy a quiet, family-friendly atmosphere right on the water and order the crispy fried Baltic herring with homemade remoulade.

Strandhalle Binz

Indulge in hearty, traditional Pomeranian beef stew served in a beautifully restored, highly welcoming historic hall.

👨‍👩‍👧 Rent a 'Bollerwagen' (wooden pull-along wagon) at your resort or beach station, which is the local secret for effortlessly transporting both tired toddlers and heavy beach gear. Always carry cash, as many small seaside snack stands and rural farm shops on the island do not accept credit cards.
