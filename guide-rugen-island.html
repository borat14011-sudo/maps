Rügen Island

Rügen Island welcomes you with its dramatic chalk cliffs, grand historic resort architecture, and pristine white-sand beaches kissing the Baltic Sea. Here, the mild maritime climate offers a soothing retreat for your family, away from the bustling crowds of southern Europe. The stroller-ready Binzer Strandpromenade stretches for miles, allowing Will and Kathryn to glide baby Mary Claire effortlessly past elegant 19th-century villas. With private beach chairs (Strandkörbe) providing a cozy, wind-shielded nook, your days are defined by quiet coastal luxury and gentle sea breezes.
🚕 Getting there: Private luxury SUV transfer from Berlin Brandenburg Airport (BER) directly to your Binz resort takes approximately 3 hours and 15 minutes, complete with a pre-installed Cybex rear-facing infant car seat. Alternatively, the premium ICE train runs directly from Berlin to Binz, offering a spacious 'Kleinkindabteil' (toddler compartment) with plenty of room for your stroller and baby essentials.

What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Seebrücke Sellin

The iconic Sellin Pier is completely stroller-accessible via a modern elevator that bypasses the steep cliff stairs. Enjoy a morning stroll over the water with Mary Claire as the sea air induces a peaceful nap.

Midday 11am-1:30pm / 2.5h

Nationalpark-Zentrum Königsstuhl

Explore the famous white chalk cliffs via the brand-new, fully barrier-free Skywalk suspended high above the Baltic. The visitor center offers quiet, stroller-friendly forest boardwalks shaded by ancient beech trees.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Baumwipfelpfad im Naturerbe Zentrum Rügen

This treetop walk in Prora features a massive wooden spiral tower designed with a very gentle incline, making it perfectly stroller-friendly and safe for carrying baby.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Rasender Roland (Rügen Light Railway)

This historic steam train is a delightful, slow-paced way to see the island. The train staff gladly assist in folding and storing your stroller in the luggage car, while Mary Claire will love the gentle chug of the ride.

Afternoon 3-4:30pm / 1.5h

Schmachter See Promenade

Walk the quiet, landscaped 'Park of the Senses' lakefront promenade in Binz. Far less windy than the open sea, this is a serene spot for baby's afternoon bottle and stroller nap.

Late afternoon 4:30-6pm / 1.5h

Binzer Strandpromenade

A paved, car-free seaside promenade lined with grand Belle Époque resort architecture. Stop at any of the luxury cafes for a glass of champagne while keeping the stroller right by your outdoor table.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Schlosspark Putbus

Known as the 'White City,' Putbus features an elegant English landscape park where you can push the stroller along wide pathways and feed the tame, free-roaming deer.

Morning 9:30am-12:30pm / 3h

Kap Arkona

Rügen's northernmost cape features paved, traffic-free paths leading to two historic lighthouses. Rent a comfortable horse-drawn carriage with space for your stroller to ride in classic coastal style.

Afternoon 1-3pm / 2h

Ostseebad Göhren Nordstrand

A beautiful, family-friendly beach with incredibly shallow, calm shoreline waters where Mary Claire can safely splash her feet. The adjacent promenade is flat, paved, and fully stroller-accessible.

🍽️ Where to eat

Restaurant Freustil

Michelin-starred Nordic dining in Binz that embraces a relaxed, friendly atmosphere. Book an early 6pm seating on the stylish garden terrace, where the quiet ambiance allows baby to rest in her stroller next to your table.

Restaurant Bernstein

Located in Sellin with panoramic Baltic views, this elegant restaurant offers high-end regional seafood. It features stroller-friendly elevator access, spacious table layouts, and welcoming highchairs.

Strandhalle Binz

A beautifully restored historic dining hall serving classic Baltic sea culinary specialties. The spacious, light-filled venue is very accommodating to families, offering highchairs and early dinner options.

Restaurant l'Orangerie

Set within a grand glass conservatory at Roewers Privathotel in Sellin, this fine-dining spot offers a quiet environment ideal for couples traveling with an infant. Highchairs and attentive service are guaranteed.

Bootshaus Binz

Enjoy premium local fish right on the beach. Their spacious, glass-sheltered outdoor terrace is perfect for keeping your stroller secure from wind while you enjoy the sound of the waves.
