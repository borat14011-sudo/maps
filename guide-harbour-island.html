Harbour Island

← back to travel board
Harbour Island offers a blissful, slow-paced sanctuary where the iconic Pink Sands Beach provides a soft, calm playground for your little one. The historic charm of Dunmore Town is easily navigated via the island's signature golf carts, offering a breeze-filled, stress-free escape. For Houston families seeking refined simplicity, the shallow, wave-free turquoise waters of the bay side offer the perfect gentle wading pool for baby Mary Claire. Experience a rare pocket of Caribbean elegance where luxury translates to intimate, unhurried days under the shade of rustling palms.
🚕 Getting there: 20-minute combined transfer: 10-minute private land taxi from North Eleuthera Airport to Gene's Bay Dock, followed by a 10-minute private water taxi to Harbour Island Government Dock, where your hotel golf cart awaits.

What to do

Early morning 8:30-10:30am

Pink Sands Beach

Stroll along three miles of world-famous cool, pale-pink sand. The morning hours offer extensive shade under rental umbrellas, and the gentle shoreline is perfect for Mary Claire's first beach steps.

Morning 9:30-11:00am

Dunmore Town Historic Walk

Explore quiet streets lined with pristine 19th-century loyalist cottages. The paved, low-traffic roads make for an easy, stroller-friendly morning walk past white picket fences and blooming bougainvillea.

Late afternoon 4:00-5:30pm

Valentine's Marina

Walk the wide, flat wooden boardwalks of this upscale marina to view luxury yachts. It is a completely flat, stroller-accessible path with a refreshing harbor breeze and spots to shade under large canvas umbrellas.

Low tide scheduled morning 9:00-10:00am

Lone Tree

Visit this iconic piece of driftwood standing upright on the harbor flats. Go during low tide when the surrounding waters are ankle-deep, calm, and perfectly warm for baby Mary Claire to wade safely.

Mid-day 11:30am-1:00pm

Girls Bank

A highly unique shallow sandbar on the harbor side of the island. The water here is completely wave-less and shallow for hundreds of yards, acting as a massive, natural warm bath for stress-free infant splashing.

Midday 1:30-2:30pm

Princess Street Gallery

Escape the midday heat inside this quiet, air-conditioned gallery featuring local Bahamian artwork. The spacious layout accommodates a stroller easily, offering a calm, visually stimulating sensory break for baby.

Late afternoon 5:00-5:45pm

Government Dock

Watch the local water taxis and supply boats arrive from Eleuthera. The wide open concrete pier offers a breezy, flat walk where parents can push the stroller and watch the gentle harbor activity.

Morning 10:30-11:15am

Dunmore Toy & Gift

A charming, air-conditioned local boutique filled with unique children's books and soft toys. It is a quiet, baby-friendly spot to pick up a keepsake book about Bahamian sea life for Mary Claire.

Lunchtime 12:00-1:00pm

Queen's Conch

Relax at this famous, colorful harbor-side shack. While parents watch fresh conch salad being prepared, baby can enjoy the cool ocean breezes from the safety of a shaded, open-air wooden deck.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Landing Restaurant

Savor historic elegance and fresh seafood in a quiet, romantic garden courtyard. Their early dinner seating at 6:00 PM is highly accommodating to families, offering highchairs and a peaceful, low-noise environment.

Sip Sip

Enjoy chic beachfront dining famous for its lobster quesadillas. The outdoor terrace offers sweeping ocean views, steady cooling breezes, and a welcoming, casual atmosphere that makes dining with an infant feel effortless.

Rock House Restaurant

An upscale British colonial-style restaurant overlooking the bay. Request a quiet table on the outdoor terrace during early seating to enjoy fine dining while Mary Claire relaxes in the gentle evening breeze.

Dunmore Restaurant

Experience refined luxury on a breezy, open-air dining pavilion. The spacious outdoor seating, spectacular ocean vistas, and attentive, high-end staff make this a comfortable and sophisticated choice for an early family dinner.

Cocoa Coffee House

The island's premier breakfast spot overlooking the harbor. It offers highchairs, air-conditioned indoor dining, and an easygoing morning crowd perfect for a relaxed breakfast with baby Mary Claire.
