What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM / 1.5h

Les Planches (Deauville Boardwalk)

Stroll along this iconic wooden boardwalk lined with private cabins named after Hollywood stars. The wide, flat terrain is perfect for strollers, and the morning ocean breeze provides a refreshing atmosphere for Mary Claire's first nap.

Late Morning 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM / 1.5h

Villa Strassburger

Visit this magnificent 1907 Belle Époque mansion surrounded by manicured lawns. The expansive shaded gardens are highly accessible and offer a quiet, picturesque setting ideal for infant tummy time under the apple trees.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM / 2h

Plage de Trouville

Spend a relaxing afternoon on the wide, soft sands of Trouville's beach. This spot features a paved walkway that is completely stroller-friendly, allowing you to enjoy the coastal views without dragging wheels through the sand.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 1.5h

Hippodrome de la Touques

Watch elegant horses train on the flat grass tracks of Deauville's historic racecourse. The peaceful morning workouts offer a quiet, uncrowded spectacle with spacious lawn seating that easily accommodates a stroller.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM / 2h

Parc des Graves (Villerville)

Explore this beautiful clifftop park overlooking the sea, featuring paved, stroller-accessible walking paths. It offers a peaceful retreat with plenty of shade trees and benches where Kathryn can comfortably feed Mary Claire while enjoying the ocean view.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM / 1.5h

Marché de Deauville

Wander through the colorful open-air market housed in a classic half-timbered building. The wide, outdoor aisles make it easy to navigate with a compact stroller while shopping for fresh Normandy cheeses and fruits.

Afternoon 1:30 PM - 3:00 PM / 1.5h

Les Franciscaines

Discover this beautifully restored cultural center featuring a library and museum. Highly stroller-accessible, quiet, and fully air-conditioned, it serves as an excellent indoor sanctuary for a peaceful afternoon escape with an infant.

Afternoon 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM / 1.5h

Jardin de l'Étoile du Berger (Honfleur)

Relax in this quiet public garden near the historic Honfleur harbor. The calm, shaded pathways and gentle botanical surroundings provide a serene environment for Mary Claire to rest undisturbed in her stroller.

Evening 5:30 PM - 6:30 PM / 1h

La Touques River Walk

Take a gentle evening stroll along the paved promenade flanking the Touques River. The path is flat, quiet, and perfectly stroller-accessible, providing scenic views of passing boats as the sun begins to set.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Belle Époque (Hôtel Barrière Le Normandy)

Savor local Sole Meunière in an opulent dining room. The restaurant offers highchairs, spacious table layouts that easily accommodate a stroller, and an early 7:00 PM dinner seating perfect for an infant's bedtime routine.

Le Central (Trouville)

Enjoy fresh Normandy oysters at this upscale, lively brasserie. The bustling, friendly atmosphere easily masks any infant babble, and the heated outdoor terrace is ideal for parking a sleeping stroller.

L'Ancre (Deauville)

Indulge in slow-cooked Normandy beef cheek at this refined local bistro. The staff is exceptionally welcoming to families, and the quiet outdoor patio offers a peaceful setting for an early lunch.

Le Spinnaker (Deauville)

Experience roasted turbot with local cider sauce at this sophisticated gourmet restaurant. It features early evening service and quiet, spaced-out tables that allow parents to dine comfortably with a sleeping infant in a carrier.

La Moulerie (Trouville)

Feast on fresh mussels in Camembert sauce at this casual-chic seafood spot. The service is fast and friendly, and the spacious outdoor seating area is highly accommodating for families with strollers.
