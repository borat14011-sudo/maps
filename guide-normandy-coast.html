The historic playground of Deauville combines Parisian sophistication with a pristine coastal escape, perfect for Will, Kathryn, and baby Mary Claire. The iconic Les Planches boardwalk offers a completely stroller-ready, flat promenade right along the wide, sandy beach. Elegant half-timbered architecture frames calm shallow waters that are ideal for dipping tiny toes during low tide. From exclusive beach club amenities to quiet historic estates, this Normandy gem makes luxury travel with an infant feel entirely seamless.
🚕 Getting there: Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) to Deauville is a 2.5-hour drive; the best luxury family transport is a pre-arranged private Mercedes V-Class transfer equipped with a rear-facing infant car seat and ample trunk space for your stroller.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM / 1.5h

Les Planches de Deauville

Stroll along this iconic 1923 wooden boardwalk featuring beach cabins named after famous Hollywood stars. The wide, flat hardwood surface is exceptionally stroller-friendly, allowing Mary Claire to nap to the soothing sound of the nearby waves.

Late Morning 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM / 2h

Plage de Deauville

This expansive sandy beach is famous for its colorful sun umbrellas and calm, shallow waters at low tide. Rent a private beach cabin for shade and enjoy a relaxing morning dip with the baby away from heavy crowds.

Early Afternoon 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM / 1.5h

Villa Strassburger

Explore the lush, manicured lawns of this stunning 1907 regional manor house. The quiet outdoor gardens offer a peaceful, shaded environment for Kathryn and Will to stroll while Mary Claire enjoys the fresh coastal air.

Afternoon 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM / 1.5h

Les Franciscaines Deauville

This ultra-modern cultural center features a beautifully designed, stroller-accessible library and quiet museum spaces. It is the perfect indoor retreat for a peaceful afternoon walk when the midday sun is too strong.

Early Morning 8:30 AM - 10:00 AM / 1.5h

Marché de Deauville

Experience a vibrant, open-air market located under half-timbered halls filled with local cheeses, fresh cider, and flowers. The lively but gentle atmosphere offers a wonderful sensory experience for an infant during the quiet early morning hours.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 2h

Hippodrome de Deauville-La Touques

Visit this legendary racecourse during morning training sessions to watch the horses run. The wide, grassy lawns are incredibly peaceful, offering a spacious and unique environment to push the stroller.

Afternoon 2:30 PM - 4:30 PM / 2h

Parc des Graves

Located in nearby Villerville, this scenic cliffside park offers stunning panoramic sea views and fully paved, stroller-friendly paths. Grassy picnic lawns are ideal for a relaxing family afternoon under the shade of mature trees.

Late Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 2h

Plage de Trouville

Take a short walk across the Touques river to Deauville's sister town to enjoy a gentler, highly family-oriented beach. The boardwalk here is lined with beautiful nineteenth-century villas and offers a quieter seaside alternative.

Afternoon 4:00 PM - 4:45 PM / 45m

Le Petit Train de Deauville

Take a gentle, slow-paced ride on the open-air tourist train that loops through the town's most scenic historic streets. It provides a breezy, low-effort sightseeing option that keeps baby Mary Claire thoroughly entertained.

🍽️ Where to eat

Le Ciro's

This legendary seaside institution serves exceptional wild turbot and fresh lobster on a spacious, stroller-friendly outdoor terrace. Highchairs are readily available, and the sound of crashing waves easily masks any baby babble.

La Belle Époque

Located inside Le Normandy hotel, this upscale restaurant serves exquisite classic French cuisine in a stunning glass atrium. The accommodating staff gladly welcomes families with highchairs, early dinner bookings, and plenty of space for strollers.

Le Central

An iconic, bustling brasserie in Trouville-sur-Mer that serves incredible seafood platters and local oysters. The vibrant atmosphere is perfect for families, as the natural ambient noise easily absorbs any infant fussiness.

Le Jardin

This charming garden-terrace restaurant offers a relaxed outdoor dining experience featuring fresh, seasonal Normandy ingredients. The open-air setting is perfect for Will and Kathryn to enjoy a leisurely lunch while Mary Claire plays on the grass.

Les Vapeurs

A highly historic and lively brasserie in Trouville-sur-Mer famous for its pots of fresh mussels and frites. The early dining service and highly welcoming staff make it an easy and stress-free dinner spot for parents traveling with a baby.
