Where Belle Époque elegance meets the wild beauty of the English Channel, Deauville offers wide sandy beaches and horse-drawn charm. It is a seaside playground where toddlers can safely splash in shallow tides while parents soak in the classic coastal French atmosphere. Perfect for slow-paced family strolls, fresh seafood markets, and clean maritime air.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min taxi or rental car from Deauville-Normandie Airport (DOL) to the central beach hotels

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Les Planches Boardwalk

Stroll down the iconic 1920s wooden boardwalk lined with beach cabins named after famous Hollywood stars. It is completely flat and stroller-friendly, giving toddlers plenty of smooth space to run.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Grande Plage de Deauville

Spend a sunny afternoon on this massive sandy beach famous for its bright, multi-colored tied umbrellas. The gentle slope of the shore makes it incredibly safe for little ones to dip their toes in the water.

Morning / 1-2h

Marché de Deauville

Explore this lively, half-timbered market filled with local cheeses, fresh berries, and warm pastries. Toddlers will love snacking on fresh Normandy apple juice and sweet crepes as you explore the stalls.

Afternoon / 1h

Le Carrousel de Deauville

Take a spin on this beautiful, vintage-style carousel located right on the beachfront. Toddlers will be enchanted by the wooden horses, carriages, and nostalgic music.

Late Afternoon / 2h

Parc des Graves

Head to this clifftop park in neighboring Villerville for sweeping sea views and lush green lawns. It features a great playground and farm animals like goats that toddlers can watch graze.

Morning / 1-2h

Villa Strassburger

Visit this stunning, fairytale-like Norman mansion surrounded by apple orchards and manicured lawns. The expansive gardens are perfect for a peaceful family picnic and toddler-friendly exploration.

Afternoon / 2h

Hippodrome de la Touques

Watch majestic racehorses train or compete at this historic, family-friendly racecourse. Toddlers will be thrilled by the close-up views of the horses and the spacious lawns to stretch their legs.

Morning / 1-2h

Trouville-sur-Mer Harbor Walk

Cross the small bridge to Deauville's sister town to watch colorful fishing boats pull into the harbor. It is a fantastic, sensory-rich walk where toddlers can watch seagulls and busy fishermen.

Morning / 1-2h

Parc Calouste Gulbenkian

Escape the coastal breeze in this beautifully forested park located on the heights of Deauville. It offers a wonderfully quiet environment for a stroller-friendly nature walk among rare trees and flowers.

🍽️ Where to eat

Chez Miocque

Order the legendary roast chicken with crispy golden frites, a guaranteed hit for both adults and toddlers.

Le Central

Get the fresh local sole meunière for yourself, and a simple, high-quality buttered pasta plate for the little one.

La Flambée

Treat the family to their authentic wood-fired pizzas followed by a slice of classic Norman apple tart.

Morny's Café

Enjoy a casual lunch on the heated terrace and order a classic, gooey Croque Monsieur.

Dupont avec un Thé

Indulge in a warm, chocolate-filled crepe and their famous hot chocolate for a cozy afternoon treat.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack a sturdy stroller with good suspension, as navigating the historic cobblestones around the market square and the sandy boardwalk can be bumpy. Always check the local tide schedule before heading to the beach, as low tide reveals massive expanses of wet sand perfect for toddlers to safely splash and search for shells.
