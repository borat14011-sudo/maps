What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 2h

Jardin du Luxembourg

This manicured palace garden offers wide, stroller-friendly gravel paths and deep shade under chestnut trees, making it perfect for a relaxed morning stroll with Mary Claire while enjoying the iconic green chairs.

Late Morning 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM / 1.5h

Jardin des Tuileries

Stretch your legs along the flat, paved central promenade of this historic royal garden, offering a quiet, sensory-rich environment with beautiful fountains and shaded groves ideal for an infant nap.

Early Afternoon 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM / 1h

Bateaux Parisiens Private Boat Cruise

Enjoy a private or small-group glass-enclosed cruise along the Seine River, allowing you to view the Eiffel Tower and Notre-Dame in absolute comfort, shielded from the wind and city noise.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM / 1.5h

Place des Vosges

Located in the historic Marais, this perfectly symmetrical square features lush grassy lawns protected by vaulted stone arcades, offering an ideal, quiet spot to spread a blanket for baby sensory play.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:30 PM / 2.5h

Jardin d'Acclimatation

Situated in the Bois de Boulogne, this refined, upscale children's park features beautifully paved walkways, shaded canopy areas, and gentle, quiet farm animals that will delight an older infant.

Early Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM / 1.5h

Musée de l'Orangerie

This intimate museum is fully stroller-accessible via smooth ramps and features Claude Monet's massive, calming Water Lilies murals, providing a serene, visually soothing environment for the whole family.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM / 1.5h

Parc Monceau

Frequented by upscale local families, this quiet park features paved stroller paths, classical columns, and abundant shade trees, offering a peaceful sanctuary far from the bustling tourist hubs.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 1.5h

Atelier des Lumières

Experience a stunning, immersive digital art display where quiet classical music and moving lights create a captivating sensory experience that can be comfortably enjoyed with Mary Claire in a baby carrier.

Late Afternoon 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM / 1h

Galeries Lafayette Rooftop Terrace

Take the spacious elevators to the top of this historic department store for a breath of fresh air and magnificent panoramic views of Paris, providing plenty of open space for a stroller.
