From watching puppet shows under chestnut trees to sailing toy boats in historic fountains, the city embraces children with open arms. Let the aroma of fresh pastries guide your slow-paced, magical adventure through these historic neighborhoods.
🚕 Getting there: ~45 min taxi or private transfer from Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) directly to central Paris hotels, avoiding the hassle of navigating metro stairs with luggage.

What to do

Morning / 2h

Eiffel Tower & Champ de Mars

Ascend this iconic iron giant for breathtaking views, then let little ones run free on the grassy lawns below. Toddlers will love riding the vintage wooden carousel at the base of the tower.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Jardin du Luxembourg

Push vintage wooden sailboats with poles across the grand octagonal pond and watch a classic puppet show. The gated Ludo Jardin playground is perfect for toddlers to safely climb and slide.

Morning / 2h

Grande Galerie de l'Évolution

Walk among hundreds of lifelike taxidermy animals marching through a spectacular, sunlit 19th-century hall. It is stroller-friendly and features a dedicated interactive gallery perfect for sensory exploration.

Full Day / 4-5h

Jardin d'Acclimatation

An enchanting amusement park tucked inside the Bois de Boulogne, featuring vintage rides, a splash pad, and a free-roaming menagerie. Toddlers will delight in the gentle river boat ride and puppet theater.

Morning / 2h

Cité des Enfants at Cité des Sciences

An interactive science museum with a brilliantly designed zone specifically for kids aged 2 to 7. Toddlers can experiment with water play, build soft structures, and explore sensory mazes.

Morning / 1-2h

Marché d'Aligre

Immerse your senses in this bustling, vibrant outdoor market filled with colorful fruit stalls and fragrant cheese vendors. Stroller-bound toddlers will be mesmerized by the lively sights and tastes of fresh berries.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Seine River Cruise with Bateaux Parisiens

Rest tired little legs on a gentle, covered glass-topped boat cruise past Notre Dame and the Louvre. It's a low-stress way for toddlers to see the sights without the walking.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Atelier des Lumières

Step inside a massive, converted foundry where vibrant art projections bring classic masterpieces to life across the walls and floors. Toddlers will be spellbound chasing the moving light patterns.

Afternoon / 2h

Ménagerie du Jardin des Plantes

Stroll through manicured botanical gardens to one of the world's oldest zoos, home to red pandas, orangutans, and flamingos. The compact size of the zoo is incredibly manageable for toddler steps.

🍽️ Where to eat

Breizh Café Le Marais

Order the classic buckwheat galette with ham, egg, and cheese, followed by a sweet apple caramel crepe that toddlers will love.

Angelina Rue de Rivoli

Indulge in their legendary, ultra-thick hot chocolate and a classic Mont-Blanc pastry in a beautiful Belle Epoque setting.

Le Train Bleu

Dine on traditional French roast lamb inside a spectacular, museum-like train station dining room that will keep kids captivated by its painted ceilings.

Bouillon Pigalle

Get the incredibly affordable French classics like steak frites and profiteroles in a lively, bustling, and highly kid-welcoming atmosphere.

L'As du Fallafel

Grab a legendary, messy, and delicious falafel pita sandwich to-go for an easy, casual park picnic that kids love.
