What to do

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Jardin du Luxembourg

Relax by the Grand Bassin while Mary Claire watches the iconic wooden sailboats glide across the water. The park features smooth, wide gravel paths perfect for a Bugaboo stroller, plus plenty of shaded chestnut groves for feeding.

Morning 10:00-11:30am / 1.5h

Musée de l'Orangerie

Marvel at Monet's Water Lilies in a serene, intimate setting that is significantly quieter and easier to navigate with a stroller than the Louvre. The elevator access is seamless, and the quiet atmosphere is perfect for a morning contact nap.

Afternoon 2:00-3:30pm / 1.5h

Jardin des Tuileries

Stroll along the grand central promenade before finding a quiet green metal chair near the fountains to rest. The wide paths are highly stroller-accessible, and the nearby cafes offer quick access to clean facilities.

Late Afternoon 4:00-6:00pm / 2h

Seine River Private Cruise

Charter a private, luxury vintage mahogany boat for a smooth ride down the Seine, completely bypassing crowded tour boats. Mary Claire can nap comfortably in the covered, climate-controlled cabin while you enjoy Champagne.

Morning 10:00am-12:00pm / 2h

Place des Vosges

Explore Paris's oldest planned square in the Marais, featuring flat grassy lawns and shaded arcades that provide excellent sun protection. It is a peaceful oasis where Mary Claire can enjoy some tummy time on a luxury picnic blanket.

Morning 9:00-11:00am / 2h

Musée d'Orsay

View breathtaking Impressionist masterpieces using the museum's ramp and elevator system designed for stroller accessibility. Booking a private, skip-the-line family tour keeps the experience brief and highly engaging for parents without overtiring the baby.

Afternoon 3:00-4:30pm / 1.5h

Parc Monceau

Discover this upscale, quiet park in the 8th arrondissement, a favorite among wealthy local families for its paved stroller paths and beautiful Renaissance colonnade. It offers a pristine, crowd-free environment perfect for a relaxed afternoon stroll.

Morning 10:00am-1:00pm / 3h

Jardin d'Acclimatation

Visit this historic children's park in the Bois de Boulogne which offers beautifully landscaped walking paths, a gentle farm animal encounter, and lush shade. The modern facilities include spacious, immaculate baby-changing stations.

Afternoon 2:00-4:00pm / 2h

Fondation Louis Vuitton

Admire the striking Frank Gehry-designed architecture and contemporary art via wide, ultra-modern elevators that easily accommodate luxury strollers. The surrounding parklands provide a peaceful, open-air escape immediately after your indoor gallery viewing.

🍽️ Where to eat

Girafe Paris

Savor exquisite seafood like the wild sea bass while enjoying an iconic, unobstructed view of the Eiffel Tower from the spacious outdoor terrace. The outdoor seating provides natural ambient noise and plenty of space to park a premium stroller.

Carette

Indulge in world-class hot chocolate and delicate macarons on the sheltered arcade terrace, perfect for a chic lunch. The outdoor seating is highly accommodating for families, offering a lively yet refined atmosphere where baby noises blend in naturally.

Benoit Paris

Experience classic Michelin-starred bistro dining by Alain Ducasse with a signature dish of snail-stuffed puff pastry. The warm, bustling atmosphere easily absorbs infant sounds, and the staff are famously welcoming to families during early lunch seatings.

L'Avenue

Enjoy upscale people-watching and modern French-Asian cuisine on the fashionable terrace of Avenue Montaigne. Request a corner table on the terrace to easily park your stroller, allowing Mary Claire to nap while you dine among the fashion elite.

Le Train Bleu

Dine beneath spectacular gilded ceilings on classic roasted leg of lamb in a magnificent, historically grand setting. The high energy and spacious layout of this legendary station-side institution make it surprisingly welcoming for families with infants.
