What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM / 1.5h

Castello Scaligero

A 13th-century lakeside fortress standing guard at the peninsula entrance. Skip the steep tower steps with Mary Claire and enjoy the flat, shaded inner courtyard while watching the swans swim in the moat.

Morning 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM / 2h

Grotte di Catullo

Grand Roman villa ruins surrounded by ancient olive trees at the tip of the peninsula. The wide gravel pathways are highly stroller-accessible, and the electric tourist train from the town center saves tired legs.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM / 2h

Spiaggia delle Bionde

A pristine beach with grassy areas, sun lounger rentals, and exceptionally calm, shallow waters. Perfect for dipping Mary Claire's toes in the lake, featuring a paved access path suitable for premium strollers.

Midday 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM / 1h

Parco Maria Callas

A tranquil, lush park dedicated to the opera diva, filled with giant cypress trees and vibrant blooms. It offers cool, deep shade for Mary Claire's afternoon nap in the stroller away from the bustling tourist crowds.

Evening 5:30 PM - 6:30 PM / 1h

Passeggiata Panoramica

A stunning, paved pedestrian pathway hugging the eastern lakeside of the Sirmione peninsula. It is entirely flat and stroller-friendly, offering sweeping views of the azure waters and gentle lake breezes.

Morning 9:30 AM - 1:00 PM / 3.5h

Isola del Garda

A breathtaking private island hosting a Venetian neo-Gothic villa and lush botanical gardens. Book a private speed-boat tour with baby-safe life vests to wander the shaded exotic pathways.

Morning 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM / 3h

Parco Natura Viva

A world-class safari and zoological park located a short drive away in Pastrengo. Families can drive their air-conditioned vehicle through the safari zone to keep Mary Claire comfortable while spotting giraffes and lions.

Morning 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM / 4h

Gardaland (Peppa Pig Land)

A nearby world-class theme park featuring a dedicated, whimsical area designed specifically for infants and toddlers. It offers gentle, slow-moving rides, plenty of nursing rooms, and fully paved, stroller-friendly pathways.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM / 3h

Punta San Vigilio

A magical, historic peninsula on the eastern shore featuring towering cypress trees and a Renaissance villa. Stroll the quiet harbor paths and enjoy a peaceful, shaded lakeside picnic under the olive grove with the baby.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ristorante La Speranzina

Elegant Michelin-listed establishment offering creative Mediterranean cuisine on an intimate lakefront deck. Book an early 7:00 PM seating to enjoy the turbot while baby rests peacefully in the stroller beside the table.

Ristorante Silk

Located inside the Grand Hotel Terme, serving refined local dishes in a spacious, climate-controlled dining room. Their staff is exceptionally accommodating to infants, offering premium highchairs and preparing simple vegetable purees on request.

Trattoria Clementina

A beloved local Osteria known for authentic handmade tortellini and a warm, welcoming Italian family atmosphere. The early dinner seating is highly welcoming to babies, featuring robust highchairs and a quiet, relaxed ambiance.

Ristorante Al Pescatore

A historic seafood institution featuring a spacious, breezy outdoor terrace overlooking the cobblestone streets. Perfect for enjoying local lake trout while the ambient street chatter gently lulls Mary Claire to sleep.

Ristorante l'Incontro

A refined yet casual dining spot offering exceptional homemade pasta and grilled meats in a secluded courtyard. The spacious outdoor seating easily accommodates a large luxury stroller without disrupting nearby diners.
