Sayulita

← back to travel board
Sayulita blends vibrant surf culture with serene jungle-lined beaches, perfect for a young family seeking laid-back luxury. The town's colorful cobblestone streets lead to Playa Sayulita's gentle waves, ideal for infant paddling. Mary Claire can nap in a shaded cabana while you sip fresh coconut water at the beachfront. For a quiet escape, the nearby Playa de los Muertos offers calm turquoise waters and stroller-friendly sand.
🚕 Getting there: Puerto Vallarta International Airport (PVR) to Sayulita is a 45-minute private transfer with Vallarta Transfers or Gray Line, offering infant car seats and stroller storage. The drive winds through lush hills and coastal vistas, ensuring a smooth arrival.

What to do

Morning 9-11am

Playa Sayulita

Main beach with gentle waves, ideal for infant paddling; stroller-friendly boardwalk, shaded palapas for nursing.

Late afternoon 4-6pm

Playa de los Muertos

Quiet, calm cove south of town; shallow water perfect for baby splash, limited crowds, easy beach access.

Evening stroll 6-7pm

Sayulita Plaza Principal

Central square with colorful church, benches under trees, stroller-friendly paths; local vendors and mariachi serenades.

Sunset 5:30-6:30pm

El Mirador de Sayulita

Scenic viewpoint overlooking the bay, accessible via a short uphill walk (carry stroller), breathtaking sunset views.

Saturdays 9am-12pm

Sayulita Farmers Market (Mercado del Mar)

Weekly market with organic fruits, baby-friendly snacks, artisan goods; stroller-friendly aisles, morning shade.

Late afternoon (check schedule)

Turtle Release Program (Campamento Tortuguero)

Seasonal release of baby sea turtles on nearby beaches; infant-safe activity, gentle handling, educational.

Morning 8-9am

Jungle Yoga at Haramara Retreat

Outdoor yoga studio in the jungle, offer family sessions with infant mats; serene atmosphere, wildlife sounds.

2-hour session early morning

Sayulita Surf Club (surf lessons)

Private surf lessons for adults while baby stays with a sitter on the beach; calm beginner waves.

Half-day 9am-1pm

Boca de Tomatlán (day trip)

Secluded beach village 15 min south, river meets ocean; shallow lagoon for baby splashing, beachfront dining.
