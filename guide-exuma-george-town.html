Exuma (George Town)

← back to travel board
Exuma offers a slow-paced, crowd-free alternative to Nassau, defined by endless miles of knee-deep, neon-blue water that acts as a natural wading pool for toddlers. George Town's laid-back harbor vibe means your family can transition seamlessly from boat charters to open-air dining without any formal dress codes. It is a haven for a dad looking to unplug, where the lack of high-rises guarantees quiet, uninterrupted bonding time with your little girl.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 to 20 minute taxi ride from Exuma International Airport (GGT) to George Town; renting a SUV at the airport is highly recommended for transporting strollers and beach gear.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Coco Plum Beach

Famous for its expansive, super-shallow sandbar at low tide where your toddler can safely splash for hundreds of yards. It's stroller-accessible right up to the high-tide line, and you can easily search for giant sand dollars together.

Mid-day / 3-4h

Chat 'N' Chill

This iconic Stocking Island beach hangout features wild but gentle stingrays that swim right up to the shoreline. The soft beach sand is perfect for a pop-up baby tent while you enjoy a fresh conch salad.

Morning / 1-2h

Hoopers Bay

A calm, wind-protected bay where resident green sea turtles swim in the shallow, crystal-clear water. Use a baby carrier for the brief walk down the access path, then wade with your toddler to see the turtles surface.

Afternoon / 2h

Jolly Hall Beach

A quiet, crescent-shaped beach lined with shady Casuarina trees that offer natural sun protection for an infant. The water here is incredibly still, making it feel like a giant, warm baby bathtub.

Morning / 2h

Exuma Point Beach (Rolleville)

A highly accessible alternative to the deep-water swimming pigs tours, where the pigs roam right on the shallow shoreline. It is much safer and less overwhelming for a toddler than jumping off a boat in deep water.

Morning / 2h

Tropic of Cancer Beach

Also known as Pelican Beach, this is a stunning crescent of powdery white sand on Little Exuma with brilliant turquoise water. Bring a beach umbrella for the baby, as there is very little natural shade on this quiet stretch.

Morning / 3-4h

Moriah Harbour Cay National Park

An pristine marine park featuring gorgeous sand dunes and ultra-shallow channels that feel like a lazy river. Charter a private boat to drop your family on a private sandbar for a quiet afternoon wading in the shallows.

Morning / 1h

George Town Straw Market

A small, colorful open-air market in the heart of George Town where local artisans weave traditional straw goods. It's a flat, paved, stroller-friendly spot to buy a personalized, custom-sized sun hat for your toddler.

Afternoon / 2h

Forbes Hill Beach

A secluded beach on Little Exuma featuring unique rock formations that create calm, shallow pools perfect for baby splashing. It is rarely crowded, offering a peaceful and private family environment.

🍽️ Where to eat

Chat 'N' Chill

Order the signature, freshly made conch salad and a cold Kalik. The open-air, sandy beach footprint means your toddler can play in the sand right at your feet while you eat.

Shirley's Fish Fry

Order the famous coconut shrimp or local snapper. Located in the casual Fish Fry area, the vibe is open-air and loud enough that nobody will notice a fussy baby.

Splash Beach Bar & Grill

Get the classic Bahamian conch fritters and a burger. Located at Hideaways resort, it sits right on the beach with high chairs available and an easy, family-friendly poolside atmosphere.

The Rusty Anchor

Order the grilled mahi-mahi with pea and rice. Located at February Point, this spot offers a more upscale but very accommodating setting with a breezy patio and quiet indoor dining.

Santanna's Bar and Grill

Order the cracked lobster or deep-fried snapper. It features a simple wooden deck over the ocean with casual bench seating, making it easy to manage a toddler during an early lunch.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Grand Isle Resort & Residences

Located on Emerald Bay, this resort offers spacious 1- to 4-bedroom villas equipped with full high-end kitchens for baby food prep, an expansive zero-entry pool, and complimentary cribs.

Hideaways Exuma

Offers comfortable beachfront cottages and villas with kitchenettes, situated right on the calm waters of Palm Bay. It features a kid-friendly pool, complimentary water sports, and direct access to Jolly Hall beach.

Peace & Plenty Resort

A historic, charming hotel in the center of George Town with a quiet harbor-side pool. It offers a convenient boat shuttle to their private beach club on Stocking Island, combining town convenience with easy beach access.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack more diapers, swim pants, and baby-safe sunscreen than you think you need, as the local markets in George Town have limited stock and high import prices. Rent a car with high clearance to easily navigate the bumpy dirt access roads to secluded beaches without waking a sleeping toddler. Always check the tide charts daily and time your beach outings with the low tide to maximize the safety and size of the shallow sandbars.
