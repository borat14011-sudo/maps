What to do

Late morning 11am-2pm / 3h

Chat 'N' Chill

This iconic Stocking Island beach hangout offers soft sands and clear, shallow waters where baby Mary Claire can splash safely. Parents can enjoy fresh conch salad while relaxing under shaded beach palapas, making it a perfect low-stress day trip via a quick water taxi.

Morning 9am-12pm / 3h

Coco Plum Beach

Famous for its expansive sandbar visible at low tide, this beach offers miles of knee-deep, crystal-clear water ideal for wading with an infant. The calm conditions mean no crashing waves to startle Mary Claire, though you should bring a pop-up tent as natural shade is limited.

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

Hoopers Bay

A tranquil, breeze-kissed bay known for its resident green sea turtles that swim close to the shallow shoreline. The calm, warm waters are incredibly peaceful, and the overhanging palm trees provide excellent natural shade for a sleepy baby in a carrier.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Tropic of Cancer Beach

Also known as Pelican Beach, this world-famous stretch features powdery white sand and breathtaking crescent views. While there are no paved boardwalks for strollers, a baby carrier is perfect for walking along the shoreline of this exceptionally quiet and clean beach.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Jolly Hall Beach

A highly shaded, scenic beach located just minutes from George Town, featuring a natural grove of casuarina trees. The water is exceptionally calm and shallow, resembling a massive saltwater swimming pool that is safe and inviting for young infants.

Morning 9am-1pm / 4h

Moriah Harbour Cay National Park

An exquisite marine park consisting of pristine sand dunes and shallow mangrove channels. Booking a private, shaded pontoon boat charter allows the family to glide through the quiet, glass-like waters while keeping Mary Claire protected from the midday sun.

Late afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Elizabeth Harbour Boat Tour

A gentle, private boat tour around the harbor offers a relaxing way to sightsee without long walks. The soothing motion of the boat often lulls babies to sleep, while parents can enjoy the spectacular views of the outer cays in complete privacy.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Compass Cay Marina

A scenic marina where gentle, docile nurse sharks swim in the shallow docks. While Mary Claire is too young to swim, she will be fascinated by the gentle movement of the sharks from the safety of her parents' arms on the sturdy wooden docks.

Morning 9-10:30am / 1.5h

George Town Straw Market

A colorful, open-air market located in the heart of George Town, offering handcrafted Bahamian souvenirs. The flat, paved terrain around the market stalls makes it easily accessible for a luxury stroller during a quiet morning walk.

🍽️ Where to eat

Palapa Grill

Located at Grand Isle Resort, this upscale poolside restaurant offers gourmet Caribbean fusion cuisine with spacious outdoor seating. They gladly provide sturdy highchairs, and the gentle poolside breeze creates a soothing atmosphere for an early family dinner.

The Club at Peace & Plenty Restaurant

This historic indoor restaurant offers an air-conditioned dining room overlooking Elizabeth Harbour, perfect for escaping the afternoon heat. The staff is exceptionally welcoming to families, offering early dinner seatings and accommodating baby food preparation requests.

Rusty Anchor

An elegant dining venue featuring fresh catch-of-the-day and fine steaks in a relaxed, spacious setting. The wide layout easily accommodates luxury strollers, and the quiet ambient noise levels ensure a peaceful environment for Mary Claire.

Shirley's Seafood Restaurant

A renowned local institution offering authentic Bahamian seafood on a breezy outdoor deck. The casual, welcoming atmosphere means parents do not have to worry about occasional baby noise, and the early dinner service is highly convenient.

Chat 'N' Chill Sunday BBQ

A vibrant outdoor beach pig roast featuring delicious grilled options in a very casual, sandy-floor setting. Parents can dine at picnic tables under the shade of coconut palms while Mary Claire relaxes on a beach blanket next to them.
