Exuma (George Town)

← back to travel board
Escape to a tropical playground where shallow, neon-blue waters stretch as far as the eye can see. Exuma offers families a laid-back slice of Bahamian paradise, where days are measured in seashell discoveries and gentle boat rides. It is the ultimate destination for parents seeking raw natural beauty without the crowded resort rush.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min taxi or rental car drive from Exuma International Airport (GGT) to George Town hotels

What to do

Morning / 4h

Pig Beach (Major Cay)

Cruise out to meet the world-famous swimming pigs who love paddling out to greet visitors. Toddlers will love watching them from the safety of the boat or shallow shoreline.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Chat 'N' Chill (Stocking Island)

Take a quick water taxi to Stocking Island to feed wild stingrays and play in the shallow, calm cove. The sandy floors and shady trees are perfect for toddlers to nap under.

Low tide / 2-3h

Coco Plum Beach

Renowned for its incredibly shallow water at low tide, this beach allows kids to walk out for yards to search for sand dollars. It acts like a giant, warm wading pool for toddlers.

Morning / 2-3h

Moriah Harbour Cay National Park

Explore pristine mangroves and shallow sandbanks teeming with baby lemon sharks and turtles. Rent a double kayak so your toddler can safely sit in your lap while you paddle.

Morning / 1-2h

George Town Straw Market

Browse colorful stalls in the heart of George Town for handmade straw hats, bags, and wooden toys. It is a vibrant, stroller-friendly spot to pick up a cute souvenir for the little ones.

Morning / 2-3h

Tropic of Cancer Beach

Visit this breathtakingly beautiful crescent of white sand crossed by the Tropic of Cancer line. The surf is generally calm, making it a serene spot for toddler sandcastle building.

Late Morning / 1-2h

Hoopers Bay

Swim with friendly green sea turtles that naturally congregate near the private piers of this calm bay. Toddlers can easily view them right from the shallow shoreline without needing to snorkel.

Afternoon / 2h

Starfish Beach

A quiet, sheltered beach where giant, colorful starfish rest in the crystal-clear shallows. Toddlers can gently touch the starfishes' bumpy backs without taking them out of the water.

Full Day / 6-8h

Minns Water Sports Pontoon Rental

Rent a safe, sturdy pontoon boat to navigate Elizabeth Harbour at your own family's pace. A shaded pontoon is the most toddler-friendly way to explore private beaches and quiet coves.

🍽️ Where to eat

Shirley's at the Fish Fry

Order the famous coconut cracked conch and peas 'n' rice, which kids love for its sweet, crispy texture.

Santanna's Bar and Grill

Feast on fresh-caught pan-fried snapper and lobster tail served right on the ocean's edge.

The Rusty Anchor

Enjoy a family-friendly upscale dinner of grilled mahi-mahi in a breezy, air-conditioned dining room overlooking the marina.

Splash Beach Bar & Grill

Dig into tropical flatbreads and kid-friendly burgers while the little ones play on the beach right next to your table.

Blu on the Water

Grab a scenic outdoor table and enjoy sweet coconut shrimp and fresh mango daiquiris while watching the boats pass.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack plenty of reef-safe sunscreen and a pop-up beach tent, as shade can be scarce on Exuma's pristine, undeveloped beaches. We highly recommend renting a car with a pre-booked car seat to navigate the bumpy dirt access roads comfortably with a toddler.
