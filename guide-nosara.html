Nosara

← back to travel board
Nestled on the Nicoya Peninsula, Nosara's Playa Guiones offers a laid-back sanctuary where lush rainforest meets a vast, golden-sand shoreline. Families can spot howler monkeys in the canopy before splashing in safe, tide-pool-protected shallows. It is a car-free-feeling paradise where wellness, surf culture, and raw nature seamlessly blend.
🚕 Getting there: ~2.5 hours via private 4x4 shuttle or rental SUV from Liberia International Airport (LIR)

What to do

Low Tide / 1-2h

Playa Guiones Tide Pools

Explore the massive, natural rock pools that form on the south end of the beach during low tide. These warm, calm waters act as natural shallow bathtubs perfect for toddlers to splash in safely.

Sunset / 2h

Ostional Wildlife Refuge

Witness thousands of olive ridley sea turtles nesting on the volcanic sands of this nearby protected beach. Toddlers will be completely mesmerized by the baby hatchlings crawling toward the ocean on guided evening walks.

Tuesday Morning / 1-2h

Nosara Farmers Market

Browse this colorful Tuesday morning market filled with local organic produce, fresh coconut water, and handmade crafts. It is a highly sensory, shaded experience featuring live music that toddlers will love dancing to.

Morning / 1.5h

Sibu Wildlife Sanctuary

Take a guided educational tour to meet rescued howler monkeys, coatis, and porcupines in a lush jungle setting. The flat, shaded walking loops are easy for tiny legs to navigate without getting overheated.

Late Afternoon / 2h

Playa Pelada Beach Walk

Stroll along this picturesque cove that features plenty of shade-giving trees right on the sand and a natural blowhole at low tide. The gentler, calmer shore break here is much more manageable for toddlers than the main surf beach.

Morning / 2h

Nosara River Safari

Float down the calm, slow-moving Nosara and Montana rivers on a quiet, shaded motorboat to spot herons, iguanas, and crocodiles. It is a peaceful, low-effort wildlife viewing experience ideal for families with young children.

Morning / 1.5h

Playa Guiones Family Surf Lesson

Playa Guiones is world-famous for its gentle, rolling whitewash waves, making it the perfect place for a family surf intro. Toddlers can safely tandem-ride on giant soft-top boards in the shallows with certified, patient instructors.

Morning / 1h

Nosara Civic Association Jungle Trails

Wander along a beautifully maintained network of flat, quiet jungle paths winding right through the town's green zone. It is a perfect shaded sanctuary for a morning walk to spot iguanas and blue morpho butterflies.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Café de Paris Playground

Let the kids burn off some energy at this iconic local hub featuring a shaded outdoor playground, a swimming pool, and French pastries. It is a relaxing spot where parents can enjoy a coffee while watching their toddlers play.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Luna

Grab a beachfront table at sunset and order the fresh wood-fired pizzas while the kids play safely in the sand steps from your table.

Rosie's Soda Tica

Savor a traditional Costa Rican casado with mild fish, black beans, rice, and sweet plantains, which makes for a healthy and toddler-friendly lunch.

Al Chile

Enjoy outstanding fresh fish tacos and tableside guacamole in a relaxed, open-air garden setting that is highly welcoming to energetic children.

Beach Dog Cafe

Try the delicious breakfast burritos and fresh organic smoothies while the toddlers play with the dedicated toy box in the casual dining area.

Destiny Cafe & Fruit Bar

Order colorful pitaya smoothie bowls and loaded avocado toast in a beautiful, shaded garden with plenty of space for toddlers to explore.
