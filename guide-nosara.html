Nosara is Costa Rica’s premier bohemian-luxury sanctuary, where pristine jungle tumbles directly into the warm Pacific. Playa Guiones offers a legendary surf break alongside an upscale wellness community that is surprisingly nurturing for young families. To ensure a seamless trip with baby Mary Claire, the area's lush canopy provides natural cooling, while the stroller-friendly paths of the Nosara Civic Association offer peaceful morning walks. It is the ultimate destination to combine high-end wellness with gentle, slow-paced family bonding.
🚕 Getting there: From Liberia International Airport (LIR), the drive to the Playa Guiones hotel strip takes exactly 2.5 hours; the best luxury family option is a private, air-conditioned Mercedes Sprinter or Toyota Prado SUV booked through Gypsy Cab Nosara, complete with a pre-installed premium rear-facing infant car seat.

What to do

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

Playa Guiones Tide Pools

These natural saltwater wading pools appear at low tide near the south end of Playa Guiones, offering perfectly calm, knee-deep water for baby Mary Claire to splash safely under the shade of overhanging palms.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Nosara Biological Reserve

Located next to Lagarta Lodge, this private reserve offers beautifully maintained, shaded boardwalks winding through mangrove forests, perfect for an ergonomic baby carrier walk to spot gentle howler monkeys.

Mid-morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Boca Nosara Mangrove Boat Tour

A private, gently gliding motorboat tour of the river mouth where the canopy provides full shade for Mary Claire, allowing Will and Kathryn to spot exotic birds and iguanas in comfort.

Late afternoon 4:30-6pm / 1.5h

Playa Pelada Sunset Cove Walk

Playa Pelada features a protective reef that creates calm, gentle waves and soft sand flats ideal for a scenic sunset stroll with Mary Claire in a carrier, finishing with a sunset view.

Morning session 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Sibu Sanctuary

A world-class rescue center for golden-mantled howler monkeys; the small-group private tours are highly educational, quiet, and fully shaded by a dense jungle canopy, making it a peaceful sensory experience for an infant.

Afternoon stroll 3-4pm / 1h

Nosara Civic Association Trail System

A beautifully preserved network of shaded walking trails that connects the residential zones of Playa Guiones, allowing for peaceful afternoon stroller naps under the dense jungle canopy away from dusty roads.

Afternoon 2-3pm / 1h

Sound Bath at Bodhi Tree Yoga Resort

An ultra-relaxing sound healing therapy held in a magnificent open-air wooden shala where parents can take turns attending while the other walks with Mary Claire through the resort’s beautiful paved gardens.

Midday 12-1pm / 1h

Organico Deli Market Shopping

A high-end, air-conditioned boutique grocer stocking premium organic snacks and European baby supplies, providing a cool, sensory-friendly escape from the midday heat.

Tica Massage at Harmony Hotel Spa

Indulge in a signature side-by-side or individual open-air garden pavilion massage using locally sourced organic coconut oil while your partner watches the baby nearby in the hotel’s lush, quiet grounds.
