Isla Mujeres

← back to travel board
Isla Mujeres welcomes families with its incredibly gentle Caribbean charm and warm, shallow waters. Your days here center around the iconic Playa Norte, a world-famous stretch of soft white sand and tide-pool-like waters perfect for baby Mary Claire's first ocean splash. Navigating the island is delightfully simple and scenic, with golf carts replacing traditional cars on the quiet streets. For a luxury finance couple from Houston, it represents an effortlessly chic escape where upscale dining meets peaceful island pacing.
🚕 Getting there: From Cancun International Airport (CUN), enjoy a 30-minute private luxury SUV transfer to the Puerto Juarez terminal, followed by a smooth 20-minute crossing in the VIP indoor cabin of the Ultramar Ferry with ample stroller storage.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Playa Norte

The island's crown jewel features incredibly shallow, wave-free turquoise water stretching far out, making it a safe, natural wading pool for baby Mary Claire. Rent a private shaded daybed from a luxury beach club to keep her cool.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM

El Centro Stroller Walk

Explore the vibrant pedestrian-only streets of downtown Isla Mujeres. The paved paths of Avenida Hidalgo are stroller-friendly during the quieter morning hours before the day-tripper crowds arrive.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Private Golf Cart Island Tour

Experience the island's most iconic activity by renting a high-end, canopy-shaded golf cart. Secure baby Mary Claire safely in your front-pack carrier for a gentle, breezy ride along the scenic coastal road.

Late Afternoon 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM

Punta Sur Sculpture Park

Drive to the southern tip of the island to view dramatic sea cliffs and the ruins of the Mayan temple dedicated to Ixchel. The paved walkways are stroller-accessible, though be mindful of the ocean breezes.

Midday 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM

MUSA Glass Bottom Boat Tour

Experience the world-famous Underwater Museum of Art without getting wet. A private glass-bottom boat charter lets you and baby Mary Claire peer down at the stunning submerged sculptures in comfort and shade.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 2:45 PM

Capilla de Guadalupe

This gorgeous, modern glass-walled chapel overlooks the ocean. It offers a wonderfully quiet, air-conditioned sanctuary where baby Mary Claire can enjoy a peaceful break from the afternoon tropical heat.

Afternoon 12:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Kin Ha Beach Club

A tranquil boutique beach club on the calm western shore featuring tier-style pools, ample natural shade, and a relaxed lawn area perfect for crawling babies.

Morning 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

Garrafon Natural Reef Park VIP Lounge

Book the VIP pass for access to the exclusive, quiet pool areas, manicured lawns, and shaded hammocks away from the main adventure crowds, ideal for a luxury family respite.

Evening 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Playa Sol Sunset Viewing

Located adjacent to Playa Norte, this calm beach offers the best westward sunset views on the island. Spread out a blanket on the soft sand for a peaceful evening winding down with the baby.

🍽️ Where to eat

Mango Cafe

Indulge in their famous stuffed French toast and excellent coffee on the open-air second floor. The staff is exceptionally welcoming to babies, and the breezy atmosphere easily masks minor infant noise.

Lola Valentina

An upscale yet highly accommodating spot in El Centro featuring gourmet Mexican-fusion fare, spacious outdoor courtyard seating, highchairs, and early dinner service.

Olivia

Savor high-end Mediterranean cuisine in a romantic, lush garden setting. Book their earliest seating at 5:00 PM to easily park a sleeping stroller right next to your intimate candlelit table.

Zama Beach Club

A luxury beachfront venue offering fresh ceviche and gourmet fish tacos. Enjoy your meal under a grand shaded palapa while baby Mary Claire relaxes on the plush daybeds steps from the calm water.

Limon

Run by an incredibly attentive chef, this hidden gem offers exceptional fine dining in a quiet, upscale neighborhood patio setting away from the downtown noise, perfect for a peaceful family dinner.
