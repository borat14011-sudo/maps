Roatán

← back to travel board
Roatán is a lush Caribbean jewel wrapped in the world's second-largest barrier reef, offering calm, shallow waters perfect for little splashers. Beyond the beaches, your family will discover vibrant wildlife encounters, friendly local culture, and laid-back island vibes. It is an easy-going paradise where jungle meets sea, designed for stress-free family bonding.
🚕 Getting there: ~20-30 min taxi or pre-arranged shuttle to West Bay or West End beach hotels.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Gumbalimba Park

Walk along paved jungle trails to meet friendly free-roaming monkeys and sloths. Toddler note: The paths are mostly stroller-friendly, but hold toddlers close during the active monkey interactions.

Morning / 3-4h

West Bay Beach

A postcard-perfect stretch of soft white sand with virtually no waves and shallow drop-offs. Toddler note: Ideal for tiny splashers to safely play and build sandcastles in the calm tide.

Morning / 1-2h

Daniel Johnson's Monkey and Sloth Hangout

A family-run animal sanctuary dedicated to the safe care of sloths, capuchin monkeys, and macaws. Toddler note: It is a calm, small-scale environment that is much less overwhelming for toddlers than larger parks.

Afternoon / 1h

The Mayak Chocolate Factory

Discover how organic Honduran cacao is transformed into delicious sweet treats with free daily tastings. Toddler note: The air-conditioned interior provides a perfect, sweet cool-down spot for tired toddlers.

Morning / 1-2h

Glass Bottom Boat Tour

Explore the vibrant Mesoamerican Reef and colorful sea life through a submarine-style glass cabin. Toddler note: This is an excellent alternative to snorkeling for toddlers who want to see fish up close.

Morning / 1-2h

Arch's Iguana and Marine Park

Get up close to over 4,000 friendly iguanas roaming freely in a conservation sanctuary. Toddler note: Toddlers will love feeding the iguanas large leaves under your close supervision.

Day Trip / 4-6h

Little French Key

A private island paradise featuring shallow, crystal-clear lagoons, beach swings, and calm waters. Toddler note: The warm, knee-deep sandbars are perfect for toddlers to safely splash and wade in all day.

Morning / 2h

Carambola Botanical Gardens

Wander through 40 acres of lush jungle trails, flowering plants, and towering fruit trees. Toddler note: Stick to the flat lower loop for an easy, toddler-paced nature walk instead of the steep summit hike.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

West End Village Walk

Stroll this quiet beachside dirt road filled with local artisan shops and delicious sweet treat stalls. Toddler note: Visit in the late afternoon when the ocean breeze keeps it cool and manageable for little ones.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Palapa at Infinity Bay

Get the fresh coconut shrimp and chicken tenders served right on the beach where kids can play in the sand.

The Thirsty Turtle

Get the famous local fish tacos or a plate of classic quesadillas while enjoying the live music and beach vibes.

Creole's Rotisserie Chicken

Get the savory quarter-chicken combo with traditional coconut rice, beans, and sweet plantains.

Kristian's Kabobs

Get the freshly grilled chicken skewers and delicious garlic butter lobster bites right on the water's edge.

Beachers Bar & Grill

Get the authentic Honduran baleadas—thick flour tortillas folded with beans, cheese, and cream.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack plenty of reef-safe sunscreen and high-quality mosquito repellent, as sandflies can be pesky on the beaches during calm days. Opt for private water taxis for quick, fun, and breezy transit between West End and West Bay with toddlers.
