Fringed by the spectacular Mesoamerican Barrier Reef, Roatan offers an idyllic, laid-back Caribbean escape tailored for sophisticated families. The island's dramatic topography blends lush, pine-forested hills with powdery white-sand beaches that shelve gently into crystal-clear waters. For Will, Kathryn, and little Mary Claire, the calm, wave-free lagoon of West Bay Beach provides a perfect, stress-free setting for an infant's first saltwater dip. Here, luxury resorts blend seamlessly into the palms, offering private pool villas and stroller-ready wooden boardwalks just steps from the shore.
🚕 Getting there: A 35-minute private SUV transfer from Juan Manuel Gálvez International Airport (RTB) to the West Bay luxury strip, pre-arranged with a premium infant car seat and ample space for a full-size stroller.

What to do

Early morning 9-11am / 2h

West Bay Beach

Famous for its powdery white sand and incredibly calm, shallow waters, this beach is an absolute dream for infants. Will and Kathryn can rent a private shaded cabana, allowing Mary Claire to nap peacefully right by the gentle tide.

Morning 8-10am / 2h

Carambola Botanical Gardens

Located in Sandy Bay, these lush gardens offer a shaded retreat from the midday sun beneath a canopy of giant ferns and orchids. A sturdy baby carrier is highly recommended here, allowing you to walk the scenic jungle paths comfortably while Mary Claire sleeps.

Morning 9:30-11:00am / 1.5h

Arch's Iguana Farm

A highly engaging yet gentle wildlife sanctuary where hundreds of iguanas roam freely. The pathways are flat and easy to navigate with a lightweight stroller, providing a colorful and quiet morning experience for the whole family.

Mid-morning 10-11am / 1h

Roatan Glass Bottom Boat

Embark on a private reef viewing tour from West Bay without getting wet, perfect for showing Mary Claire the vibrant coral reef. The cabin is air-conditioned and completely shaded, making it a safe, cool, and comfortable marine adventure for an infant.

Morning 9am-12pm / 3h

Gumbalimba Park

Explore beautiful, well-maintained paths where friendly capuchin monkeys and colorful macaws delight visitors. The park's wide, paved pathways are highly stroller-accessible, and there are plenty of shaded resting spots and an onsite cafe.

Lunchtime 12-2pm / 2h

Roatan Island Brewing Co.

Nestled in the lush valley of Palmetto Bay, this sprawling artisanal brewery features massive shaded grassy lawns. It is a fantastic, ultra-relaxed spot where parents can sample local craft beers while Mary Claire rolls happily on a picnic blanket under the trees.

Day trip 10am-3pm / 5h

Little French Key

An exclusive private island paradise boasting exceptionally shallow, current-free turquoise lagoons perfect for wading with an infant. Book a private VIP cabana to secure ultimate shade, personalized service, and a quiet space for nursing or napping.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Punta Gorda

Experience the vibrant, centuries-old Garifuna culture with a peaceful morning stroll along the coastline. Visiting during the quieter mid-week mornings avoids the loud afternoon street parties, ensuring a serene, culturally rich walk for the family.

Morning 9am-1pm / 4h

Camp Bay Beach

Roatan's wild and undeveloped east end offers a pristine, crowd-free stretch of white sand far from the busy tourist paths. It is the ultimate spot for a peaceful family picnic, offering whispering palms for natural shade and a tranquil atmosphere.

🍽️ Where to eat

Luna Muna

Located at the Ibagari Boutique Hotel, this open-air luxury restaurant serves exquisite global-islander fusion cuisine. The gentle ocean breeze, spacious deck, and early dinner service starting at 5:30 PM make it incredibly welcoming for parents dining with an infant.

La Sirena de Camp Bay

This iconic, rustic-chic restaurant is built on stilts directly over the calm waters of the east end, specializing in fresh lionfish and rum punch. The breezy, shaded outdoor terrace offers a relaxed ambiance where baby can marvel at the water below.

Gio's Restaurant

A legendary family-owned institution in Flowers Bay famous for its world-class King Crab. They offer highchairs, spacious indoor and outdoor seating options, and an accommodating staff that caters beautifully to young families during early seatings.

The Lotus Restaurant

Set in a tropical garden oasis at Xbalanque, this upscale venue serves delicious Mediterranean-Asian fusion. It offers an intimate, low-noise environment with wide-spaced tables, perfect for a peaceful dinner with a stroller parked tableside.

Silversides Restaurant & Bar

An upscale, breezy marina-side dining spot at Barefoot Cay offering premium seafood and high-quality burgers. Highly family-friendly with highchairs, a relaxed atmosphere, and an adjacent pool deck that provides a soothing backdrop for an early family dinner.
