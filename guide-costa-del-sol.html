The Costa del Sol beckons with its sparkling Mediterranean waters, whitewashed villages, and the glamorous, yacht-lined shores of Marbella. Here, the golden sands of Playa de Alicate meet world-class dining and high-end boutiques designed for discerning travelers. For families traveling with an infant like Mary Claire, the paved, stroller-friendly Paseo Marítimo Marbella provides miles of breezy, unobstructed oceanfront walking right at your doorstep. From quiet morning strolls along the Golden Mile to peaceful afternoons under shaded cabanas, it is a seamless baby-friendly paradise.
🚕 Getting there: Malaga Airport (AGP) to Marbella is a scenic 40-minute drive via the AP-7 toll road. The ultimate luxury family option is a pre-arranged private Mercedes V-Class transfer booked through Marbella Absolute, complete with a rear-facing Cybex infant car seat and ample luggage space for your Bugaboo stroller.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 2h

Paseo Marítimo de Marbella

Stroll along this beautifully paved, wide boardwalk that stretches for miles along the coast. It is completely stroller-accessible, lined with palms, and offers plenty of breezy shade for a sleeping baby during morning walks.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM / 1.5h

Plaza de los Naranjos

This historic, orange-tree-filled square in Marbella's Old Town offers beautiful shade and scent. It is perfect for an afternoon resting spot; while the surrounding streets are cobblestoned, the plaza itself is flat and easy to navigate with a stroller.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 2h

Puerto Banús Marina

Admire the luxury yachts and high-end boutiques along the wide, flat harbor walls. Visit during the quiet morning hours to avoid crowds, allowing Mary Claire to enjoy a peaceful nap in the stroller while you window shop.

Late afternoon 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM / 1.5h

Parque de la Constitución

A tranquil oasis in the heart of Marbella featuring lush tropical gardens, paved walking paths, and a small astronomical observatory. It is highly shaded and features a lovely children's playground where older infants can enjoy the baby swings.

Morning 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM / 3h

Bioparc Fuengirola

A highly-rated, immersive zoo designed with natural barriers instead of cages, located just a short drive from Marbella. The park is completely stroller-accessible, heavily shaded by rainforest canopy, and offers quiet feeding rooms for nursing parents.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:30 AM / 2h

Playa de Cabopino

This beach is famous for its gentle waves, shallow golden shores, and the protected Artola Dunes. The wooden boardwalk leading to the beach is stroller-friendly, and the calm, shallow water is perfect for dipping Mary Claire's toes.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM / 1.5h

Orchidarium Estepona

A stunning glass-domed botanical garden showcasing over 1,300 species of orchids alongside indoor waterfalls. The paths are fully paved, climate-controlled, and offer a quiet, sensory-rich environment that is highly engaging for infants.

Late afternoon 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM / 2h

Bulevar de San Pedro de Alcántara

A modern, architecturally impressive park built over the main highway, offering vast pedestrian-only areas. It features several themed playgrounds, stroller-friendly paths, and open-air cafes where parents can grab a drink while baby watches the sights.

Morning 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM / 4h

Selwo Aventura

An expansive, open-air animal park in Estepona where species roam in large, semi-free environments. Opt for the paved, stroller-accessible walking routes to see the elephants and giraffes, which will delight a curious infant.

🍽️ Where to eat

Lobito de Mar

Enjoy world-class, fresh Andalusian seafood and exquisite tuna carpaccio at this vibrant eatery by chef Dani García. The energetic atmosphere easily absorbs any baby noise, highchairs are readily available, and early evening reservations are perfect for a family dinner.

Chiringuito El Ancla

Indulge in their signature salt-baked sea bass while sitting on a spacious outdoor terrace overlooking the sea. The restaurant is incredibly spacious, allowing easy stroller parking right next to your table, and features a relaxed, ocean-side ambiance.

Sea Grill

Savor elegant Mediterranean grilled dishes on a stunning beachfront terrace at the Puente Romano resort. The staff is exceptionally welcoming to young families, offering premium highchairs and accommodating early seatings at 7:30 PM.

Serafina

Delight in classic Italian handmade pasta on a romantic deck overlooking a historic Roman bridge. This upscale venue accommodates families beautifully during their early dinner service, providing stroller access and a gentle, ambient river breeze.

Tikitano Beach Restaurant

Relish fresh Mediterranean-Asian fusion cuisine on a gorgeous beachfront terrace with panoramic ocean views. The restaurant features a sprawling grassy lawn where babies can enjoy supervised crawling, and the staff is highly attentive to infant needs.
