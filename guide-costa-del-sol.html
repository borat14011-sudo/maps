What to do

Early morning 9-11am / 2h

Paseo Marítimo de Marbella

This flat, wide, and paved beachfront boardwalk stretches for miles, making it the premier location for scenic stroller walks. Will and Kathryn can enjoy the ocean breeze while Mary Claire naps under the stroller shade, with plenty of upscale cafes along the route for a quick espresso break.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Plaza de los Naranjos

Located in the heart of Marbella's Old Town, this historic square is filled with sweet-scented orange trees that offer excellent natural shade. The vibrant green leaves and central fountain provide a soothing sensory experience for Mary Claire, though parents should navigate the surrounding cobblestones with a sturdy multi-terrain stroller.

Midday 12-1:30pm / 1.5h

Orchidarium Estepona

A stunning, fully air-conditioned indoor botanical garden housed under three glass domes featuring over a thousand species of orchids and a dramatic indoor waterfall. The paved, gently sloping pathways are completely stroller-accessible, offering a cool and tranquil retreat from the midday sun.

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Puerto Banús Marina

Stroll along the wide, flat concrete docks of this world-famous marina to admire the mega-yachts and luxury boutiques. The area is highly accessible for strollers, allowing the family to enjoy a quiet walk before the bustling evening crowds arrive.

Afternoon 3-4:30pm / 1.5h

Parque de la Constitución

A peaceful oasis near the center of Marbella, this park features manicured gardens, towering palm trees, and wide paved paths. It offers a wonderfully quiet and shaded environment for an afternoon contact nap or some gentle playtime on the grassy lawns with Mary Claire.

Late afternoon 5-6:30pm / 1.5h

Playa de Cabopino Dunes

Explore the Artola Dunes via a series of elevated wooden boardwalks that are entirely stroller-friendly and keep you off the hot sand. This unique natural reserve offers breathtaking views of the Mediterranean and a calm, breezy environment for a gentle family walk.

Morning 10am-1pm / 3h

Bioparc Fuengirola

An award-winning, highly shaded zoo designed around the concept of zoo-immersion, placing animals in recreated natural tropical rainforest habitats. The paved pathways are easy to navigate with a stroller, and the lush canopy keeps the entire family cool while Mary Claire observes the gentle lemurs.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Parque de la Paloma

Located in Benalmádena, this expansive park features large green lawns, paved walking paths, and a picturesque central lake. Mary Claire will love watching the peacocks, ducks, and rabbits that roam freely through the shaded garden paths.

Early morning 8:30-10am / 1.5h

Senda Litoral de Mijas

A beautiful wooden coastal boardwalk that hugs the shoreline of Mijas Costa, providing flat and uninterrupted ocean views. It is exceptionally well-maintained and stroller-friendly, allowing for a peaceful morning walk directly above the crashing waves.

🍽️ Where to eat

Sea Grill

Located inside the prestigious Puente Romano Beach Resort, this elegant seaside restaurant serves exceptional grilled sea bass and lobster. The spacious outdoor terrace easily accommodates strollers, and the early lunch seating allows for a luxury dining experience before Mary Claire's afternoon nap.

MC Beach

Marbella Club's upscale beachfront chiringuito features a breezy, relaxed atmosphere with tables set right on the edge of the sand. Parents can savor fresh seafood paella while keeping the stroller parked comfortably beside the table on the wooden deck.

Chiringuito Trocadero Arena

Set on the Rio Real beach, this stylish restaurant boasts exotic African-safari decor and wide, open-air dining spaces. The staff is incredibly welcoming to families, offering highchairs and spacious table layouts that allow for a stress-free lunch near the sea.

El Lago

Enjoy sophisticated Michelin-starred dining on a tranquil, shaded lakeside terrace in Elviria. The peaceful waterfront setting provides a calming backdrop that keeps infants relaxed, and the early evening service is ideal for parents dining with a baby.

La Meridiana Alabardero

This refined Mediterranean institution features expansive, lush garden terraces that provide a quiet and breezy setting for families. The spacious outdoor tables are ideal for dining with a sleeping baby in a stroller, and the staff is highly accommodating to young children.
