What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Duke Kahanamoku Lagoon

This five-acre man-made salt-water lagoon features incredibly calm, shallow waters with no waves, making it the absolute safest spot in Honolulu for baby Mary Claire to experience her first dip in the ocean while parents relax on the sandy shoreline.

Early Morning 8:30-10am / 1.5h

Foster Botanical Garden

A peaceful oasis in the center of the city featuring massive canopy trees that provide exceptional shade, paved stroller-friendly paths, and a tranquil atmosphere perfect for a quiet morning walk during baby's first nap window.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Waikiki Aquarium

Located right on the edge of Kapiolani Park, this intimate aquarium is quiet, fully air-conditioned, and stroller-accessible, offering colorful tropical fish displays right at an infant's eye level for a stimulating yet low-stress morning.

Morning 9am-12pm / 3h

Hoʻomaluhia Botanical Garden

Stroll along the paved, stroller-accessible main paths of this breathtaking rain forest garden nestled against the dramatic Ko'olau Range; it offers endless cool breezes and spectacular, quiet spots for a luxury picnic.

Afternoon 2-4:30pm / 2.5h

Ala Moana Center Luxury Wing

Browse premier fashion houses in a beautiful, open-air, stroller-friendly environment featuring wide walkways, elevator access, and high-end family lounges equipped with spotless nursing rooms and changing stations.

Late Afternoon 4-5:30pm / 1.5h

Kapiolani Regional Park

Spread a luxury blanket beneath the shade of towering ironwood and banyan trees for tummy time with baby Mary Claire, enjoying sweeping views of Diamond Head and cool ocean breezes away from the crowded beach strip.

Midday 11am-1:30pm / 2.5h

Bishop Museum

Explore Hawaii's rich royal heritage inside spacious, whisper-quiet, air-conditioned galleries that are fully stroller-accessible and provide a serene, sensory-friendly escape from the midday sun.

Saturday Morning 8-10:30am / 2.5h

Kaka'ako Farmers Market

An upscale Saturday morning market featuring wide, flat, stroller-friendly outdoor aisles where you can sample organic local fruits and artisanal treats while enjoying live, gentle Hawaiian acoustic music.

Morning 8-10:30am / 2.5h

Manoa Falls Trail

Experience Hawaii's lush interior bamboo forests on this highly maintained path; parents should use a premium front-facing baby carrier like an Ergobaby rather than a stroller to safely navigate the gravel terrain under the cool rainforest canopy.

🍽️ Where to eat

Orchids at Halekulani

Savor exquisite oceanfront dining with their signature steamed Onaga fish; the staff is exceptionally accommodating to families, providing high-end wooden highchairs and spacious seaside table placement during their quiet early dinner seating.

Hoku's

Located at the quiet Kahala Resort, this upscale venue features refined global Hawaiian cuisine like their famous whole fried fish; booking an early 5:30 PM reservation ensures a quiet dining room with stunning ocean views and effortless stroller parking.

Merriman's Honolulu

Enjoy fresh Hawaii Regional Cuisine on a spacious, covered outdoor terrace in Ward Village where the gentle ambient hum of the neighborhood easily masks any happy baby noises from Mary Claire.

The Veranda at The Kahala

An elegant, open-air lounge famous for its refined afternoon tea and light bites, offering wide spaces between tables that easily accommodate full-sized luxury strollers while overlooking the resort's quiet dolphin lagoon.

Monkeypod Kitchen by Merriman Waikiki

A highly vibrant, stylish, yet relaxed open-air venue at the Outrigger Reef featuring legendary mai tais and wood-fired pizzas; the lively acoustic music and friendly, energetic atmosphere make it perfect for dining with an infant without fear of disturbing others.
