What to do

Early morning 9-11am / 2h

Playa de la Barrosa

This pristine blue-flag beach features exceptionally wide sands and a flat wooden boardwalk, making it ideal for a morning stroller walk. Mary Claire will love dipping her toes in the gentle shoreline tide pools during low tide.

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Parque Genovés

A shaded historical botanical garden in Cádiz filled with exotic palms, symmetrical hedges, and a quiet lake with a gentle waterfall. The smooth, paved paths are perfect for an afternoon nap in the stroller while enjoying the cool sea breeze.

Late afternoon 5-7pm / 2h

Playa de la Caleta

Nestled between two historic castles, this iconic bay offers calm, shallow waters sheltered from strong waves, making it the safest spot for Mary Claire to splash. The nearby promenade is completely stroller-friendly and boasts dramatic sunset views.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Alcázar de Jerez

A magnificent 11th-century Moorish fortress featuring tranquil orange groves, olive trees, and quiet, shaded courtyards. The smooth gravel paths are highly stroller-accessible, allowing parents to explore Andalusian history in a serene setting.

Morning 9-11:30am / 2.5h

Baelo Claudia Archaeological Site

These spectacularly preserved Roman ruins sit directly on the shores of Bolonia beach. High-quality wooden boardwalks run through the site, providing excellent stroller accessibility alongside breath-taking ocean views.

Mid-morning 10:30am-12pm / 1.5h

Plaza de las Flores

This bustling, colorful square in the heart of historic Cádiz is filled with fresh flower stalls and outdoor cafes. It offers sensory delight for the baby with its vibrant colors and sweet scents, plus easy level ground for walking.

Early morning 8:30-10am / 1.5h

Paseo Marítimo de Cádiz

A stunning, modern waterfront promenade that mirrors Havana's Malecón, featuring wide, barrier-free paved paths. It is highly stroller-accessible and perfect for an energetic morning walk with sweeping views of the cathedral's golden dome.

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Parque Natural de la Bahía de Cádiz

A protected wetland sanctuary offering flat, well-maintained wooden boardwalks winding over peaceful salt marshes. It is incredibly quiet and peaceful, making it a wonderful spot for birdwatching and a restorative baby nap.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Royal Andalusian School of Equestrian Art Gardens

While the indoor horse show can be loud, the palace's surrounding botanical gardens and historic carriage museum are quiet, shaded, and incredibly stroller-friendly. It offers a luxurious and peaceful retreat for the family to wander.

🍽️ Where to eat

Restaurante El Faro de Cádiz

Indulge in their legendary crispy shrimp tortillitas on the spacious, elegant outdoor terrace. They offer comfortable highchairs, early 8:00 PM dinner reservations, and a welcoming atmosphere that easily absorbs baby babble.

El Farito

A sophisticated beachfront institution in Chiclana specializing in salt-baked sea bass and fresh local tuna. The restaurant features a spacious outdoor terrace, ample room for stroller parking, and an incredibly accommodating waitstaff.

Cataria

Located inside the Iberostar Selection, this ultra-premium culinary destination showcases exquisite charcoal-grilled seafood. The spacious outdoor seating, quiet ambient sea noise, and highly professional service ensure a stress-free luxury dinner.

La Castillería

Set in a lush, open-air garden canopy near Vejer, this world-class steakhouse offers a naturally soothing atmosphere with soft ambient music and waterfalls. The spacious table layouts easily accommodate a luxury travel stroller.

Ventorrillo El Chato

This historic 18th-century beachside tavern offers exquisite traditional Andalusian tapas on a modern sea-facing terrace. The early dining availability and ocean breezes make it exceptionally comfortable for a family dining experience.
