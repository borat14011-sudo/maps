Ixtapa / Zihuatanejo

← back to travel board
Nestled between the Sierra Madre mountains and the Pacific, Ixtapa-Zihuatanejo offers the perfect blend of modern resort luxury and authentic Mexican fishing village charm. The calm, wave-sheltered waters of Playa La Ropa provide a pristine, safe environment for baby Mary Claire to experience the ocean for the very first time. Parents Will and Kathryn will appreciate the stroller-friendly paved paths of the Marina Ixtapa and the effortless luxury of beachfront dining. It is a peaceful, upscale tropical retreat where family comfort and high-end relaxation harmonize beautifully.
🚕 Getting there: A 20-minute private luxury SUV transfer from Ixtapa-Zihuatanejo International Airport (ZIH) directly to your resort, equipped with a pre-installed rear-facing infant car seat.

What to do

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Playa La Ropa Sunset Stroll

Enjoy the soft, golden sands of this mile-long beach where ultra-calm waves allow for gentle baby wading under the shade of a rental palapa.

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

Ciclopista de Ixtapa

A beautifully paved, flat, and heavily shaded nature trail perfect for a morning run or walk with a high-end jogging stroller.

Morning 9am-12pm / 3h

Playa Las Gatas Boat Excursion

Take a brief, private water taxi ride to this reef-protected beach with crystal-clear, pool-like shallow water ideal for baby's splash time.

Evening 6-8pm / 2h

Paseo del Pescador

Stroll along Zihuatanejo's charming fisherman's boardwalk, offering a smooth, stroller-friendly concrete surface with beautiful bay views and refreshing breezes.

Morning 9am-1pm / 4h

Isla Ixtapa (Playa Cuachalalate)

A short boat ride leads to a highly sheltered cove with exceptionally calm, shallow waters and beachfront restaurants offering shaded lounge chairs.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Playa Linda Crocodile Sanctuary

Walk along a secure, elevated wooden boardwalk to view crocodiles, turtles, and roseate spoonbills in their natural habitat, completely stroller-accessible.

Evening 5-7pm / 2h

Marina Ixtapa Promenade

A quiet, paved harborfront boardwalk perfect for a peaceful evening stroller walk while admiring the luxury yachts and feeding local fish.

Midday 12-1pm / 1h

Museo Arqueológico de la Costa Grande

A small, peaceful, and fully air-conditioned museum showcasing local history, providing a cool sanctuary from the midday tropical heat.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Playa Quieta Beach Day

Living up to its name 'Quiet Beach,' this bay is highly protected, offering almost zero waves and ample shade, making it perfect for infant swimming.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Perla Restaurant & Bar

Savor fresh local red snapper with your toes in the sand; they offer highchairs, plenty of shade, and an incredibly welcoming attitude toward babies.

Ceniza Restaurant

Upscale, open-air beachfront dining at the Thompson resort featuring contemporary coastal Mexican cuisine, with spacious seating and soothing ambient wave sounds.

Angustina Mezcal y Cocina

High-end gourmet dining in downtown Zihuatanejo featuring an elegant outdoor terrace, gourmet seafood dishes, and early reservation slots that fit baby's schedule.

Bandido's

A vibrant yet highly family-friendly local institution offering authentic grilled fajitas, spacious seating, highchairs, and a lively atmosphere that easily masks baby chatter.

Otilia

Located right on the sand at Playa Las Gatas, this lunch spot offers fresh lobster, shaded daybeds, and immediate access to calm waters for baby.
