What to do

Early morning 9-11am / 2h

Timanfaya National Park

Witness a dramatic volcanic landscape from the comfort of an air-conditioned tour bus that traverses the Ruta de los Volcanes. Strollers must be checked at the El Diablo restaurant, so bring a structured baby carrier to enjoy the geothermal demonstrations where Mary Claire can watch steam geysers in safety.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Jameos del Agua

An awe-inspiring volcanic tunnel transformed by César Manrique, featuring an underground salt lake home to blind albino crabs. Because of the steep volcanic stone steps, leave the stroller in your rental car and use a baby carrier to navigate the quiet, wind-sheltered subterranean paths.

Morning 9:30-11am / 1.5h

Marina Rubicón

Stroll along this upscale marina filled with luxury yachts, high-end boutiques, and smooth wooden boardwalks that are entirely stroller-friendly. It is a peaceful morning walk for Will and Kathryn to sip flat whites while Mary Claire naps soundly under a canopy.

Midday 12-1pm / 1h

Mirador del Río

A spectacular glass-walled viewpoint cut into high northern cliffs, offering panoramic views of La Graciosa island. The indoor pavilion is fully wind-sheltered, temperature-controlled, and stroller-accessible, allowing you to enjoy the views and a quiet coffee without exposing the baby to the strong trade winds.

Late afternoon 4-5:30pm / 1.5h

Jardín de Cactus

Another Manrique masterpiece, this amphitheater-style garden features over 10,000 cacti from around the world. The lower pathways are relatively flat and packed, making it manageable with an all-terrain stroller, and the quiet, structured layout offers many shaded stone benches perfect for feeding times.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Playa Dorada

A manicured, golden-sand beach in Playa Blanca protected by breakwaters that guarantee exceptionally calm, shallow waters. Ideal for dip-testing Mary Claire's toes in the Atlantic, it is flanked by a wide paved promenade packed with luxury facilities and shaded cabanas for rent.

Afternoon 2-3:30pm / 1.5h

Fundación César Manrique

Built over five natural volcanic bubbles, this stunning mid-century modern home seamlessly blends art and nature. The upper level and outdoor pool gardens are highly accessible and incredibly peaceful, offering a visually stimulating environment for a carrier-bound baby.

Late afternoon 5-6pm / 1h

Charco de los Clicos (El Golfo)

Marvel at the striking contrast of a vivid green lagoon sitting directly on a black sand beach, framed by volcanic cliffs. The main viewing platform is accessed via a short, flat gravel path that is easily navigated with a stroller, offering dramatic views without any strenuous hiking.

Evening 6-7:30pm / 1.5h

Puerto Calero Marina

Lanzarote's most exclusive, low-density marina is incredibly peaceful and offers a beautifully paved waterfront devoid of mass crowds. It is perfect for a sunset stroller walk, looking out over catamarans and enjoying the gentle ocean breeze.

🍽️ Where to eat

Restaurante Kamezí Deli & Bistro

Savor high-end Canarian gastronomy featuring ocean-to-table seafood and volcanic-soil wines. Located in Playa Blanca, they offer an early evening tasting menu on a spacious, ocean-view outdoor terrace where a sleeping baby in a stroller is welcomed warmly by the staff.

Amura

Located in Puerto Calero, this upscale establishment serves some of the best black rice and fresh local cherne fish on the island. The restaurant features a completely flat, stroller-accessible terrace overlooking the water and offers highchairs and an early lunch service popular with luxury family travelers.

Restaurante El Risco

Set right on the coast of Famara, this Michelin-recommended venue serves spectacular fresh seafood with stunning views of the cliffs. Book an early outdoor terrace table where the soothing sound of the crashing waves effortlessly drowns out any baby noise, and enjoy their signature octopus carpaccio.

La Cantina

An elegant, historic restaurant in the heart of Teguise offering creative local tapas and fine local malvasía wines. Ask for a table in their serene, secret inner courtyard which provides a quiet, wind-sheltered sanctuary with plenty of room for a stroller.

Coentro

An acclaimed modern Canarian bistro in Puerto Calero helmed by Chef João Faraco. The atmosphere is upscale yet intimate, and the outdoor dining patio is perfectly suited for early-evening diners traveling with an infant, offering highly attentive service and flexible seating.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Princesa Yaiza Suite Hotel Resort

$250-400/night

A stunning five-star beachfront resort in Playa Blanca that is widely regarded as one of Europe's top family-friendly luxury properties. They provide wooden cribs with premium linens, spacious family suites with separate living areas, multiple heated pools, and the massive Kikoland kids' club, alongside direct access to the stroller-friendly coastal promenade.

Hotel Fariones

$300-450/night

A newly renovated, ultra-chic five-star hotel in Puerto Carmen offering direct beach access, lush tropical gardens, and sleek family suites. The resort provides high-end wooden cribs, baby-friendly heated infinity pools, and blackout curtains that ensure excellent daytime napping conditions for Mary Claire.

Paradisus by Meliá Salinas Lanzarote

$280-420/night

An iconic luxury hotel in Costa Teguise originally designed by César Manrique, now operating as a refined, family-welcoming resort. It features stunning lagoon-style pools, spacious suites, professional babysitting services on request, and incredibly tranquil botanical gardens that are perfect for quiet afternoon stroller walks.

👨‍👩‍👧 For infant essentials like organic formula, diapers, and baby sunscreen, bypass standard supermarkets and head to a local 'Farmacia', which stocks high-quality European brands like Hipp Organic. While resort promenades are perfectly paved and stroller-friendly, Lanzarote's volcanic sites are highly rugged, so a high-quality baby carrier is essential. For any medical needs, Hospiten Lanzarote in Puerto del Carmen is a world-class private hospital with excellent English-speaking pediatricians and 24/7 emergency services.
