San Diego

← back to travel board
San Diego welcomes families with its golden beaches, year-round sunshine, and a laid-back coastal vibe that makes traveling with little ones a breeze. From world-class wildlife encounters to gentle ocean breezes, every corner promises outdoor adventure and effortless relaxation.

What to do

Morning / 4-5h

San Diego Zoo

Explore massive, lush habitats housing pandas, koalas, and polar bears in this world-famous park. Toddlers will love the double-decker bus tour and the dedicated, hands-on children's zoo.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Balboa Park Miniature Railroad & Gardens

Wander through stunning Spanish-Renaissance architecture, lush gardens, and let kids ride the historic model train. The flat, paved paths are perfect for strollers and tired little legs.

Morning / 1-2h

La Jolla Cove

Get up close to wild, sunbathing sea lions barking on the rocks and splashing in the tide pools. Keep toddlers at a safe distance on the paved walkway above for a stroller-friendly wildlife view.

Morning / 2-3h

The New Children's Museum

A multi-level creative playground where kids can climb, paint, and explore hands-on art installations. There are dedicated, quiet soft-play spaces designed specifically for toddlers and crawlers.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Liberty Public Market

A bustling indoor food hall with endless culinary options, from empanadas to craft ice cream, satisfying every family member. The outdoor grassy lawn areas are perfect for energetic toddlers to roam.

Morning / 3h

Coronado Beach

Build castles on glittering sand made of mica and splash in the calm, gentle waves of this iconic beach. The flat, wide shoreline makes it incredibly easy to navigate with a beach stroller.

Morning / 2-3h

USS Midway Museum

Climb aboard a real, massive aircraft carrier to explore historic planes and interactive cockpit exhibits. Toddlers will love the open-air flight deck, though you'll want a baby carrier instead of a stroller for the narrow lower decks.

Afternoon / 2h

Old Town San Diego State Historic Park

Step back in time to 19th-century California with historic adobe buildings, lively mariachi music, and vibrant colors. The car-free plaza offers a safe, open space for toddlers to toddle around.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Waterfront Park

Beat the heat at this downtown park featuring massive splash fountains, modern playgrounds, and views of the harbor. The shallow interactive splash zones are absolutely perfect for water-loving toddlers.

Where to eat

  • Lucha Libre Gourmet Taco Shop — This vibrant, wrestling-themed joint serves up incredible California burritos in a fun, colorful space kids will love.
  • The Crack Shack (Little Italy) — Feast on gourmet fried chicken and biscuits in a casual, open-air backyard patio featuring a giant chicken statue and lawn games.
  • Wayfarer Bread & Pastry — Grab world-class croissants and wood-fired pizzas to enjoy on the nearby grass while the kids play.
  • Donut Bar — Indulge in oversized, creative donuts like Homer Simpson pink glaze or French Toast flavors that double as an exciting morning treat.
