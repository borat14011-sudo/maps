Maui welcomes your family with its velvet trade winds, dramatic volcanic backdrops, and the soothing rhythm of the Pacific. For Will, Kathryn, and little Mary Claire, this tropical haven strikes the perfect balance between high-end relaxation and infant-friendly exploration. The pristine, stroller-friendly Wailea Beach Path offers miles of paved oceanfront walking, perfect for breezy morning strolls with the pram. Here, calm shallow lagoons like Baby Beach in Lahaina ensure safe, memorable dips for your little one.
🚕 Getting there: A 35-minute private SUV transfer from Kahului Airport (OGG) to the Ka'anapali coast is the most seamless option, allowing you to pre-book a premium infant car seat and comfortably pack your upscale stroller.

🍽️ Where to eat

Mama's Fish House

Book well in advance; request an early beachfront seating so Mary Claire can look at the tide pools while you enjoy the stuffed Mahi Mahi. Highchairs and spacious outdoor seating make this feel welcoming yet ultra-exclusive.

Monkeypod Kitchen by Merriman

Known for its lively, noisy atmosphere which easily masks any baby babble. Try the lobster deviled eggs and fresh fish tacos on the spacious outdoor terrace with highchairs.

Leoda's Kitchen and Pie Shop

A casual yet high-quality stop in Olowalu with delicious savory pies. The fast-casual service model is perfect for a quick, low-stress lunch before baby gets fussy.

Kimo's Maui

This waterfront icon offers a relaxed outdoor deck overlooking the ocean. Order the legendary Hula Pie and enjoy the early dinner breeze with friendly staff who readily accommodate strollers.

Hula Grill Ka'anapali

Dine right on the sand in the Barefoot Bar area where Mary Claire can play with a plastic cup in the sand while you savor fresh macadamia nut crusted crab cakes.
