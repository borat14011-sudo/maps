Bornholm

← back to travel board
Bornholm welcomes families with its dramatic rocky cliffs, ultra-fine sand beaches, and whimsical round medieval churches. This peaceful Danish island offers a slow-paced paradise where toddlers can safely run wild in nature and discover ancient castle ruins. It is the ultimate Scandinavian escape for stress-free family bonding.
🚕 Getting there: ~10 min taxi to Rønne hotels, or ~30 min rental car drive to coastal towns like Allinge-Sandvig

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Hammershus Castle Ruins

Scramble through Northern Europe's largest castle ruins perched high on a cliff, offering breathtaking Baltic Sea views. The modern visitor center has stroller-friendly ramps, making it easy to reach the dramatic lookout points.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Dueodde Beach

Famous for its sand so fine it was once used in hourglasses, this beach features a wide wooden boardwalk leading right to the shore. Toddlers will love splashing in the shallow, calm waters while parents relax under the pine trees.

Morning / 1h

Østerlars Round Church

Explore Denmark's largest and oldest round church, which looks like a whitewashed fairytale fortress. The spacious, grassy churchyard is a peaceful spot for toddlers to stretch their legs after seeing the unique interior.

Full Day / 4-5h

Joboland (Brændesgårdshaven)

This vintage amusement park features a hand-operated carousel, a heated water park, and a petting zoo with friendly goats. It is incredibly low-stress and tailor-made for toddlers and younger children.

Morning / 2-3h

NaturBornholm

An interactive science center in Aakirkeby where kids can touch real fossils, see live crocodiles, and play in a dinosaur-themed indoor playground. It is the perfect rainy-day refuge for active toddlers.

Afternoon / 45m

Svaneke Bolcher

Watch master candy makers pull, roll, and snip colorful hard candies by hand in a historic half-timbered building. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the sweet aromas and the mesmerizing, traditional production process.

Morning / 1-2h

Ekkodalen (Echo Valley)

Walk along the flat, stroller-friendly wooden paths of Bornholm's longest rift valley to test your voice against the cliffs. Kids love shouting into the rock face to hear the immediate, clear echo bounce back.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Bornholms Middelaldercenter

Step back into the Middle Ages at this open-air museum featuring historic timber houses, farm animals, and hands-on medieval games. Toddlers can safely roam the grassy village and pet the resident sheep.

Morning / 2h

Gaarden (Melstedgård)

This living agricultural museum lets families experience historic farm life, complete with historic kitchens and old-fashioned horse carriages. Toddlers can get up close to pigs, cows, and chickens in a beautiful, relaxed setting.

🍽️ Where to eat

Svaneke Røgeri

Sit at outdoor picnic tables and feast on freshly smoked herring served warm on rye bread with raw egg yolk and radishes.

Fru Petersen's Café

Indulge in a legendary, all-you-can-eat traditional Danish cake table filled with tarts, buns, and whipped cream inside a cozy, vintage-styled parlor.

Svaneke Bryghus Restaurant

Enjoy hearty burgers and local fish-and-chips in a bustling, family-friendly brewery restaurant that offers plenty of space for strollers.

Hallegaard

Visit this organic farm cafe in the countryside for award-winning, house-made sausages and charcuterie boards served in a sunny, rustic courtyard.

Gudhjem Røgeri

Try the iconic seafood buffet with various smoked fish options, which makes it easy for kids to sample different bites while overlooking the harbor.

👨‍👩‍👧 Rent a car directly at Rønne Airport, as local bus schedules can be too infrequent for strict toddler nap times. Always pack a windproof jacket for the little ones, as coastal breezes can pick up quickly even on sunny beach days.
