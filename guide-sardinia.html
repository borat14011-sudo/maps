What to do

Early morning 9-11am / 2h

Poetto Beach (Spiaggia del Poetto)

Enjoy miles of soft sand with an ultra-flat, stroller-accessible wooden boardwalk. Private beach clubs offer rentable luxury gazebos for vital shade, and the incredibly calm, shallow water is perfect for dipping baby Mary Claire's toes.

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Parco di Molentargius - Saline

A stunning natural regional park famous for its nesting colonies of bright pink flamingos. The wide, flat paved paths are perfect for a smooth stroller ride while the gentle breeze lulls your baby to sleep.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Bastione di Saint Remy

This majestic limestone landmark offers sweeping panoramic views of Cagliari. Skip the steep steps and take the modern, fully stroller-accessible elevator straight to the wide, breezy terrace for a scenic family photo.

Mid-morning 11am-1pm / 2h

Orto Botanico dell'Università di Cagliari

A lush, shaded botanical oasis right in the city center. It features towering palms and quiet, winding pathways where parents can find peaceful alcoves for nursing or relaxing under the cool canopy.

Morning 9am-1pm / 4h

Spiaggia di Tuerredda

A breathtaking, sheltered Caribbean-style cove located a short drive from Cagliari. Book a private double sunbed with an oversized umbrella at the exclusive beach club to keep the baby completely out of the direct sun.

Early morning 8-9:30am / 1.5h

Mercato Civico di San Benedetto

Europe's largest indoor market is a sensory delight of fresh local fruits, cheeses, and baked goods. The wide, bustling aisles are fully accessible, though an early morning visit is recommended to beat the local crowds.

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Nora Archaeological Site

Explore ancient Roman and Phoenician ruins situated right on the water's edge in nearby Pula. Use a high-end baby carrier here rather than a stroller to easily navigate the flat but unpaved dirt paths.

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Giardini Pubblici di Cagliari

Cagliari's oldest public gardens feature beautiful neoclassical avenues, giant weeping figs, and lush lawns. It is a wonderfully quiet, shaded park perfect for spreading out a blanket for baby playtime.

Afternoon 2-3pm / 1h

Santuario di Nostra Signora di Bonaria

A grand hilltop sanctuary featuring a wide, breezy esplanade overlooking the sea. The spacious interior is cool and exceptionally quiet, offering a peaceful sanctuary if you need to escape the midday heat.

🍽️ Where to eat

Dal Corsaro

This Michelin-starred institution offers refined Sardinian gastronomy in a sophisticated setting. Book an early 7:30 PM seating on the elegant veranda where staff are incredibly accommodating to families.

Antica Cagliari

Famous for its fresh local seafood and handmade culurgiones pasta. The spacious outdoor terrace is perfect for a lively family dinner, and the warm Italian staff love accommodating babies with highchairs.

Ristorante Martinelli's

An intimate, upscale seafood restaurant where the chef customizes dishes to your liking. Book the first evening slot so you can park your premium stroller directly at your table without worrying about crowds.

L'Imbarco

A gorgeous beachside eatery right on Poetto Beach. Savor fresh grilled fish and cold Vermentino wine on the shaded wooden deck while the ambient sound of the ocean waves acts as a natural white noise machine.

Sa Domu Sarda

A cozy, authentic restaurant serving traditional Sardinian dishes. Their spacious, breezy inner courtyard provides plenty of room for strollers, highchairs, and a highly welcoming, relaxed family atmosphere.
