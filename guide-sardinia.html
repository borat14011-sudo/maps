Sardinia’s southern capital, Cagliari, blends ancient history with a laid-back coastal vibe perfect for a luxury family escape. The iconic Poetto Beach offers a stroller-friendly promenade and shallow, calm waters where baby Mary Claire can safely splash. For a touch of luxury, book a suite with a private pool at a boutique hotel overlooking the Gulf of Angels. Real Sardinian charm meets infant comfort in this sun-drenched Mediterranean paradise.
🚕 Getting there: Cagliari Elmas Airport (CAG) to Poetto Beach hotel strip: 20 minutes by private transfer with infant car seat and stroller—book through Sardinia Limousine or similar premium service.

What to do

Morning 9-11am (2h)

Poetto Beach

Stroller-friendly boardwalk, calm shallow waters, shaded lidos with umbrella rentals. Perfect for a morning outing with baby.

Late morning 10am-12pm (2h)

Castello Quarter Exploration

Wander cobblestone alleys but use a rugged stroller. Visit the Cattedrale di Santa Maria (quiet interior) and Bastione Saint Remy for panoramic views. Baby can enjoy the breeze.

11am-12pm (1h)

Bastione Saint Remy

Grand terrace with stroller access via elevator. Shaded benches, city views, and a gentle walk. Ideal for a midday pause with baby.

Afternoon 2-4pm (2h)

Cala Mosca Beach

Small cove with shallow, calm water. Rocky entrance—best with a carrier. Fewer crowds, shade from cliffs. Great for a quiet afternoon.

Late morning 10:30am-12:30pm (2h)

Museo Archeologico Nazionale

Air-conditioned, stroller accessible, fascinating Nuragic artifacts. Baby can nap in quiet galleries. Limited loud exhibits.

Morning 9-10:30am (1.5h)

Orto Botanico di Cagliari

Shaded botanical garden with easy paths, ponds, and exotic plants. Stroller-friendly, quiet, and cool. Baby can watch birds and fish.

Late afternoon 5-6:30pm (1.5h)

Sella del Diavolo Easy Hike

Short, gentle trail (30 min) with carrier. Panoramic sea views, but avoid midday heat. Best at sunset with baby in a baby carrier.

Evening 6-7pm (1h)

Marina Piccola Harbor Stroll

Flat, stroller-friendly promenade along the harbor. Watch boats, grab gelato. Calm atmosphere, shade from trees.

Morning 9am-12pm (3h)

Golfo degli Angeli Boat Tour

Private boat charter with shade canopy. Calm waters near the coast. Baby can wear a life vest. Includes swimming stops at secluded beaches.

🍽️ Where to eat

Su Cumbidu

Traditional Sardinian cuisine with outdoor terrace. Highchair available, early dinner seating 6:30pm. Signature dish: malloreddus with sausage. Quiet ambiance.

La Pariglia

Beachfront restaurant on Poetto. No loud music, highchair, baby-changing room. Specializes in fresh seafood and fregola with clams.

Il Portico

Historic pizzeria in Castello. Kids welcome, no minimum age. Thin-crust pizzas and quiet courtyard. Highchair and stroller space.

Ristorante Lilloru

Elegant yet family-friendly with a garden terrace. Highchairs, flexible menu, early 7pm seating. Specialty: suckling pig (porceddu). Baby can nap in shaded corner.

Sa Schironata

Rustic trattoria with outdoor seating away from traffic. Highchair, calm atmosphere, classic Sardinian pastas like culurgiones. No loud noise.
