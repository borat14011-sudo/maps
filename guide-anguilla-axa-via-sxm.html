Anguilla offers an incredibly safe, low-key escape with some of the world's most breathtaking, uncrowded beaches perfect for an infant's first dip. The vibe is sophisticated yet thoroughly relaxed, meaning high-end dining spots welcome toddlers with open arms and zero pretension. By flying into SXM and taking a quick, scenic boat charter, you bypass long connection times and land straight in a Caribbean paradise tailored for slow-paced family bonding.
🚕 Getting there: Approx 25-minute private speed boat charter from the terminal near SXM Airport to Blowing Point Ferry Terminal, followed by a 15-minute taxi to West End resorts.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Meads Bay Beach

Enjoy calm morning waters and exceptionally wide, flat white sand that is perfect for setting up a pop-up baby tent. It is highly walkable, making it easy to stroll with a rugged beach stroller along the shoreline.

Morning / 3h

Shoal Bay East

Famous for its vibrant turquoise waters, the shore features shallow, gentle tidal pools under the shade of sea grape trees. It is highly accessible with parking close to the sand, minimizing long gear-hauling walks.

Late Morning / 3-4h

Sandy Island Shuttle

Board a brief, exciting 10-minute motorboat shuttle from Sandy Ground to a tiny, pristine offshore cay. The island's lagoon side is completely protected, acting as a giant, shallow saltwater baby pool.

Lunchtime / 2-3h

Scilly Cay

Take a free 2-minute skiff ride from Island Harbour to this tiny coral island for a unique lunch spot. The surrounding water is incredibly shallow and clear, perfect for holding your toddler while wading.

Afternoon / 2h

Rendezvous Bay Beach

This sweeping, crescent-shaped bay offers exceptionally still, dune-sheltered waters with zero drop-offs. It is the absolute safest beach on the island for floating peacefully with an infant.

Morning / 2h

Cove Bay Beach

An expansive, quiet beach with gentle waves and scenic views of neighboring St. Martin. It is rarely crowded, offering total privacy and space to let your toddler play without distraction.

Late Afternoon / 1h

The West End Arch

A dramatic natural limestone arch rising out of the sea that serves as an iconic photo backdrop. The flat, short dirt path from the parking spot is easy to navigate while wearing a baby carrier.

Early Morning / 1h

Road Salt Pond Birdwatching

An expansive, historic salt pond located right behind Sandy Ground beach where you can spot nesting egrets and herons. The roadside viewing areas are flat and completely stroller-accessible for a quick, engaging walk.

Midday / 2h

Crocus Bay Beach

A cliff-sheltered bay that remains completely protected from the open ocean winds, keeping the water as flat as a lake. It offers plenty of natural cliffside shade during the intense midday sun.

🍽️ Where to eat

Blanchards Beach Shack

Order the excellent mahi-mahi tacos and lobster rolls at this casual Meads Bay staple. The open-air, feet-in-the-sand picnic tables allow your toddler to play in the sand while you wait.

Sunshine Shack

Savor Garvey's legendary slow-smoked ribs and chicken right on Rendezvous Bay. The laid-back reggae music and gentle lapping waves provide the ultimate acoustic backdrop for a stroller nap.

Straw Hat

Try the local red snapper crudo and blackened fish sandwich on a spacious oceanfront deck. They offer early dinner seating starting at 5:00 PM, highly accommodating staff, and plenty of high chairs.

Picante

Feast on upscale, fresh Caribbean-Mexican cuisine like chili-lime shrimp tacos in a lush garden setting. The high-energy, bustling atmosphere easily drowns out any sudden toddler outbursts.

da'Vida Bayside Grill

Order the wood-fired pizzas and fresh chicken wraps directly on the calm shores of Crocus Bay. Shaded beachside lounge chairs allow you to dine while keeping your baby completely out of the direct sun.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Four Seasons Resort and Residences Anguilla

Features massive multi-bedroom oceanfront residences, top-tier wooden cribs, a dedicated shallow family pool, and highly vetted, professional in-room babysitting services.

Malliouhana

Perched dramatically over Meads Bay, this resort features spacious, bright rooms, a dedicated family swimming pool, and beautifully manicured, stroller-friendly cliffside paths.

Zemi Beach House

Set on the tranquil shores of Shoal Bay East, offering quiet multi-room suites with full kitchens, a zero-entry family pool, and a remarkably calm beachfront.
