What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Ponderosa Adventure Park

An expansive wildlife safari where Mary Claire can see giraffes and zebras from the safety of a shaded, slow-moving open-air safari vehicle. Highly recommended for a gentle morning animal encounter.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM

Parque Central

The historic green heart of Liberia features smooth, stroller-friendly paved walkways and towering shade trees, perfect for a relaxed family stroll and people-watching.

Late Morning 11:30 AM - 12:00 PM

La Agonía

A striking 19th-century whitewashed church offering a quiet, cool oasis; the serene atmosphere is perfect for keeping baby Mary Claire calm and comfortable during a midday nap in her carrier.

Early Morning 8:30 AM - 9:00 AM

Sabanero Statue

A landmark monument dedicated to Guanacaste's cowboy culture, surrounded by a flat, easily accessible plaza ideal for a quick, stroller-friendly photo opportunity.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Museo de Guanacaste

Housed in a restored historic building, this museum offers wide, stroller-accessible corridors and fascinating cultural exhibits that provide a quiet, air-conditioned escape from the midday heat.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Oropendola Waterfall

Located near the national park entrance, this spectacular waterfall is viewable via a sturdy, well-maintained trail. Best experienced with Mary Claire in a high-quality front carrier to enjoy the cool, misty mountain air.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 2:45 PM

La Gobernación

A beautifully restored historic government building with elegant architecture and wide, flat surrounding sidewalks that are highly stroller-friendly.

Morning 9:00 AM - 9:45 AM

Iglesia Inmaculada Concepción de María

Liberia's central white parish church featuring a vast, flat concrete plaza that is perfect for pushing a stroller while admiring the dramatic architectural lines.

Morning 8:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Leona Waterfall

Book a highly rated, slow-paced private walk with Maleku Tours to the riverbank. This allows Will and Kathryn to witness the stunning turquoise waters in a customized, baby-safe itinerary.

🍽️ Where to eat

Piso e Tierra

An authentic Costa Rican grill featuring a spacious, breezy outdoor garden terrace. It is highly accommodating for families, offering an excellent baby-friendly early dinner setting.

Sabor Porteño Restaurante

Famous for its exceptionally fresh local seafood and classic ceviche. The indoor air-conditioned dining room provides a comfortable climate for infants, complete with highchairs on request.

Cafe de Posada

A charming, quiet cafe near Parque Central boasting a tranquil courtyard. It is the perfect spot for Will and Kathryn to enjoy premium Costa Rican pour-overs while Mary Claire naps peacefully.

Restaurante Tierra Mar

A refined steak and seafood establishment known for its professional service, quiet early evening ambiance, and spacious booths that easily accommodate a portable infant seat.

El Callejón

A lovely open-air courtyard restaurant with relaxed acoustic music and generous table spacing, making it exceptionally easy to park a stroller right at your table.
