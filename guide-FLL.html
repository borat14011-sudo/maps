Fort Lauderdale

← back to travel board
With its shimmering blue waterways and golden sands, Fort Lauderdale is a tropical paradise tailor-made for families. Kids can captain water taxis, explore lush butterfly havens, and splash in gentle Atlantic waves under the warm Florida sun. It is a laid-back coastal escape where unforgettable memories are made at every turn.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Fort Lauderdale Beach Park

Splash in the calm, shallow ocean waters and play on the fully equipped beachside playground. Toddlers will love the soft sand, and parents will appreciate the plentiful shade trees and clean restroom facilities.

Afternoon / 3h

Museum of Discovery and Science

Dive into hands-on science exhibits, watch playful North American river otters, and ride a simulated airboat. Toddlers have a dedicated safe-play zone called the Discovery Center, perfect for sensory exploration.

Late Afternoon / 2h

Water Taxi Fort Lauderdale

Hop on and off this open-air boat to cruise past mega-mansions and giant yachts while feeling the cool sea breeze. Strollers are easily accommodated on board, and the gentle rocking motion is highly soothing for little ones.

Morning / 2h

Hugh Taylor Birch State Park

Escape the city bustle in this coastal oasis where you can rent surrey bikes or spot wild gopher tortoises. The paved, shady loop trail is perfect for smooth, stroller-friendly nature walks.

Morning / 2-3h

Butterfly World

Walk through giant, lush aviaries filled with thousands of colorful, free-flying butterflies and exotic birds. The gentle, fluttering creatures are mesmerizing for toddlers, but be sure to keep them on the paved pathways.

Morning / 3h

Flamingo Gardens

See native Florida wildlife like alligators, panthers, and a flock of bright pink flamingos in a gorgeous botanical setting. Toddlers can safely get up close and even hand-feed the friendly roaming flamingos.

Evening / 2h

Las Olas Boulevard & Riverwalk

Stroll down this charming, tree-lined thoroughfare to browse local boutiques and grab sweet treats. The wide, flat sidewalks are stroller-friendly, and the adjacent Riverwalk offers open grassy areas for toddlers to stretch their legs.

Weekend Morning / 2h

Yellow Green Farmers Market

Explore this massive, vibrant covered market packed with fresh tropical fruits, artisanal foods, and live music. It can get busy, so arrive early when the aisles are wider and easier to navigate with a toddler stroller.

Midday / 2h

Funderdome

Let the kids burn off energy at this giant indoor play space featuring obstacle courses, slides, and climbing walls. There is a gated, padded Toddler Zone specifically designed for safe play for children under toddler age.

Where to eat

  • Jaxson's Ice Cream Parlour — Step back in time at this legendary, country-store-themed eatery famous for its giant, kid-pleasing kitchen sink sundaes and classic American diner fare.
  • Coconuts — Enjoy casual, upscale seafood right on the Intracoastal Waterway where kids can watch yachts sail by while munching on toddler-approved coconut shrimp.
  • Quarterdeck Restaurants — A relaxed, beachy neighborhood spot featuring a diverse menu with great burgers, sushi, and a highly customizable, toddler-friendly kids' menu.
  • The Alchemist Cafe — Tucked away in a rustic, leafy garden, this hidden gem serves up delicious open-faced 'slicers' and iced coffees in a whimsical outdoor space perfect for wiggly toddlers.
👨‍👩‍👧 Utilize the free Lauderdale-by-the-Sea Pelican Hopper shuttle for easy coastal transport without the hassle of car seats and parking. Always pack high-SPF sunscreen and insect repellent, especially when visiting humid nature preserves like Flamingo Gardens.
