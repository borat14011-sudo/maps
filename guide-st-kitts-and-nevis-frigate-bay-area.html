St. Kitts and Nevis (Frigate Bay area)

Nestled on the narrow isthmus of St. Kitts, Frigate Bay offers a dual-coast paradise where the tranquil Caribbean Sea meets the dramatic Atlantic waves. This upscale enclave provides Houston finance couples with an effortless blend of high-end dining, pristine shorelines, and relaxed island sophistication. For little Mary Claire, the gentle, virtually wave-free waters of South Frigate Bay beach provide the perfect introduction to ocean splashing. Stroller-ready resort boardwalks and paved beachside pathways make navigating the lush tropical landscape with an infant completely seamless.
🚕 Getting there: Robert L. Bradshaw International Airport (SKB) to Frigate Bay: 10-minute private luxury SUV transfer with pre-installed, rear-facing premium infant car seat and ample trunk space for your folding stroller.

What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

South Frigate Bay Beach

This sheltered Caribbean beach offers exceptionally calm, shallow waters ideal for baby's first ocean dip. Rent a shaded luxury cabana for a comfortable basecamp away from the midday sun.

Morning departure 8:30-11:30am / 3h

St. Kitts Scenic Railway

Take an open-air, double-decker train ride through historic sugar plantations. The steady, gentle movement and warm Caribbean breezes are incredibly soothing and perfect for an infant nap.

Late afternoon 4-4:30pm / 30m

Timothy Hill Overlook

A paved, easily drive-up lookout spot showcasing the dramatic meeting of the Atlantic and Caribbean oceans. A quick photo-stop requiring no strenuous hiking, perfect for a stroller-friendly view.

Mid-morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Romney Manor & Caribelle Batik

Explore lush, manicured tropical gardens shaded by a magnificent 350-year-old Saman tree. Paved pathways make pushing a high-end stroller easy while parent-and-baby enjoy the vibrant textile crafts.

Early afternoon 1-3pm / 2h

The Palms Court Gardens

A tranquil, gated sanctuary featuring peaceful garden walks, a sparkling pool, and panoramic harbor views. Quiet and shaded spots are abundant, offering a peaceful environment for Mary Claire's afternoon bottle.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Brimstone Hill Fortress National Park

A magnificent UNESCO world heritage site. Stick to the lower paved grassy courtyards with a baby carrier to enjoy the historical architecture and sweeping sea views without navigating the steep stone stairs.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Clay Villa Plantation House & Gardens

A peaceful, 10-acre historic eco-estate featuring lush green gardens and rescue animals. The shade is excellent and the uncrowded atmosphere ensures a highly sensory but quiet stroll for a young family.

Late afternoon 4:30-5:30pm / 1h

North Frigate Bay Beach Walk

Take a peaceful late afternoon walk on the wide, breezy Atlantic shoreline. Use a sturdy stroller on the firm-packed sand or adjacent resort boardwalks to enjoy the dramatic, cooler ocean air.

Early morning 7:30-9am / 1.5h

Royal St. Kitts Golf Club

Even as non-golfers, families can enjoy an early morning stroller walk along the paved cart paths or a quiet breakfast at the clubhouse overlooking the immaculate, manicured greens.

🍽️ Where to eat

Marshall's Restaurant

An upscale local institution offering exquisite seafood and poolside fine dining. Request an early outdoor terrace seating at 6:00 PM to enjoy the sunset with Mary Claire while she is in a relaxed mood.

Carambola Beach Club

Sophisticated beachfront dining featuring high-end international cuisine. They offer comfortable highchairs, an accommodating staff, and flat boardwalk access right on the calm South Friars beachfront.

La Cucina

Located inside the Marriott, this elegant Northern Italian venue features a spacious, quiet dining room and high-end highchairs. It is highly accommodating to families during early evening seatings.

Shipwreck Bar & Grill

A highly beloved, casual beachfront spot nestled on South Friars. Perfect for an early lunch where baby can marvel at the wild green monkeys playing in the trees from the safety of a shaded deck.

Calypso Restaurant

A bright, airy, and spacious restaurant offering fantastic breakfast buffet options. The wide table layouts easily fit strollers, and the staff is famously warm and attentive to infants.

The only hotel situated directly on the calm Caribbean waters of South Frigate Bay. Families can book the spacious ocean-view one-bedroom suites with full kitchens, perfect for sterilizing bottles and preparing baby meals, steps away from a gentle swimming beach.

$200-300/night

Set within lush tropical gardens in Frigate Bay, this quiet property offers great value with a premium feel. Families enjoy spacious oceanfront cottages, two outdoor swimming pools, and a remarkably peaceful atmosphere free of loud, bustling crowds.
