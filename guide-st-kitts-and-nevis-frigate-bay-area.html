St. Kitts and Nevis (Frigate Bay area)

← back to travel board
Nestled between the calm Caribbean Sea and the breezy Atlantic, Frigate Bay is a sun-drenched playground perfect for families. Here, green vervet monkeys play in the palms, gentle waves lap golden sands, and historic railways spark young imaginations. It is an easygoing tropical escape where toddlers can splash safely and parents can truly unwind.
🚕 Getting there: ~10 min taxi ride from Robert L. Bradshaw International Airport (SKB) to Frigate Bay hotels.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

South Frigate Bay Beach

This sheltered Caribbean beach offers calm, shallow waters perfect for little splashers. Toddlers will love building sandcastles on the soft golden shore while parents relax under rented umbrellas.

Afternoon / 3h

St. Kitts Scenic Railway

Ride the double-decker train through lush sugarcane fields for a breezy, narrated island loop. Kids will be mesmerized by the open-air upper deck and the cheerful acapella singers.

Morning / 2h

Brimstone Hill Fortress National Park

Explore a massive UNESCO-listed fortress with panoramic ocean views and massive stone walls. The grassy parade grounds are perfect for toddlers to burn off energy, though strollers should be left in the car.

Morning / 1-2h

Caribelle Batik at Romney Manor

Stroll through magnificent botanical gardens featuring a massive, 350-year-old Saman tree. Toddlers will love watching the artisans dye colorful fabrics, and the paved paths are stroller-friendly.

Morning / 1h

Basseterre Public Market

Introduce your little ones to tropical sights and smells at this bustling local market. It is a fantastic sensory experience where you can grab fresh mangoes and bananas for toddler snacks.

Anytime / 15 mins

Timothy Hill Overlook

Take in the famous, dramatic view where the Atlantic Ocean and Caribbean Sea almost touch. It is a quick, roadside stop with secure barriers, perfect for a memorable family photo.

Morning / 4-6h

Nevis Ferry Day Trip

Board the passenger ferry from nearby Basseterre for a scenic, breezy 45-minute boat ride to sister island Nevis. The short boat ride is an exciting voyage for toddlers, landing right near shallow Pinney's Beach.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Clay Villa Plantation House & Gardens

Visit this eco-heritage site to wander through quiet, shaded gardens filled with vibrant flowers. Kids can spot rescued tortoises and colorful birds in a peaceful, uncrowded setting.

Morning / 3h

Cockleshell Bay Beach

A beautiful stretch of beach offering calm waters and a stunning view of Nevis across the channel. Toddlers can paddle in the shallows while older kids enjoy the nearby water trampolines.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Shipwreck Beach Bar & Grill

Get the classic conch fritters and fresh fruit smoothies while wild green vervet monkeys and mongooses play in the surrounding brush.

Spice Mill Restaurant

Get the grilled catch of the day with coconut rice at this relaxed beachfront eatery with plenty of space for toddlers to play on the sand.

Rituals Coffee House

Get the Nutella crepes and iced lattes at this cozy spot, ideal for a quick, kid-friendly breakfast before heading to the beach.

Carambola Beach Club

Get the fresh sushi rolls and crispy chicken tenders served right to your beach lounger on South Friars Bay.

Mr. X's Shiggidy Shack

Get the sizzling jerk chicken and fresh local fruit juices at this iconic, casual beachfront shack on the Frigate Bay Strip.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack plenty of reef-safe sunscreen and high-strength insect repellent, as the lush tropical foliage means mosquitoes are active around dusk. Renting a car with a pre-booked car seat is highly recommended for exploring, as local taxis do not typically carry toddler seats.
