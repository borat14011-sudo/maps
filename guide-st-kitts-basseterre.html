Brimstone Hill Fortress National Park

A magnificent UNESCO World Heritage site offering sweeping ocean views; the main paved areas are accessible, but bring a baby carrier for the steep, historic stone ramparts.

South Friars Bay

An incredibly calm beach cove with gentle waters perfect for dipping baby Mary Claire's toes, featuring shaded beach chairs and pristine facilities at Carambola Beach Club.

St. Kitts Scenic Railway

A historic double-decker train ride where the lower level is fully air-conditioned and stroller-friendly, offering a gentle, rocking rhythm that naturally lulls infants to sleep.

The Strip at Frigate Bay

Independence Square

A historic public park in Basseterre with wide, flat paved paths, plenty of mature shade trees, and a soothing central fountain ideal for an easy stroller stroll.
