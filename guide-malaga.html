What to do

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Jardín Botánico Histórico La Concepción

A lush, historic botanical garden offering dense tropical shade, gentle gravel paths suitable for robust strollers, and quiet spots perfect for an outdoor infant feeding session.

Early morning 9-10:30am / 1.5h

Alcazaba of Málaga

A stunning Moorish fortress with panoramic views; highly scenic but step-heavy, so skip the stroller and use a luxury baby carrier like an Artipoppe for this climb.

Afternoon 2-3pm / 1h

Málaga Cathedral

The majestic 'La Manquita' cathedral features soaring, cool interiors that offer an excellent, quiet escape from the afternoon sun while baby Mary Claire naps in her stroller.

Late morning 11am-12:30pm / 1.5h

Centre Pompidou Málaga

A highly modern, fully air-conditioned art museum beneath a colorful glass cube; completely stroller-accessible with spacious elevators and sensory visual art baby will love.

Late afternoon 4:30-6pm / 1.5h

Playa de la Malagueta

Málaga's iconic city beach featuring a wide, paved promenade perfect for stroller walks, clean facilities, and calm shores ideal for dipping baby's toes in the water.

Evening 6-7pm / 1h

Palmeral de las Sorpresas

A striking waterfront promenade designed with flat, smooth pathways, modern shaded canopies, and soothing harbor views perfect for a breezy afternoon stroll.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Museo Picasso Málaga

Housed in a beautiful palace, this stroller-friendly museum offers quiet gallery spaces and excellent climate control, making it highly comfortable for infants.

Morning 9:30-10:30am / 1h

Mercado Central de Atarazanas

A vibrant historic food market; visit early in the day when the aisles are less crowded for a sensory experience of colorful Spanish produce with easy stroller navigation.

Midday 12:30-1:30pm / 1h

Parque de Málaga

A gorgeous botanical park running parallel to the port, featuring completely flat, shaded dirt avenues, beautiful fountains, and quiet areas away from city noise.

🍽️ Where to eat

José Carlos García

A Michelin-starred culinary experience at Muelle Uno featuring a sleek, quiet outdoor terrace that comfortably accommodates strollers during early evening seatings.

El Pimpi

An iconic Andalusian tavern with a vast, lively outdoor terrace facing the Roman Theatre; highly welcoming to families with plenty of space for highchairs and ambient noise.

Los Mellizos Málaga

A premier seafood institution renowned for its fresh fish; offers a relaxed, upscale outdoor dining area and early dinner hours perfect for an infant's schedule.

Chiringuito El Cabra

An upscale beachfront restaurant in Pedregalejo serving exceptional grilled espetos on a breezy, quiet terrace where the soothing sound of waves keeps baby calm.

La Cosmopolita

A high-end, sophisticated bistro offering gourmet Andalusian tapas; request an outdoor table on the quiet pedestrian street to easily park your luxury stroller.
