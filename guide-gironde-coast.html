What to do

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

Dune du Pilat

Europe's tallest sand dune offers breathtaking panoramas over the Banc d'Arguin reserve, and while you cannot use a stroller on the sand, climbing with Mary Claire in an ergonomic carrier is highly manageable via the seasonal wooden stairs.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Parc Mauresque

Nestled in the historic Ville d’Hiver district, this lush park features tranquil botanical paths, a children's play area, and is easily accessible from the town center via a stroller-friendly public elevator that eliminates any steep climbing.

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Plage de Pereire

This pristine stretch of golden sand is backed by fragrant maritime pine trees, featuring a wide, completely flat paved promenade that is perfect for pushing a luxury stroller while taking in the serene, wave-free waters.

Morning 10am-1pm / 3h

Le Phare du Cap Ferret

Take a luxury water taxi across the bay to Cap Ferret and explore the lighthouse grounds which are paved and highly stroller-accessible, though parents will need to take turns climbing the steps for panoramic bay views.

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Réserve Naturelle des Prés Salés

This serene salt marsh reserve features wide, flat wooden boardwalk paths that are perfectly suited for strollers, offering a quiet, shaded nature walk where you can spot wild birds without disturbing the baby's nap.

Evening 6:30-7:30pm / 1h

Jetée de Thiers

The historic central pier of Arcachon is wide, flat, and completely stroller-accessible, making it the perfect spot for an early evening walk to watch the luxury yachts sail past as the sun begins to set.

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Port d'Audenge

A charming oyster port featuring calm, shallow sea-water pools that are safely cordoned off, providing a wonderfully tranquil environment for dipping Mary Claire's feet in the water away from open ocean currents.

Afternoon 2-3pm / 1h

La Source des Abatilles

Visit the beautiful historic park surrounding Arcachon's famous natural mineral spring, where you can stroll through shaded, leafy pathways and purchase pristine, baby-safe mineral water directly from the source.

Mid-day 11am-1pm / 2h

L'Île aux Oiseaux Boat Tour

Charter a private, luxury catamaran from the Arcachon pier to cruise past the famous stilted cabins, ensuring a smooth, stable ride with plenty of shaded deck space to keep Mary Claire comfortable and out of the sun.

🍽️ Where to eat

Le Pinasse Café

Savor fresh local oysters and wild turbot on a spacious, upscale wooden deck directly overlooking the water, featuring plenty of space for stroller parking and a welcoming, relaxed afternoon atmosphere.

Chez Hortense

An iconic Cap Ferret institution famous for its secret-recipe mussels and ocean views, offering an open-air terrace with large tables that easily accommodate families and a warm, buzzy vibe that easily masks infant giggles.

Le Patio

Indulge in Michelin-starred French cuisine by Thierry Renou in a serene, intimate courtyard setting that offers early evening reservations, allowing parents to enjoy high gastronomy while baby sleeps peacefully in her stroller.

Club Plage Pereire

Enjoy chic beachfront dining with your feet in the sand at this highly rated venue, featuring a relaxed outdoor lounge layout, exceptional seafood, and a gentle breeze that keeps the midday heat manageable for infants.

Chez Yvon

A premium, authentic oyster shack located in the picturesque village of L'Herbe, offering fresh seafood platters on a quiet, shaded wooden terrace that is incredibly welcoming to families with young children.
