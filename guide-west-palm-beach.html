West Palm Beach

← back to travel board
West Palm Beach blends tropical charm with vibrant, family-friendly culture along Florida's Atlantic coast. From gentle lagoon waters and lush botanical sanctuaries to interactive science hubs, it is a playground for all generations. Families can easily transition from slow-paced beach mornings to exciting wildlife encounters by afternoon.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min ride via rideshare or rental car from Palm Beach International Airport (PBI) to downtown hotels

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Cox Science Center and Aquarium

This interactive museum features touch tanks, a digital planetarium, and an outdoor science trail. Toddlers will love the sensory play areas and the backyard splash pad.

Morning / 3-4h

Lion Country Safari

Drive your own vehicle through a preserve home to rhinos, lions, and zebras, followed by an adventure park walkthrough. Toddlers can safely feed the giraffes and cool off in the water play park.

Morning / 1-2h

Manatee Lagoon

An eco-discovery center dedicated to Florida manatees, offering hands-on exhibits and prime viewing during cooler months. Stroller-friendly boardwalks make it easy to spot wild manatees with toddlers.

Morning / 2-3h

Palm Beach Zoo & Conservation Society

This lushly landscaped zoo features shaded pathways, exotic animals, and a great carousel. The central fountain is a toddler favorite for cooling down on warm days.

Morning / 2h

McCarthy's Wildlife Sanctuary

Get up close to rescued panthers, tigers, and birds of prey on a private guided tour. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the close-up animal encounters, but book weeks in advance.

Saturday Morning / 2h

West Palm Beach GreenMarket

A massive, award-winning Saturday market along the scenic waterfront filled with fresh pastries, local fruits, and crafts. The grassy lawn provides the perfect open space for toddlers to toddle to live music.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Mounts Botanical Garden

A peaceful oasis featuring 25 spectacular gardens, a giant koi pond, and a children's maze. Feeding the hungry koi fish from the floating bridge is an absolute thrill for toddlers.

Morning / 3-4h

Peanut Island Park

Take a short water taxi ride to this island park renowned for its crystal-clear water and shallow snorkeling. The extremely calm, shallow lagoon is a dream wading spot for toddlers.

Evening / 2h

The Square

A beautifully designed outdoor shopping and dining district with a gorgeous wishing tree and interactive water features. Let toddlers run safely on the astroturf lawn and play in the pop-up fountains.

🍽️ Where to eat

Sloan's Ice Cream

Get a scoop of Tracy's Scrumptious Cookie Salad ice cream in this famous, whimsically pink parlor.

Howley's Restaurant

Order the giant buttermilk pancakes and a chocolate milkshake at this retro 1950s family diner.

Pizza Al Fresco

Order the wood-fired Margherita pizza to share under the colorful umbrellas in this beautiful, historic courtyard.

Cholo Soy Cocina

Grab their award-winning, scratch-made steak tacos and yuca fries to enjoy in the casual, colorful backyard patio.

Dune Dog Cafe

Try the famous char-grilled Dune Dog in a fun, ultra-casual, open-air tiki hut atmosphere.
