West Palm Beach welcomes discerning families with its blend of upscale metropolitan charm and tranquil coastal beauty. The city's crown jewel for young families is the waterfront Lake Trail, offering miles of paved, traffic-free strolling beneath swaying palms. Here, finance-forward parents can unwind along the Intracoastal Waterway while baby Mary Claire enjoys the gentle Atlantic breezes in her stroller. With its manicured parks, quiet luxury boutiques, and accessible luxury resorts, it is the ultimate stress-free sanctuary for a high-end infant getaway.
🚕 Getting there: A quick 10-minute drive from Palm Beach International Airport via a pre-arranged private luxury SUV transfer equipped with a premium rear-facing infant car seat.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM (2.5 hours)

Palm Beach Zoo & Conservation Society

A lush, shaded oasis featuring easy-to-navigate paved paths perfect for strollers. The quiet, canopy-covered walkways keep baby Mary Claire cool while you observe tropical birds and panthers.

Afternoon 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM (2 hours)

Cox Science Center and Aquarium

Offering a fully air-conditioned escape from the afternoon sun, this interactive center features a beautiful sensory-rich aquarium. The peaceful, glowing fish tanks provide a calming visual experience for a 12-month-old.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM (2 hours)

The Square

This beautifully landscaped, pedestrian-only shopping district is completely stroller-friendly. Enjoy the soothing water fountains and open grassy lawns where Mary Claire can practice crawling during the cooler morning hours.

Early Morning 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM (2 hours)

Mounts Botanical Garden

Palm Beach County's oldest and largest public garden offers exceptionally tranquil, paved walking paths. The gentle sound of trickling streams and vibrant tropical flowers make it a serene, low-sensory environment for an infant nap.

Morning 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM (1.5 hours)

Palm Beach Lake Trail

Just across the bridge, this iconic, paved trail offers breathtaking views of the Intracoastal Waterway. It is completely free of motor vehicles, making it the premier spot for an uninterrupted sunrise stroller jog.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM (1.5 hours)

Manatee Lagoon

An educational waterfront center offering a barrier-free boardwalk perfect for viewing wild manatees in the warm water discharges. The facility is fully ADA-accessible with spacious elevators and clean, family-friendly changing restrooms.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM (2 hours)

Norton Museum of Art

Boasting world-class exhibitions, the wide and quiet galleries of the Norton are highly stroller-accessible and air-conditioned. The peaceful outdoor sculpture garden offers a beautiful space to wander if baby gets fussy.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM (2 hours)

Phipps Ocean Park

A pristine beach park that offers convenient wooden boardwalks extending close to the shoreline. The park features shady sea-grape trees, making it easy to set up a beach tent and let Mary Claire dip her toes in the calm Atlantic tide.

Saturday Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM (2 hours)

West Palm Beach GreenMarket

Ranked as one of the best outdoor markets in the country, this Saturday morning staple along the waterfront is vibrant yet highly navigable. Wide walkways allow easy stroller passage while you sample gourmet local pastries.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Regional Kitchen & Public House

Acclaimed Chef Lindsay Autry's chic spot features a spacious, breezy outdoor terrace. The signature Southern table-side preparation is a delight, and the early dinner seating at 5:00 PM easily accommodates high chairs and sleepy infants.

Elisabetta's Ristorante

This sprawling waterfront Italian eatery offers a lively, energetic atmosphere where minor baby noises blend seamlessly into the background. Request a patio table overlooking the water to enjoy handmade pasta while keeping the stroller right next to you.

Galley

Located inside the Hilton, this chic restaurant features a gorgeous outdoor garden patio with comfortable fireside seating. Their wood-fired pizzas are outstanding, and the staff is remarkably accommodating with high chairs and infant requests.

Table 26 Palm Beach

A sophisticated neighborhood classic known for global comfort food like lobster mac and cheese. Book an early 5:00 PM table inside their plush dining room, where the exceptionally professional service team caters to families with quiet grace.

Rocco's Tacos and Tequila Bar

A highly energetic Clematis Street staple featuring fresh tableside guacamole. The bustling, upbeat atmosphere is perfect for masking occasional baby cries, and they gladly offer spacious booth seating with plenty of room for an infant carrier.
