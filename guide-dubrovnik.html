Baby Mary Claire will love the gentle, wave-free waters of quiet local coves, while parents can indulge in world-class seafood overlooking the ancient city walls. This curated itinerary balances high-end Mediterranean leisure with seamless logistics designed for an upscale infant getaway.

What to do

Early morning 8-10am

The Stradun (Placa) Morning Walk

Stroll Dubrovnik's limestone main street early before the crowds arrive. The polished stone is entirely flat and highly stroller-accessible, with plenty of morning shade from the towering stone buildings.

Morning 9:30am-12:30pm

Lokrum Island Botanical Gardens

Take a scenic 15-minute ferry ride to this car-free island oasis. The shaded pine forest pathways are perfect for a baby carrier, and Mary Claire will be delighted by the friendly peacocks roaming the lush gardens.

Late afternoon 4-6pm

Lapad Peninsula Promenade

A gorgeous, entirely car-free paved coastal path fringed by pine trees. Offers gentle ocean breezes, plenty of shade, and smooth terrain for a premium stroller, ending near calm baby-friendly shallow bays.

Afternoon 2-5pm

Cavtat Riva Stroller Walk

An upscale, quieter seaside town just south of Dubrovnik. Its flat, palm-fringed harbor promenade is ideal for a relaxed stroller walk away from the Old Town crowds, offering beautiful yacht-watching for parents.

Morning 9-10:30am

Dubrovnik Cable Car to Mount Srd

Ascend Mount Srd in a fully enclosed, spacious cabin that easily accommodates a folded stroller. The panoramic summit offers jaw-dropping views of the old town and a quiet indoor viewing area to keep baby out of the wind.

Morning 9-11:30am

Banje Beach Club Morning Cabana

Reserve a private, luxury shaded daybed right on the beach. The morning hours offer calm, crystal-clear shallow waters perfect for dipping Mary Claire's toes in the Adriatic before the afternoon music and crowds start.

Midday 11am-1h

Trsteno Arboretum

A historic, lush Renaissance garden overlooking the sea. The shaded pathways, ancient fountain with goldfish, and peaceful atmosphere make it an exceptional spot for a midday contact nap in the baby carrier.

Midday 12-1:30pm

Rector's Palace Courtyard

Step inside this Gothic-Renaissance palace to escape the midday heat. The grand, stone courtyard is heavily shaded, cool, and flat on the ground floor, offering a tranquil sanctuary for parents and baby.

Afternoon 3-6pm

Copacabana Beach Luxury Daybeds

An upscale family-friendly beach in Lapad featuring concrete access ramps for easy stroller wheeling. Large luxury cabanas provide excellent sun protection, and the gentle, shallow sea entry is ideal for infants.

🍽️ Where to eat

Proto Fish Restaurant

Savor Michelin-recommended seafood on the elegant, quiet second-floor outdoor terrace. Book an early 6:00 PM seating for high-class service, highchairs on request, and a peaceful ambiance before the peak dinner rush.

Panorama Restaurant

Dine on the peak of Mount Srd with spectacular vistas. The open-air terrace is spacious enough for a stroller, and the early evening breeze provides a soothing sensory experience for baby while parents enjoy fine Mediterranean fare.

Bowa Šipan

An ultra-luxury day-trip beach restaurant located on Šipan Island. Book a private overwater wooden cabana where baby Mary Claire can safely nap on a shaded daybed while you enjoy fresh-caught fish and premium Croatian wines.

Restaurant Dubrovnik

Fine dining situated on a quiet, upscale rooftop terrace in the heart of the Old Town. Known for its highly attentive staff who are incredibly accommodating to families during their early evening service.

Pantarul

A highly rated, contemporary bistro in Lapad that warmly welcomes families. They offer top-tier highchairs, a relaxed upscale atmosphere, and a menu of fresh, locally sourced ingredients that can easily be customized for a toddler's palate.

$250-380/night (Shoulder Season)

A world-class luxury resort located just outside the city. It features spacious multi-bedroom residences with full kitchens, a dedicated family pool, lush stroller-friendly botanical gardens, and professional baby-sitting services on request.

$320-400/night (Shoulder Season)
