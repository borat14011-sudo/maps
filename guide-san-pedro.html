What to do

Morning / 3h

Hol Chan Marine Reserve

Snorkel among vibrant coral gardens teeming with friendly nurse sharks and gentle stingrays. Toddlers will love peering over the boat side into the crystal-clear, shallow water to spot sea turtles.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Secret Beach

Located on the sheltered leeward side of the island, this beach features warm, waist-deep water with zero waves. It is the absolute perfect natural wading pool for toddlers to splash safely all afternoon.

Evening / 2h

The Truck Stop

This lively shipping container food park features outdoor family games, a swimming pool, and delicious local ice cream. It is highly kid-friendly with plenty of open space for toddlers to toddle around.

Morning / 1h

Belize Chocolate Company

Take a short, sweet class to learn how organic cacao beans are transformed into delicious chocolate. Toddlers will love tasting the sweet chocolate samples and playing with the cacao beans.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Central Park Playground

This oceanfront beach park features a colorful climbing structure where local and visiting kids play together. Parents can relax on the sand while toddlers swing with a view of the barrier reef.

Morning / 2h

Glass Bottom Boat Reef Tour

Experience the wonder of the Belize Barrier Reef without getting wet on a guided glass-bottom boat excursion. It is an ideal, stress-free way for toddlers and non-swimmers to view colorful fish.

Morning / 1.5h

Marco Gonzalez Maya Site

Explore an ancient Maya archaeological site surrounded by jungle and mangroves on the south end of the island. The shaded boardwalk trail is short and easy for little legs to explore.

Morning / 4h

Bacalar Chico National Park

Take a scenic boat tour through the historic mangrove canals dug by ancient Mayas. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the nesting coastal birds and the chance to spot wild manatees.

Afternoon / 1h

San Pedro House of Culture

This small, welcoming community center showcases local art, music history, and cultural exhibits. It is a quiet, air-conditioned sanctuary perfect for a sensory-friendly toddler break.

🍽️ Where to eat

Elvi's Kitchen

Order the famous Maya-style coconut curry chicken while your kids enjoy playing in the unique indoor sand floor.

Estel's Dine By the Sea

Get the fluffy, fresh-fried Mayan fry jacks served beachfront so toddlers can dig their toes in the sand.

Caramba! Restaurant

Pick your own fresh catch from the daily ice display and have it prepared family-style as coconut-crusted fish fingers.

Lily's Treasure Chest

Feast on world-class conch ceviche at beachside tables where kids can watch boats pull up to the dock.

Robin's Kitchen

Savor incredibly tender, perfectly spiced jerk chicken served in a casual roadside yard with a warm, family atmosphere.
