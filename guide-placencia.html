Placencia

Placencia offers a sleepy, high-end Caribbean vibe along its golden sixteen-mile peninsula. Will and Kathryn can unwind on the peaceful shores of Maya Beach, where the ocean remains as calm and warm as a bath. The famous pedestrian-only Placencia Sidewalk provides a completely stroller-ready promenade lined with colorful boutiques and lush hibiscus. With private beach loungers and shaded palms just steps from your suite, transitioning baby Mary Claire from naptime to playtime is effortless.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 2h

Placencia Sidewalk

Strap Mary Claire into her stroller for a morning walk along this famously narrow, paved pedestrian-only boardwalk. It is completely car-free, highly shaded by tropical palms, and lined with gentle art stalls.

Late Afternoon 3:30 PM - 5:30 PM / 2h

Maya Beach

A pristine stretch of soft golden sand with exceptionally calm, shallow waters. Perfect for setting up a pop-up beach tent so Mary Claire can safely dip her toes in the tranquil Caribbean Sea.

Morning to Afternoon 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM / 5h

Laughing Bird Caye National Park

Book a private catamaran charter to this protected island. The island features flat sandy beaches, private shaded palapas, and crystal-clear shallow shores ideal for a relaxed family picnic and wading.

Evening 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM / 1.5h

Placencia Municipal Pier and Plaza

A wide, fully stroller-accessible harbor pier located at the southern tip of the peninsula. Perfect for catching the cool sunset breeze while watching sailboats glint on the water.

Early Morning 8:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 3h

Cockscomb Basin Wildlife Sanctuary

Opt for a gentle baby-wearing hike along the flat, heavily shaded 'River Overlook' trail. The dense canopy keeps the heat low, allowing Kathryn and Will to enjoy lush rainforest sights without stroller obstacles.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 1.5h

Seine Bight Village

A cultural excursion to experience traditional Garifuna drumming. Arrange a private, low-volume mid-morning demonstration where baby Mary Claire can enjoy the rhythmic beats without overwhelming noise.

Morning 8:30 AM - 12:30 PM / 4h

Monkey River Boat Tour

A private, covered motorboat tour up the scenic Monkey River. Spot gentle howler monkeys and toucans from the safety and shade of the boat canopy; infant life vests and noise-canceling headphones are recommended.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM / 1.5h

The Moorings Marina

Take a peaceful stroll down the concrete docks of the marina. It is highly stroller-friendly, safe, and offers lovely views of luxury yachts and calm lagoon waters.

Morning to Afternoon 9:00 AM - 1:30 PM / 4.5h

Silk Cayes

Charter a private boat to these tiny, picture-perfect sand spits. Set up a spacious sun shelter on the soft sand while Will and Kathryn take turns snorkeling the vibrant, shallow reef surrounding the island.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Maya Beach Hotel Bistro

Award-winning beachside eatery offering a signature cacao-pork tenderloin. Features an outdoor wooden deck right on the sand, accommodating highchairs, and an early dinner seating that aligns with infant bedtime routines.

Rumfish y Vino

An upscale, airy gastro-bar serving fresh local snapper crudo. Their second-story veranda offers breezy outdoor seating, excellent highchairs, and a lively but gentle ambient noise level that easily masks baby babbles.

Inky's 19th Hole Restaurant

Spacious and fully air-conditioned family restaurant at Sirenian Bay. Known for gourmet flatbreads, extensive highchairs, kid-friendly staff, and a modern, stroller-accessible layout.

Tutti Frutti Gelateria

An iconic, authentic Italian gelato spot right on the main street. Features a stroller-friendly entrance ramp and delicious, fresh fruit sorbets perfect for keeping everyone cool in the afternoon heat.

Omar's Creole Grub

A welcoming, casual local institution serving outstanding coconut curry lobster. The outdoor garden patio is highly relaxed, making it a stress-free spot for Will and Kathryn to dine with an active toddler.
