Placencia

Welcome to Placencia, a peaceful peninsula in southern Belize where gentle turquoise waves meet golden sands. This car-free village boasts colorful wooden boardwalks and lush jungle rivers, offering a safe, slow-paced paradise for families. From spotting wild howler monkeys to building sandcastles under swaying palms, it is the ultimate hassle-free tropical getaway.
🚕 Getting there: ~30 min local flight from BZE to Placencia airstrip + 10 min taxi, or a scenic ~3 hour private shuttle ride direct to your beach resort

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Cockscomb Basin Wildlife Sanctuary

Hike the flat, lush trails of the world's first jaguar sanctuary, keeping an eye out for colorful birds and exotic frogs. Toddlers will love splashing in the shallow, cool river streams along the easier paths.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

The Placencia Sidewalk

Wander the world's narrowest main street, filled with colorful wooden shops, local art, and overhanging palms. It is completely car-free, making it extremely safe for toddlers to toddle along beside you.

Morning to Afternoon / 4-5h

Laughing Bird Caye Snorkeling

Take a gentle boat ride to this picture-perfect national park caye for easy, shallow reef snorkeling right off the white sand beach. The calm, shallow shorelines are ideal for toddlers to splash safely under palm shade.

Morning / 3-4h

Monkey River Boat Tour

Cruise down the jungle river to spot wild howler monkeys, sunbathing iguanas, and crocodiles from the safety of a boat. The deep jungle sounds are thrilling for toddlers, and the boat ride is breezy and relaxed.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Placencia Municipal Pier and Beach

Walk down to the southern tip of the peninsula to watch local fishermen and play on the calm, wave-free beach. The adjacent playground is perfect for toddlers to burn off energy before sunset.

Afternoon / 1.5h

Taste Belize Chocolate Workshop

Learn how to make traditional Maya chocolate from organic cacao beans in a fun, sensory-rich workshop. Toddlers will delight in mashing the warm chocolate paste and tasting the sweet, natural treats.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Lebeha Drumming Center in Seine Bight

Introduce your kids to vibrant Garifuna culture with interactive drumming lessons just a short drive north of town. Toddlers can easily beat along on miniature drums to the lively, welcoming rhythms.

Morning / 1h

Placencia Farmers Market

Browse the vibrant, open-air stalls near the main road for sweet local bananas, mangoes, and fresh coconut water. Toddlers can enjoy sampling sweet tropical fruits while watching the bustling local life.

Morning / 4-5h

Nim Li Punit Mayan Site

Venture inland to this shady, grassy Maya site featuring impressive stelae carvings and easy-to-climb ancient plazas. The wide grassy lawns are perfect for toddlers to run around safely beneath giant forest canopies.

🍽️ Where to eat

Tutti Frutti Gelateria

Treat the family to authentic, creamy Italian gelato with tropical flavors like mango and coconut that toddlers will love.

The Shak Beach Cafe

Grab a shaded beachside table and order the famous banana-chocolate smoothies and fluffy coconut pancakes.

Wendy's Creole Restaurant & Bar

Enjoy a relaxed, family-friendly dinner of traditional Belizean stewed chicken with rice and beans.

Rumfish y Vino

Sit on the airy upper deck and savor fresh fish tacos or local lobster sliders in a vibrant but kid-welcoming atmosphere.

Cha Chi's

Feast on delicious wood-fired pizzas and fresh pastas in a casual, open-air space with live music that keeps kids entertained.

👨‍👩‍👧 Bring a lightweight, sturdy stroller or a toddler carrier for the narrow wooden sidewalk, as sand can make heavy wheels difficult. Always pack eco-friendly, reef-safe sunscreen and toddler-strength insect repellent for jungle excursions.
