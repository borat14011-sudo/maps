Valencia

← back to travel board
Valencia marries avant-garde architecture with golden Mediterranean shores, creating a sun-drenched sanctuary for discerning families. The city's crown jewel, the lush Turia Gardens, snakes through the center, offering miles of completely flat, stroller-friendly paved pathways beneath shaded palms. For Will and Kathryn, the wide boardwalk of Playa de la Malvarrosa provides seamless beachfront access perfect for morning strolls with baby Mary Claire. With its relaxed Spanish pace, gentle sea breezes, and world-class culinary scene, Valencia delivers a sophisticated yet incredibly stress-free luxury escape.
🚕 Getting there: Valencia Airport to the beachfront or city center takes exactly 15 to 20 minutes; the best luxury family transport is a private Mercedes V-Class minivan transfer pre-booked with a premium Cybex infant car seat and ample trunk space for a full-size stroller.

What to do

Early morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

L'Oceanogràfic

Europe's largest aquarium features breathtaking underwater tunnels and climate-controlled galleries, offering a sensory wonderland for Mary Claire that is entirely stroller-accessible and peacefully quiet during morning hours.

Late afternoon 4:00-6:00pm / 2h

Turia Gardens (Jardí del Túria)

This lush green lung built in a diverted riverbed is entirely flat and shaded by orange trees; perfect for a long, smooth stroller walk to let Mary Claire nap while you admire the bridges.

Morning 10:00am-12:30pm / 2.5h

Bioparc Valencia

An immersive, barrier-free zoo designed with 'zoo-immersion' concepts, offering wide, smoothly paved pathways and lush canopies that provide excellent shade and gentle animal viewing for infants.

Morning 9:00-10:30am / 1.5h

Playa de la Malvarrosa Promenade

The wide, flat paved boardwalk flanking the beach is completely free of steps, allowing Will and Kathryn to enjoy the sea breeze with the stroller while stopping for a refreshing drink.

Mid-day 12:00-1:30pm / 1.5h

Valencia Cathedral (La Seu)

Steeped in history, the cathedral's cool stone interior provides instant heat relief for baby; the main nave is stroller-accessible, though you should skip the narrow spiral steps of the Miguelete tower.

Morning 9:00-10:30am / 1.5h

Mercado Central

A masterpiece of modernist architecture, this bustling market features wide aisles and vibrant displays of fresh fruit; visit early to easily navigate the stroller before the heavy lunch crowds arrive.

Afternoon 3:00-4:15pm / 1.25h

Jardines de Monforte

A stunning, neoclassical formal garden filled with marble statues, quiet ponds, and shaded bougainvillea tunnels, offering a tranquil sanctuary away from city noises for a peaceful feeding.

Morning 11:00am-12:00pm / 1h

La Lonja de la Seda

This UNESCO-listed late Gothic silk exchange features towering spiral columns and a serene, walled courtyard filled with orange trees, providing a cool, quiet outdoor space for a sensory break.

Late afternoon 5:00-6:30pm / 1.5h

Parque de Cabecera

Located at the western end of the Turia Gardens, this scenic park features a large lake, gentle grassy hills, and shaded wooden boardwalks ideal for a quiet family picnic under the trees.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Pepica

This legendary beachfront institution offers world-class traditional paella on a spacious outdoor terrace overlooking the sea, complete with sturdy highchairs and a lively ambiance where baby noises easily blend in.

Panorama

Perched over the Mediterranean at the Marina, this upscale venue offers breathtaking coastal views, a shaded outdoor terrace, a gentle sea breeze to keep baby cool, and plenty of space for strollers.

Vertical

Boasting a Michelin star and sweeping views of the City of Arts and Sciences, this sophisticated spot accommodates families beautifully during their early lunch seating with spacious table layouts.

Casa Montaña

A historic and highly refined tapas tavern in El Cabanyal; booking an early 1:00 PM lunch slot guarantees a spacious corner table to accommodate a stroller alongside gourmet local wines and cured meats.

Ricard Camarena Restaurant

Located in the Bombas Gens art center, this double Michelin-starred gem offers an ultra-chic, calm, and spacious dining room where refined gastronomy meets a welcoming, modern Spanish family attitude.
