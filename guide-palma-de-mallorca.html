Palma de Mallorca

← back to travel board
Palma de Mallorca blends centuries-old grandeur with a laid-back Mediterranean rhythm, making it ideal for a luxury family escape. The old town's honey-hued sandstone and the vast Gothic La Seu cathedral create a dramatic backdrop for stroller-friendly promenades. For your infant, the shallow, turquoise waters of Cala Major offer safe, calm swimming just steps from a sunbed. Many hotels provide cribs, blackout curtains, and private plunge pools to ensure both parents and baby Mary Claire feel pampered.
🚕 Getting there: From Palma de Mallorca Airport (PMI) to central Palma's luxury hotel strip is a 10-minute drive. Book a private transfer with a car seat (e.g., Mallorca Transfers) – they accommodate strollers and infant luggage seamlessly, avoiding taxi queues.

What to do

9-10:30am

Catedral de Mallorca (La Seu)

Awe-inspiring Gothic cathedral with a giant rose window. Stroller-accessible via ramp; shaded cloister for a break. Skip if loud echoes bother baby – visit early morning for calm.

10:30am-12:30pm (2h)

Palma Aquarium

Indoor marine park with huge shark tank and touch pools. Fully stroller-friendly, climate-controlled, and quiet zones for infant feeding. Great for a midday escape from heat.

4-5:30pm (1.5h)

Bellver Castle

Circular castle on a hill with panoramic views. Stroller accessible via paved path (gentle slope). Shady pine grove nearby for picnic. Skip if baby naps – the climb may wake them.

10am-12pm (2h)

Fundació Pilar i Joan Miró

Studio and garden of artist Joan Miró. Stroller-friendly paths through sculpture garden. Quiet indoor galleries with minimal noise. Great for a cultural stroll with baby.

9-11am (2h)

Playa de Cala Major

Urban beach with calm, shallow waters. Soft sand, shade umbrellas, and a boardwalk for strollers. Perfect for baby's first splash. Lifeguards present.

4-5:30pm (1.5h)

Jardines de Marivent

Royal botanical gardens with sculpted hedges and fountains. Stroller-friendly paved paths, plenty of shade, and benches for feeding. Quiet and serene.

10am-12pm (2h)

Pueblo Español

Open-air architectural museum with replica Spanish buildings. Stroller-friendly flat paths. Fun for older kids but calm for infants. Shaded courtyards available.

5-6pm (1h)

Parc de la Mar

Lakeside park beneath the cathedral. Stroller-friendly boardwalk, gentle reflections, and shade from palm trees. Perfect for a relaxed stroll or baby nap.

11am-12:30pm (1.5h)

Es Baluard Museum of Modern Art

Contemporary art museum with spacious galleries and a terrace. Stroller elevator access. Quiet ambiance, baby changing facilities. Great for a cultural hour.

🍽️ Where to eat

Marc Fosh

Michelin-starred Mediterranean tasting menu. Highchairs available, quiet early seating (7pm), terrace with shade. Baby-friendly without compromising luxury.

Ola del Mar

Seafood restaurant with sea views at Club de Mar. Highchairs, outdoor terrace, calm atmosphere. Signature grilled lobster. Ideal for early dinner with baby.

Forn de Sant Joan

Traditional Mallorcan cuisine in a historic stone building. Highchairs, quiet courtyard, early bird menu. Try the lamb canelons. Perfect for a relaxed family meal.

La Rosa de Nadal

Cozy Mediterranean bistro with a terrace. Highchairs, moderate noise, early seating at 7pm. Signature paella. Infant-friendly atmosphere.

Can Eduardo

Family-run seafood restaurant near the marina. Highchairs, gentle lighting, and a calm interior. Fish of the day grilled. Good for a casual luxury lunch.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pharmacies (e.g., Farmacia Moll) are plentiful in Palma and sell infant formula, nappies, and baby sunscreen – stock up upon arrival. The old town's cobblestone can be bumpy for strollers; consider a travel-friendly stroller with large wheels or use the boardwalk along the marina for smoother terrain. For medical concerns, Hospital Universitari Son Espases has a pediatric emergency unit open 24/7; keep their number handy. Manage heat by planning outdoor activities for early morning or late afternoon, and use lightweight cotton clothing and a UV-protective stroller cover.
