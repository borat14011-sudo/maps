Sun-drenched shores and gentle waves for tiny explorers

Cozumel

← back to travel board
Cozumel welcomes families with its crystal-clear, shallow turquoise waters and a warm, laid-back island rhythm. Beyond the pristine beaches, you'll discover lush jungle parks, gentle wildlife encounters, and vibrant local plazas perfect for stroller walks. It is a safe, tropical playground where little ones can splash safely while parents soak up the Caribbean sun.

What to do

Morning / 4-5h

Chankanaab Beach Adventure Park

Swim in a calm, naturally sheltered lagoon and walk through tropical gardens filled with replica Mayan ruins. Toddler note: The shallow, sandy inlet is completely wave-free, making it perfect for tiny splashers.

Midday / 3-4h

Playa Mia Grand Beach Park

Enjoy a bustling beach club featuring a dedicated Buccaneer's Bay kids' pool with mini-slides and water cannons. Toddler note: The splash zone is shallow and fully fenced, allowing parents to relax on nearby loungers.

Morning / 3-4h

Punta Sur Eco Beach Park

Climb a historic lighthouse for panoramic views and spot wild crocodiles from safe wooden boardwalks over the lagoon. Toddler note: Stick to the calm Celarain beach area where the water is like a giant, shallow bathtub.

Morning / 4h

Cozumel Pearl Farm

Ride a gentle boat to a secluded, family-owned pearl farm nestled in a protected mangrove cove. Toddler note: The boat ride is smooth and short, and the private beach has hammocks perfect for toddler naptime.

Evening / 1-2h

San Miguel Central Plaza

Stroll around the vibrant main square to watch the dancing fountains and listen to occasional live music. Toddler note: The plaza is completely pedestrianized, giving toddlers plenty of safe space to toddle around.

Morning / 1-2h

Mercado Municipal

Immerse your senses in this bustling local market filled with colorful tropical fruits and handcrafted souvenirs. Toddler note: Keep little ones in a carrier as the aisles can be narrow, but they will love sampling sweet, fresh mango slices.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Kun Che Park

Experience Mayan purification rituals, traditional ball games, and folkloric dancing in a lush jungle setting. Toddler note: The shaded walking paths are flat and easy for small feet, keeping kids cool under the forest canopy.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Playa Palancar

Unwind at this tranquil, rustic beach club known for its soft white sand, calm waters, and shady palm trees. Toddler note: The gentle slope of the shoreline makes it incredibly safe for toddlers to play with buckets at the water's edge.

Midday / 2h

Cozumel Planetarium Cha'an Ka'an

Escape the midday heat in this modern planetarium featuring interactive science exhibits and colorful dome shows. Toddler note: The virtual reality area is for older kids, but toddlers will be mesmerized by the glowing ocean-themed projection room.

Where to eat

  • El Moro — Tucked away from the tourist strip, this cozy, family-run spot serves authentic Mexican dishes like Cochinita Pibil in a colorful patio setting that welcomes kids with open arms.
  • Buccanos — Enjoy fresh, upscale seafood on a beautiful oceanfront deck where kids can watch fish swim in the clear waters below while eating toddler-friendly sides.
  • Las Palmitas — Grab a quick, budget-friendly breakfast inside the municipal market, featuring fresh fruit smoothies and soft, handmade empanadas that are easy for toddlers to hold.
  • Chocolateria Isla Bella — Treat the family to artisan, locally made chocolates and refreshing dairy-free fruit popsicles that are perfect for cooling down on a warm afternoon.
