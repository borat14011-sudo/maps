Cozumel

← back to travel board
Cozumel welcomes families with its crystal-clear turquoise waters, vibrant coral reefs, and laid-back island rhythm. Beyond the bustling cruise ports lies a pocket of paradise where lush jungles meet shallow, wave-free beaches perfect for tiny explorers. It is a safe, easy-to-navigate haven where multi-generational memories are made under the Mexican sun.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Chankanaab Beach Adventure Park

Swim in a calm, shallow lagoon surrounded by tropical fish and explore lush botanical gardens featuring replica Mayan ruins. Toddlers will love the sea-lion show and playing in the soft, shaded sand areas.

Morning / 3-4h

Punta Sur Eco Beach State Park

Climb the historic Celarain Lighthouse for panoramic views, spot wild crocodiles from a secure wooden boardwalk, and splash in the shallowest waters on the island. The calm, wave-free shores of the internal lagoon are ideal for toddlers.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Playa Palancar

Enjoy a tranquil, rustic beach club with soft white sand, shade-giving palm trees, and incredibly gentle waves. It is much quieter than the mega-parks, making it easy to keep a relaxed eye on toddlers playing by the shoreline.

Morning / 2h

Pueblo del Maíz

Immerse your family in ancient Mayan culture through interactive theater, music, and hands-on traditional chocolate making. Toddlers will love the sensory experience of clay painting and tasting sweet, natural treats.

Morning / 1-2h

Mercado Municipal Benito Juárez

Wander through this bustling local market filled with colorful tropical fruits, handcrafted souvenirs, and sizzling authentic food stalls. Go early to beat the heat and crowds, and pick up fresh, pre-sliced papaya or mango for a toddler-friendly snack.

Afternoon / 2h

Discover Mexico Park Cozumel

Walk through a beautiful outdoor garden featuring intricate, scale models of Mexico's most famous pyramids and historic Spanish colonial buildings. The completely paved, stroller-friendly paths and on-site chocolate workshop make this an incredibly easy walk for little legs.

Morning to Afternoon / 4-5h

Playa Mia Grand Beach Park

Experience a bustling beach park featuring Bucaneers' Bay water park, complete with miniature water cannons, slides, and a pirate ship. The shallow splash pool area is specifically designed for safe, wet fun for toddlers and babies.

Morning / 2h

San Gervasio Mayan Archaeological Site

Explore the ancient sanctuary of the Mayan goddess Ixchel, nestled deep within a shady jungle filled with curious iguanas. While rocky paths make a sturdy baby carrier necessary rather than a stroller, toddlers will be captivated by the lizards sunning on ancient stones.

Morning / 3-4h

El Cielo Sandbar

Take a short boat ride to a breathtaking, waist-deep sandbar famous for its crystal-clear water and thousands of giant starfish. Booking a private boat tour lets you customize the schedule so toddlers can safely splash in what looks like a giant, calm swimming pool.

Where to eat

  • El Moro — A colorful, family-run hidden gem off the beaten path serving incredibly authentic Yucatan dishes like Cochinita Pibil in a welcoming, casual courtyard.
  • Buccanos Beach Club — Enjoy oceanfront dining with upscale Mexican seafood, while the kids play safely in the nearby sand or the restaurant's ocean-side pool.
  • Chocolatería Isla Bella — A charming, family-owned chocolate shop where you can taste handmade artisan chocolates and even watch the delicate crafting process.
  • Taqueria El Foco — A lively, laid-back spot downtown famous for its delicious tacos al pastor and friendly staff who are exceptionally welcoming to children.
