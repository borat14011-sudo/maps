Zürich

← back to travel board
Zürich blends pristine lakeside beauty with incredibly efficient, family-friendly spaces where toddlers can roam freely. From cobblestone old town lanes to mountaintop playgrounds, this clean, walkable city feels like an urban fairy tale. Parents will love the seamless public transit and the abundance of green parks right on the water's edge.
🚕 Getting there: ~10-15 minutes via direct S-Bahn train from Zurich Airport to Zurich Main Station, which is highly stroller-accessible and faster than a taxi.

What to do

Afternoon / 1.5h

Lake Zürich Boat Cruise (Zürichsee)

Board a historic steamboat for a scenic cruise around the sparkling lake to enjoy the cool breeze. Toddlers will love watching the ducks and swans from the open-deck seating area.

Morning / 3h

Uetliberg Lookout & Playground

Take the train up Zürich's home mountain for panoramic views and easy walking paths. There is a fantastic hilltop playground and a tower that older kids can climb.

Morning / 3-4h

Zürich Wilderness Park Langenberg

Spot bears, wolves, elk, and wild boars in large, natural forest enclosures just south of the city. Toddlers can easily walk the flat paths and enjoy the petting zoo area.

Afternoon / 2h

Lindt Home of Chocolate

Marvel at the world's tallest free-standing chocolate fountain and indulge in interactive exhibits. Toddlers will enjoy the sensory tasting stations and the massive chocolate shop.

Morning / 4h

Zürich Zoo (Zoologischer Garten)

Explore the world-class Lewa Savanna and the indoor Masoala Rainforest hall. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the elephant park and the iconic penguin parade in winter.

Afternoon / 1h

Lindenhof Park

This peaceful, car-free hilltop oasis offers stunning views of the Limmat river and the Old Town. Toddlers can safely toddle around the gravel paths while parents watch giant chess games.

Lunchtime / 1.5h

Viadukt Market (Im Viadukt)

Stroll through this trendy covered market built under historic railway arches to sample fresh local cheeses and pastries. Toddlers will love the vibrant atmosphere and the adjacent Josefwiese park playground.

Morning / 1.5h

Zürich Toy Museum (Spielzeugmuseum)

Tucked away in the Old Town, this charming museum showcases antique toys, trains, and dolls from across Europe. It's a delightful, quiet indoor option that sparks toddler imagination.

Afternoon / 1h

Chinese Garden (Chinesischer Garten)

A tranquil lakeside garden featuring a pond filled with colorful koi fish, ornate pavilions, and winding bridges. Toddlers will love feeding the fish and running along the flat, paved paths.

🍽️ Where to eat

Zeughauskeller

Order the hearty Kalbsgeschnetzeltes served with golden, crispy rösti in this historic, cavernous, and family-welcoming former armory.

Haus Hiltl

Visit the world's oldest vegetarian restaurant for an incredible, kid-friendly buffet where you pay by weight, making it easy to sample small bites for picky toddlers.

Sternen Grill

Grab a legendary, snappy St. Galler Bratwurst with a hard roll and gold-standard mustard for an easy, delicious, and quick lakeside picnic.

Café Schober

Treat the family to their famous, rich hot chocolate served with fresh whipped cream and a slice of traditional Swiss cake in a whimsical, fairy-tale salon.

Frau Gerolds Garten

Enjoy a relaxed outdoor meal of melted Swiss fondue or wood-fired pizza in this vibrant, casual container garden with plenty of open space for kids to move.

👨‍👩‍👧 Purchase the Zürich Card for unlimited travel on trams, trains, and boats, as well as free or discounted entry to most family-friendly attractions. Keep in mind that Swiss public transit is exceptionally stroller-accessible, with low-floor boarding on almost all modern city trams.
