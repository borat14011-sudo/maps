What to do

Late morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Lake Zürich Promenade

A perfectly flat, paved pathway tracing the waterfront. Stroller-accessible and lined with shady chestnut trees, it is ideal for a gentle afternoon walk while Mary Claire naps to the sound of lapping water.

Morning 9am-12pm / 3h

Zoo Zürich

Widely regarded as one of Europe's finest zoos, featuring the spectacular, climate-controlled Masoala Rainforest hall. The entire park is highly stroller-accessible, with plenty of quiet nursing corners and shaded paths.

Afternoon 3-4:30pm / 1.5h

Lindenhof

A historic oasis elevated above the city, offering stunning views of the Limmat River. Accessible via a gentle stroller-friendly path, its wide gravel terrace shaded by linden trees is a peaceful spot to relax.

Morning 9:30-11am / 1.5h

Botanischer Garten Zürich

The University of Zürich's botanical garden features futuristic dome greenhouses and lush outdoor lawns. It is incredibly serene, making it the perfect spot to escape city noise and let Mary Claire enjoy some gentle sensory stimulation.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Rieterpark

Zürich's largest and most beautiful historic park, wrapping around the Museum Rietberg. Its sprawling, manicured lawns are ideal for laying out a blanket and letting Mary Claire play under the shade of ancient beech trees.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

FIFA Museum

A highly interactive and modern museum that is surprisingly infant-friendly. It features spacious elevators, extra-wide carpeted galleries, clean diaper-changing facilities, and quiet morning hours.

Morning 9am-12pm / 3h

Uetliberg Lookout

Take the S10 train from Hauptbahnhof, which is fully accessible for strollers. Enjoy the crisp mountain air and panoramic Alpine views from the paved terrace; skip the steep lookout tower and relax at the mountaintop cafe instead.

Afternoon 1-3pm / 2h

Landesmuseum Zürich

The Swiss National Museum features a stunning modern wing that is completely step-free and stroller-friendly. The quiet, spacious exhibition halls provide an ideal, climate-controlled environment to escape midday heat or rain.

Late afternoon 4-5:30pm / 1.5h

Chinagarten Zürich

A serene, walled temple garden gifted by Zürich's sister city, Kunming. It features calm reflection ponds, flat paved pathways, and peaceful pavilions that offer a tranquil sanctuary for families.

🍽️ Where to eat

Kronenhalle

Zürich's legendary dining institution where masterworks by Picasso and Matisse adorn the walls. Book an early 11:30 AM lunch seating to enjoy their signature Zürcher Geschnetzeltes in a calmer, spacious atmosphere with baby Mary Claire.

Restaurant Zeughauskeller

Housed in a historic 15th-century armory, this lively Swiss beer hall is incredibly welcoming. Its natural ambient volume easily masks any baby babble, and the spacious layout makes navigating to your table with a highchair simple.

Restaurant Chuchi

Located in the Old Town, this highly rated restaurant is famous for authentic Swiss fondue. Opt for an early dinner on their lovely outdoor pedestrian terrace, allowing you to park your stroller right at the table.

Haus Hiltl

Guinness World Record holder for the oldest continuously open vegetarian restaurant. Extremely family-friendly with a spacious multi-level layout, an elevator, clean baby-changing stations, and highchairs readily available.

Confiserie Sprüngli (Paradeplatz)

The grand dame of Swiss cafes, perfect for premium coffee and delicate Luxemburgerli macarons. The refined upstairs salon is accessible via a quiet lift, offering an elegant but highly accommodating environment for parents with strollers.
