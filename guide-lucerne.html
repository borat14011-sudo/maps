Nestled along the shores of Lake Lucerne, this medieval gem blends Swiss precision with breathtaking Alpine panoramas. The city's walkable, pedestrian-only Old Town is exceptionally stroller-ready, making it effortless to navigate with baby Mary Claire. Parents can soak in the opulent lakeside architecture while enjoying smooth, paved promenades designed for leisurely family walks. From tranquil boat cruises to historic plazas, Lucerne offers a seamless, high-end escape tailored for discerning parents and infants alike.

What to do

Mid-morning 10-11am / 1h

Kapellbrücke (Chapel Bridge)

Stroll across Lucerne's iconic 14th-century covered wooden bridge. While the wooden planks can be slightly bumpy, the bridge features wide step-free entry ramps at both ends, making it fully accessible for your stroller during quiet mid-morning hours.

Late morning 11am-1pm / 2h

SGV Lake Lucerne Boat Cruise

Board a historic paddle steamer for a first-class cruise. The spacious, climate-controlled indoor cabins offer quiet nursing areas and clean changing tables, while the gentle, rhythmic motion of the water is guaranteed to lull Mary Claire into a peaceful nap.

Morning 9am-1pm / 4h

Mount Rigi Cogwheel Railway

Take the ferry to Vitznau and board Europe's oldest cogwheel train up Mount Rigi. The panoramic cars are stroller-friendly, and the peak features smooth, paved walking paths with majestic Alpine views far away from any loud city noise.

Afternoon 2-5pm / 3h

Swiss Museum of Transport

This world-class, sprawling indoor-outdoor museum is highly interactive and features wide, paved, stroller-friendly pathways. It includes pristine, private baby-care rooms with changing mats and bottle warmers for a stress-free afternoon.

Afternoon 1-4pm / 3h

Lido Luzern

Relax at this premium lakeside beach park featuring manicured lawns, a sandy shoreline, and a dedicated, heated shallow paddling pool. It is the perfect spot for Mary Claire to splash safely while you lounge under the shade of mature oak trees.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Gletschergarten (Glacier Garden)

Explore prehistoric glacier potholes and an indoor subterranean sandstone labyrinth. The paths are well-lit, cool on warm days, and mostly stroller-accessible, offering a sensory-rich, tranquil environment for infants.

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Inseli Park

A gorgeous, quiet peninsula park directly on the lake. It is shaded by large trees, entirely step-free, and ideal for laying down a blanket so Mary Claire can enjoy some tummy time while you take in views of the yachts.

Morning 11am-12:30pm / 1.5h

Sammlung Rosengart

Admire works by Picasso and Klee in this spacious, quiet museum. It features wide galleries, stroller-accessible elevators, and a hushed, calm atmosphere that is excellent for a peaceful sensory stroll with your baby.

Early afternoon 1-3pm / 2h

Richard Wagner Museum

Visit this historic country manor situated on a peaceful country estate. The expansive, manicured lakeside lawns are stroller-friendly and provide a quiet sanctuary away from the tourist crowds.

🍽️ Where to eat

Old Swiss House

Savor their legendary, tableside-prepared Wiener Schnitzel in an elegant, historic setting. They happily accommodate families with early dinner seatings, spacious table layouts, and sturdy wooden highchairs upon request.

Scala Restaurant

Enjoy upscale Mediterranean cuisine on a heated outdoor terrace with sweeping views of the lake. The white-tablecloth atmosphere is welcoming to families, offering baby-friendly early dinner slots and plenty of room for stroller parking.

Mill'Feuille

Positioned right on the Reuss River, this trendy spot serves delicious, fresh breakfasts. The outdoor riverside terrace is flat, stroller-accessible, and lively enough to mask any infant babbles or cries.

Wirtshaus Galliker

Experience authentic Swiss comfort food in a cozy, welcoming tavern. Operating for generations, this family-friendly institution provides highchairs and a warm, tolerant staff who adore babies.

Rathaus Brauerei

Located under the historic Town Hall arcade, this spot offers hearty local dishes. The spacious outdoor seating along the pedestrian riverfront means you can easily park a stroller table-side and enjoy a relaxed lunch.
