San Diego

San Diego sparkles with golden beaches, lush parks, and a laid-back Southern California vibe that makes family travel feel like a breeze. From world-class animal encounters to splashing in calm bay waters, the city offers endless outdoor adventures for all generations. It’s a sunny haven where stress melts away under swaying palm trees and gentle Pacific breezes.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min taxi or rideshare to downtown hotels, or ~25 min to Mission Bay beach resorts.

What to do

Morning / 4-5h

San Diego Zoo

Explore this world-famous lush habitat housing giant pandas, penguins, and koalas. The double-decker guided bus tour is a lifesaver for resting toddler legs while still seeing the sights.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Balboa Park & Pepper Grove Playground

Wander past stunning Spanish-Renaissance architecture, lush gardens, and wide-open lawns. The Pepper Grove playground features excellent, dedicated play structures specifically designed for toddlers.

Morning / 2h

La Jolla Cove

Watch wild sea lions sunbathe on the rocks and listen to them bark in the ocean breeze. Keep toddlers at a safe distance on the paved pathway for an easy, stroller-friendly wildlife viewing experience.

Morning / 2-3h

USS Midway Museum

Walk through a massive, real aircraft carrier loaded with historic planes parked right on the flight deck. The open-air flight deck is completely flat and stroller-friendly, perfect for toddlers to marvel at the giant helicopters.

Lunch / 1-2h

Liberty Public Market

A bustling indoor food hall featuring local vendors selling everything from gourmet empanadas to artisanal ice cream. Grab your food to go and head to the adjacent grassy lawns where kids can run freely.

Morning / 2-3h

The New Children's Museum

A multi-level creative space filled with interactive, touchable art installations designed to inspire young minds. Toddlers will love 'sucrose', a dedicated, soft-play climbing zone made just for the littlest visitors.

Afternoon / 3h

Coronado Beach

Play on sparkling, mineral-rich sandy beaches situated directly in front of the historic, red-roofed Hotel del Coronado. The gentle shoreline waves here are ideal for shallow, safe toddler splashing.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Waterfront Park

Located right downtown, this modern park features giant grassy lawns, a massive playground, and interactive splash fountains. Pack a swimsuit and towel so your toddler can safely splash in the 10-inch deep wading pools.

Morning / 2h

Birch Aquarium at Scripps

Peer at glowing jellyfish, playful little blue penguins, and a massive two-story kelp forest tank overlooking the Pacific. The outdoor touch pools are low enough for toddlers to gently feel starfish and sea anemones.

🍽️ Where to eat

Station Tavern

Order the local craft beers and gourmet burgers while the kids play in the dedicated, fenced-in backyard play area.

The Crack Shack

Order the 'Senor Croque' fried chicken sandwich and mini chicken nuggets, then sit at the lively, open-air communal tables.

Puesto at the Headquarters

Feast on award-winning Maine lobster or filet mignon tacos wrapped in crispy, melted-to-perfection cheese.

Wayfarer Bread & Pastry

Grab their famous flaky morning buns, cinnamon rolls, or fresh wood-fired pizzas to eat picnic-style by the ocean.

Coronado Brewing Company

Enjoy a flight of local IPAs paired with wood-fired flatbreads alongside a great, kid-approved kids' menu.
