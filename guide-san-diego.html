San Diego welcomes discerning families with its perfect blend of laid-back Southern California charm and sophisticated coastal luxury. This coastal haven ensures parents can savor gourmet dining and stunning ocean views while easily managing baby schedules.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM

San Diego Zoo

Walk the shaded, stroller-accessible Canopy Walkway to see pandas and koalas, utilizing the quiet private nursing stations located near the front entrance.

Late morning 10:30 AM - 12:00 PM

Balboa Park Rose Garden

Stroll along wide, flat paved pathways surrounded by fragrant blooms, offering plenty of shade under historic pergolas for a quiet baby sensory experience.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

La Jolla Shores Beach

Enjoy the calm, gentle waves of this protected sandy beach, perfect for dipping baby's toes in the water, with flat boardwalk access for easy stroller maneuvering.

Mid-morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM

The New Children's Museum

Visit the dedicated toddler and crawler soft-play spaces designed for sensory development in a highly curated, air-conditioned artistic environment.

Late afternoon 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM

Seaport Village Boardwalk

Walk along the scenic waterfront promenade with panoramic bay views, perfectly flat for high-end strollers and featuring plenty of shaded benches.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:30 AM

San Diego Botanic Garden

Explore the peaceful, paved tropical sections that offer a serene, quiet atmosphere for a contact nap on the go under lush canopies.

Saturday morning 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Little Italy Mercato Farmers' Market

Navigate the bustling but wide-street market early in the morning to sample artisanal goods while baby enjoys the gentle morning breeze and colorful sights.

Early afternoon 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Old Town San Diego State Historic Park

Enjoy a gentle stroll through the car-free, grassy plaza area lined with historic adobe buildings, offering plenty of shaded spots to relax under olive trees.

Afternoon 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM

Birch Aquarium at Scripps

Delight baby with the glowing, hypnotic jellyfish displays and gentle indoor blue lighting, perfect for a sensory-rich and climate-controlled outing.
