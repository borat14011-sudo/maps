What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 2h

The Garden Society of Gothenburg (Trädgårdsföreningen)

A magnificent 19th-century park in the heart of the city. Stroller-friendly paved pathways wind through thousands of roses, leading to the magnificent Palm House, a tranquil, warm glasshouse perfect for a peaceful, sensory-rich morning walk with Mary Claire.

Midday 11:30 AM - 1:30 PM / 2h

World of Volvo

This stunning, newly opened architectural masterpiece offers expansive, light-filled exhibition spaces with wide, flat surfaces and state-of-the-art elevators. It is exceptionally quiet and stroller-accessible, making it an easy, high-design indoor activity.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM / 2h

Slottsskogen Park

Gothenburg's premier green space features wide, paved, stroller-friendly avenues shaded by ancient oaks. The park houses a free, open-air zoo where Mary Claire can gently spot majestic moose and harbor seals in a quiet, peaceful setting.

Morning 9:30 AM - 12:30 PM / 3h

Saltholmen to Vrångö Ferry

Board a modern, vibration-free passenger ferry from Saltholmen. The boats feature dedicated pram parking areas, expansive windows, and smooth boarding ramps, leading to a car-free island oasis with paved, flat walking trails along the water.

Afternoon 1:30 PM - 3:30 PM / 2h

Marstrand Island Waterfront Walk

Take the ultra-short, stroller-accessible ferry to Marstrand. Stroll along the flat seaside boardwalk surrounding the harbor to admire luxury yachts and colorful wooden villas; skip the steep, cobblestone climb to Carlsten Fortress to keep the ride smooth for the baby.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 2.5h

Gothenburg Botanical Garden (Göteborgs botaniska trädgård)

One of Europe's largest and most beautiful botanical gardens. It features impeccable asphalt and smooth gravel paths, lush canopy shade, and quiet seating alcoves that are ideal for outdoor feeding and sensory relaxation.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM / 3h

Hönö Klåva Harbor

A picturesque archipelago fishing village on the island of Hönö. The wide, flat wooden boardwalks allow for smooth strolling past boutique design shops, offering refreshing sea breezes and calm coastal views away from the city bustle.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:30 PM / 2.5h

Gunnebo House and Gardens (Gunnebo Slott)

A stunning 18th-century neoclassical estate just south of the city. The formal gardens feature wide, stroller-friendly gravel paths and peaceful, shaded lawns perfect for laying down a blanket, complemented by an organic, upscale estate cafe.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM / 1.5h

Haga Historic District Stroll

Stroll through Gothenburg's oldest neighborhood, famous for its wooden houses. While the main street is cobblestone, the smooth side-pavements and spacious outdoor seating at upscale cafes make it perfect for a leisurely afternoon 'fika' (coffee and giant cinnamon buns).

🍽️ Where to eat

Sjömagasinet

A Michelin-recommended waterfront institution housed in a 1775 wooden building. Specializing in refined local seafood like steamed cod and hand-peeled shrimp, it offers a spacious outdoor pier terrace, highly professional service, and early 5:00 PM dining slots perfect for baby's schedule.

Fiskekrogen

Known as Gothenburg's 'Fish Tavern,' this elegant, upscale restaurant serves legendary seafood platters in a sophisticated dining room. The staff is exceptionally accommodating to families, offering high-end wooden highchairs and spacious corner booths with plenty of room for a diaper bag.

Grand Tenan

Located inside the historic Grand Hotel Marstrand, this refined dining room serves classic Swedish West Coast specialties like Baltic herring and beef tenderloin. The elegant but relaxed maritime atmosphere easily accommodates well-behaved infants, particularly during early dinner seatings.

Villa Sjötorp

A magnificent, historic lakeside villa in Ljungskile serving gourmet, locally sourced organic cuisine. Its spacious, wrap-around veranda and lush garden seating provide a quiet, tranquil atmosphere with stunning fjord views, making it incredibly baby-friendly.

Taverna Averna

A vibrant, high-end Italian-Swedish bistro in the trendy Linné neighborhood. It features high ceilings, a spacious layout that easily accommodates premium strollers directly at the table, and a lively atmosphere that easily absorbs any occasional baby noises.
