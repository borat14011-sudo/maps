Rovinj's pastel-colored Venetian facades cascade down to a sparkling Adriatic Sea, offering a sophisticated Mediterranean escape rich in Istrian charm. The car-free Old Town exudes historical romance, while the lush golden-cape forest park of Zlatni Rt provides a cool, shaded canopy ideal for coastal strolls. For families, the flat, stroller-friendly Lungomare promenade offers seamless access from luxury resorts directly to the harbor's edge. This coastal gem perfectly balances high-end Istrian gastronomy with gentle, wave-lapped pebble beaches ideal for dipping tiny toes.

What to do

Early morning 9-11am / 2h

Zlatni Rt Forest Park

Perfect for a morning stroll, these flat, dirt paths are heavily shaded by ancient pines, shielding baby Mary Claire from the midday sun while allowing you to push the stroller right alongside the turquoise sea.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Port of Rovinj

A picturesque, paved harbor walk that is completely flat and stroller-friendly, ideal for Will and Kathryn to admire the bobbing fishing boats and elegant yachts against the backdrop of pastel Venetian-style buildings.

Late afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Mulini Beach

This high-end beach club offers plush loungers, a pristine paved promenade, and clean changing facilities, making it incredibly easy to dip Mary Claire's toes into the calm, shallow waters of the Adriatic.

Late afternoon 5-6:30pm / 1.5h

St. Euphemia's Basilica Plateau

Ascend to this iconic landmark using a sturdy baby carrier rather than a stroller to navigate the steep, polished Venetian cobblestones, rewarding yourselves with breezy, panoramic views of the Istrian archipelago.

Morning 9:30am-12pm / 2.5h

Katarina Island

Board the accessible, short taxi boat for a 5-minute ride to this peaceful, car-free islet, where you can stroll along wide, shaded dirt paths and enjoy secluded rocky coves away from the bustling town.

Midday 11am-1pm / 2h

Lone Bay

A sheltered, crescent-shaped pebble beach bordered by the lush Golden Cape forest, providing natural afternoon shade and exceptionally calm, current-free waters perfect for an infant's first swim.

Early morning 8-9:30am / 1.5h

Rovinj Town Market

An open-air market rich in colors and aromas of fresh Istrian truffles, figs, and lavender, offering a paved, flat layout that is easy to navigate with a stroller during the cooler morning hours.

Midday 1-2pm / 1h

Batana Eco-Museum

A small, quiet, and fully air-conditioned cultural space dedicated to Rovinj's traditional vessels, providing a cool sanctuary for Mary Claire to rest in her stroller during the peak afternoon heat.

Morning 9-11:30am / 2.5h

Pula Arena

Take a 40-minute private transfer to this spectacular Roman amphitheater, which offers a smooth, flat outer path and spacious grassy areas perfect for a relaxed family photo session under the ancient stone arches.

Indulge in a multi-course Istrian vegetable-forward tasting menu with sweeping harbor views; the luxury venue offers pristine highchairs and spacious outdoor terrace seating perfect for an early evening reservation.
