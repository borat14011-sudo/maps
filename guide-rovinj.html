Rovinj

← back to travel board
Rovinj rises like a pastel-colored dream from the Adriatic, where cobblestone alleys wind upwards to a towering church spire. It is a car-free playground where toddlers can safely wander past gelato shops, fishing boats, and rocky beaches. From pine-scented forest parks to sunset boat rides, this Istrian jewel offers a slow-paced Mediterranean escape perfect for little feet.
🚕 Getting there: ~40 min taxi or private transfer from Pula Airport (PUY) directly to the edge of the pedestrian Old Town or your seaside resort.

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

St. Euphemia Cathedral (Crkva Sv. Eufemija)

Climb up to the town's highest point for breathtaking panoramic sea views. Toddlers will love chasing pigeons in the wide, paved piazza directly outside the church doors.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Golden Cape Forest Park (Zlatni Rt)

A lush, vehicle-free nature reserve perfect for renting a family bike with a child seat or pushing a stroller under cool pine trees. Toddlers can safely throw pebbles into the calm, crystal-clear coves along the path.

Morning / 1h

Rovinj Green Market (Valdibora)

Sample sweet local figs, fresh berries, and local honey at this vibrant open-air market right by the harbor. Toddlers will love the colorful fruit displays and the bustling, friendly atmosphere.

Morning / 2-3h

Mulini Beach

A modern, gently sloping pebbled beach with calm, shallow waters and a stroller-friendly promenade. It is the ideal spot for toddlers to safely wade and splash in the Adriatic.

Evening / 1.5h

Dolphin Watching Sunset Cruise

Board a gentle boat from the harbor to spot wild bottlenose dolphins playing in the sunset glow. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the boat ride and the splashing animals.

Morning / 1-2h

Mini Croatia Exhibition

Walk like giants through this outdoor miniature park showcasing Croatia’s famous landmarks at kid-height. It is highly interactive and very easy for toddlers to safely explore on foot.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Rovinj Town Harbor

Stroll along the safe, pedestrianized harbor to watch traditional wooden batana fishing boats bobbing in the water. Toddlers can easily spot colorful fish swimming right from the pier edge.

Morning / 1h

Grisia Street Art Wander

Explore this famous, winding cobblestone street lined with colorful art galleries and vibrant outdoor displays. Hold toddler hands tightly here as the polished stone steps can be quite slippery.

Full Day / 4-6h

Amarin Beach Resort Day

Spend a day at this highly family-oriented resort area featuring soft-play zones, outdoor playgrounds, and shallow pools designed specifically for toddlers. The pine-shaded lawns offer perfect spots for a midday toddler nap.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Puntulina

Book an early outdoor table right on the rocks to enjoy fresh seafood pasta while the kids watch the gentle waves lap below.

Pizzeria Da Sergio

Grab a giant, thin-crust Margherita pizza at this legendary, casual spot that is incredibly welcoming to messy toddlers.

Balbi

Located in a charming square, order the handmade Istrian fuzi pasta with mild chicken or cheese, which is a guaranteed hit for little palates.

Gelateria Italia

Treat the family to authentic, artisanal gelato made by an Italian family, with creamy chocolate and fruit flavors that toddlers will adore.

Kantinon Tavern

Enjoy a relaxed, rustic atmosphere right on the harbor and order the traditional, kid-friendly local platters like mild cured meats and warm, freshly baked bread.

👨‍👩‍👧 Bring a sturdy baby carrier rather than a stroller for the Old Town, as the historic cobblestones are highly polished, steep, and extremely slippery. Always pack water shoes for toddlers to protect their feet from the pebbled shores and rocky entries at the local beaches.
