St. Lucia

← back to travel board
St. Lucia welcomes families with its dramatic volcanic spires, lush rainforests, and calm Caribbean coves. Kids will marvel at drive-in volcanoes and splash in warm, shallow waters, while parents soak in the laid-back island rhythm. It is a tropical playground where adventure and relaxation meet seamlessly.
🚕 Getting there: ~60-90 min private taxi or shuttle from Hewanorra International Airport (UVF) to the family resorts of Rodney Bay

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Splash Island Water Park

St. Lucia's first open-water sports park features inflatable hurdles and slides perfect for older kids. Toddlers can safely play on the calm shoreline of Reduit Beach right next to the park.

Morning / 1-2h

Sulphur Springs Mud Baths

Dip into the warm, mineral-rich mud baths of the world’s only drive-in volcano for a unique sensory experience. While the hot springs are too warm for toddlers, they will love watching the bubbling mud pools from the safe boardwalks.

Morning / 1-2h

Castries Central Market

Introduce your senses to local culture at this bustling historic market filled with colorful tropical fruits, spices, and handmade crafts. It is stroller-friendly in the main aisles, making it easy to grab fresh bananas for a toddler snack.

Morning / 3-4h

Pigeon Island National Landmark

Hike up historic ruins and enjoy panoramic views, followed by a picnic on two sheltered, white-sand beaches. Toddlers will love splashing in the exceptionally calm, wave-free waters on the south side of the island.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Diamond Falls Botanical Gardens

Walk along paved, flat pathways winding through lush tropical flora to view a stunning, color-changing waterfall. The easy, stroller-accessible walk is perfect for little legs and offers plenty of shade.

Morning / 1h

Tet Paul Nature Trail

This gentle 45-minute loop trail offers some of the most spectacular, unobstructed views of the Pitons. It is incredibly easy to navigate, with handrails and flat steps that are safe and manageable for young toddlers.

Morning / 2-3h

Rainforest Adventures Aerial Tram

Glide safely above the rainforest canopy in an open-air gondola to spot vibrant birds and giant ferns. The slow, smooth, and quiet ride is safe and magical for toddlers who aren't ready for ziplining.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Marigot Bay Marina and Beach

Take a quick, fun 2-minute ferry ride across this picturesque, yacht-filled bay to a secluded sandspit shaded by coconut palms. The ultra-calm waters are like a giant bath, ideal for toddlers to safely splash.

Morning / 2h

Lushan Country Life

Take a guided family walk through a family-owned orchard to taste fresh-picked fruits and learn about traditional island life. Toddlers can interact with friendly local birds and enjoy organic fruit snacks straight from the trees.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Coal Pot

Order the fresh catch of the day in a rich, traditional creole sauce while dining right on the water's edge.

The Naked Fisherman

Try the grilled lobster or crispy fish tacos while kids play in the soft sand right next to your beachside table.

Orlando's Restaurant

Savor a farm-to-table Caribbean tasting menu featuring child-friendly portions of sweet potato pudding and grilled chicken.

Marie's Local Kitchen

Feast on a hearty, buffet-style Creole lunch with local favorites like macaroni pie, plantains, and stewed chicken.

Buzz Seafood and Grill

Enjoy a relaxed family dinner of coconut shrimp and garlic butter snapper in a charming, kid-friendly garden setting.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack plenty of reef-safe sunscreen and toddler-friendly bug spray, as mosquitoes can be active in the lush rainforest areas. Opt for private boat charters instead of long road transfers to avoid car sickness on St. Lucia's winding, mountainous roads.
