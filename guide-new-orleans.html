What to do

Early morning 9-11am / 2h

Audubon Zoo

Stroll under ancient oak canopies along wide, paved stroller-friendly paths. The quiet Louisiana Swamp exhibit offers a calm atmosphere, and private nursing suites are available for Mary Claire.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Sydney and Walda Besthoff Sculpture Garden

A highly tranquil, free-entry outdoor museum set among mature pines and live oaks. Wide gravel and paved pathways are perfect for a smooth stroller ride, offering plenty of shade and quiet spots.

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Louisiana Children's Museum

Located in City Park, this state-of-the-art facility features 'Play Power' sensory areas designed specifically for infants and toddlers, offering a quiet, clean, and air-conditioned retreat.

Midday 12-2pm / 2h

New Orleans Museum of Art (NOMA)

An upscale, highly air-conditioned sanctuary with spacious, flat galleries. Easily navigate the world-class collections with a luxury stroller while Mary Claire naps in the quiet environment.

Late afternoon 4-5:30pm / 1.5h

Jackson Square

An iconic historic square with flat, pedestrian-only paved areas. Stroll past local artists and enjoy the lively atmosphere; keep a slight distance from loud street performers to protect baby's ears.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Audubon Aquarium

Completely indoor, highly air-conditioned, and fully stroller-accessible. The soothing blue lighting and quiet underwater tunnels are incredibly calming and visually stimulating for an infant.

Morning 9:30-10:30am / 1h

St. Charles Avenue Streetcar

Experience a historic, breezy ride past stunning Garden District mansions. Opt for a private charter or ride during off-peak mid-morning hours; be prepared to fold your stroller before boarding.

Sunset 5-6pm / 1h

Woldenberg Park

A beautiful, flat riverfront promenade offering sweeping views of the Mississippi River. The wide concrete pathway is perfect for a sunset stroller walk, cooled by gentle river breezes.

Afternoon 1-2pm / 1h

Preservation Hall

Secure a private daytime acoustic preview or attend an early-afternoon family courtyard session to enjoy authentic traditional jazz in a controlled, non-crowded, and safe-volume environment.

🍽️ Where to eat

Commander's Palace

Savor their legendary turtle soup and pecan-crusted Gulf fish. Book an early 11:30am lunch slot; the upscale yet celebratory hum easily absorbs soft infant noises, and highchairs are readily provided.

Brennan's

Famous for Bananas Foster flambéed tableside. Request a table in the gorgeous, quiet outdoor courtyard, which provides plenty of space for a stroller and a serene atmosphere for an upscale breakfast.

Galatoire's

Enjoy classic Crabmeat Sardou in a historic setting. Opt for an early lunch on the first floor where the bustling, lively atmosphere naturally masks any of Mary Claire's vocalizations.

Lilette

This sophisticated Garden District bistro serves exquisite French-Italian fare like duck confit. The quiet, shady outdoor patio is ideal for an early 5:30pm dinner while baby sleeps soundly in her stroller.

Pêche Seafood Grill

A highly acclaimed, spacious restaurant serving pristine rustic seafood. The airy dining room easily accommodates luxury strollers, and the early evening service is vibrant but highly family-friendly.
