From exclusive boat charters in historic Winter Park to peaceful garden strolls, this Floridian haven ensures a seamless luxury getaway for the entire family.

What to do

Early morning 9-10am / 1h

Winter Park Scenic Boat Tour

Enjoy a historic, one-hour guided pontoon cruise through the tranquil canals of Winter Park. The gentle, rocking motion under a fully shaded canopy is perfect for lulling Mary Claire to sleep.

Morning 10-11:30am / 1.5h

Kraft Azalea Garden

Walk beneath towering, moss-draped cypress trees on a quiet, stroller-ready paved path along Lake Maitland. This secluded sanctuary offers deep shade and peaceful breezes, ideal for a relaxed mid-morning picnic.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Harry P. Leu Gardens

Explore 50 acres of paved, stroller-accessible paths winding through vibrant tropical flora and a beautiful rose garden. The abundant shade from majestic oaks protects delicate infant skin from the midday sun.

Afternoon 1-3pm / 2h

SeaLife Orlando Aquarium

A fully air-conditioned, stroller-friendly indoor oasis featuring gentle bubble-curtain displays and floor-to-ceiling acrylic tunnels. The soothing blue light and swimming fish provide a calm, hypnotic sensory experience.

Early morning 8:30-10am / 1.5h

Lake Eola Park

Stroll along the wide, flat 0.9-mile paved lakeside loop in downtown Orlando, offering gentle breezes and beautiful views of the historic fountain. Early mornings are wonderfully quiet and offer ample shade near the playground.

Morning 10am-1pm / 3h

Orlando Science Center

Head straight to the air-conditioned KidsTown exhibit, which features a dedicated, soft-play Toddler Town sensory area perfect for crawling and interactive water play. It is clean, safe, and highly stimulating for a baby.

All day 9am-3pm / 6h

Discovery Cove

Book a private Elite Cabana for ultimate shade, a stocked fridge, and nap-ready lounge chairs. The pristine, white-sand beaches and gentle, wave-free Explorer's Aviary offer a quiet, highly controlled environment for baby's first splash.

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Disney Springs

Arrive during the quiet morning hours to enjoy a peaceful walk along the wide, stroller-friendly waterfront promenades. You can easily roll your stroller onto the free, gentle water taxi for a scenic loop around Lake Buena Vista.

Early morning 8:30-10am / 1.5h

Mead Botanical Garden

A quiet, peaceful natural escape in Winter Park featuring wide, flat boardwalks and hard-packed dirt paths easily navigated by a luxury stroller. It is an exceptionally quiet, low-sensory environment for a soothing morning nap on the go.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Boathouse

Savor fresh Floridian seafood and USDA Prime steaks on the spacious outdoor waterfront deck at Disney Springs. The lively outdoor ambiance easily masks baby noises, and the staff is highly accommodating with premium highchairs.

Prato

Indulge in wood-fired pizzas and handmade pasta on the covered outdoor patio along Winter Park's Park Avenue. An early 5:00 PM seating ensures a relaxed, upscale atmosphere where Mary Claire is warmly welcomed with highchairs.

Highball & Harvest

Enjoy sophisticated, farm-to-table Southern cuisine inside the JW Marriott Grande Lakes. The dining room is spacious with wide aisles for strollers, and the early dinner service offers a quiet, upscale setting perfect for families.

Sanaa

Dine on flavorful Indian-African fusion dishes while gazing through floor-to-ceiling windows at roaming zebras and giraffes. The visual distraction keeps infants captivated, and the early lunch hours are delightfully calm.

The Ravenous Pig

Relish award-winning gastropub fare and house-brewed beers in the spacious, shaded outdoor beer garden in Winter Park. It is highly family-friendly with plenty of room for strollers, lawn space for a crawling baby, and highchairs.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Wyndham Grand Orlando Resort Bonnet Creek

$200-380/night

A Spanish-Mediterranean treasure nestled on a private lake, offering spacious family suites with separate areas that double as quiet crib spaces. Features a zero-entry pool, lazy river, complimentary wooden cribs on request, and heavy blackout curtains for perfect daytime naps.

Signia by Hilton Orlando Bonnet Creek

$220-390/night

Boasts a spectacular, heated lazy river and zero-entry lagoon pool, ideal for wading safely with an infant. The spacious guest rooms easily accommodate Pack 'n Plays, and the quiet resort corridors, excellent room service, and dark blackout drapes make naptime seamless.

JW Marriott Orlando Bonnet Creek Resort & Spa

$280-400/night

A modern luxury oasis featuring a dedicated family pool, rooftop terrace, and spacious Executive Suites with sliding doors for privacy during baby's bedtime. Cribs and bottle warmers are provided upon request, and the overall peaceful, upscale atmosphere is perfect for a refined family retreat.
