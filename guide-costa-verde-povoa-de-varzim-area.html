What to do

Afternoon / 2-3h

Praia da Póvoa de Varzim

This vast golden beach offers soft sand and sheltered areas near the sea walls. Toddlers will love building sandcastles here, protected from the stronger Atlantic winds.

Sunset / 1-2h

Póvoa de Varzim Boardwalk

A beautiful, flat wooden walkway stretching along the coastal dunes for miles. It is completely stroller-accessible and perfect for an easy seaside stroll.

Morning / 2h

Parque da Cidade

A massive green park featuring a scenic lake, walking paths, and plenty of grassy areas to run around. Toddlers will love feeding the ducks and playing in the enclosed wooden playground.

Morning / 1-2h

Mercado Municipal da Póvoa de Varzim

A bustling local market filled with fresh fruits, flowers, and local goods. It is a fantastic sensory experience for toddlers, and a great place to pick up fresh berries for snack time.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Santa Clara Aqueduct & Monastery

A striking historical landmark with dozens of massive stone arches located in neighboring Vila do Conde. Toddlers can safely run around the grassy park areas at the base of the aqueduct.

Morning / 1h

Nau Quinhentista

A fascinating, full-scale replica of a 16th-century Portuguese explorer ship docked in the river. Toddlers will feel like little pirates climbing the safe lower decks of this wooden vessel.

Morning / 1-2h

Monte de São Félix

The highest point in the municipality, offering panoramic coastal views and historic windmills. Drive to the top instead of climbing the stairs to keep it stress-free with a stroller.

Afternoon / 1h

Diana Bar Beach Library

An iconic, circular Art Deco building sitting right on the beach sand, now serving as a beachside library. It features a cozy kids' corner with books, offering a perfect air-conditioned respite from the sun.

Morning / 1-2h

Caxinas Fishing Quarter

A colorful, authentic neighborhood showcasing traditional tiles and maritime life. Toddlers will be fascinated watching the colorful fishing boats docked at the nearby harbor.

🍽️ Where to eat

Restaurante 31 de Janeiro

Order the fresh grilled sea bass or their famous seafood rice, which is rich yet mild enough to share with little ones.

O Bodegão

Try the authentic Rabanada Poveira, a delicious, sweet, local style of French toast that toddlers go crazy for.

Garfo Marinho

Get the ultra-fresh grilled sardines served with simple boiled potatoes, a healthy and easy-to-feed option for toddlers.

Romando

Indulge in high-end sushi and seafood while the kids enjoy their simple, kid-approved pasta dishes in a stylish, welcoming environment.

Pastelaria Forte

Stop by to grab a box of fresh pastéis de nata and local sweet breads for a fun afternoon beach picnic.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack a windbreaker even in the summer, as the northern Atlantic breeze can get quite chilly during late afternoon beach walks. Rely on the flat, paved seaside boardwalks for seamless stroller navigation between Póvoa de Varzim and Vila do Conde.
