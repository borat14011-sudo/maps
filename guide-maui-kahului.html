Maui (Kahului)

← back to travel board
Maui welcomes families with its warm trade winds, dramatic volcanic backdrops, and gentle turquoise waters perfect for little splashers. From scenic coastal drives to lush garden valleys, this island paradise offers an effortless blend of adventure and relaxation. It is a place where toddlers can build sandcastles under swaying palms while parents soak in the breathtaking Pacific views.
🚕 Getting there: ~20-40 min rental car drive from Kahului Airport (OGG) to most major resort areas like Wailea or Ka'anapali

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Maui Ocean Center

Walk through a stunning glass tunnel surrounded by sharks, rays, and tropical fish. The interactive tide pools are perfect for hands-on toddlers to touch sea stars.

Morning / 2h

Baby Beach at Baldwin Beach Park

This beautiful beach features a protected offshore reef that creates a calm, wave-free lagoon. It is ideal for toddlers to safely splash around without strong currents.

Afternoon / 2h

Maui Tropical Plantation

Take a narrated tram tour through fields of sugarcane and tropical fruits, then feed the ducks by the lagoon. Toddlers will love the wide-open grassy spaces to run around.

Morning / 1-2h

Kepaniwai Park & Heritage Gardens

Explore cultural pavilions, beautiful stone paths, and gentle streams nestled in a lush valley. The paved walkways are stroller-friendly, making it easy to stroll with tired toddlers.

Morning / 1-2h

Ali'i Kula Lavender Farm

Walk through fragrant lavender fields with sweeping views of the island from the slopes of Haleakala. Toddlers will enjoy the whimsical garden paths and searching for hidden fairy gardens.

Saturday Morning / 2h

Maui Swap Meet

This vibrant outdoor market features local crafts, tropical fruits, and tasty Hawaiian shave ice. Strollers navigate the flat asphalt stalls easily while toddlers enjoy the sights.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Keopuolani Regional Park

Maui's largest park features multiple playgrounds, wide paved walking paths, and a beautiful sensory garden. It is a fantastic, low-key spot for toddlers to burn off energy.

Morning / 2h

Spreckelsville Baby Beach

This hidden gem features a natural rock barrier that blocks large waves, leaving a tranquil wading pool. Toddlers can search for tiny crabs in the shallow, warm water.

Early Morning / 3-4h

Haleakala National Park Summit District

Drive up above the clouds to witness the otherworldly volcanic landscape of Haleakala's summit. While the altitude can be chilly, toddlers will be mesmerized by looking down at the clouds.

🍽️ Where to eat

Tin Roof Maui

Order the famous Mochiko Chicken or Garlic Noodles from Chef Sheldon Simeon for an incredibly flavorful, family-friendly take-out lunch.

Ululani's Hawaiian Shave Ice

Treat the family to ultra-fine shave ice topped with fresh tropical syrups and a scoop of vanilla ice cream at the bottom.

Kihei Caffe

Fuel up with massive portions of banana macadamia nut pancakes and pork fried rice in a casual, bustling outdoor seating area.

Geste Shrimp Truck

Grab a plate of finger-licking Hawaiian scampi or spicy pineapple shrimp to enjoy picnic-style near the harbor.

Tasaka Guri-Guri

Taste a beloved local Maui tradition with a cup of Guri-Guri, a creamy, sherbet-like frozen treat in strawberry or pineapple flavors that kids adore.
