Vienna

Vienna enchants families with its grand palaces, lush green spaces, and a timeless, whimsical charm. Toddlers and parents alike will love wandering through royal gardens, riding historic carousels, and indulging in world-famous pastries. It is an exceptionally clean, walkable city where fairy-tale dreams seamlessly blend with easy, modern living.
🚕 Getting there: ~20 min taxi ride or a direct 16-minute City Airport Train (CAT) to the city center

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Schönbrunn Palace & Gardens

Explore the vast imperial gardens and let kids run wild in the maze and dedicated children's museum. Toddlers will love the giant hedge labyrinth and the nearby carriage museum.

Afternoon / 3h

Schönbrunn Zoo

The world's oldest zoo offers pandas, koalas, and a historic imperial pavilion in a compact, stroller-friendly layout. Toddlers will love the petting zoo area and the close-up view of the penguins.

Late Afternoon / 2-3h

Prater Amusement Park

Ride the historic Giant Ferris Wheel for panoramic views and enjoy a nostalgic stroll past kid-friendly retro rides. Toddlers can enjoy the gentle minitrain winding through the green park.

Morning / 2h

Haus der Musik

An interactive museum where kids can conduct a virtual Vienna Philharmonic orchestra and play giant floor pianos. Toddlers will love the sensory sound steps and tapping on various wall instruments.

Afternoon / 1.5h

Stadtpark

A lush, central green oasis perfect for a relaxing family stroll past duck ponds and gilded statues. Toddlers can burn off energy at the large, well-equipped playground situated right in the park.

Midday / 1.5h

Naschmarkt

Vienna's most famous open-air market is a feast for the senses, offering colorful fruit stands, falafel, and Austrian treats. Strollers fit best along the wider outer aisles, and toddlers will love sampling fresh berries.

Morning / 2h

Zoom Children's Museum

An incredible hands-on museum with sensory-rich play zones tailored specifically for different age groups, including toddlers. The 'ZOOM Ocean' area is a magical, padded play space perfect for crawlers.

Morning / 3-4h

Lainzer Tiergarten

A massive, wild nature reserve where families can hike paved trails and spot free-roaming wild boars and deer. Toddlers can safely observe the animals from the safety of a stroller or enjoy the woodland playgrounds.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Belvedere Palace Gardens

Wander through the gorgeous, terraced baroque gardens filled with fountains, gravel paths, and manicured hedges. Toddlers will enjoy chasing the splashing water features and watching the ducks in the large reflection pools.

🍽️ Where to eat

Café Central

Indulge in a classic slice of Wiener Apfelstrudel (apple strudel) or rich Sachertorte in a grand, historic cafe setting.

Figlmüller

Order the famous, ultra-thin, crispy Wiener Schnitzel that is large enough for parents and toddlers to share.

Café Sacher

Treat the family to the legendary Original Sacher-Torte, a decadent chocolate cake with apricot jam, served with whipped cream.

Schweizerhaus

Located in the Prater, this lively beer garden serves legendary roasted pork knuckles alongside a great, fenced-in kids' play area.

Kolariks Luftburg

Feast on 100% organic Austrian classics in a super family-friendly venue featuring massive outdoor bouncy castles for kids.

👨‍👩‍👧 Vienna's public transit system is highly stroller-friendly, with elevators at nearly every U-Bahn subway station and low-floor trams. Always carry small change for public restrooms, and consider purchasing a Vienna City Card for unlimited rides and family discounts.
