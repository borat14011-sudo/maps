Mallorca's dramatic Tramuntana cliffs melt into tranquil turquoise coves, creating an idyllic haven for discerning families. The island balances sophisticated historic charm in Palma with pristine, family-friendly beaches that stretch along the northern bays. For Will and Kathryn, the stroller-ready beachfront boardwalk of Playa de Muro offers a seamless, flat path for breezy morning walks with Mary Claire. Here, Michelin-starred dining and world-class beach clubs coexist beautifully with a deeply rooted Spanish culture that welcomes infants with open arms.
🚕 Getting there: Palma de Mallorca Airport (PMI) to Playa de Muro or the Palma beachfront takes approximately 15 to 45 minutes. The ultimate choice for luxury family transport is a pre-arranged private Mercedes V-Class transfer booked through Mallorca Executive, complete with a rear-facing infant car seat and ample space for a premium travel stroller.

What to do

Morning 9-11:35am / 2.5h

Playa de Muro Beach

This stunning Blue Flag beach features extremely shallow, crystal-clear water and fine white sand, making it incredibly safe and gentle for Mary Claire's first splash in the Mediterranean. Rent a private daybed under a shady palapa for ultimate sun protection.

Late morning 11am-12:30pm / 1.5h

Palma Cathedral (La Seu)

Marvel at Antoni Gaudí's interior additions and the magnificent rose window. The soaring nave stays blissfully cool in the midday heat, and the main entrance features smooth, ramped stroller access.

Afternoon 3-4:30pm / 1.5h

Parc de la Mar

Located right below the cathedral, this sprawling park features wide, flat paved paths that are perfect for pushing a luxury stroller. Enjoy the cooling breeze from the giant saltwater lake while Mary Claire naps under the shade of palm trees.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Jardines de Alfabia

A peaceful historic estate nestled in the Tramuntana mountains, filled with lush pergolas, water features, and tranquil shaded paths. It offers a cool, sensory garden experience for baby Mary Claire away from the coastal sun.

Afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Passeig del Born

Palma's most elegant, tree-shaded pedestrian boulevard is lined with designer boutiques and historic stone architecture. It is completely flat and stroller-friendly, ideal for a relaxed afternoon walk and upscale window shopping.

Evening 6-7:30pm / 1.5h

Puerto Portals Marina

Stroll along the ultra-chic paved marina to admire the superyachts and luxury boutiques. The entire dockside pathway is flat and barrier-free, making it easy to navigate with a premium travel stroller.

Midday 12-2:30pm / 2.5h

Palma Aquarium

A fully air-conditioned, world-class indoor aquarium featuring a spectacular 360-degree coral farm. It offers a highly stimulating sensory experience for infants and is entirely stroller-accessible with clean baby-changing facilities.

Morning 9:30-10:30am / 1h

Jardins del Rei

These historic royal gardens feature beautiful fountains, quiet seating areas, and geometric hedges. It is a peaceful, shady oasis in the heart of Palma, perfect for nursing or calming a tired infant.

Late afternoon 5-6:30pm / 1.5h

Castell de Bellver

This unique circular gothic castle offers panoramic views of Palma Bay. While the interior has steps, the flat, pine-shaded outer ring pathway is perfect for a breezy walk with an infant carrier.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ponderosa Beach

An upscale, feet-in-the-sand dining experience on Playa de Muro serving gourmet paella. Book an early 1:00 PM lunch seating on the wooden terrace for a vibrant yet relaxed atmosphere where infant noise is easily masked by the ocean waves.

Ca's Patró March

A famous rustic-chic seafood restaurant perched over the water in Cala Deià. Book the first lunch seating at 12:30 PM to secure a shaded edge table with plenty of space for a stroller and gorgeous coastal views.

Il Chiringo

A stylish, family-friendly beach restaurant in Palmanova offering high-end Italian-Mediterranean fare. It features a spacious outdoor terrace directly on the sand, highchairs on request, and a highly accommodating staff.

Bens d'Avall

A Michelin-starred restaurant offering breath-taking cliffside sea views. While highly sophisticated, their outdoor terrace is spacious, and booking an early lunch slot allows you to enjoy world-class cuisine in a calm, outdoor setting.

Sa Llotja Palma

An elegant seafood restaurant overlooking the harbor in Palma. It features wide spacing between tables, a modern interior, and a professional, welcoming staff that makes early evening dining with an infant seamless.
