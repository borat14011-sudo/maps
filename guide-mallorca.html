Mallorca offers a stunning blend of dramatic limestone cliffs, historic charm, and pristine azure waters tailored for sophisticated travelers. The island features the stroller-friendly Paseo Marítimo in Palma, making seaside strolls effortless with baby Mary Claire. Parents can indulge in world-class dining and private beach clubs while enjoying exceptionally calm, shallow waters perfect for infant wading. It is a European paradise where historic elegance seamlessly meets effortless family-centric luxury.
🚕 Getting there: Palma de Mallorca Airport (PMI) to the main hotel strips takes approximately 20 to 45 minutes depending on your resort location. The absolute best luxury family transport is a pre-booked private Mercedes V-Class transfer through Mallorca Private Transfer, equipped with a premium, rear-facing Maxi-Cosi infant car seat and ample space for a Bugaboo stroller.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM / 1.5h

Catedral-Basílica de Santa María de Mallorca

Known as La Seu, this gothic masterpiece offers a cool, cavernous interior perfect for escaping the midday heat with Mary Claire. The wide, paved exterior plazas are completely stroller-friendly, allowing parents to admire the breathtaking rose window in comfort.

Morning 9:30 AM - 12:30 PM / 3h

Playa de Muro

This blue-flag beach features exceptionally shallow, crystal-clear turquoise waters and soft golden sand, making it the safest spot on the island for an infant's first dip. Shaded daybeds are available for rent at the nearby luxury beach clubs.

Late Morning 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM / 2h

Jardines de Alfabia

Located in Bunyola, these historic Moorish gardens feature lush palms, shaded stone pathways, and soothing water fountains that provide a sensory, tranquil environment for baby's morning nap. Stroller access is highly manageable along the main garden paths.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM / 2h

Passeig del Born

Palma's most elegant boulevard is completely flat and shaded by towering plane trees, making it ideal for a luxury stroller stroll. Will and Kathryn can enjoy high-end window shopping at Louis Vuitton and local boutiques while Mary Claire rests.

Midday 11:30 AM - 1:30 PM / 2h

Palma Aquarium

This world-class facility is fully air-conditioned and stroller-accessible, featuring a massive shark tunnel and mesmerizing jellyfish displays that will fascinate an infant. It serves as the perfect indoor retreat if the Mediterranean afternoon sun gets too intense.

Late Afternoon 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM / 1.5h

Castell de Bellver

A unique circular castle offering panoramic views of Palma Bay, surrounded by aromatic pine forests. The paved outer rings are stroller-friendly, and the gentle sea breezes provide natural heat relief for a comfortable outdoor family excursion.

Early Morning 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM / 2h

S'Albufera Natural Park

This serene wetland reserve near Playa de Muro features flat, stroller-friendly wooden boardwalks that wind through peaceful reed beds. It is an ideal spot for a quiet, shaded morning walk to observe wild birds and enjoy fresh coastal air.

Afternoon 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM / 2h

Puerto Portals Marina

A glamorous marina filled with superyachts and high-end boutiques, featuring a wide, flat harbor-side promenade that is seamless for strollers. Enjoy a relaxed afternoon walk followed by a glass of local wine at an upscale, open-air harbor cafe.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 1.5h

Es Baluard Museum of Modern Art

This spacious modern museum features wide, step-free galleries and elevators, making it incredibly easy to navigate with a luxury stroller. Parents can soak in world-class contemporary art in peaceful, climate-controlled comfort.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ola del Mar

Located in the trendy Portixol harbor, this upscale seafood restaurant offers a spacious outdoor terrace overlooking the water. They welcome families warmly during the early 7:30 PM dinner seating, serving pristine sea bass and providing high-quality highchairs.

Es Fanals by Javier de las Muelas

Perched high above Port de Sóller, this refined restaurant offers spectacular clifftop sunset views. The outdoor terrace is spacious enough for a sleeping stroller, and the staff are incredibly accommodating with early family dining reservations.

Restaurante Il Paradiso

An exquisite, upscale Italian-Mediterranean restaurant in Palma with a sprawling cliffside terrace. The spacious outdoor seating allows for easy stroller parking, and the soothing sound of the waves below provides excellent ambient white noise for Mary Claire.

Béns d'Avall

A legendary Michelin-starred clifftop restaurant that embraces families on its spectacular outdoor terrace during lunch services. Parents can savor high-end Balearic tasting menus while baby enjoys the gentle sea breeze in a comfortable highchair.

Ca'n Toni

A beloved traditional Mallorcan institution in Palma that opens early for dinner and offers spacious, air-conditioned indoor seating. They are highly accommodating to families, offering highchairs and serving excellent slow-roasted suckling pig.
