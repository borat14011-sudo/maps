San Pancho

← back to travel board
San Pancho offers the perfect antidote to chaotic resort towns, blending a sophisticated, artsy community with laid-back Nayarit charm. The town's walkable, compact footprint makes navigating with a stroller incredibly easy, while the gentle, unhurried pace allows parents to truly decompress. It is exceptionally welcoming to young children, offering shady community spaces, open-air dining, and pristine nature without the wild party scene of neighboring Sayulita.
🚕 Getting there: ~50-minute private SUV transfer from Puerto Vallarta Airport (PVR), booking ahead with a service that provides pre-installed car seats.

What to do

Late Afternoon / 1h

Grupo Ecológico de la Costa de Verde

This volunteer-run turtle nursery offers sunset releases of baby sea turtles directly into the ocean. It is a magical, calm evening activity that your toddler can easily watch from the safety of your arms.

Morning / 1-2h

EntreAmigos Community Center

A world-class, eco-friendly community hub featuring a giant kids' library, an indoor play space, and beautiful art installations. It is the absolute best spot to escape the midday heat and let your toddler play with local toys.

Evening / 1h

Plaza del Sol

The town's central plaza is paved, flat, and completely car-free, making it perfect for stroller pushing. In the evenings, local families gather here, providing a vibrant yet safe environment for your toddler to stretch her legs.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Playa San Pancho Sunset Walk

While the surf is too strong for toddler swimming, the wide golden sands are perfect for sensory play. Set up a beach blanket near the main town entrance for an easy exit when the baby gets fussy.

Sunday Morning / 2-3h

La Patrona Polo & Equestrian Club

A stunning, ultra-premium polo club nestled in the jungle that hosts spectacular Sunday brunches. The expansive, manicured grass lawns are stroller-friendly and ideal for a leisurely family walk.

Friday Morning / 1-2h

Mercado del Sol

An artisan market held in the plaza featuring local organic snacks, fresh fruit, and handmade wooden toys. The wide aisles between stalls make it highly manageable with a baby carrier.

Morning / 1h

Estero San Pancho

A quiet freshwater lagoon right next to the beach where dozens of species of tropical birds gather. It is a peaceful, flat walk where your toddler can point at herons and ducks without any steep hiking required.

Morning / 2-3h

Playa Las Clavelinas

A hidden, sheltered cove located just a 10-minute drive from town with incredibly calm waters. Unlike the main beach, this is the perfect safe spot for your toddler to splash in the gentle tide.

Daytime / Flexible

San Pancho Golf Cart Rentals

Rent a gas or electric golf cart to zip around the cobblestone streets of the town. It turns transit into an exciting, breezy ride that toddlers love, while saving you from pushing a stroller uphill.

🍽️ Where to eat

Bistro Organico at Hotel Cielo Rojo

Order the artisanal passion fruit french toast on the quiet, shaded garden patio. The calm atmosphere and gentle morning breeze make it highly accommodating for sleeping infants in strollers.

Taqueria y Cerveceria Los Arbolitos

Savor the legendary pastor and arrachera tacos in a casual, bustling open-air joint. The ambient street noise easily drowns out any toddler tantrums, and service is lightning-fast.

La Ola Rica

Enjoy fresh coconut shrimp and local catch of the day at this beloved, spacious dinner spot. They open early by Mexican standards and happily provide sturdy high chairs for little ones.

Café de Olla

A legendary breakfast spot serving massive plates of traditional chilaquiles and fresh fruit smoothies. The staff is famously warm with babies, often offering to help entertain your toddler while you eat.

Cervecería Artesanal San Pancho

An open-air craft brewery with a massive, enclosed gravel courtyard and excellent wood-fired pizzas. It is highly casual, allowing your toddler to safely wander the outdoor space while you enjoy a cold IPA.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Hotel Cielo Rojo

A refined, eco-chic boutique hotel offering a peaceful courtyard, organic dining, and highly attentive service. They happily provide clean cribs and are located in a quiet, flat section of town perfect for morning walks.

Pal.Mar Hotel Tropical

This stylish, modern hotel features a sparkling pool and incredibly useful kitchenettes in the suites. Having a fridge and sink in-room makes washing baby bottles and prepping infant snacks completely hassle-free.

Maraica San Pancho

An eco-resort located on the quieter northern end of the beach, boasting beautiful ocean views and wide grassy lawns. The spacious layout gives your toddler plenty of room to roam away from the busier downtown streets.

👨‍👩‍👧 Cobblestones dominate San Pancho’s streets, so ditch the small-wheeled stroller for an all-terrain stroller or a rugged baby carrier. The ocean current at the main beach is very strong with a steep drop-off, so stick to sandplay and save swimming for hotel pools or nearby calm coves. Stock up on toddler snacks, fresh milk, and bottled water early in your trip at local mini-supers like Mini Super Las Quilas.
