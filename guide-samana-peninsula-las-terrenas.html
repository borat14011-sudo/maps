The Samaná Peninsula offers an upscale, European-inflected Caribbean escape where pristine jungles meet calm, turquoise waters. In Las Terrenas, the unique paved beachside path of Playa Bonita provides a seamless, stroller-friendly corridor directly alongside the surf. Will and Kathryn can enjoy French-Dominican fine dining with their toes in the sand while baby Mary Claire relaxes under the shade of towering coconut palms. This is a rare tropical haven where wild, untouched nature is balanced by sophisticated, baby-friendly luxury.
🚕 Getting there: Santo Domingo Airport (SDQ) to Las Terrenas: 2 hours via the scenic Juan Pablo II Highway in a private, pre-arranged luxury SUV equipped with a certified rear-facing infant car seat.
