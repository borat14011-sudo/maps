Chic French-Caribbean vibes meet effortless toddler-friendly beach days

Samaná Peninsula (Las Terrenas)

← back to travel board
Las Terrenas blends European sophistication with untouched Dominican nature, making it a dream for a young family seeking an authentic escape without sacrificing convenience. The beaches here feature exceptionally calm, reef-protected waters where your toddler can safely splash while you enjoy the shade of leaning coconut palms. With a highly walkable beach path and a distinct French expatriate influence, you'll have access to high-end infant amenities, gourmet bakeries, and pristine nature all within a 15-minute radius.
🚕 Getting there: ~30 minute private SUV transfer from Samaná El Catey International Airport (AZS) via the smooth, scenic Boulevard Turístico del Atlántico

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Playa Las Ballenas

An incredibly calm, shallow beach with almost no waves, making it the safest spot in Las Terrenas for a toddler to splash. The dirt road running parallel is flat enough for an easy stroller walk right next to the water.

Morning / 1-2h

Playa Bonita Pedestrian Path

A beautiful, paved, car-free walking path lined with palm trees and boutique hotels directly on the sand. It is completely stroller-accessible, allowing for a breezy morning walk while your baby naps.

Morning / 3-4h

Caño Frío at Playa Rincón

A crystal-clear, slow-moving freshwater river that flows directly into the ocean, shaded by a dense canopy of trees. The shallow sandy bottom is ideal for cooling off with an infant away from the midday sun.

Afternoon / 1h

Supermercado Lindo

An impressive French-owned supermarket stocked with high-quality European baby formulas, organic purees, French cheeses, and diapers. It is the ultimate logistical safety net for a traveling dad to stock up on essentials.

Evening / 1-2h

Pueblo de los Pescadores

A colorful beachfront strip of former fishermen's homes converted into charming, open-air cafes and restaurants. The wide wooden boardwalk is stroller-friendly and perfect for an early evening seaside stroll.

Morning / 3-4h

Casca El Limón via Parada El Limón

Samaná's iconic waterfall; while not stroller-friendly, parents can safely hike or ride horses with baby in a sturdy carrier. The lush jungle path ends at a majestic falls pool where you can dip your toes.

Morning / 3-4h

Playa La Playita

A picture-perfect, shallow cove located in Las Galeras with white sand and calm, pool-like water. You can rent loungers with wide umbrellas to keep the baby completely shaded while you enjoy fresh coconut water.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Playa Punta Popy

A lively, wind-protected beach very close to the town center featuring warm, shallow tide pools during low tide. It is highly accessible with plenty of shade trees right up to the water's edge.

Morning / 4-5h

Los Haitises National Park Boat Tour

A scenic, covered boat tour starting from Samaná Port through spectacular mangrove forests and ancient caves. The calm boat ride is highly engaging for young kids, and baby carriers are perfect for exploring the cave interiors.

🍽️ Where to eat

18 El Dieciocho

Order the wood-fired lobster pizza. This spacious, open-air beachfront restaurant allows your toddler to play safely in the clean sand right next to your table while you dine.

Atlantis Hotel Restaurant

Order the wild snapper simmered in local coconut milk. Set on a quiet, breezy garden terrace at Playa Bonita, the tables are widely spaced, easily accommodating a large stroller.

El Mosquito Al Mar

Order the fresh mahi-mahi tacos and a passionfruit mocktail. It features a sprawling, secure oceanfront grassy lawn where toddlers can freely toddle around away from the tide.

Zucca

Order the homemade pumpkin gnocchi. The staff is incredibly welcoming to young families, providing sturdy high chairs and fast service before the toddler bedtime meltdown.

Boulangerie Française

Order the freshly baked almond croissants and quiche Lorraine. Opening early at 7:00 AM, it is the perfect spot for families with early-rising babies to enjoy a world-class French breakfast.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Sublime Samana Hotel & Residences

Offers luxury multi-bedroom suites with fully equipped kitchens, ideal for storing milk and preparing baby food. The property features a shallow, expansive canal-style pool and a private, quiet stretch of Playa Bonita.

Bahia Principe Grand El Portillo

An upscale family-friendly resort with a dedicated kids' water park, baby club, and family suites. The resort sits on a calm, reef-protected beach lagoon with virtually no waves, perfect for infant swims.

Hotel Playa Colibri

Features spacious beachfront apartments with kitchenettes situated right on the calm shores of Playa Las Ballenas. It features a peaceful, gated garden pool and highly attentive staff who can arrange reliable local babysitting.
