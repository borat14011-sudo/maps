Santa Barbara

← back to travel board
Nestled between the dramatic Santa Ynez Mountains and the sparkling Pacific, Santa Barbara welcomes families with its red-tiled roofs and palm-fringed beaches. This coastal paradise blends outdoor exploration with relaxed California vibes, making it effortless to navigate with strollers in tow. From tide pooling to historic plaza wandering, every corner offers a gentle, sun-soaked adventure for all ages.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min rideshare or taxi from Santa Barbara Airport (SBA) to downtown or waterfront beach hotels.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Santa Barbara Zoo

Renowned for its manageable size and gorgeous ocean views, this zoo lets kids get up close to giraffes. The park features a giant grassy hill perfect for toddlers to roll down safely.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Stearns Wharf & Sea Center

Stroll along California's oldest working wooden wharf to spot wild sea lions and grab ice cream. Toddlers will love the interactive, low-to-the-ground marine touch tanks at the Sea Center located directly on the pier.

Morning / 2-3h

MOXI, The Wolf Museum of Exploration + Innovation

A fully interactive science museum where kids can build race cars, play with wind tunnels, and splash in rooftop water tracks. The ground floor features excellent sensory exhibits ideal for curious toddlers.

Morning / 2h

Santa Barbara Botanic Garden

Wander through towering redwood groves and sensory-rich native plant paths that are wide and stroller-friendly. Toddlers will love exploring the dedicated natural play area in the Backcountry section.

Afternoon / 1h

Alice Keck Park Memorial Garden

This lush botanical oasis features a massive koi and turtle pond that will keep little ones mesmerized for hours. Toddlers can safely wander the sensory pathways, play on the wooden gazebo, and watch the ducks glide by.

Morning or Afternoon / 2-3h

Leadbetter Beach

With its wide sandy shore and gentle, rolling waves, this beach is the safest spot in town for toddlers to splash. A grassy park area right next to the sand is perfect for stroller-rolling and shaded picnics.

Lunch / 1-2h

Santa Barbara Public Market

A bustling indoor food hall where everyone can choose their own meal, from artisanal tacos to wood-fired pizza. Toddlers will love the high-energy, casual atmosphere and the hand-scooped ice cream from Rori's.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Shoreline Park

Perched on a cliff overlooking the ocean, this park features a fantastic marine-themed playground and paved paths perfect for stroller walks. Keep an eye out for migrating whales from the telescopes while toddlers enjoy the swings.

Afternoon / 1h

Santa Barbara County Courthouse

Ride the elevator up the clock tower for spectacular 360-degree views of the city's red-tiled roofs. Toddlers have plenty of open, grassy space in the sunken gardens below to run around and burn off energy.

🍽️ Where to eat

McConnell's Fine Ice Creams

Treat the family to a scoop of legendary, locally-made Double Peanut Butter Chip in a fresh waffle cone.

The Boathouse at Hendry's Beach

Enjoy fresh local seafood right on the sand while the kids play in the tide lines just steps from your outdoor patio table.

Los Agaves

Order the crowd-pleasing chicken quesadillas for the kids and the famous sizzling Molcajete for the adults at this ultra family-friendly Mexican favorite.

Lucky Penny

Grab a wood-fired artisanal pizza to share on the sunny, casual patio covered in thousands of real copper pennies.

Jeannine's Restaurant & Bakery

Start your day with decadent challah French toast and fresh pastries on their bustling, kid-welcoming outdoor patio.
