What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Acropolis Museum

Marvel at the Caryatids in this architectural masterpiece, which is fully stroller-accessible with spacious elevators, wide ramps, and air-conditioned nursing rooms.

Midday 12-2pm / 2h

National Garden

Escape the midday heat under the shade of giant palms and orange trees, featuring wide dirt paths perfect for stroller walks and a duck pond to entertain Mary Claire.

Afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Stavros Niarchos Foundation Cultural Center

Stroll the expansive, flat, stroller-friendly park, enjoy the dancing fountains, and relax in the air-conditioned, ultra-modern baby changing facilities.

Early morning 8-9:30am / 1.5h

Temple of Olympian Zeus

Walk the flat, gravel paths of this majestic open-air archaeological site, offering plenty of space to wheel a stroller without the steep climbs of the main Acropolis.

Sunset 6-7:30pm / 1.5h

Dionysiou Areopagitou Street

Walk this iconic, wide, paved pedestrian promenade that wraps around the Acropolis, offering breathtaking views, street musicians, and an entirely stroller-smooth path.

Morning 10am-1pm / 3h

Lake Vouliagmeni

Experience this thermal spa lake in the Athenian Riviera, featuring calm, warm waters, luxury sun loungers, and paved paths ideal for a peaceful nap in the shade.

Evening 5-7pm / 2h

Flisvos Marina

Walk along this upscale mega-yacht harbor featuring a wide, flat boardwalk, ideal for pushing the stroller while enjoying sea breezes and viewing spectacular yachts.

Afternoon 2-3:30pm / 1.5h

Museum of Cycladic Art

View minimalist ancient sculptures in a quiet, climate-controlled mansion with elevator access and a serene, stroller-friendly cafe for a quiet bottle feeding.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Plaka District

Explore the lower, flatter streets of Athens' oldest neighborhood, avoiding the steep steps of Anafiotika, to shop for high-end Greek cotton baby clothes and olive wood souvenirs.

🍽️ Where to eat

Nolan

Savor Michelin-starred Greek-Asian fusion like steam buns with cod; their outdoor terrace is perfect for an early 7:00 PM seating before Mary Claire's bedtime.

Ithaki Restaurant

Enjoy fresh sea bass with panoramic sea views in Vouliagmeni; the spacious outdoor terrace accommodates strollers easily, and the gentle sea breeze acts as a natural lullaby.

Strofi Tavern

Relish traditional lamb kleftiko on the rooftop with Acropolis views; request an early outdoor table on the lower terrace for easier stroller access and lower noise levels.

Geros Tou Moria

Experience a historic taverna in Plaka with outdoor street-side seating, offering delicious moussaka and a lively yet welcoming atmosphere where baby noise blends right in.

Vassilenas

Dine on modern seafood like cod carpaccio in a chic, contemporary setting that welcomes families during early dining hours, complete with spacious highchairs and attentive service.
