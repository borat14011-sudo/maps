Corfu enchants families with its lush, cypress-covered hills, shallow turquoise waters, and cobblestone town squares. Children will love exploring hidden fortresses and eating gelato in the shade of elegant French-style arcades. It is the perfect blend of Greek island relaxation and rich, accessible history.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min taxi to Corfu Town or ~40 min private transfer to north coast beach resorts

What to do

Morning / 2h

Old Fortress of Corfu

Scale the ramparts of this 15th-century Venetian fortress for panoramic sea views. The wide paths are perfect for active kids, but use a carrier instead of a stroller for the final steep climb.

Morning to Afternoon / 4h

Paleokastritsa Beach

This famous bay features crystal-clear, calm waters ideal for splashing. Rent a motorboat to explore nearby sea caves, which toddler-aged adventurers will find absolutely magical.

Midday / 1-2h

Corfu Aquarium

A small, highly engaging aquarium showcasing local marine life and rescued reptiles. It is the perfect air-conditioned escape where toddlers can see sea stars and iguanas up close.

Late Afternoon / 2h

Spianada Square & Liston Promenade

Let the kids run free on the grassy lawns of Greece's largest square before grabbing gelato under the Venetian-style arcades. The entire area is pedestrian-friendly and highly stroller-accessible.

Morning / 2h

The Governor's Olive Mill

Walk through ancient, giant olive trees and taste award-winning oils while the kids enjoy the open-air space. They offer family-friendly tours where children can touch the traditional press.

Full Day / 5-6h

Aqualand Corfu Water Park

One of Europe's largest water parks, featuring a dedicated, massive shallow pool and fantasy playground for toddlers. Older kids will love the lazy river and winding slides.

Morning / 1-2h

Corfu Town Central Market

Savor local life at this bustling open-air market filled with fresh figs, local honey, and freshly caught fish. Toddlers will love the vibrant colors and tasting sweet kumquat treats.

Morning / 2-3h

Mon Repos Palace & Gardens

The birthplace of Prince Philip, this estate is surrounded by a shady, forested park leading down to a quiet beach. Wide, shaded dirt paths make it ideal for a peaceful stroller walk.

Afternoon / 2h

La Grotta Beach Bay

A stunning cove nestled between high cliffs with vibrant green waters. While the steps are too steep for strollers, families with older toddlers will love watching brave divers jump from the cliffs.

🍽️ Where to eat

Spiro's Taverna

Order the fresh grilled sea bream and traditional Greek salad while the kids play steps away on the beach.

Rex

Try the traditional Corfiot 'Sofrito' (pan-fried beef in a white wine garlic sauce) at this historic, family-friendly spot off the Liston.

The Venetian Well

Indulge in handmade ravioli in a quiet, romantic courtyard that is spacious enough for toddlers to sit comfortably.

Taverna Klimataria

Get the famous zucchini carpaccio and baked feta in this cozy, family-run tavern with ultra-welcoming service.

Bougatsa Chaniotiko

Grab hot, crispy bougatsa pastry filled with sweet custard and dusted with powdered sugar, a guaranteed hit for kids.

👨‍👩‍👧 Rent a car with pre-booked car seats to easily navigate the winding coastal roads and reach secluded family beaches. Always carry a lightweight stroller for Corfu Town's flat areas, but pack a baby carrier for the steps and rocky historical sites.
