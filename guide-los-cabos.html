What to do

Afternoon 3-5pm / 2h

Plaza Mijares

The historic main square of San José del Cabo is exceptionally spacious, offering plenty of shade under palm trees and broad, stroller-friendly paved walkways. Will and Kathryn can enjoy a relaxing afternoon stroll here while Mary Claire naps peacefully in her stroller away from the beachfront winds.

Thursday Evening 5-7pm / 2h

San Jose del Cabo Art Walk

Held on Thursday evenings between November and June, this vibrant walk through the cobblestone gallery district is an cultural must. To navigate the uneven historic streets with Mary Claire, skip the stroller and use a premium baby carrier so you can easily browse the modern fine art.

Morning 9-11am / 2h

El Arco

Book a private luxury catamaran charter to view Los Cabos' iconic rock arch where the Pacific meets the Sea of Cortez. Private charters provide a quiet, shaded cabin interior for Mary Claire to rest, avoiding the loud, crowded water taxis and ensuring infant life jackets are on board.

Early Morning 8-10am / 2h

Medano Beach

As Cabo’s main swimmable beach, the calm waters are ideal for dipping Mary Claire's toes in the ocean. Arrive early in the morning when the beach is serene and the party crowd has yet to wake up, and secure a shaded, private oceanfront beach cabana.

Morning 9am-12pm / 3h

Chileno Bay

Known for having some of the calmest, safest swimmable waters in the region, this protected cove is perfect for a family beach day. Set up an upscale UV beach tent on the soft sand so Mary Claire can play safely in the shade while Kathryn and Will take turns snorkeling.

Lunch & Stroll 11:30am-2:30pm / 3h

Flora Farms

A lush 25-acre organic oasis tucked into the foothills of the Sierra de la Laguna Mountains. The peaceful farm features manicured, stroller-friendly grass lawns and shaded pathways where Mary Claire can observe the organic gardens in a quiet, tranquil environment.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Acre

This ultra-hip organic farm and sanctuary offers a relaxing escape amid dense palm trees. Will and Kathryn can wander the peaceful, shaded walking paths with a stroller and introduce Mary Claire to the gentle rescue animals, including the farm's resident donkey.

Midday 1-2pm / 1h

Iglesia San Jose

This historic 1730 buttery-colored mission church sits at the heart of San José del Cabo. The quiet, cool, air-conditioned interior offers a respectful, serene respite to escape the midday Baja sun with Mary Claire while admiring the local colonial architecture.

Morning 11am-1pm / 2h

Los Tamarindos

Located just a short drive from San José del Cabo, this historic organic farm dating back to the 19th century is remarkably peaceful. Take a gentle morning walk with Mary Claire through the shaded fields and citrus groves before enjoying a quiet, open-air farm-to-table lunch.

🍽️ Where to eat

Flora's Field Kitchen

Indulge in farm-fresh wood-fired pizzas and organic handmade pastas on a stunning outdoor terrace. The early 5:00 PM dinner seating is highly welcoming to luxury families, and the gentle buzz of the open-air restaurant easily masks any happy baby babble from Mary Claire.

Acre Restaurant

Savor refined global-modern cuisine in a lush, jungle-like outdoor sanctuary. The spacious layout allows plenty of room for an upscale stroller at the table, and the soothing ambient music paired with early dinner reservations makes for a stress-free culinary experience with an infant.

Los Tamarindos

Enjoy exquisite Mexican-Mediterranean farm-to-table dishes on a historic, elevated stone patio. The atmosphere is incredibly spacious and peaceful, offering highchairs upon request and a calm, breezy setting that keeps Mary Claire comfortable during an early dinner.

El Farallon

Tucked into the cliffs at Waldorf Astoria, this dramatic oceanfront venue offers high-end seafood. Reserve the earliest 5:30 PM sunset seating; the natural roaring sound of the crashing waves below acts as the perfect white noise to soothe Mary Claire right to sleep.

La Lupita Taco & Mezcal

For a vibrant yet sophisticated taste of local culture, head to their lovely open-air courtyard in San José del Cabo. Arriving right at their 5:00 PM opening ensures a relaxed environment, complete with highchairs and delicious, upscale gourmet tacos for Will and Kathryn.
