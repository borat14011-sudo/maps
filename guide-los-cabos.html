Where the golden Baja desert crashes into the brilliant turquoise Sea of Cortez, Los Cabos offers a magical playground for families. From gentle, swimmable bays to lively boat rides past iconic stone arches, this coastal paradise balances relaxation with vibrant Mexican culture. Little ones will be captivated by breaching whales and soft sandy beaches, making every moment an unforgettable family memory.
🚕 Getting there: ~35-45 min private shuttle or rental car from Los Cabos International Airport (SJD) to San Jose del Cabo or Cabo San Lucas hotels

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Land's End & El Arco Boat Tour

Take a glass-bottom boat to see the famous stone arch where the sea meets the ocean, watching for lazy sea lions along the way. Toddlers will love peering through the glass bottom to spot colorful fish.

Morning / 3-4h

Chileno Beach

This calm, blue-flag certified bay is one of the safest swimming spots in Cabo, perfect for snorkeling or building sandcastles. Toddlers can safely splash in the gentle waves and explore clean tide pools.

Early Morning / 1-2h

San Jose del Cabo Estuary

Stroll along the flat, shaded pathways of this freshwater sanctuary to spot colorful birds and turtles. Toddlers will enjoy the easy, stroller-friendly boardwalks and quiet atmosphere away from the beach crowds.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Flora Farms

Explore this lush, 25-acre organic farm tucked into the foothills, complete with beautiful gardens and resident farm animals. Toddlers can run on the grassy lawns and visit the friendly turtles in the pond.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Wirikuta Botanical Cactus Garden

Wander through thousands of cacti species and giant stone sculptures in this quiet desert garden. Toddlers will be fascinated by the quirky cactus shapes, but keep them close to avoid prickly encounters.

Evening / 1-2h

San Jose del Cabo Main Plaza

Run around the vibrant, pedestrian-friendly historic plaza and enjoy some local ice cream while admiring the colonial architecture. Toddlers can safely wander the open plaza while parents enjoy the lively evening atmosphere.

Morning / 2-3h

Medano Beach

Cabo’s most popular beach offers soft sand and calm waters, perfect for renting a shaded umbrella and watching the boats zip by. Toddlers will love the gentle shoreline, but watch out for vendor traffic on the sand.

Morning / 1-2h

Cuadra San Francisco

A professional equestrian center offering gentle trail rides for older kids and pony rides for the little ones. Toddlers will love petting the miniature horses and taking a supervised, slow lead-line ride.

Morning / 2h

Whale Watching Catamaran Tour

Board a catamaran to watch majestic humpback whales breach and play in the warm waters (seasonal from December to April). Toddlers will be thrilled by the splashy action, but opt for a larger, stable catamaran for a smoother ride.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Office on the Beach

Dig your toes into the sand at this legendary beachfront spot while kids eat quesadillas and parents enjoy fresh fish tacos.

Flora's Field Kitchen

Savor incredible wood-fired artisan pizzas made with ingredients harvested just steps from your table.

Tacos Guss

Feast on casual, budget-friendly, and authentic al pastor tacos with a self-serve salsa bar that kids love customizing.

Maria Corona

Enjoy traditional Mexican mole and hand-pressed tortillas in a beautiful, spacious courtyard with live mariachi music.

La Lupita Taco & Mezcal

Try the gourmet duck or pork belly tacos in a vibrant, family-friendly patio setting with live music.

👨‍👩‍👧 Always double-check beach safety flags before letting kids swim, as strong undertows make many Cabo beaches non-swimmable. Rent a sturdy all-terrain stroller or carry a baby carrier, as the sandy spots and historic cobblestones can be tough on standard strollers.
