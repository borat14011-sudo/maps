Passions on the Beach

Savor fresh local snapper right on the sand of Eagle Beach. Booking the early 5:30 PM seating ensures a gorgeous sunset view, while the ambient sound of gentle waves keeps Mary Claire calm, with highchairs available on request.

The West Deck

An open-air, casual-chic deck serving Caribbean jerk chicken and fresh seafood right over the water. The lively but relaxed outdoor atmosphere easily masks baby noises, and there is ample space next to the tables for a stroller.

Elements Restaurant

Featuring high-end pan-seared sea bass, this oceanfront deck offers a spacious layout. An early dinner booking allows Will and Kathryn to enjoy a sophisticated meal while Mary Claire comfortably sleeps in her stroller next to the table.

Eduardo's Beach Shack

Famous for its fresh mango smoothies and vibrant acai bowls, this beachside shack features flat, paved access from the boardwalk. It is the perfect spot for a quick breakfast while Mary Claire enjoys fresh mashed fruit.

Barefoot Restaurant

An elegant dining experience where you can eat with your feet in the sand. Excellent early-evening service, highly accommodating staff who provide sturdy highchairs, and a spacious layout that easily fits a luxury stroller.
