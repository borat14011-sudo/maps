Aruba

← back to travel board
Fringed by powdery white sands and calm, turquoise waters, Aruba is a playground designed by nature for young explorers. Beyond the gentle shores of Palm Beach, families can discover windswept desert landscapes, colorful butterfly gardens, and vibrant local culture. It’s an easygoing paradise where warm breezes and shallow shores make for a stress-free Caribbean escape.
🚕 Getting there: ~20 min taxi or pre-arranged private shuttle from Queen Beatrix International Airport to the low-rise or high-rise beach hotels

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Baby Beach

An incredibly shallow, calm lagoon where toddlers can safely splash and wade in crystal-clear water while you spot small colorful fish near the shore.

Morning / 1-2h

The Butterfly Farm

Walk through a lush tropical rainforest enclosure filled with hundreds of exotic butterflies that might land on your colorful clothes, a magical sight for toddlers.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Philip's Animal Garden

A wonderful shelter and rehabilitation center where kids can feed friendly alpacas, sheep, and monkeys with provided bags of healthy treats.

Morning / 1h

Aruba Aloe Factory Museum and Store

Take a quick, fascinating tour to see how soothing aloe is harvested and made into products, with plenty of room for toddlers to wander outdoors.

Morning / 3-4h

Arikok National Park Fontein Cave

Explore ancient cave drawings and let toddlers dip their feet in the fresh water stream where tiny fish gently tickle their toes.

Late Afternoon / 2-3h

Eagle Beach

Consistently rated one of the world's best beaches, its wide sandy shores and iconic, low-slung Fofoti trees provide great shade and space for building sandcastles.

Morning / 1-2h

Donkey Sanctuary Aruba

A peaceful sanctuary where children can pet and feed friendly, rescued wild donkeys that love human interaction.

Evening / 2h

Palm Beach Plaza Mall

Perfect for an evening stroll, featuring kid-friendly fountains, open-air play areas, and a small bowling alley to keep little ones entertained.

Late Afternoon / 1h

California Lighthouse

Climb to the top for panoramic coastal views, or stay on the grassy base with toddlers to enjoy a fresh coconut from a local vendor.

🍽️ Where to eat

Eduardo's Beach Shack

Grab a refreshing, nutrient-packed acai bowl or a fresh mango smoothie right on the beach, perfect for a quick and healthy toddler snack.

L.G. Smith's Steak & Chop House

Treat the family to an upscale but welcoming dinner featuring their incredibly tender USDA Choice filet mignon and decadent mac and cheese side.

The Dutch Pancake House

Order giant, paper-thin Dutch pancakes loaded with fresh strawberries and whipped cream, or savory options with Gouda cheese that kids will love.

Barefoot Restaurant

Enjoy upscale dining with your toes literally in the sand, where kids can play quietly at the water's edge while you savor the fresh catch of the day.

Zeerovers

An ultra-casual, over-water local favorite where you buy fresh fried red snapper and shrimp by the basket, accompanied by tasty local pan bati.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack plenty of reef-safe sunscreen and a high-quality wind clip for your stroller, as Aruba's constant trade winds are delightfully cooling but can easily blow away loose items. Stick to the calm, western coastlines like Palm Beach and Baby Beach when swimming with toddlers, as the rough eastern coast has dangerous undertows.
