What to do

Early morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM / 1.5h

Palermo Cathedral

Stunning Norman architecture with a flat interior and external marble paths that are stroller-accessible, allowing you to avoid the steep roof climb with the baby.

Late morning 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM / 2h

Orto Botanico di Palermo

A lush 25-acre botanical garden filled with giant ficus trees, offering cool shade, flat gravel pathways perfect for strollers, and a peaceful atmosphere for Mary Claire's morning nap.

Morning 8:30 AM - 11:30 AM / 3h

Mondello Beach

Pristine white sands and shallow, calm turquoise waters just north of the city; reserve a private shaded cabana at a luxury lido like Albaria for ultimate baby comfort.

Early morning 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM / 2h

Palazzo dei Normanni and Cappella Palatina

Gilded Byzantine mosaics and royal chambers; use the elevator access for strollers and visit early to enjoy the glittering gold in a calm, quiet setting.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM / 1.5h

Villa Giulia

Palermo's oldest public park features symmetrical pathways, marble fountains, and plenty of benches under lush palm trees, making it ideal for a relaxed stroller walk.

Afternoon 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM / 1h

Teatro Massimo

Italy's largest opera house boasts a monumental grand plaza; while the interior tours have steps, the exterior square is entirely flat, car-free, and perfect for pushing a stroller.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM / 1.5h

Castello della Zisa

A 12th-century Norman castle surrounded by quiet, landscaped gardens with cooling water fountains, providing a shady refuge from the Sicilian sun.

Morning 9:30 AM - 10:15 AM / 45m

Piazza Pretoria

Admire the magnificent fountain from the flat pedestrian perimeter, which allows for great photos with Mary Claire without negotiating the fountain's stone steps.

Morning 8:00 AM - 9:30 AM / 1.5h

Mercato del Capo

A colorful historical street market; visit during the early morning hours before the crowds peak to enjoy the vibrant sights and smells from the safety of a sturdy stroller.

Savor the classic sfincione and panelle on their spacious outdoor terrace, allowing Mary Claire to watch the piazza bustle without indoor noise.

Gagini Restaurant

A Michelin-starred culinary journey; book the earliest evening seating in their quiet, air-conditioned dining room and request a corner table for infant quietness.

Bye Bye Blues

Award-winning seafood in Mondello featuring a beautiful, breezy outdoor patio perfect for keeping baby cool while indulging in fresh sea urchin pasta.

Ristorante Palazzo Branciforte

Elegant dining situated inside a historic library courtyard, providing a quiet, peaceful, and visually stunning atmosphere with plenty of space for a stroller.
