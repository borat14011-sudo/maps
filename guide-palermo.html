What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 2h

Orto Botanico di Palermo

A lush, 30-acre historic botanical garden boasting wide, shaded gravel pathways and towering giant ficus trees that offer a cool, quiet sanctuary perfect for Mary Claire's morning nap.

Late Afternoon 5:30 PM - 7:00 PM / 1.5h

Foro Italico Promenade

A vast, grassy coastal lawns area with a completely flat, paved boardwalk ideal for smooth stroller pushing while enjoying gentle sea breezes and striking harbor views.

Morning 8:30 AM - 11:30 AM / 3h

Mondello Beach (Lido Valdesi)

Escape to Palermo's premier soft-sand beach; reserve a private shaded cabana at Lido Valdesi where the calm, shallow turquoise waters are ideal for dipping baby's toes.

Early Morning 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM / 2h

Palazzo dei Normanni and Cappella Palatina

Marvel at the dazzling golden Byzantine mosaics using a high-quality baby carrier to navigate the grand historic staircases comfortably and beat the midday crowds.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM / 1.5h

Villa Giulia

Palermo's oldest public park, featuring elegant neoclassical fountains, symmetric shaded avenues, and quiet benches perfect for an uninterrupted outdoor feeding session.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 1.5h

Cattedrale di Palermo Piazza

Admire the breathtaking crown jewel of Sicilian-Norman architecture from the wide, stroller-friendly pedestrianized piazza, ideal for photos without dealing with indoor steps.

Evening 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM / 1h

Teatro Massimo External Esplanade

Stroll around Italy's largest opera house; the monumental stone stairs lead to a sprawling pedestrian plaza where the baby can watch street performers in a spacious, open setting.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM / 2h

Cantieri Culturali alla Zisa

A converted industrial cultural space with flat paved areas, contemporary art galleries, and plenty of open air, offering a peaceful alternative to the busy historic center.

Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM / 1.5h

Mercato del Capo (Early Tour)

Experience a sensory feast of colorful Sicilian produce and fresh seafood; visit right at 9:00 AM with a baby carrier before the narrow aisles become packed and noisy.
