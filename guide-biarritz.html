Biarritz

← back to travel board
Biarritz blends Belle Époque elegance with a relaxed, kid-friendly surf culture on the rugged Basque coast. Families will love the sandy coves, interactive marine life, and stroller-friendly coastal paths. It is the perfect blend of French sophistication and laid-back beach adventure.
🚕 Getting there: ~15-minute taxi or local line 4 bus ride from Biarritz Pays Basque Airport (BIQ) to central beach hotels

What to do

Morning / 2-4h

Grande Plage

The heart of Biarritz's beach scene, offering wide golden sands perfect for sandcastle building. Strollers can easily roll along the flat, paved promenade bordering the beach.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Rocher de la Vierge

Walk across the dramatic metal footbridge designed by Gustave Eiffel to reach this iconic rock formation surrounded by crashing waves. Toddlers will love watching the ocean spray, but hold their hands tightly.

Morning / 2h

Aquarium de Biarritz

Discover sharks, rays, and colorful fish in an Art Deco building right on the cliffs. Toddlers will be absolutely captivated by the seal-feeding sessions held twice daily on the terrace.

Morning / 1-2h

Les Halles de Biarritz

A bustling undercover market filled with local Basque cheeses, fresh berries, and gateau Basque. It is a fantastic sensory experience for kids, with easy finger foods available at every stall.

Afternoon / 1h

Phare de Biarritz

Climb the 248 steps of this historic lighthouse for panoramic views of the coast. For families with toddlers, the surrounding grassy lawns are perfect for running around safely.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Plage du Port Vieux

This tiny, horseshoe-shaped cove is sheltered from the heavy Atlantic swells and wind, making it the safest spot in town for toddlers to splash. The calm water acts like a natural swimming pool.

Morning / 2-3h

Cité de l'Océan

A modern, highly interactive museum dedicated to the science of the ocean, featuring 3D virtual reality surf experiences and 4D cinema. Toddlers will love the sensory light installations and hands-on water play tables.

Midday / 1-2h

Parc Mazon

A peaceful public park located just a short walk from the center, featuring a fantastic, fenced-in children's playground. Shady trees offer a welcome respite from the midday sun for toddlers to play safely.

Evening / 30 mins

Le Carrousel de Biarritz

Located right near the Grande Plage, this beautiful, vintage-style double-decker carousel is a must-visit for young kids. Toddlers will delight in choosing their favorite horse or carriage for a magical spin.

🍽️ Where to eat

Maison Balme

Grab a casual stool inside the vibrant Les Halles market and order the delicious, kid-approved truffle scrambled eggs or a simple truffle croque monsieur.

Le Carlos

Head to this relaxed clifftop spot for family sunset dining and order the crispy Basque patatas bravas and fresh fish tacos.

Miremont Patisserie

Indulge in a classic, decadent hot chocolate and a slice of their famous, melt-in-the-mouth Gateau Basque while enjoying sweeping ocean views.

Chez Albert

Sit on the lively harbor-side terrace at Port des Pêcheurs and order the fresh grilled sea bass served with simple, kid-approved buttered potatoes.

Milwaukee Café

This highly welcoming, family-friendly spot is perfect for a hearty brunch of fluffy blueberry pancakes, fresh fruit smoothies, and homemade cookies.

👨‍👩‍👧 Keep in mind that Biarritz is quite hilly with lots of steps down to the beaches, so a lightweight, sturdy stroller with good suspension is highly recommended. Always check the daily tide tables before heading to the beaches, as high tide can quickly swallow up the sandy shores where kids play.
