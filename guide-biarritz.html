Biarritz

← back to travel board
Biarritz blends Belle Époque prestige with a laid-back, world-class surf culture, making it exceptionally welcoming for families who do not want to compromise on travel style. The wide, paved coastal promenades are a dream for high-end strollers, while the Atlantic breeze keeps midday temperatures manageable for sensitive baby skin. From fresh market tastings at Les Halles to sunset drinks overlooking the waves, you will experience authentic French-Basque living at a highly relaxed, toddler-friendly pace.
🚕 Getting there: An easy 10-15 minute private Mercedes van transfer from Biarritz Pays Basque Airport (BIQ) straight to your central hotel.

What to do

Morning / 2h

Aquarium de Biarritz

Watch your toddler's eyes light up at the seal feeding pool and the giant shark tank. The entire facility is fully stroller-accessible via wide ramps and elevators.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Rocher de la Vierge

Walk the dramatic footbridge designed by Gustave Eiffel for sweeping views of the rugged coastline. The paved path leading up is perfectly flat and stroller-friendly.

Morning / 1-2h

Les Halles de Biarritz

Navigate this bustling covered market to sample local sheep's cheese, Bayonne ham, and fresh Gâteau Basque. Go early around 8:30 AM when it is spacious enough to easily push a stroller through the aisles.

Afternoon / 30m

Le Carrousel de Biarritz

A beautiful, vintage double-decker carousel positioned right on the scenic edge of the Grande Plage. It is a magical, low-speed ride perfect for a toddler sitting on your lap while enjoying the sea breeze.

Morning / 2h

Plage de la Milady

This wide sandy beach features the excellent Aire de Jeux de la Milady, a playground specifically designed with soft safety flooring and baby swings. The adjacent promenade is ideal for a smooth stroller walk while watching local surfers.

Afternoon / 2h

Lac de Marion

Escape the coastal wind at this tranquil, shaded lake park just five minutes from the city center. It features a fantastic fenced wooden playground for toddlers and a flat 1.2-kilometer walking path lined with giant oaks.

Morning / 1h

Port des Pêcheurs

Stroll down to this historic, quiet fishing port nestled between the cliffs to see the colorful wooden crampottes. The paved, car-free harbor is perfect for a peaceful morning walk with a sleeping baby in the carrier.

Morning / 1h

Grande Plage Promenade

Walk the wide, completely flat paved seawall promenade stretching from the casino to the Bellevue headland. It is the ultimate spot for a morning coffee run with the stroller, offering unobstructed views of the crashing Atlantic waves.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Esplanade du Phare

Relax on the manicured grassy lawns surrounding the historic Biarritz Lighthouse, perfect for spreading out a blanket for toddler tummy time. While the lighthouse climb is too steep for toddlers, the clifftop gardens offer stroller-friendly paths and panoramic views.

🍽️ Where to eat

Chez Albert

Order the fresh, wild-caught sea bass cooked à la plancha. The spacious outdoor terrace at the Port des Pêcheurs allows plenty of room for a stroller, and the ambient sound of the ocean easily masks any baby noise.

Etxola Bibi

Grab a clifftop table and order a simple charcuterie board. This completely outdoor, casual café overlooking Côte des Basques is ultra-forgiving for toddler noise and lets you watch the sunset over the surf.

Bar Jean

Sip a glass of Rioja and enjoy high-end pintxos right next to the market. The lively, high-energy atmosphere is incredibly family-friendly because a crying baby won't disrupt the bustling crowd.

Le Royalty

Indulge in a classic steak frites on the wide, heated pavement terrace of this legendary brasserie. The service is fast and highly professional, and they readily accommodate strollers at their outdoor tables.

Le Carlos

Enjoy fresh ceviche and local craft beers on the spacious, trendy outdoor deck. Located right on the Côte des Basques beach, it is perfect for an early 7 PM dinner before the late-night crowd arrives.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Hôtel du Palais Biarritz

The ultimate luxury oceanfront palace featuring a magnificent heated outdoor pool, kid-friendly wading areas, and premium cribs. Their professional babysitting services allow you and your wife to enjoy a quiet Michelin-starred dinner downstairs.

Sofitel Biarritz Le Miramar Thalassa Sea & Spa

Boasts a stunning heated outdoor saltwater pool and direct, step-free access to the quiet Plage du Miramar. The spacious family suites easily fit high-end travel cribs, and the staff happily assists with bottle warming and baby amenities.

Regina Biarritz Hotel & Spa

A gorgeous Belle Époque hotel offering quiet, spacious rooms with high ceilings and excellent soundproofing for a sleeping infant. The heated outdoor pool and calmer location near the lighthouse make for a highly relaxing family retreat.

👨‍👩‍👧 Biarritz is highly walkable, but several beach access points have steep steps, so bring a lightweight, sturdy stroller with good suspension for the cobblestones alongside a high-quality baby carrier. Most local restaurants do not open for dinner until 7:30 PM, so adapt to late lunches at Les Halles or head to casual beach clubs like Etxola Bibi for early evening bites. Public baby-changing tables are rare in historic cafés, so keep a portable changing mat in your diaper bag and utilize the spacious facilities at the local department store, Galeries Lafayette.
