Lake Constance (Bodensee)

← back to travel board
Lake Constance offers a high-yield family vacation where Germany, Austria, and Switzerland meet along a paved, stroller-friendly shoreline. It combines clean Swiss-style efficiency with relaxed Bavarian hospitality, making it incredibly easy to navigate with an infant. You get Michelin-caliber dining, historic lake towns, and sweeping Alpine views without the chaotic logistics of deep mountain travel.
🚕 Getting there: ~60-75 minute private Mercedes V-Class transfer or direct SBB train from Zurich Airport (ZRH) to Konstanz

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Insel Mainau

A spectacular car-free flower island featuring manicured Mediterranean gardens, a massive butterfly house, and wide, paved paths. The designated 'Wasserwelt' playground has excellent splash areas and toddler-safe climbing structures, all highly accessible with a Bugaboo stroller.

Morning / 2-3h

Pfänderbahn

A panoramic cable car that whisks you up Pfänder mountain for sweeping views of 240 Alpine peaks. The cable car cabins easily accommodate double strollers, and the flat, gravel loop at the summit wind past a mountain wildlife park with deer and ibex.

Afternoon / 2h

Affenberg Salem

Germany's largest outdoor monkey enclosure where over 200 Barbary macaques roam freely in a secure pine forest. The smooth gravel path is completely stroller-friendly, and the sheer novelty of the animals keeps toddlers completely captivated.

Afternoon / 2h

Zeppelin Museum

A world-class aviation museum housed in a spacious, climate-controlled Art Deco harbor station. The wide galleries, large elevators, and interactive displays make it an effortless indoor option for a rainy day with a stroller.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Lindau Harbor Promenade

The most iconic harbor on the lake, framed by a massive stone Bavarian Lion and a historic lighthouse. Walk the perfectly flat, paved sea wall with a sleeping baby before grabbing a high-quality espresso at a harbor-front cafe.

Morning / 3h

Bodensee-Therme Überlingen

A premium thermal bath complex featuring a dedicated, warm-water kids' zone with slides, fountains, and shallow splash pools. They offer family changing rooms with changing tables and direct, grassy access to the cooler lake waters.

Morning / 1-2h

Wochenmarkt Konstanz

A vibrant bi-weekly farmers market at Stephansplatz filled with artisanal cheeses, fresh lake fish, and seasonal berries. It is a fantastic, sensory-rich morning stroll where you can easily source premium organic snacks for your toddler.

Morning / 2-3h

Haustierhof Reutemühle

An impeccably maintained, park-like petting zoo home to over 200 species of domestic and exotic animals. The wide gravel walkways are easy to push a stroller through, and toddlers can feed sheep, goats, and alpacas directly.

Morning / 3h

Rheinfall (via Schloss Laufen)

Europe's most powerful waterfall, located just west of the lake. Accessing the falls via the Schloss Laufen side provides panoramic glass elevators and paved, barrier-free pathways directly to the viewing platforms high above the roaring water.

🍽️ Where to eat

Konzil Konstanz

Order the classic, rich Kässpätzle (traditional cheese noodles) at this historic lakefront hall. The cavernous dining room easily absorbs toddler noise, and the expansive lakeside beer garden offers plenty of room to park a stroller next to your table.

Wirtshaus am See

Savor a perfectly crisp Wiener Schnitzel while sitting directly on the Austrian lakefront. The service is fast and professional, and the lively outdoor terrace is loud enough that you won't have to worry if your infant gets fussy.

Restaurant Strandhaus

Feast on premium, slow-smoked beef brisket and local craft beers at this exceptionally family-welcoming lakeside barbecue spot. It features a relaxed beach-club vibe with an outdoor play area where toddlers can stretch their legs while you wait for your food.

Restaurant Bad Schachen

Enjoy refined, freshly caught local whitefish (Felchen) on an elegant, upscale terrace right on the water. It offers premium fine dining where staff are highly accommodating to families, providing sturdy high chairs and early dinner service.

Seerestaurant Belvedere

Stop on the Swiss side of the lake for a premium Zürcher Geschnetzeltes (sliced veal in cream sauce). The space is modern, bright, and highly accessible, offering clean changing facilities and beautiful floor-to-ceiling harbor views.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Hotel Riva Konstanz

An ultra-premium 5-star superior lakeside hotel featuring a heated rooftop pool and spacious, soundproofed family suites. They provide high-end wooden cribs, premium baby amenities on request, and an exceptional on-site Michelin-starred dining option.

Hotel Bad Schachen

A grand, historic lakeside resort with park-like grounds, a private lido beach, and a massive heated indoor pool. It is highly accommodating to families, offering generous room layouts, professional babysitting services, and stroller-friendly pathways throughout the property.

Steigenberger Inselhotel

Housed in a former Dominican monastery on its own private island, this luxury hotel offers quiet seclusion just steps from downtown Konstanz. The vast lawns leading down to the water are perfect for baby-carrier walks, and the family rooms are exceptionally quiet.

👨‍👩‍👧 Carry a mix of Euros and Swiss Francs (CHF) as you will cross international borders frequently, though credit cards are widely accepted for premium services.
