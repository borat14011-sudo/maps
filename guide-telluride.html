What to do

Morning 9:00 AM / 1h

Telluride Mountain Village Gondola

A free, 13-minute aerial ride connecting historic downtown with Mountain Village. The enclosed cabins are completely stroller-friendly, quiet, and offer breathtaking box-canyon views that will mesmerize Mary Claire.

Morning 10:30 AM / 1.5h

San Miguel River Trail

A flat, gravel-packed trail running directly parallel to the river. It is perfect for an outdoor stroller walk with dense shade trees and a soothing river soundtrack for a morning baby nap.

Afternoon 2:00 PM / 1.5h

Telluride Town Park

A scenic community park featuring lush grassy fields, baby-friendly swings, and shaded pavilion areas. Ideal for laying out a blanket for Mary Claire's tummy time while enjoying dramatic mountain backdrops.

Morning 10:00 AM / 1h

Wilkinson Public Library

An award-winning public library boasting a dedicated, vibrant children's room. It features soft climbing mats, high-quality wooden toys, and baby board books in a peaceful, temperature-controlled indoor space.

Friday Morning 11:00 AM / 1.5h

Telluride Farmers' Market

Held on South Oak Street, this vibrant market offers organic local produce and artisanal goods. The flat street is highly stroller-accessible, allowing you to pick up fresh organic fruit purees for the baby.

Afternoon 3:30 PM / 1h

Bridal Veil Trailhead Viewpoint

Witness Colorado's tallest free-falling waterfall from the easily accessible gravel trailhead at the end of the canyon. You can drive right up to the viewpoint, avoiding strenuous hiking with an infant carrier.

Morning 11:00 AM / 1.5h

Telluride Historical Museum

Housed in a historic 1896 miners' hospital, this quiet museum features smooth stroller-friendly ramps. The calm, dimly lit galleries are perfect for keeping a sleeping baby cool and comfortable.

Afternoon 4:00 PM / 1.5h

Heritage Plaza in Mountain Village

A European-style, pedestrian-only plaza with soft artificial turf patches, comfortable outdoor seating, and fire pits. Safe and clean enough for Mary Claire to practice crawling in the fresh mountain air.

Afternoon 1:30 PM / 1h

Elks Park

A grassy, shaded park located right on Colorado Avenue in downtown Telluride. It is the perfect central spot to relax under a mature tree during a break from boutique shopping.

🍽️ Where to eat

Allred's Restaurant

Savor high-end Colorado lamb chops at 10,500 feet. Request an early 5:30 PM seating by the window; the staff is incredibly accommodating, and the early hour ensures a quiet space before baby's bedtime.

Floradora Saloon

A historic main-street staple serving up the famous Flatliner Burger. The atmosphere is casual, lively, and welcoming to families, featuring sturdy wooden highchairs and quick service.

Brown Dog Pizza

World-championship Detroit-style square pizzas in a bustling, casual sports-bar environment. The cheerful ambient noise easily drowns out any unexpected baby babble or fussiness.

Smuggler Union Restaurant & Brewery

Outstanding local craft beers and premium fish and chips. They feature a spacious, heated outdoor patio with plenty of clearance for parked luxury strollers.

The Chop House at New Sheridan

Upscale steakhouse dining with early evening patio seating on the historic sidewalk. Enjoy prime Rocky Mountain elk tenderloin while Mary Claire rests comfortably in her stroller beside the table.
