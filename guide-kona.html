Kona

← back to travel board
Kona welcomes families with its dramatic volcanic landscapes, balmy turquoise waters, and laid-back aloha spirit. From splashing in shallow tide pools alongside green sea turtles to tasting sweet shave ice, this sun-drenched coast is a natural playground for all generations. It is a place where tropical adventures and relaxing beach days blend seamlessly into the perfect family escape.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min drive via rental car or pre-booked airport shuttle to downtown Kailua-Kona hotels.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Kahaluu Beach Park

This sheltered cove is the ultimate spot for family snorkeling with colorful fish and calm, shallow waters. Toddlers will love splashing in the sandy, protected shallows while looking for green sea turtles.

Morning / 1-2h

Kona Farmers Market

Wander through vibrant stalls filled with exotic tropical fruits, locally grown Kona coffee, and handmade Hawaiian crafts. Toddlers will enjoy sampling sweet apple bananas and fresh mangoes.

Morning or Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Kaloko-Honokohau National Historical Park

Walk the flat, stroller-friendly trail to see ancient Hawaiian fishponds and spot wild sea turtles resting on the sand. Toddlers can easily handle the short, scenic walk to the beach.

Morning / 2-3h

Kamakahonu Beach

Located right in downtown Kailua-Kona, this ultra-calm, reef-protected beach is like a giant, gentle swimming pool. It is the absolute safest spot for toddlers to test their ocean legs and build sandcastles.

Morning / 3-4h

Kealakekua Bay Family Boat Cruise

Take a gentle morning boat cruise to this pristine marine sanctuary to see spinner dolphins and vibrant coral reefs. Many family charters provide toddler-sized life vests and glass-bottom viewing options.

Midday / 1-2h

Mountain Thunder Coffee Plantation

Escape the coastal heat and tour a scenic coffee farm nestled in a lush cloud forest. Toddlers will love meeting the friendly farm animals while parents sample award-winning Kona coffee.

Late Afternoon / 2h

Anaehoomalu Bay

This picturesque, crescent-shaped beach offers calm waters, tide pools, and spectacular sunset views. Toddlers can safely splash in the shallow tide pools while parents relax on the soft sand.

Morning / 1-2h

Puuhonua o Honaunau National Historical Park

Explore a beautifully preserved ancient Hawaiian sanctuary featuring towering wooden tiki statues and flat walking paths. Toddlers will be fascinated by the giant wooden carvings and the calm ocean cove.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Harold H. Higashihara Park

This massive, community-built wooden playground features castles, slides, and swings with beautiful ocean views. Toddlers can safely run around the dedicated tot lot while burning off extra energy.

🍽️ Where to eat

Da Poke Shack

Grab a bowl of incredibly fresh, locally caught poke paired with family-friendly sides like sesame garlic edamame.

Kona Brewing Co.

Enjoy artisanal, island-inspired pizzas and craft beers in a lively, highly kid-friendly outdoor garden courtyard.

Ululani's Hawaiian Shave Ice

Treat the family to ultra-fine shave ice with natural tropical syrups, topped with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

On the Rocks

Dine right on the sandy shoreline with live Hawaiian music where kids can play in the sand while you wait for fresh fish tacos.

808 Grindz Cafe

Feast on a budget-friendly local breakfast featuring fluffy macadamia nut pancakes topped with rich coconut sauce.
