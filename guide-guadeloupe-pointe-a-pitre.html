Scenic coastal viewpoint with gentle breeze and shallow rock pools. Stroller not ideal on sand, but baby can be carried. Skip if too windy.

Wide sandy beach with calm waters and lifeguards. Stroller-friendly path along shore. Bring umbrella for baby.
