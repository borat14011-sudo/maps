The Budva Riviera charms discerning travelers with its dramatic limestone cliffs, sparkling turquoise Adriatic waters, and medieval walled towns. For Will, Kathryn, and baby Mary Claire, this sun-drenched coast offers a sophisticated European escape without the frantic pace of the French Riviera. The flat, stroller-friendly promenade connecting Budva Marina to Rafailovići ensures breezy sunset walks with the luxury of seaside dining steps away. From the quiet, shaded paths of Miločer Park to the calm, gentle ripples of Pržno Cove, it is an idyllic haven for infants and parents alike.
🚕 Getting there: Tivat Airport (TIV) to the Budva hotel strip is a scenic 30-40 minute drive. The ultimate luxury family transport is a private Mercedes V-Class transfer pre-arranged through your resort, equipped with a top-tier rear-facing infant car seat (like a Cybex or Maxi-Cosi) and generous cargo capacity to comfortably fit an UppaBaby Vista stroller and multi-week luggage.

What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Miločer Park

Stroll along shaded, paved pathways lined with ancient olive trees and fragrant pine forests. This former royal estate is completely stroller-accessible, offering cool shade for Mary Claire's morning nap and breathtaking views of the sea.

Early morning 8-10am / 2h

Budva Old Town (Stari Grad)

Explore the historic, walled citadel using a high-quality baby carrier rather than a stroller to navigate the charming medieval cobblestones. The early morning hours offer quiet streets, cool stone architecture, and quiet corners perfect for peaceful nursing breaks.

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Pržno Cove

A picturesque, former fishing village featuring a calm, sheltered bay protected by stone reefs. The shallow, wave-free waters are exceptionally safe for dipping baby Mary Claire's toes, and the stone promenade offers easy stroller access to waterfront cafes.

Morning 8-10am / 2h

Mogren Beach

Reached via a dramatic cliffside pedestrian path starting from Old Town, this beach is scenic but best visited with a baby carrier. The early morning hours guarantee quiet shores, soft golden sands, and spectacular limestone backdrops before the midday crowds arrive.

Evening 6-7pm / 1h

Budva Marina

Admire sleek yachts docked against medieval stone walls along a wide, flat, completely paved harbor front. Perfect for an effortless stroller walk with Mary Claire, offering refreshing sea breezes and plenty of upscale benches for resting under the palms.

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Petrovac Promenade

A quieter coastal enclave south of Budva featuring a beautifully paved, traffic-free boardwalk. Stroll past red-roofed stone buildings with the stroller, enjoying gentle sea spray, soft Mediterranean breezes, and excellent shade from towering pine trees.

Midday 11am-2pm / 3h

Dukley Beach Club

Enjoy a premium, private beach day featuring plush, oversized daybeds, pristine white sand, and highly attentive service. Parents can lounge under wide canvas umbrellas providing ample shade for Mary Claire while sipping fresh mocktails with direct, level sand access.

Late afternoon 5-6pm / 1h

Sveti Stefan Viewpoint

Take in the iconic, red-roofed islet from the designated grassy terraces of the public park overlooking the causeway. Completely paved and stroller-friendly, it offers a stunning, windswept backdrop for a high-end family portrait without navigating steep stairs.

Morning 9am-12pm / 3h

Jaz Beach

One of the widest sandy stretches on the Riviera, offering incredibly shallow, calm entry waters perfect for infants. Rent a premium luxury cabana for a half-day, ensuring total sun protection, easy beach stroller parking, and convenient access to clean seaside facilities.

🍽️ Where to eat

Dukley Seafront Restaurant

Indulge in wild sea bass carpaccio on a spacious, multi-level wooden deck suspended over the Adriatic. The open-air luxury setting easily accommodates large strollers, has high-end wooden highchairs available, and features gentle sea breezes that act as natural white noise for a sleeping infant.

Olive Restaurant

Located directly opposite the iconic Sveti Stefan islet, this upscale venue serves premium Mediterranean lamb under ancient olive trees. The spacious outdoor terrace is exceptionally stroller-friendly, offers a sophisticated atmosphere with early dinner seating options, and has highly attentive staff.

Konoba Langust

Savor fresh, line-caught grilled fish right on the water's edge in peaceful Pržno Cove. A quiet, high-end yet unpretentious seafood institution where the gentle lapping of the waves keeps baby calm, and the outdoor terrace easily fits a stroller bedside.

Demizana

A refined seafood establishment near the entrance of Old Town featuring a quiet, leafy garden terrace that provides excellent natural shade. Celebrated for its traditional octopus salad and superb local wines, the spacious table layout ensures privacy for families with young children.

Jadran Kod Krsta

While bustling, this highly-regarded waterfront institution offers spacious outdoor seating directly on the Budva promenade. Perfect for an early 6:00 PM dinner, the friendly staff goes out of their way to welcome infants, and the ambient hum of the seaside easily masks any baby babble.
