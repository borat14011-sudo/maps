What to do

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

The Florida Aquarium

An incredible, fully air-conditioned marine sanctuary featuring massive viewing windows. Perfect for Mary Claire to watch colorful fish in a calm, quiet environment with wide, stroller-friendly paths.

Late afternoon 4:00-5:30pm / 1.5h

Tampa Riverwalk

A beautifully paved, waterfront promenade that is entirely stroller-accessible. Stroll along the sparkling Hillsborough River past contemporary architecture with plenty of shaded benches for quick feedings.

Morning 10:00am-12:00pm / 2h

Glazer Children's Museum

Located right on the Riverwalk, this museum features the cozy 'Tugboat Tots' area, specifically designed for infants and toddlers under 3 to explore safely away from older kids.

Afternoon 2:00-3:30pm / 1.5h

Henry B. Plant Museum

Set within a striking 1891 Victorian palace, the surrounding Plant Park features quiet, shaded paths under towering oak trees, offering a peaceful, low-sensory outdoor retreat for the baby.

Early evening 5:00-6:30pm / 1.5h

Sparkman Wharf

An upscale outdoor entertainment plaza with a spacious, Astroturfed lawn. It is the perfect spot for Mary Claire to practice crawling while parents enjoy craft drinks and beautiful harbor views.

Morning 11:00am-12:30pm / 1.5h

Tampa Museum of Art

A highly sophisticated, quiet indoor museum with exceptionally wide galleries. The serene atmosphere is wonderful for a sleeping baby in a stroller while parents admire modern and classical art.

Morning 9:00-11:00am / 2h

Lettuce Lake Park

A peaceful nature sanctuary featuring an extensive, shaded wooden boardwalk over scenic hardwood swamps. Perfect for a quiet, nature-filled stroll to spot gentle turtles and birds without any city noise.

Morning 8:30-11:00am / 2.5h

Pass-a-Grille Beach

A relaxed, luxury-tier stretch of Gulf coastline just west of Tampa. It features calm, shallow, lagoon-like waters and soft white sand that is gentle on a baby's sensitive skin.

Morning 10:00am-12:00pm / 2h

Serengeti Express Train at Busch Gardens

Skip the rollercoasters and board this open-air, slow-moving train. It offers a gentle, breezy ride through the Serengeti Plain where Mary Claire can safely spot free-roaming rhinos and zebras.
