Tampa

← back to travel board
Tampa blends Gulf Coast sunshine with vibrant culture and thrilling wildlife encounters perfect for all ages. From strolling along the world's longest continuous sidewalk to meeting manatees face-to-face, this historic harbor town offers endless family fun. It is an easy-going destination where toddlers can play in splash pads and parents can soak in the historic Ybor City charm.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min rideshare or taxi from Tampa International Airport (TPA) to downtown hotels, or ~30 min to beachside resorts.

What to do

Morning / Full Day

Busch Gardens Tampa Bay

This massive African-themed park features world-class roller coasters alongside a vibrant zoo, plus a dedicated Sesame Street Safari of Fun area with water play and gentle rides perfect for toddlers.

Morning / 3-4h

The Florida Aquarium

Walk through a simulated Florida wetlands trail to see river otters and alligators, then let the toddlers cool off at the fantastic outdoor Splash Pad.

Late Afternoon / 2h

Tampa Riverwalk

This 2.6-mile pedestrian path connects major parks and museums along the waterfront, offering an easy stroller walk with a great playground stop at Cotanchobee Fort Brooke Park.

Morning / 3-4h

ZooTampa at Lowry Park

Voted one of the best family zoos in the country, it features up-close animal encounters and a shaded water play area designed specifically for little ones.

Morning / 2-3h

Glazer Children's Museum

An interactive indoor wonderland where toddlers can splash at water tables, climb a giant playhouse, or pretend to run a veterinary clinic.

Lunchtime / 2h

Armature Works / Heights Public Market

A historic mixed-use food hall where parents can sample diverse gourmet bites while toddlers run around freely on the spacious outdoor riverfront lawn.

Afternoon / 1h

TECO Line Streetcar

Ride these fully restored, air-conditioned historic yellow streetcars for free between downtown and Ybor City, which toddlers find just as exciting as a theme park ride.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Tampa Electric's Manatee Viewing Center

Walk along safe, elevated boardwalks to spot hundreds of wild manatees gathering in the warm water, and let toddlers touch gentle rays in the tidal basin tank.

Morning / 2h

Lettuce Lake Park

Spot wild alligators and turtles from the safety of a massive boardwalk looping through a cypress swamp, offering a peaceful, stroller-friendly nature escape.

🍽️ Where to eat

Columbia Restaurant

Order the famous tableside '1905' Salad and the child-friendly Cuban sandwich inside Florida's oldest historic dining rooms.

Ulele

Try the chargrilled oysters and native Florida alligator hushpuppies while the kids play safely on the fenced-in outdoor grassy lawn.

Goody Goody Burgers

Grab their legendary Butterscotch Pie and classic smash burgers in a bright, retro diner setting that welcome messy toddlers.

Watervue Grille

Savor fresh Florida grouper tacos on the outdoor patio where toddlers can watch boats cruise along the Garrison Channel.

Taco Bus

Order the kid-friendly cheese quesadillas and authentic street tacos served straight out of a brightly painted school bus.

👨‍👩‍👧 Buy a Tampa Bay CityPASS to save over 50% on top attractions like the Zoo and the Aquarium. Always pack a change of clothes and a towel in your stroller, as almost every major Tampa sight features an enticing toddler splash pad.
