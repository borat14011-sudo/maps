Formentera

Formentera captures the ultimate Mediterranean allure with its crystalline waters and relaxed, exclusive atmosphere. Will and Kathryn will find solace on the powdery white sands of Platja de Ses Illetes, which mimic a private Caribbean lagoon perfect for baby Mary Claire's first sensory dips. The island’s flat topography and wooden beach boardwalks make navigating with a premium stroller effortlessly smooth. Between upscale beachfront dining and sunset viewings, this island provides a serene, low-stress paradise for discerning young families.
🚕 Getting there: From Ibiza Airport (IBZ), take a 15-minute private Mercedes V-Class transfer to Ibiza Port, board the Balearia fast ferry (30 minutes) in a reserved first-class cabin, and arrive at La Savina port for a 10-minute private resort transfer to your hotel.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Platja de Ses Illetes

This iconic white-sand beach offers incredibly calm, shallow, crystal-clear waters resembling a giant natural swimming pool, ideal for dipping baby Mary Claire's toes safely without rough waves.

Late afternoon 5:30 PM - 7:00 PM

Far de Barbaria

An iconic lighthouse set on dramatic cliffs; the access road is entirely flat and paved, making it a peaceful, stroller-friendly walk with spectacular, breezy ocean views.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:30 PM

San Francesc Xavier

The island's charming capital features a completely pedestrianized historic center with boutique shopping, shaded plazas, and smooth stone paving perfect for an easy stroller stroll.

Early morning 8:30 AM - 10:00 AM

S'Estany Pudent (Ses Salines)

A tranquil, flat nature reserve path where you can spot pink flamingos; use a baby carrier to enjoy the serene morning breeze and peaceful birdwatching away from crowds.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM

Far de la Mola

The oldest lighthouse on the island perched on high cliffs, featuring a flat, easily accessible viewing area with dramatic scenery and a quiet, peaceful atmosphere during early hours.

Evening 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM

Es Pujols Beach Promenade

A wide, paved pedestrian boardwalk lined with shops and restaurants, providing a refreshing sea breeze and effortless stroller pushing for a relaxing family sunset walk.

Late afternoon 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Platja de Migjorn Boardwalk

A beautiful, rustic wooden boardwalk weaving along the south coast's dunes, allowing you to walk comfortably above the sand with baby Mary Claire in a carrier.

Late afternoon 5:00 PM - 7:30 PM

La Mola Craft Market

A colorful, upscale artisan market held on Wednesdays and Sundays, featuring local craft stalls, soft acoustic music, and shaded pathways suitable for a leisurely family walk.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM

La Savina Marina

A sleek, completely flat waterfront marina perfect for checking out luxury yachts, enjoying gentle sea breezes, and pushing a stroller with zero steps or obstacles.

🍽️ Where to eat

Juan y Andrea

Savor their famous lobster sautéed with garlic (Bogavante frito con ajos) at tables set directly in the sand, where the staff easily accommodates strollers right at your table under large shade sails.

Es Molí de Sal

Enjoy a signature Arroz a la Banda (seafood rice) inside a beautifully converted windmill, offering a spacious, elegant outdoor terrace with spectacular sea views and plenty of space for highchairs.

Chezz Gerdi

Feast on fresh Mediterranean catch-of-the-day and wood-fired pizzas on a stylish, wide wooden terrace that welcomes families with highchairs, colorful decor, and a friendly, relaxed staff.

Can Carlitos

Indulge in creative, upscale tapas by Chef Nandu Jubany on a lovely seaside terrace in La Savina, featuring early evening dinner reservations perfect for baby's bedtime schedule.

Beso Beach Formentera

Taste the fresh wild sea bass baked in a salt crust at this stylish beach club, which offers shaded wooden dining platforms that are highly accommodating for strollers during their early, quieter lunch seatings.
