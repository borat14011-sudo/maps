Formentera

← back to travel board
Formentera offers the ultimate barefoot luxury getaway for a young family, trading Ibiza's high-octane party scene for untouched turquoise waters and a relaxed, low-key rhythm. The island's intimate scale means you are never more than a 15-minute drive from shallow, wave-free beaches that act as natural wading pools for toddlers. It is a sophisticated sanctuary where a finance-minded dad can unplug completely while his wife and daughter enjoy safe, sun-drenched strolls along wooden boardwalks.
🚕 Getting there: ~45 minutes total: a 15-minute taxi from Ibiza Airport to Ibiza Port, a 30-minute fast ferry to La Savina Port, and a 10-minute taxi to your beach resort.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Playa de Ses Illetes

This world-famous beach features calm, shallow turquoise waters that mimic a giant swimming pool, perfect for a toddler's first dip. Use the flat wooden boardwalks to easily wheel your stroller close to the shoreline without getting stuck in deep sand.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Far de la Mola

This iconic cliffside lighthouse offers dramatic panoramic views of the sea and houses a small, stroller-accessible cultural museum. The entire outdoor viewing plaza is flat and paved, making it incredibly easy to push a stroller while enjoying the ocean breeze.

Evening / 2h

Far de Cap de Barbaria

A breathtaking, rugged lighthouse landscape that feels like the end of the earth and is famous for its sunsets. The access road is closed to private vehicles, providing a wide, flat, paved 1.5-kilometer stroller walk down to the structure.

Evening / 1-2h

La Savina Marina Market

An upscale evening market set along the harbor selling chic local crafts, linen clothing, and leather goods. The wide, flat wooden marina boardwalk is completely pedestrianized and ideal for an evening stroller cruise.

Morning / 3h

Playa de Migjorn (Es Arenals)

A vast, pristine stretch of sand on the south coast with excellent family-friendly facilities and shallow entry. The extensive wooden boardwalk running parallel to the beach allows for smooth stroller rolling right alongside the sea.

Morning / 2h

Centro de Interpretación de Can Marroig

A tranquil nature reserve featuring shaded pine forests, unique rock formations, and quiet picnic spots. The main paths are flat, hard-packed dirt that can easily handle a rugged stroller or a baby carrier walk.

Morning / 2h

Plaza de la Constitución (Sant Francesc Xavier)

The charming, car-free main square of Formentera's capital, lined with whitewashed buildings and a fortress-like church. This wide, flat pedestrian zone is perfect for letting your toddler safely toddle around while you grab an espresso.

Evening / 1-2h

Es Pujols Beach Promenade

A vibrant wooden pedestrian boardwalk overlooking the ocean, bustling with evening energy and local artisan stalls. The promenade is fully paved and features a nearby children's playground for easy toddler entertainment.

Morning / 1-2h

Estany Pudent Path (Camí des Brolls)

A scenic, flat walking path wrapping around a tranquil saltwater lagoon known for spotting pink flamingos. The flat, hard-packed dirt trail is easily navigable with a sturdy stroller or a baby carrier.

🍽️ Where to eat

Juan y Andrea

Order the signature salt-baked sea bass ('lubina a la sal') at this legendary beachfront establishment. It is located directly on the sand of Ses Illetes, meaning your toddler can play happily at your feet while you dine.

Chezz Gerdi

Order the artisanal burrata pizza and fresh seafood pasta at this chic, energetic spot. It features a relaxed, spacious outdoor terrace with plenty of room for strollers, plus a famous red vintage camper van that keeps toddlers visually entertained.

Es Molí de Sal

Order the traditional Formentera lobster stew with fried eggs and potatoes. Housed in a converted mill, its wide, flat outdoor deck easily accommodates double strollers and offers cool ocean breezes to keep baby comfortable.

Es Caló Restaurant

Order the fresh grilled local squid and seafood paella. The restaurant features a sprawling wooden deck over a rocky cove, offering plenty of white noise from the waves to soothe a fussy baby.

Chiringuito Bartolo

Order the classic, no-fuss cheeseburger and fresh blue cheese croquettes at this iconic blue wooden shack. Located at the quiet end of Migjorn beach, it has a highly relaxed, casual vibe where baby noise is easily absorbed by the open air.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Gecko Hotel & Beach Club

Located directly on Migjorn Beach, it offers spacious rooms, lush manicured lawns perfect for crawling babies, a family-friendly pool, and complimentary high-quality wooden cribs.

Hotel Riu La Mola

Boasts a dedicated children's pool, direct access to the quietest stretch of Migjorn beach, and spacious family suites that comfortably fit a crib and stroller.

Es Pas Formentera Agroturismo

A quiet, high-end rural estate with massive private terraces, self-catering kitchenettes perfect for prepping baby food, and a gated, peaceful pool area.

👨‍👩‍👧 Rent a small SUV or a car with a pre-booked car seat directly at La Savina Port, as local taxis rarely carry infant seats. Stock up on premium diapers, baby formula, and organic pouches at the Syp Eroski supermarket in Sant Francesc, which has the largest selection on the island. Keep a high-quality baby carrier handy, as the wooden beach boardwalks are excellent but the deep, powdery sand of Ses Illetes will stop any stroller in its tracks.
