Jurmala

Jurmala blends the soothing therapy of towering pine forests and shallow Baltic waters with vibrant pedestrian streets and historic wooden architecture. It is a playground where children can build sandcastles on quartz sands while parents breathe in the crisp, therapeutic sea air. This seaside escape offers the perfect mix of active outdoor fun and relaxing nature therapy for families of all ages.
🚕 Getting there: ~20-25 min taxi ride from Riga International Airport directly to Majori beach hotels.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Jurmala Beach (Majori & Dzintari areas)

This legendary 26-kilometer stretch of white quartz sand features shallow, calm waters ideal for paddling. Toddlers will love the ultra-fine sand for digging and the gentle slope into the sea.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Dzintari Forest Park

A massive pine forest park featuring modern playgrounds, obstacle courses, inline skating paths, and a tall observation tower. Toddlers have their own dedicated, fenced-off play areas cushioned with soft woodchips.

Morning or Afternoon / 3-4h

Līvu Akvaparks

One of the largest indoor and outdoor water parks in Northern Europe, packed with slides and pools. Toddlers will enjoy Captain Kid's Land, a safe, shallow zone featuring gentle sprayers and mini-slides.

Morning / 1-2h

Jūrmala Open-Air Museum

Step back in time at this interactive museum displaying a 19th-century fishing village with authentic cottages and nets. Toddlers can safely run on the grassy paths and climb on the historic wooden fishing boats.

Early Morning / 2h

Great Kemeri Bog Boardwalk

A breathtaking walk through a unique wetland ecosystem over wooden planks, surrounded by stunted pines and dark pools. Toddlers can easily walk the shorter 1.4 km loop, but bring a carrier as the boardwalk has no side railings.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Jomas Street Promenade

The main pedestrian heartbeat of Jurmala, lined with gingerbread-style wooden architecture, cafes, and street performers. Toddlers can safely toddle around the car-free street and enjoy watching bubble blowers and musicians.

Morning / 1-2h

Ragakāpa Nature Park

A peaceful pine forest reserve featuring scenic wooden boardwalks, wind-blown dunes, and viewing platforms overlooking the Lielupe River mouth. Toddlers will love finding pinecones along the easy, stroller-friendly paths.

Morning / 1h

Kauguri Market

A lively local market filled with the sights and smells of fresh Baltic produce, flowers, and local smoked fish. Toddlers will delight in tasting fresh, locally grown sweet strawberries, raspberries, and blueberries.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Dzintari Concert Hall Gardens

The beautiful gardens surrounding the historic wooden concert hall are filled with manicured paths, modern sculptures, and floral displays. Toddlers can run safely on the smooth stone paths while parents admire the unique architecture.

🍽️ Where to eat

Lighthouse Grill

Order the juicy grilled chicken skewers from the dedicated kids' menu while enjoying the cozy, glass-enclosed terrace on Jomas Street.

Double Coffee Jurmala

Get the delicious sweet crepes with fresh berries and ice cream at this highly casual, family-welcoming spot on the main promenade.

36.Line Grill Restaurant

Savor the freshly grilled Baltic salmon right on the beach while your kids play in the clean sand just steps from your outdoor table.

Zem Burām

Order the traditional, crispy Latvian potato pancakes served with cold sour cream, which toddlers love to dip and eat.

Madam Brioš

Pick up their famous freshly baked butter croissants and colorful macarons, and let the kids play in their lovely indoor play corner.
