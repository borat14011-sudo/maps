Luxury family beach escape in Baltic serenity

Jurmala

← back to travel board
Jurmala offers a serene Baltic coastline with a 33-kilometer stretch of fine white sand, perfect for a luxury family retreat. The gentle waves of the shallow lagoon provide safe paddling for infants, while the stroller-ready Jomas iela boardwalk leads to chic cafes and boutiques. Your baby will love the soft sand and calm sea air, and the beachfront resorts offer private pool villas for splashing in privacy. This is a refined seaside haven where modern comfort meets untouched nature.
🚕 Getting there: From Riga International Airport (RIX), the drive to Jurmala's hotel strip takes approximately 25 minutes. Book a private transfer with an infant car seat and stroller storage—companies like Riga Airport Transfers or Baltic Taxi offer luxury sedans or minivans. Alternatively, the direct train from Riga Central Station to Majori station takes 30 minutes (with elevators and wide aisles for strollers) but a private car is more comfortable with a baby.

What to do

Early morning 9-11am

Jurmala Beach

The main beach is wide, clean, and gently sloping—ideal for a baby to sit in shallow water. Plenty of shade under pine trees and lifeguards on duty. Early morning is calmest for a family walk.

Morning 10am-12pm

Dzintari Forest Park

A peaceful nature reserve with wooden boardwalks through pine forest and dunes. Completely stroller-accessible, with shaded paths and a small playground. Great for a quiet morning stroll away from the crowds.

2-3 hours after 10am

Livu Aqua Park

Indoor and outdoor water park with a dedicated toddler zone, shallow pools, and gentle slides. The wave pool and lazy river are baby-friendly. Bring swim nappies. Located inside the Livu complex with cafes.

10am-12pm

Jurmala Open-Air Museum

A collection of historic wooden houses showcasing Latvian rural life. Stroller-friendly paths through the grounds. Quiet and educational, with shaded benches for feeding or changing. Best in late morning before heat.

Late afternoon 4-6pm

Ragakapa Nature Park

A protected dune area with elevated wooden walkways over white sand and pine forests. Stroller accessible, with stunning views of the Baltic Sea. Calm and uncrowded, perfect for a gentle walk with a baby carrier.

Afternoon 2-3pm

Lielupe River Cruise

A scenic 1-hour boat trip along the Lielupe River, past pine forests and wooden villas. Covered boats with shade and seating. Baby can enjoy the breeze; bring a blanket.

Check schedule

Dzintari Concert Hall

Historic venue hosting classical and jazz concerts. Check for family-friendly daytime performances. The grounds have gardens for strolling. Not suitable for loud late shows with an infant.

11am-12:30pm

Jurmala City Museum

Small museum in a charming wooden building detailing Jurmala's resort history. Quiet, air-conditioned, and stroller-friendly. Perfect for a brief cultural break during hot midday.

Late afternoon 4-5pm

Majori Promenade

The main pedestrian street (Jomas iela) lined with shops, ice cream parlors, and galleries. Wide, flat, and stroller-perfect. Early morning is peaceful with fewer crowds. Stop at a café for a treat.

🍽️ Where to eat

36.line

Upscale seafood restaurant with a terrace overlooking the sea. Signature dishes include grilled Baltic fish and lobster bisque. Highchairs available, early seating at 6pm recommended. Noise level moderate; good for a relaxed family dinner.

Restaurant Upe

Modern Latvian cuisine in a stylish setting with outdoor garden. Try the venison steak or dill-cured salmon. Highchairs and a children's menu available. Quiet ambiance suitable for babies.

Fabrika

Industrial-chic bistro serving Mediterranean-Latvian fusion. Known for truffle pasta and wood-fired pizza. Spacious interiors with highchairs and a play corner. Early dinner before 7pm is calm.

Paved Restorāns

A cosy spot on Jomas iela with a large terrace. Offerings include Latvian pancakes and hearty soups. Highchairs present, and the terrace is stroller-friendly. Ideal for a casual lunch.

Jūras Brīze

Beachfront restaurant with panoramic windows. Specializes in grilled fish and local seafood. Early bird seating (5-6pm) quieter. Highchair and booster seat available. Reservations recommended with infant.

👨‍👩‍👧 Pack sunscreen, hats, and a UV umbrella for the beach—Jurmala's sun can be intense midday, but the sea breeze keeps it comfortable. The main promenade (Jomas iela) and all beaches have smooth, stroller-friendly wooden walkways; however, some forest trails are sandy—use a baby carrier for Ragakapa. For medical needs, the Jurmala Hospital (Slimnīcas iela 1) has a pediatrician, and nearby pharmacies in Majori stock formula and diapers. Forum Pharmacy (Jomas iela 45) is reliable.
