Nestled along the Gulf of Riga, Jurmala offers a serene escape defined by historic Art Nouveau wooden villas, therapeutic pine forests, and a legendary 26-kilometer strip of powdery white sand. For Will, Kathryn, and baby Mary Claire, this seaside retreat provides an ultra-low-stress environment featuring stroller-ready wooden boardwalks that glide directly over the sand dunes. The shallow, tide-free waters of the Baltic Sea act as a giant, calm wading pool perfect for an infant's gentle introduction to the ocean. From private beachfront spa sanctuaries to breezy, high-end dining terraces, Jurmala seamlessly blends Baltic wellness with refined family comfort.
🚕 Getting there: 20 minutes from Riga International Airport (RIX) to the Jurmala hotel strip via a private luxury Mercedes V-Class transfer equipped with a pre-installed Cybex infant car seat and ample luggage capacity.

What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Dzintari Forest Park

A lush pine park featuring smooth, paved, and wooden stroller-friendly boardwalks winding through towering trees. Perfect for morning strolls with Mary Claire, offering fresh air, plenty of shade, and upscale, quiet coffee kiosks for Will and Kathryn.

Morning 8-10am / 2h

Majori Beach Boardwalk

Stretching directly onto the sand, this wooden beach path allows seamless stroller access to the shoreline without sinking into the soft sand. Early mornings offer a tranquil breeze and empty shores, ideal for a peaceful nap in the stroller.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Jomas Street Pedestrian Promenade

A completely car-free, smooth pedestrian boulevard lined with historic architecture, boutiques, and outdoor cafes. It is highly stroller-accessible, lively yet spacious enough to avoid crowds during mid-afternoon walks.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Ragakāpa Nature Park

An eco-trail featuring well-maintained wooden boardwalks and viewing platforms cutting through ancient pine forests and dunes. Offers pristine tranquility, filtered sunlight for baby Mary Claire, and stunning sea vistas.

Morning 11am-1pm / 2h

Jūrmala Open-Air Museum

Located inside Ragakapa park, this quiet historic museum showcases 19th-century fisherman farmsteads. The shaded gravel paths are smooth enough for a sturdy stroller, offering a quiet, low-sensory environment for a morning nap.

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Kemeri Historical Park

A beautifully restored resort park featuring romantic bridges, sulfur spring pavilions, and wide, flat gravel pathways shaded by mature trees. Exceptionally stroller-friendly and incredibly peaceful, far from any highway noise.

Afternoon 4-5pm / 1h

Dzintari Concert Hall Gardens

The manicured gardens of this legendary concert venue are open for public walks during non-show hours. The beautifully paved, quiet grounds are perfect for a brief, peaceful stroll with Mary Claire in the stroller.

Afternoon 3-4pm / 1h

Aspazija's House

A stunning, baby-blue wooden summer villa with a quiet, sun-dappled courtyard. While the interior is a brief, quiet museum, the outdoor garden is an ideal spot for Will and Kathryn to sit in the shade while Mary Claire plays on a blanket.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Līvu Akvaparks Baby Splash Zone

Northern Europe's largest water park features a dedicated, warm, shallow water play area called Orpheus Land. Ideal for gentle water play with Mary Claire, equipped with family changing cabins and baby-friendly amenities.

🍽️ Where to eat

36. Line Grill Restaurant

This Michelin-recommended, high-end beachfront institution offers fresh grilled wild Baltic cod and panoramic sea views. Highly accommodating to families, featuring sturdy highchairs, an expansive outdoor terrace, and a quiet, breezy atmosphere perfect for an early 6:00 PM seating.

Laivas

A sophisticated riverfront restaurant overlooking the Lielupe River, specializing in upscale Baltic seafood and smoked fish. It offers a spacious, tranquil terrace, plenty of room to park a premium stroller next to the table, and highchairs on request.

Faces

An upscale, modern dining spot on Jomas Street featuring a beautiful, quiet backyard garden terrace. Their early evening seating is highly relaxed, offering gourmet Mediterranean-inspired dishes, high-quality highchairs, and excellent service for families.

Light House Grill

Directly overlooking the beach, this luxury glass-enclosed pavilion offers premium steaks and seafood. The stroller-accessible ramp makes entry effortless, and the crashing waves provide natural white noise to keep Mary Claire sleeping.

Kinza House

A highly rated, chic Georgian-Mediterranean fusion spot with a lovely, quiet summer terrace. Outstanding hospitality, spacious seating arrangements that easily accommodate highchairs, and a warm, family-welcoming staff.
