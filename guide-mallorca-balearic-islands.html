What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 2.5h

Playa de Muro

This stunning Blue Flag beach features exceptionally shallow, crystal-clear turquoise waters stretching far out, making it incredibly safe for baby Mary Claire to splash. Flat wooden boardwalks provide easy stroller access right to the water's edge.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 1.5h

Palma Cathedral (La Seu)

Step inside this gothic masterpiece during the quiet morning hours to enjoy the cool interior and spectacular rose-window light displays. The main areas are completely flat and stroller-accessible, offering a serene escape from the outdoor heat.

Late Afternoon 4:30 PM - 6:30 PM / 2h

Puerto de Pollensa Promenade

Widely known as the Pine Walk, this completely pedestrianized, paved coastal path is shaded by ancient pine trees. It is perfect for a peaceful late-afternoon stroller walk while Mary Claire naps to the gentle sound of the lapping waves.

Morning 9:30 AM - 11:30 AM / 2h

Jardines de Alfabia

These historic Moorish gardens offer a lush, shaded oasis of palm trees, fountains, and exotic plants. While some stone paths require mindful stroller steering, the canopy of green and gentle water features create a highly soothing environment for a baby.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM / 2h

Passeig des Born

Palma's most elegant boulevard is fully pedestrianized, tree-lined, and incredibly wide. Will and Kathryn can easily push the stroller under the shade of massive plane trees while window shopping at high-end boutiques and stopping for a quiet cafe au lait.

Midday 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM / 2h

Cuevas del Drach

These spectacular caves house one of the world's largest underground lakes. Strollers must be checked at the entrance, so wear your luxury baby carrier to enjoy the cool underground temperatures and the classical music concert on the illuminated water.

Early Morning 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM / 2h

Parc Natural de s'Albufera

This expansive wetland reserve features flat, hard-packed dirt trails that are highly stroller-accessible. It offers a very quiet, nature-filled escape where you can spot wild birds and enjoy fresh air away from the busy resort crowds.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 2h

Jardins de la Cartoixa

Located in the charming mountain village of Valldemossa, these formal palace gardens feature flat, paved pathways and manicured cypress hedges. The mountain air is noticeably cooler, offering a refreshing, peaceful stroller walk with gorgeous valley views.

Morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 2h

Ferrocarril de Sóller

Take a scenic ride on this historic wooden train through the Tramuntana mountains. The vintage carriages require you to fold your stroller and store it, but the gentle, rhythmic chugging of the train and the spectacular mountain views are highly captivating for an infant.

🍽️ Where to eat

El Olivo

Set within the ultra-luxury Belmond La Residencia in Deià, this world-class restaurant offers an outdoor garden terrace with breathtaking cliffside views. Request an early 7:30 PM seating to enjoy high-end Mediterranean cuisine while the staff warmly accommodates your stroller.

Sea Club at Cap Rocat

An open-air waterfront bistro carved into a cliffside, offering spectacular ocean views and fresh grilled seafood. The spacious outdoor layout easily accommodates strollers, and the gentle sea breeze and relaxed open-air atmosphere are perfect for families.

Es Baluard Restaurant & Lounge

Located inside the perimeter walls of Palma's modern art museum, this chic eatery features a massive, flat outdoor terrace overlooking the harbor. It is highly stroller-friendly with plenty of space between tables and offers an early evening menu of upscale Spanish tapas.

Ca's Patró March

Perched dramatically over the pristine waters of Cala Deià, this iconic rustic-chic seafood spot is famous for its fresh lobster. While the walk down is steep, arriving early with a baby carrier allows you to secure a breezy edge table where the ocean murmurs will keep baby calm.

Restaurante Toque

A highly rated, family-run Belgian-Mediterranean bistro in Palma that offers exceptional fine dining in a relaxed, warm atmosphere. The staff is famously welcoming to infants, providing highchairs, accommodating early seatings, and adjusting dishes for family comfort.
