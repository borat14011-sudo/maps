Beyond its legendary nightlife, Ibiza sparkles as a serene Mediterranean paradise perfect for families. Children will love paddling in shallow turquoise waters, exploring ancient castle walls, and discovering bohemian markets. With its laid-back island rhythm, this Spanish gem offers a wonderfully relaxed pace for parents and toddlers alike.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min taxi or private transfer from Ibiza Airport to Ibiza Town and nearby family beach hotels

What to do

Late Afternoon / 2-3h

Dalt Vila (Ibiza Old Town)

Climb the ancient cobbled fortress walls for stunning sea views and hidden plazas. Keep toddlers in a carrier rather than a stroller due to the steep, bumpy stone paths.

Morning / 3-4h

Cala Llonga Beach

A wide, sheltered bay with shallow, calm waters and a playground right on the sand. It is incredibly safe for toddlers to splash around while parents relax on loungers.

Wednesday Morning / 2-3h

Punta Arabí Hippy Market

Ibiza’s oldest market features colorful stalls, live music, and a dedicated kids' creative craft area. Toddlers will love the vibrant colors and sensory-friendly morning atmosphere.

Morning / 1-2h

Ses Salines Natural Park

Spot pink flamingos wading in the sparkling salt flats and walk along flat, easy boardwalks. The gentle terrain is stroller-friendly and perfect for curious little walkers.

Sunset / 1h

Es Vedrà Lookout

Witness the mystical, towering rock island rising dramatically from the sea. Sit safely at the main clearing, as the rocky cliff edges require close toddler supervision.

Full Day / 6-8h

Ferry to Formentera

Take a smooth 30-minute ferry ride to Ibiza’s sister island for Caribbean-like beaches and crystal-clear waters. Toddlers will love the boat ride, and stroller-friendly boardwalks line the main beaches.

Afternoon / 1h

Cap Blanc Aquarium

Located inside a natural sea cave once used by lobstermen, this unique aquarium showcases local marine life. It is small, cool, and easily digestible for short toddler attention spans.

Morning / 1h

Mercat Vell (Old Market)

This historic open-air pavilion in Ibiza Town is filled with fresh local fruits, pastries, and vibrant flowers. It's a quick, sensory-rich stop where toddlers can snack on sweet local strawberries.

Morning or Late Afternoon / 2h

Parc de s'Illa (Can Misses)

A massive, modern playground featuring sensory play equipment, climbing structures, and plenty of shaded seating. It is fully enclosed, making it the ultimate spot for toddlers to burn off energy safely.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Paloma

Enjoy fresh, organic Mediterranean dishes like homemade focaccia under the shade of orange trees while toddlers play in the garden play area.

El Chiringuito Es Cavallet

Get the fresh grilled sea bass while the kids play safely in the supervised Little Chiringuito kids' club corner on the sand.

Beachouse Ibiza

Order the healthy avocado toast or fresh seafood paella while the little ones enjoy beachside creative workshops at the Little Beachouse.

Bar Costa

Feast on traditional Spanish cured meats and toasted pan con tomate in a bustling pedestrian plaza where kids can run around freely.

Chiringuito Blue

Try the shakshuka or crispy calamari while enjoying a relaxed beachside vibe with an eco-conscious kids' corner on weekends.

👨‍👩‍👧 Rent a compact car with a pre-booked car seat to easily access the island's secluded, family-friendly coves. Pack a sturdy baby carrier for exploring Dalt Vila's steep steps, and stick to baby sun-suits as the midday Mediterranean sun is intense.
