What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Cala Llonga Beach

The incredibly shallow, wave-free waters and soft white sand make this cove the absolute safest spot on the island for a toddler's first dip. A beachfront playground and stroller-friendly boardwalk mean easy transitions from the sand to shade.

Late Afternoon / 2h

Plaza del Parque

Explore the lively, pedestrian-only heart of lower Ibiza Town, avoiding the steep cobblestones of the upper fortress while still enjoying the historic atmosphere. It is highly stroller-friendly with plenty of open space for a toddler to toddle while you enjoy an iced cafe con leche.

Morning / 1.5-2h

Paseo Marítimo de Santa Eulària

This wide, palm-fringed paved promenade is entirely car-free and perfect for a smooth stroller ride along the sea. Stop by the modern playground near the marina or dip your toes in the gentle, clean waters of the adjoining town beach.

Morning (Saturdays) / 2h

Las Dalias Hippy Market

Navigate the vibrant, shaded pathways of this iconic market early in the day to beat the crowds and heat with your toddler. The colorful stalls, live gentle acoustic music, and lush garden spaces offer sensory-rich but manageable stimulation for a baby.

Morning / 2h

Can Muson Ecological Farm

This lovely ecological farm lets toddlers feed friendly goats, pigs, and ducks in a beautifully shaded, rustic countryside setting. Parents can relax at the on-site organic cafe serving fresh-squeezed juices and local farm-to-table bites.

Midday / 1h

Aquarium Cap Blanc

Housed inside a cool, natural lobster cave, this unique aquarium is a brilliant, quick escape from the midday sun for a toddler. It is small enough to navigate easily without getting overwhelmed, showcasing colorful local marine life from wooden boardwalks.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Cala Bassa Beach Club (CBbC)

Relax under the shade of ancient pine trees on luxurious daybeds while your toddler plays in the shallow, rock-free pools of the cove. Wooden boardwalks stretch across the sand, making it highly accessible for premium travel strollers.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Parque de la Paz

A lush, grassy oasis in the heart of Ibiza Town featuring modern, safe playground equipment suited for infants and toddlers. It's the perfect spot to let your daughter burn off energy under the shade of Mediterranean trees before dinner.

Afternoon / 1.5h

Ibiza Botánico Biotecnológico

A beautifully manicured biotechnology park showcasing indigenous Balearic flora through interactive, easy-to-walk paths. The stroller-friendly layout and gentle trickling water features create a highly soothing environment for an afternoon contact nap.

🍽️ Where to eat

Chiringuito Blue

Order the salt-baked sea bass and shakshuka while your baby plays in the sand directly next to your beachside table. It is incredibly spacious, features a dedicated kids' play area on weekends, and opens early for lunch.

La Paloma

Order the homemade organic focaccia and their legendary citrus-infused lamb under the shade of orange groves. The bohemian garden setting is highly forgiving of baby noises, with plenty of room to park a stroller next to your rustic wooden table.

Chiringuito Cala Gracioneta

Savor their masterfully prepared seafood paella at a shaded, terraced table overlooking a tiny, calm cove. The early afternoon lunch seating is perfect for families, and the gentle waves provide natural white noise for a stroller nap.

Yemanja

Feast on traditional Ibicencan 'Bullit de Peix' (fish stew) right on the sand. This upscale beach restaurant is loud enough to easily drown out toddler squeals, and the staff are notoriously welcoming to young families.

Aubergine by Atzaró

Try the farm-to-table roasted aubergine with tahini in a gorgeous, relaxed countryside estate. The vast garden layout provides ample space to walk a fussy baby, and there is a lovely, quiet playground on-site.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

7Pines Resort Ibiza

This clifftop luxury resort offers expansive multi-bedroom suites with full kitchenettes, perfect for preparing toddler meals and washing bottles. The family pool area is stunning and quiet, and they provide top-tier wooden cribs upon request.

Nobu Hotel Ibiza Bay

Set on quiet Talamanca beach, this high-end resort features a brilliant family pool and a highly praised, shaded kids' club welcoming toddlers. The rooms are incredibly spacious, and the location allows for easy stroller walks straight down the beach boardwalk.

ME Ibiza

A stylish luxury hotel near Santa Eulària featuring quiet, baby-friendly family pools and direct access to a calm beach cove. They provide excellent in-room amenities for infants, including bottle warmers, custom cribs, and trusted babysitting services.
