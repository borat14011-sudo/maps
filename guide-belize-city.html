Belize City

← back to travel board
Where the Belize River meets the Caribbean Sea, Belize City serves as a vibrant, historic gateway to tropical wonders. Families can explore ancient Mayan mysteries, encounter exotic wildlife, and soak in the laid-back coastal culture. It's an energetic starting point for unforgettable reef and rainforest adventures.
🚕 Getting there: ~25 min taxi ride from Philip S.W. Goldson International Airport (BZE) to downtown Belize City or the water taxi terminals

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

The Belize Zoo

Encounter native wildlife like jaguars, tapirs, and howler monkeys in a natural forest setting. The flat, shaded gravel pathways are highly stroller-friendly for little ones.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Museum of Belize

Housed in a historic former prison, this museum offers a fascinating look at Maya history and insect exhibits. It's air-conditioned, providing a cool, sensory-friendly escape for toddlers.

Midday / 3-4h

Old Belize & Cucumber Beach

Explore Belizean history in an indoor exhibit, then splash on the sandy beach with a giant water slide. The shallow, calm salt-water lagoon is perfect for toddlers to safely splash.

Morning / 3h

Altun Ha Mayan Ruins

Climb the ancient Temple of the Masonry Altars and run around the wide-open, grassy plazas. Toddlers will love chasing butterflies across the manicured lawns while parents marvel at the history.

Late Afternoon / 15-30m

Belize Sign Monument

Snap a colorful family photo at this vibrant sign set against the backdrop of the Caribbean Sea. It's a quick, easy stop with plenty of space for toddlers to stretch their legs.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Fort George Lighthouse and Baron Bliss Tomb

Stroll along the seawall to see this historic red-and-white lighthouse and enjoy the cool ocean breeze. The wide seaside paved path is perfect for pushing a stroller.

Morning / 1-2h

Michael Finnegan Market

Immerse your senses in this bustling market packed with colorful tropical fruits, fresh coconut water, and local crafts. Keep toddlers close or carrier-bound to navigate the energetic crowds.

Full Day / 5-6h

Goff's Caye Day Trip

Take a short 30-minute boat ride to a tiny, picture-perfect sandy island surrounded by crystal-clear waters. The gentle, shallow shorelines are ideal for toddlers to build sandcastles.

Morning / 1-2h

St. John’s Cathedral & House of Culture

Visit the oldest Anglican church in Central America and the nearby historic House of Culture with its lush seaside lawns. The expansive green gardens are great for toddlers to safely wander.

🍽️ Where to eat

Wet Lizard

Grab a table on the deck overlooking the harbor and order the crispy fish fingers or classic Belizean stew chicken.

Nerie's

Try the authentic Belizean stewed chicken with rice and beans, potato salad, and sweet fried plantains.

Riverside Tavern

Order their famous gourmet burgers made with local Belizean beef, served in a spacious, air-conditioned dining room.

Celebrity Restaurant & Bar

Get the fresh seafood platter or the family-style chicken quesadillas in an inviting, colorful atmosphere.

Sumathi Restaurant

Savor delicious, mild butter chicken and fresh garlic naan that even picky toddlers will love dipping.
