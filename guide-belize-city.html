Belize City serves as a vibrant, historic gateway where colonial architecture meets lush tropical wonders and coastal breezes. Perfect for families, it offers quick access to wildlife sanctuaries, ancient ruins, and kid-friendly marine adventures. From spotting gentle manatees to exploring local markets, it is an energetic base for your family's Belizean journey.
🚕 Getting there: ~20-25 min taxi ride from Philip S.W. Goldson International Airport (BZE) to downtown Belize City hotels or water taxi terminals.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

The Belize Zoo and Tropical Education Center

Meet rescued native animals like jaguars, tapirs, and colorful macaws up close in a natural forest setting. Toddlers will love the stroller-friendly dirt paths and the chance to see animals at eye level.

Morning / 3h

Altun Ha Maya Ruins

Explore ancient pyramids with wide grassy lawns perfect for little legs to run around and burn off energy. Toddlers can easily walk the flat central plaza area while older kids climb the temples.

Late Afternoon / 30m

The Belize Sign Monument

Snap a colorful family photo at this iconic waterfront sign located right along the Baron Bliss Promenade. It is a quick, easy stop with a nearby grassy park perfect for a toddler stretch break.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Museum of Belize

Housed in a historic former prison, this air-conditioned museum features fascinating Mayan jade artifacts and insect exhibits. It is a great cool-down spot, and toddlers will be captivated by the colorful butterfly displays.

Midday / 3-4h

Cucumber Beach at Old Belize

A family-friendly, man-made beach with calm, shallow waters and a water slide that is safe for younger swimmers. Toddlers can splash safely in the protected marina-style cove while parents relax on beach loungers.

Morning / 3h

Community Baboon Sanctuary

Walk through the jungle to hear and see wild black howler monkeys swinging right above your heads. The local guides are incredibly family-friendly, and toddlers will be mesmerized by the loud monkey calls.

Evening / 1h

Baron Bliss Lighthouse and Memorial

Walk along the seaside seawall to see this historic red-and-white lighthouse guiding ships into the harbor. The paved paths are fully stroller-accessible, offering a refreshing, breezy walk for a fussy toddler.

Morning / 4h

Crooked Tree Wildlife Sanctuary

Take a peaceful boat ride or walk the boardwalks to spot giant jabiru storks and turtles in their natural wetland habitat. It is a calm, quiet escape where toddlers can watch birds splash from the safety of a boat.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Government House and House of Culture

Wander through the beautiful colonial architecture and lush, shaded beachside gardens of this historic estate. The sprawling green lawns provide a safe, enclosed space for toddlers to toddle under giant shade trees.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Wet Lizard

Grab a table on the breezy deck overlooking the harbor and order the crispy conch fritters or kid-approved chicken tenders.

Nerie's Restaurant

Treat the family to authentic Belizean stew chicken with rice and beans, plus sweet fried plantains that toddlers will love.

Sumathi Indian Restaurant

Enjoy delicious, mild butter chicken and soft garlic naan bread that make a perfect, easy-to-share meal for toddlers.

Celebrity Restaurant & Bar

Order the fresh seafood platter for the adults and a plate of cheesy quesadillas from their extensive, toddler-friendly kids' menu.

Riverside Tavern

Sit back in this air-conditioned local favorite and order their famous gourmet Belizean beef burgers alongside a side of crispy, thick-cut fries.
