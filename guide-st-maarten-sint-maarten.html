Upscale French-Dutch escape for effortless beachside toddler days

St. Maarten (Sint Maarten)

← back to travel board
Split between vibrant Dutch Sint Maarten and chic French Saint Martin, this dual-nation island offers a seamless Caribbean escape for discerning families. The island's unique layout combines world-class French dining in Grand Case with calm, reef-protected bays ideal for a toddler's first dip. Easy-to-navigate roads and a high standard of medical care provide peace of mind for parents traveling with infants. It's an upscale yet deeply relaxing destination where you can trade finance spreadsheets for sunset strolls with a stroller in hand.
🚕 Getting there: ~20 to 30 minutes via a pre-arranged private SUV transfer from Princess Juliana International Airport (SXM) to most luxury resorts on the island.

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Parrot Ville Bird Sanctuary

An interactive aviary where your toddler can feed friendly, colorful parrots right from their hands. The gravel pathways are flat enough for a sturdy stroller, and the enclosed space ensures a safe, controlled environment.

Morning / 3-4h

Le Galion Beach

Locally known as Baby Beach due to its protective coral reef, this bay offers incredibly calm, shallow, wave-free waters. You can easily roll a stroller right up to the shaded palapas for a stress-free beach day.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Philipsburg Boardwalk

A wide, fully paved, mile-long pedestrian boardwalk lined with shops, restaurants, and views of Great Bay. It is the absolute best spot on the island for an effortless sunset stroller walk without vehicular traffic.

Morning / 2h

Seaside Nature Park

A quiet, 30-acre seaside park featuring a lovely petting zoo where toddlers can interact with miniature goats, ducks, and donkeys. The dirt trails are easy to navigate with a baby carrier, offering a serene escape from the resort crowds.

Morning / 1-2h

Marigot Market

An open-air market filled with vibrant colors, fresh tropical fruits, and local crafts on the French-side waterfront. The paved harbor area is highly stroller-accessible, allowing you to easily browse the stalls while your baby naps.

Midday / 1h

Maho Beach

World-famous for watching commercial jets fly incredibly low over the sand to land at the adjacent airport. While the sand can get windy from jet blast, you can safely watch the spectacle from the paved deck of the Sunset Bar and Grill with your toddler.

Morning / 3-4h

Loterie Farm

A lush nature sanctuary nestled at the foot of Pic Paradis featuring a beautiful, spring-fed swimming pool surrounded by tropical gardens. While the hiking trails are too rugged for strollers, the L'Eau Safe family pool area is a tranquil oasis perfect for a shaded baby swim.

Morning / 4-5h

Pinel Island Ferry

Take a flat, gentle 5-minute ferry ride to this pristine offshore islet featuring crystal-clear, knee-deep waters. The lack of waves makes it a natural wading pool for toddlers, and beach chairs can be rented directly on the sand.

Late Afternoon / 2-3h

Grand Case Beach

A calm, picturesque beach on the French side famous for its gentle waves and spectacular sunset views over Anguilla. The soft white sand is perfect for toddler sensory play, and the proximity to beachfront cafes makes diaper changes convenient.

🍽️ Where to eat

Sky's the Limit Lolo

Order the incredibly flavorful creole grilled ribs with side portions of mac and cheese and plantains. This casual, open-air beachside dining spot has a lively, noisy atmosphere where baby babbles won't bother anyone.

Rainbow Café

Savor the fresh mahi-mahi ceviche or a gourmet lobster roll while dining directly on the beach deck. The spacious layout easily accommodates strollers, and the gentle lapping of the waves right next to your table provides a soothing white noise for napping infants.

Karibuni

Indulge in the freshly caught grilled snapper served right at your beach lounger. Its location on calm Pinel Island allows parents to dine with their feet in the sand while their toddler safely splashes in the adjacent, waveless shallows.

Topper's Restaurant & Bar

Try the famous slow-cooked prime rib or the coconut shrimp in this spacious, air-conditioned American-Caribbean venue. It opens early for dinner at 4 PM, making it ideal for matching a toddler's early bedtime schedule.

Le Cottage

Experience high-end French gastronomy like the pan-seared duck breast in a surprisingly welcoming environment. If you book an early reservation at 6:00 PM, the staff is incredibly accommodating to infants, offering ample space for a stroller bedside.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

La Samanna, A Belmond Hotel

This ultra-luxury resort offers top-tier, quiet beachfront villas, professional babysitting services, and high-quality wooden cribs. The calm, exclusive beach and zero-entry infinity pools provide a serene paradise for families seeking a seamless luxury experience.

Grand Case Beach Club

Features spacious oceanfront suites with fully equipped kitchens, making it incredibly easy to sterilize bottles and prepare toddler-friendly meals. The property features two private, wave-free beaches steps from your room and provides complimentary pack-and-plays.

Sonesta Maho Beach Resort

A massive, hassle-free all-inclusive resort featuring a dedicated kids' aqua park with a shallow splash pool perfect for toddlers. They provide spacious family suites, reliable crib rentals, and round-the-clock dining options to satisfy erratic infant schedules.

👨‍👩‍👧 Opt for a rental SUV over taxis to easily store your stroller and navigate the island's steep, winding hills at your toddler's pace. Pack plenty of reef-safe baby sunscreen and mosquito repellent, as high-quality organic brands can be difficult to locate outside of large French-side pharmacies like Pharmacie de Grand Case. Stick to the French-side beaches like Le Galion for wave-free, shallow waters that are significantly safer and more relaxing for infant swimming than the rougher Atlantic-facing shores.
