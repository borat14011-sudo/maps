St. Maarten blends cosmopolitan European flair with a relaxed, sun-drenched Caribbean rhythm that caters perfectly to discerning parents. The island's dual-nation heritage offers access to both the bustling, stroller-ready Philipsburg Boardwalk and the tranquil culinary coves of the French side. For families traveling with baby Mary Claire, the exceptionally calm, wave-free waters of Le Galion Beach provide a safe, natural wading pool for gentle infant dips. From private oceanside terraces to lush valley retreats, this tropical haven ensures a seamless, upscale vacation with your little one.
🚕 Getting there: A 25-minute private SUV transfer from Princess Juliana International Airport (SXM) to the main hotel strips features pre-installed rear-facing infant car seats and generous cargo space for a full-size stroller.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Le Galion Beach

Known locally as Baby Beach, this protected lagoon offers completely calm, crystal-clear, knee-deep water and plenty of shade, making it the absolute safest spot on the island for baby Mary Claire to experience her first ocean dip.

Early Morning 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Philipsburg Boardwalk

This wide, flat, two-mile-long oceanfront promenade is entirely stroller-friendly, lined with upscale boutiques and breezy cafes, ideal for a morning walk away from traffic.

Morning to Afternoon 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

Pinel Island

Take a brief, flat 5-minute ferry from French Cul-de-Sac to this tiny offshore paradise; the shallow, wave-free waters and shady palapas are perfect for a relaxed family beach day.

Morning 9:30 AM - 10:30 AM

Parrot Ville Bird Sanctuary

A delightful, quiet, and fully shaded sanctuary where gentle, colorful birds fly around; it is sensory-rich for an infant but quiet enough to avoid overstimulation.

Midday 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Loterie Farm Jungle Oasis Pool

An upscale nature sanctuary at the foot of Pic Paradis; book a private family cabana near the spring-fed pool where the canopy provides deep shade for baby's naptime.

Sunset 5:30 PM - 7:00 PM

Grand Case Boulevard

This iconic oceanfront street on the French side is perfect for a gentle, sunset-lit stroll past historic Creole cottages just as the evening cooling breeze begins.

Early Morning 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM

Mullet Bay Beach

A gorgeous stretch of soft white sand bordered by shady sea grape trees; visit in the early morning for ultra-calm waters and a peaceful, uncrowded beachfront setting.

Afternoon 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM

Emilio Wilson Park

This historical estate offers beautifully manicured, expansive grassy lawns shaded by massive centuries-old trees, providing a clean and safe environment for Mary Claire to crawl.

Late Afternoon 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM

Fort Amsterdam

A historic site on a peninsula near Philipsburg with stroller-accessible paved paths leading to stunning, breezy vistas of Great Bay where you can watch elegant brown pelicans nest.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ocean Lounge

Located at Holland House, this chic beachfront terrace offers pristine fresh seafood and highchairs. Enjoy an early seating with gentle ocean breezes that naturally soothe a baby.

Sunset Cafe

Situated at Grand Case Beach Club, this open-air, cliffside spot features stunning Caribbean views and delicious French-Caribbean cuisine, offering a relaxed environment with plenty of highchairs.

Karibuni Restaurant

This beachside gem on Pinel Island serves freshly caught lobster right on the sand, featuring shady tables, highchairs, and a peaceful lagoon view where baby can watch calm waves.

Bistrot Caraïbes

An upscale dining institution in Grand Case offering world-class French cuisine on a quiet, intimate terrace; booking an early 6:00 PM slot ensures a peaceful, low-noise culinary experience.

L'Auberge Gourmande

A refined French bistro housed in a charming historic Creole cottage, offering a warm atmosphere and highly attentive staff who readily accommodate families during early dinner seatings.

A serene, upscale resort offering spacious two-bedroom suites with full kitchens and private patios. Located steps from the tranquil, wave-free waters of Petite Plage, it provides excellent crib options, a quiet oceanfront pool, and a highly family-friendly ambiance.
