While the steep cliffside steps of Oia present challenges, the flatter, stroller-friendly paved promenade of Kamari Beach offers seamless luxury wandering for parents. Baby Mary Claire will love the gentle Aegean breezes while you lounge under upscale thatched cabanas on the black sands of Perissa. Your Greek island getaway perfectly balances high-end volcanic vistas with practical, infant-friendly coastal ease.

What to do

Early morning 9-11am

Kamari Beach Promenade Walk

Stroll along this wide, flat, completely paved pedestrian-only oceanfront promenade. It is perfectly stroller-accessible, offering lovely sea breezes, plenty of shade from beachfront tamarisk trees, and quiet morning hours.

Midday 11am-1pm

Akrotiri Archaeological Site

Explore the 'Minoan Pompeii' inside a fully enclosed, temperature-controlled modern canopy. The wide wooden suspended walkways are entirely stroller-friendly, keeping Mary Claire cool and shaded while you admire ancient ruins.

Afternoon 2-5pm

Perivolos Beach Lounge

Relax at a high-end beach club with wide, flat wooden boardwalks leading to private, shaded daybeds. The volcanic black sand is flat here, making it easy to set up a shaded baby play tent near the calm, shallow shoreline.

Late afternoon 4-6pm

Megalochori Village Square

Wander this quiet, traditional inland village which is much flatter and less crowded than Fira. The cobblestones are manageable with a sturdy stroller, and the central square offers beautiful bougainvillea shade and peaceful cafes.

Sunset 6-8pm

Santo Wines Estate

Enjoy a luxury flight of Assyrtiko wine on a spacious, flat, multi-tiered outdoor terrace overlooking the caldera. They offer highchairs, easy ramp access, and breathtaking sunset views without the dangerous cliffs or steps of Oia.

Early afternoon 1-2:30pm

Museum of Prehistoric Thera

An air-conditioned indoor sanctuary in Fira featuring ancient Minoan frescoes and artifacts. It is completely flat and wheelchair/stroller accessible, making it a perfect quiet-time escape for Mary Claire's midday nap.

Morning walk 9-10:30am

Pyrgos Village Lower Paths

Explore the lower, gently sloping paths of Santorini's historic hilltop capital. Using a baby carrier, you can easily navigate the winding whitewashed alleys to see blue-domed churches without the intense vertical steps of Fira.

Late afternoon 5-6:30pm

Vlychada Marina Yacht Viewing

Walk along the quiet, flat harbor of Vlychada to watch luxury catamarans sail in. The area is highly accessible, peaceful, and free of tourist crowds, offering a calm sensory experience for an infant.

Morning drive 10-11:30am

Prophet Elias Monastery Viewpoint

Take a private luxury chauffeured drive to the highest peak of the island. You can step out of the air-conditioned van directly onto flat ground to enjoy sweeping 360-degree island views with zero hiking required.

🍽️ Where to eat

Metaxi Mas

This legendary tavern in Exo Gonia offers high-end Cretan cuisine in a spacious, shaded garden courtyard. The staff treats babies like royalty, providing sturdy highchairs and a relaxed, noisy open-air atmosphere where a crying baby won't disrupt anyone.

To Psaraki

Overlooking the quiet marina of Vlychada, this upscale seafood spot features a completely flat, stroller-accessible entrance and outdoor terrace. Enjoy fresh sea bass and local fava while baby relaxes in the cool sea breeze.

Seaside Santorini

A luxury beachfront restaurant in Perivolos offering sophisticated Mediterranean fusion on a wide, flat wooden deck. They offer early dinner seatings, spacious booth seating options, and direct, step-free access to the sand.

Selene

For an upscale culinary experience, Selene offers refined dining in a historic, spacious courtyard in Fira. Booking an early evening slot ensures a peaceful atmosphere with attentive staff who happily accommodate strollers in their outdoor seating area.

Argo Restaurant

An upscale Fira favorite featuring a highly accessible lower terrace with beautiful caldera views. The professional staff is exceptionally family-friendly, welcoming strollers during their early 6:00 PM dinner service.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Kastelli Resort

$200-400/night

A peaceful 5-star boutique resort tucked away in Kamari, offering complimentary premium cribs, four sparkling swimming pools, and flat, lush grass lawns perfect for baby's tummy time. The resort features spacious family suites and blackout curtains, all within a 2-minute flat walk to the paved beach promenade.

Bella Santorini

$200-400/night

Located near the flat sands of Perivolos, this luxury property offers large family suites with private kitchenettes ideal for preparing infant formula. The property features a large, quiet swimming pool, flat step-free pathways, baby cot options, and excellent blackout blinds for morning naps.

Antinea Suites & Spa Hotel

$200-400/night

An elegant hotel in Kamari built with traditional Cycladic style, featuring spacious junior suites with private ground-floor pools or outdoor jacuzzis. The hotel provides highchairs, cribs, and a highly accessible layout that avoids the steep cliff drops of the caldera.
