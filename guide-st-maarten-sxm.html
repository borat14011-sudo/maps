St. Maarten (SXM)

← back to travel board
St. Maarten offers a unique blend of dual-culture sophistication with French culinary flair and Dutch vibrant energy. For upscale families traveling with an infant like Mary Claire, the calm, shallow waters of Le Galion Beach provide a natural, wave-free paddling pool. The island features exceptional private villa resorts and stroller-accessible beachfront boardwalks that make navigating with a little one completely stress-free. From sunset yacht charters to upscale dining in Grand Case, St. Maarten seamlessly blends high-end romance with practical baby-friendly conveniences.
🚕 Getting there: Princess Juliana International Airport (SXM) to the main luxury hotel strips takes approximately 20 to 30 minutes. The best option is a pre-arranged private luxury SUV transfer from SXM VIP Services, which provides pre-installed rear-facing infant car seats and ample trunk space for a full-size stroller.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Le Galion Beach

Known locally as Baby Beach, this sheltered cove is protected by a coral reef, creating incredibly calm, shallow water perfect for wading with baby Mary Claire. The soft white sand is highly accessible, and nearby surf schools offer shaded lounge chairs for rent. It is the ultimate worry-free beach spot for parents of infants.

Morning 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

Pinel Island

A brief, gentle five-minute ferry ride transports you to this pristine island enclave with shallow, crystal-clear waters. Book a private shaded palapa at Karibuni Beach and enjoy the tranquil, wave-free shoreline. It is an ideal spot for baby's first dip in the sea.

Morning 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM

Philipsburg Boardwalk

This wide, flat, paved promenade stretches along Great Bay and is completely stroller-friendly. Enjoy a peaceful morning stroll with beautiful ocean views, cool ocean breezes, and plenty of shaded cafes to stop for an iced coffee. It offers a smooth ride for a sleeping baby.

Midday 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Loterie Farm

Nestled in the lush hillside, this private nature reserve offers a serene tropical forest experience. While the hiking trails are not suitable for strollers, the beautifully manicured spring-fed pool area features luxurious, shaded private cabanas. It is a peaceful oasis to relax under the canopy.

Late Afternoon 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM

Grand Case Beach

This tranquil stretch of coastline features calm Caribbean waters and spectacular sunset views. The soft sand is perfect for sitting with an infant, and the gentle lapping of the waves provides natural white noise. It is an excellent spot for a relaxed late afternoon family stroll.

Morning 9:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Friar's Bay Beach

A scenic, family-friendly bay sheltered from strong winds and swell. The water here is consistently calm and shallow, making it safe for wading with an infant. Two excellent beachfront restaurants provide easy access to shade, drinks, and lunch right on the sand.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Emilio Wilson Estate

This historic park features expansive, flat grassy lawns shaded by massive, centuries-old trees. It is the perfect, quiet spot to lay down a blanket for baby Mary Claire to crawl around safely. The peaceful atmosphere provides a lovely break from the coastal heat.

Early Morning 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Mullet Bay Beach

A gorgeous stretch of fine white sand bordered by shade-giving sea grape trees. The beach is easily accessible from the parking area, minimizing the distance you need to carry baby gear. Come early in the morning to enjoy calm waters and pristine, quiet conditions.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Marina Port La Royale

A charming, stroller-friendly marina in Marigot lined with cafes and boutiques. It is a wonderful location for an afternoon stroll along the docks to look at the luxury yachts. The harbor breeze keeps the temperature comfortable for a baby in a carrier or stroller.

🍽️ Where to eat

Ocean 82

This French-Caribbean bistro in Grand Case offers a spacious, open-air terrace directly overlooking the water. The gentle sound of the ocean waves beautifully masks any baby babbles. They offer early dinner seatings and are highly accommodating to families with infants.

Rainbow Café

A chic, bohemian beach club in Grand Case that serves fresh, healthy dishes on a beautiful seaside deck. Book an early dinner slot to witness a spectacular sunset while dining. The staff is exceptionally warm and welcoming to families traveling with young children.

Karibuni Restaurant

Located on the sands of Pinel Island, this restaurant serves fresh lobster and tropical dishes in a relaxed, open-air setting. They provide shaded beach lounge service so you can dine while baby rests comfortably under a wide umbrella. It is a quintessential Caribbean lunch experience.

Bistrot Caraïbes

A legendary high-end dining institution in Grand Case offering world-class French cuisine. The cozy, elegant outdoor courtyard is best enjoyed during their earliest seating when the atmosphere is quiet. The attentive staff makes dining with an infant feel effortless and dignified.

Le Cottage

An upscale French-Caribbean restaurant in Grand Case renowned for its welcoming hospitality and excellent wine pairings. The cozy indoor dining room is fully air-conditioned, providing a cool sanctuary from the tropical heat. An early reservation ensures a peaceful dining experience for the whole family.
