What to do

Early morning 9-11am

Diocletian's Palace

Explore the spectacular Roman ruins early in the morning. Opt for a premium baby carrier instead of a stroller to navigate the historic, highly uneven cobblestones of the Peristyle.

Late afternoon 3-5pm

Marjan Forest Park

Stroll through shady pine forests on paved, stroller-friendly paths. Offers breathtaking viewpoints of the Adriatic and quiet spots for an outdoor infant feeding session.

Morning 8-10am

Bačvice Beach

Dip Mary Claire’s toes in the calm, shallow waters of Split's most famous sandy beach. Arrive early to rent a luxury canopy lounger and enjoy the soft shore.

Evening 6-8pm

Riva Promenade

Take a sunset stroll along this wide, completely flat palm-lined boardwalk. It is incredibly stroller-friendly, lively, and boasts ocean breezes to keep baby cool.

Afternoon 1-3pm

Meštrović Gallery

Wander through the quiet, beautifully manicured gardens of this art museum. The spacious outdoor lawns are stroller-accessible and perfect for a peaceful afternoon walk.

Afternoon 2-4pm

Sustipan Park

A serene clifftop park with lush grass and towering cypress trees. This tranquil green space is ideal for laying down a blanket for Mary Claire's afternoon nap under the shade.

Morning 10am-12pm

Bene Beach

Located deep in Marjan park, this beach is heavily shaded by pine trees. Accessible via a flat walk or the local tourist eco-train, it offers quiet, shallow waters.

Morning 9-10:30am

West Coast Promenade

Enjoy a morning walk along Split's modern harbor extension. Flanked by luxury yachts, this wide, obstacle-free path is perfect for uninterrupted stroller sleep.

Late morning 11am-12:30pm

Split Archaeological Museum Gardens

Step away from the crowds into the museum's quiet, shaded lapidarium garden. The smooth pathways and silent atmosphere provide a cool, sensory-friendly retreat for an overstimulated infant.

🍽️ Where to eat

Dvor

Indulge in signature grilled sea bass on a romantic, cliffside garden terrace overlooking the sea. The spacious outdoor seating easily accommodates a stroller, and the gentle waves provide soothing white noise.

Zrno Soli

Savor exquisite lobster pasta on a sleek outdoor terrace overlooking luxury yachts in the ACI Marina. The professional staff are highly welcoming to infants and provide premium highchairs.

Kadena

Enjoy premium Dalmatian beef tenderloin on a spacious, upscale terrace with panoramic Adriatic views. Early dinner seating at 6 PM is quiet and perfect before baby's bedtime.

Bokeria Kitchen & Wine

Feast on black truffle handmade pasta in a vibrant, high-ceilinged space. Book an early lunch spot when the restaurant is calmer, allowing plenty of room for your stroller.

Chops Grill

Relish high-end wagyu steaks on a wide, breezy outdoor terrace right in the city center. The staff are incredibly accommodating to families, offering prompt service and highchairs.
