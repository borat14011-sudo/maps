St. Kitts welcomes families with its lush emerald peaks, historic railway, and calm Caribbean shores. Kids can climb ancient fortress walls, splash in gentle turquoise waters, and spot wild green vervet monkeys swinging through the canopy. It is a laid-back island paradise where historical adventures meet effortless tropical relaxation.
🚕 Getting there: ~15-20 min taxi from Robert L. Bradshaw International Airport (SKB) to the Frigate Bay resort area

What to do

Morning / 2h

Brimstone Hill Fortress National Park

Explore this UNESCO World Heritage site with sweeping ocean views and stone ruins perfect for pretend play. Toddlers will love running on the grassy lawns, but keep a close eye on them near steep drop-offs.

Afternoon / 2-3h

St. Kitts Scenic Railway

Ride the historic double-decker train through lush sugarcane fields while enjoying folk music and island breezes. The open-air upper deck is exciting for toddlers, but bring quiet toys for the 2-hour journey.

Morning / 3h

South Friars Bay (Carambola Beach)

Relax on this calm beach where gentle waves make it safe for toddlers to splash safely. The soft sand is perfect for building castles, and there are plenty of shady lounge chairs to rent.

Morning / 1h

Basseterre Public Market

Immerse your senses in this bustling local market filled with colorful tropical fruits, sweet potatoes, and local spices. It's a great spot to introduce toddlers to sweet, fresh bananas and mangoes right from the vendors.

Morning / 1-2h

Clay Villa Plantation House & Gardens

Stroll through beautiful, eco-friendly tropical gardens filled with colorful flowers, resident rescue animals, and history. Toddlers will adore seeing the free-roaming turtles and peacocks in a safe, enclosed environment.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Frigate Bay Strip

Walk along this lively stretch of sand packed with casual beach shacks, shallow waters, and a vibrant local atmosphere. Toddlers can safely play in the gentle surf during the day while parents enjoy fresh coconut water.

Afternoon / 1h

Black Rocks

Marvel at the dramatic, dark volcanic rock formations created by ancient lava flows hitting the crashing ocean. Toddlers will be fascinated by the tide pools and the grassy park area where you can often spot grazing goats.

Morning / 1-2h

Romney Manor and Caribelle Batik

Wander through spectacular botanical gardens featuring a massive 350-year-old Saman tree and watch live batik wax-dyeing. Toddlers will love the wide, shady pathways and the vibrant, colorful fabrics on display.

Afternoon / 30 mins

Timothy Hill Overlook

Drive up to this breathtaking lookout to see where the turbulent Atlantic Ocean meets the calm Caribbean Sea side-by-side. It's a quick, stroller-accessible photo stop where toddlers can look for wild monkeys nearby.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Shipwreck Bar & Grill

Get the famous grilled mahi-mahi while green monkeys and wild mongooses play in the sand nearby.

Spice Mill Restaurant

Order the fresh passionfruit snapper while kids enjoy the safe, beachfront grassy play area and calm waves.

Sprat Net Bar & Grill

Dig into their legendary wood-fired lobster and ribs served in a casual, highly family-friendly open-air setting.

Arthur's Restaurant

Treat yourself to the fresh catch of the day on a shaded deck right on a unique black sand beach.

Carambola Beach Club

Savor high-end sushi or grilled chicken skewers right on the beach while toddlers play in the sand beside your table.
