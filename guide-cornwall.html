What to do

Morning / 3-4h

The Eden Project

Walk through giant, futuristic biomes housing indoor rainforests featuring fully paved, stroller-friendly paths and immaculate baby-changing facilities. The outdoor gardens offer massive, flat grassy lawns perfect for a toddler to stretch her legs safely.

Afternoon / 2-3h

The Lost Gardens of Heligan

A magical, restored 200-acre estate featuring giant mud sculptures like the Mud Maid that will fascinate young eyes. Most main paths are wide and stroller-accessible, though a baby carrier is recommended for the deeper jungle boardwalks.

Morning / 2-3h

St Michael's Mount

A historic island castle accessible by a dramatic stone causeway at low tide or a short boat ride at high tide. Ditch the stroller for a baby carrier due to the steep, ancient cobblestones, but enjoy the flat harbor front for safe toddler wandering.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

The Gannel Estuary

At low tide, this sheltered estuary near Newquay offers vast stretches of flat, soft sand and shallow, warm tide pools perfect for a toddler to splash safely away from heavy surf. Access is easy and wind-protected, making it highly relaxing for parents.

Morning / 2-3h

Trebah Garden

A sub-tropical paradise featuring a steep but fully paved ravine path that winds down to a private, sheltered shingle beach on the Helford River. It is entirely stroller-accessible, allowing you to watch sailboats pass while your child plays by the calm water.

Morning / 3h

Lanhydrock House and Garden

A grand Victorian country estate with exceptionally smooth gravel paths winding through magnificent rhododendron gardens, ideal for strollers. The estate boasts a dedicated, fenced-in wooden play area and a top-tier café right next to the path.

Afternoon / 2h

Padstow Harbour and the Camel Trail

Stroll along the completely flat, paved harbor wall of this famous fishing port to watch the colorful boats. The Camel Trail starts here, offering a flat, car-free paved path that is perfect for pushing a stroller along the picturesque estuary.

Afternoon / 2h

Fistral Beach Promenade

The home of British surfing features a wide, flat concrete promenade at the northern end, allowing you to wheel a stroller right up to the sand's edge. The Fistral Beach Complex offers spacious, family-friendly facilities and easy-access oceanfront dining.

Morning / 1-2h

The Minack Theatre

A world-famous, open-air theatre carved directly into the granite cliffs overlooking a turquoise bay. While the steep stone steps require a baby carrier, the dramatic vistas and early morning storytelling puppet shows are incredibly engaging for toddlers.

🍽️ Where to eat

Rick Stein's Cafe

Order the fresh, locally caught cod and chips; the relaxed, bustling daytime atmosphere absorbs toddler noise perfectly. They offer high-end high chairs and an early-evening opening time of 5:30 PM for stress-free family dinners.

The Beach Hut

Order the legendary Extreme Hot Chocolate and the fresh Cornish crab claws while looking out over Watergate Bay. The vibe is ultra-casual, meaning sand on the floor and active toddlers are met with warm smiles, plus there is easy ramp access for strollers.

Porthminster Beach Café

Order the crispy salt and pepper squid while sitting right on the beach with panoramic views of Godrevy Lighthouse. It offers upscale, award-winning dining with a relaxed, open-air beachside terrace that makes handling an active toddler stress-free.

The Hidden Hut

Grab a fresh, hot spiced lamb pasty to eat outdoors on the heavy wooden benches overlooking Porthcurnick Beach. It is entirely outdoor and casual, meaning your toddler can roam the grassy cliffside while you eat world-class beachside food.

Lusty Glaze Beach Restaurant

Order the pan-seared scallops or a stone-baked pizza in this cove-side sanctuary. Nestled at the bottom of the cliffs, it features cozy wood fires, lots of space between tables for strollers, and a massive sandy beach right outside the door.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Bedruthan Hotel & Spa

A legendary family-focused hotel featuring stunning sea views, indoor and outdoor pools, an indoor soft-play area, and a complimentary baby-listening service. They provide wooden cribs, bottle warmers, and premium strollers on request.

Fowey Hall Hotel

This luxury Victorian manor offers 90 minutes of free childcare per day, an indoor pool, and a spectacular outdoor play area. It balances premium, high-end design for parents with comprehensive baby equipment and toddler-proof lounges.

Sands Resort Hotel

This resort features spacious two-room family suites that allow parents to stay up after the baby goes to sleep, along with a dedicated baby pool and soft play. They offer an early kids' high tea and a reliable baby intercom monitoring service.
