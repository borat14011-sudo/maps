Cornwall

← back to travel board
Cornwall welcomes you with its dramatic granite cliffs, pristine turquoise waters, and sub-tropical gardens that feel worlds away from the everyday. For Will, Kathryn, and baby Mary Claire, the rugged coastline is softened by buggy-friendly harbor walls and wide, flat sandy shores. Spend your afternoons breathing in the fresh Atlantic breeze along the stroller-ready promenade of Gyllyngvase Beach or resting in the sheltered, calm waters of St Ives Bay. This is a coastal playground where high-end British heritage effortlessly accommodates the needs of an infant traveler.
🚕 Getting there: 45-minute drive from Cornwall Airport Newquay (NQY) to your coastal hotel via a pre-arranged private Mercedes V-Class executive transfer, complete with a rear-facing ISOFIX infant car seat and ample luggage space.
