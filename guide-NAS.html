Nassau

← back to travel board
Nassau blends colonial charm with pristine, toddler-friendly beaches that feel like giant wading pools. From counting pink flamingos to tasting fresh coconut, this tropical playground offers effortless island magic for the entire family. Its laid-back rhythm ensures parents can unwind while little ones explore safely.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Cable Beach

Calm, shallow turquoise waters and soft sand make this the ultimate spot for building castles. Toddlers can safely splash in the gentle tide with zero drop-offs.

Morning / 2h

Ardastra Gardens & Wildlife Conservation Centre

Walk among marching pink flamingos and feed colorful rainbow lorikeets right from your hands. The paved paths are highly stroller-friendly, and the shaded trees offer a cool escape.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Pirates of Nassau Museum

Step aboard a replica pirate ship and experience interactive exhibits that bring the golden age of piracy to life. It is stroller-friendly, though sensitive toddlers might find the dim lighting a bit spooky.

Morning / 3h

Clifton Heritage National Park

Explore quiet walking trails and play on shallow beaches like Jaws Beach, famous for calm, crystal-clear water. Toddlers will love looking for tiny crabs along the shoreline.

Morning / 1-2h

Nassau Straw Market

Navigate a bustling, colorful maze of local handicrafts, hand-woven straw bags, and carved wooden toys. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the sights, but keep them close in a stroller as the aisles are narrow.

Early Morning / 1h

Queen's Staircase & Fort Fincastle

Walk down the majestic 66 steps carved from solid limestone, surrounded by lush tropical plants and a cool breeze. Strollers must be carried up the stairs, but toddlers will love counting the steps themselves.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Baha Bay Water Park

A luxury beachfront waterpark featuring a dedicated, sprawling kids' zone called Turtle Cove with gentle slides and splash pools. Toddlers will spend hours safely wading through the shallow spray pads.

Morning / 4-5h

Blue Lagoon Island

Take a scenic boat ride to a private island paradise with a completely sheltered, wave-free lagoon. Toddlers can safely sit on the shallow sandy flats while watching dolphins swim by.

Afternoon / 1h

Graycliff Chocolatier

Watch master chocolatiers craft delicious treats through large viewing windows and enjoy kid-friendly chocolate-making classes. It is a sweet, cool indoor escape that toddlers can easily navigate while snacking.

Where to eat

  • The Poop Deck — Overlooking the marina, this casual spot serves fresh grilled snapper for parents and toddler-approved, crispy chicken fingers right on the water.
  • Arawak Cay (The Fish Fry) — A colorful strip of local shacks where parents can enjoy world-class conch fritters while kids munch on sweet, buttery Johnny cakes.
  • Dino's Gourmet Conch Salad — A legendary, laid-back roadside stand serving tropical conch salads alongside refreshing fresh-squeezed tropical juices perfect for little ones.
  • McKenzie’s Fresh Fish & Conch — Located under the bridge at Potter's Cay, this casual spot lets you watch fresh ingredients being prepared in an open, breezy setting that kids find fascinating.
👨‍👩‍👧 Stick to the breezy, pedestrian-friendly areas of downtown or Cable Beach, and always pack a sturdy, compact stroller for navigating historic sites with uneven cobblestones.
