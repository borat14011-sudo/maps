Puerto Escondido blends wild Pacific beauty with a laid-back, family-friendly coastal vibe. From calm, toddler-safe bays to vibrant local food markets, it offers a gentle introduction to Mexico's surf culture. Watch baby turtles scramble to the sea under a golden sunset while enjoying warm Oaxacan hospitality.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 min taxi from Puerto Escondido International Airport (PXM) to the main beach hotels

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Carrizalillo Beach (Playa Carrizalillo)

This sheltered, turquoise bay features gentle waves perfect for little splashers, though you will need to carry toddlers down the 150 stone steps.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Bacocho Beach Turtle Release

Children can help release newborn sea turtles into the ocean at sunset, an unforgettable and educational conservation experience run by Vive Mar.

Evening / 2h

Manialtepec Lagoon Bioluminescence Tour

Take a calm evening boat ride through the mangroves to see the water glow, a magical sight for older toddlers, though watch out for bedtime.

Morning / 1-2h

Mercado Benito Juárez

A colorful sensory adventure where toddlers can sample sweet local fruits like mangoes while parents shop for handmade Oaxacan crafts.

Morning / 2-3h

Playa Puerto Angelito

Featuring virtually no waves, this harbor-like beach is the ultimate safe spot for toddlers to wade in the water and play in the soft sand.

Early Morning / 2-3h

Dolphin Watching Boat Tour

Board a safe, shaded local boat from Playa Principal to spot hundreds of playful wild dolphins jumping just off the coast.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

La Punta Tide Pools

Explore the gentle tide pools at the rocky end of La Punta beach during low tide to search for tiny crabs, shells, and small fish.

Evening / 1-2h

Parque El Adoquín Pedestrian Zone

Stroll this car-free coastal street in the evening to enjoy street performers, balloon vendors, and easy, stroller-friendly walking.

Afternoon / 1h

Playa Principal Playground

Let the kids burn off energy at the beachfront playground, then grab a refreshing, toddler-friendly scoop of natural coconut ice cream nearby.

🍽️ Where to eat

El Cafecito

Order the giant, fluffy banana pancakes and fresh fruit smoothies that kids love in a casual, breezy palapa setting.

Sativa

Get the fresh fish tacos and avocado toast while sitting directly on the sand, allowing toddlers to play at your feet.

La Olita

Try the gourmet cheese-crusted tacos and fresh-pressed juices in a vibrant, highly family-welcoming courtyard.

Pizzeria Elisabeth

Dig into authentic, wood-fired thin-crust pizzas that are guaranteed crowd-pleasers for picky young eaters.

Almoraduz

Treat the family to elevated, kid-welcoming Oaxacan cuisine like rich chicken mole or fresh catch of the day in a relaxed garden atmosphere.

👨‍👩‍👧 Keep baby-safe, reef-friendly sunscreen and bug spray handy at all times, as coastal insects emerge at dusk. Standard taxis do not have car seats, so bringing a lightweight, portable harness or planning short, slow-speed rides is highly recommended.
