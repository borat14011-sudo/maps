Nestled on Oaxaca's emerald coast, Puerto Escondido blends wild Pacific beauty with an increasingly sophisticated design scene. For discerning families, the area offers a dramatic backdrop of golden sands and palm-fringed bays, with calm waters tucked away in its protected coves. Will, Kathryn, and baby Mary Claire will find a perfect sanctuary in the gentle, shallow waters of Carrizalillo Bay, which offers a peaceful oasis safe from the area's famous surf breaks. From private poolside villas to open-air palapa dining, this coastal haven balances rugged nature with high-end, infant-friendly comforts.
🚕 Getting there: 15-minute private Suburban transfer from Puerto Escondido International Airport (PXM) straight to the hotel strip, equipped with a pre-arranged rear-facing infant car seat for Mary Claire and ample space for a full-sized stroller.

What to do

Morning 8:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Playa Carrizalillo

A breathtaking, sheltered bay with calm turquoise waters perfect for baby Mary Claire to safely splash in. Note that the descent involves around 160 stone steps, so using a high-quality baby carrier is highly recommended over a stroller.

Early Morning 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM

Laguna de Manialtepec

A serene, shaded boat tour through lush mangroves where you can spot exotic birds. The gentle rocking of the boat is incredibly soothing for an infant, and the canopy provides excellent sun protection.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Puerto Angelito

One of the calmest swimming bays in the region, featuring flat water and sandy shores. Shaded palapas line the beach, offering a perfect spot to relax with a baby out of the direct midday sun.

Late Afternoon 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Playa Bacocho Turtle Release

Run by the Vive Mar conservation group, this sunset activity allows you to help release newborn sea turtles into the ocean. The flat, wide beach is easy to navigate with a carrier, and the quiet sunset ambiance is peaceful for infants.

Morning 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM

La Punta Pedestrian Walkway

A relaxed morning stroll along the dirt pathways of La Punta before the midday heat. It features trendy boutiques and cafes, best navigated with a rugged stroller or carrier to explore the bohemian atmosphere.

Midday 11:30 AM - 1:00 PM

Mercado Benito Juárez

A vibrant, shaded indoor market offering a rich sensory experience with colorful local fruits and textiles. The indoor aisles are sheltered from the sun, making it a cool midday escape when carrying baby Mary Claire.

Morning 8:30 AM - 11:00 AM

Playa Coral

A secluded, tranquil beach with very quiet shores and shallow tide pools. It provides a peaceful setting away from the crowds, ideal for a quiet family picnic under the natural shade of trees.

Day Trip 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

San Agustinillo Day Trip

A scenic drive to a nearby fishing village with incredibly gentle waves and a highly relaxed pace of life. The beachfront is lined with quiet, sophisticated restaurants that welcome families.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

El Higo Eco-Park

A lush, shaded botanical garden walk that offers a cool, green sanctuary away from the coastal heat. The flat, well-maintained paths are highly accessible for a premium stroller.

🍽️ Where to eat

Almoraduz Cocina de Autor

Exquisite Oaxacan fine dining featuring a gorgeous, spacious outdoor garden terrace. They gladly accommodate early seatings for families, allowing you to enjoy a quiet, upscale meal while baby sleeps in a stroller.

Kakurega Omakase

A high-end Japanese dining experience set inside a stunning open-air bamboo structure. The peaceful, natural architectural design provides a serene environment perfect for an early dinner with an infant.

Metri

A beautiful, design-forward restaurant serving fresh local seafood in an airy garden courtyard. The spacious seating easily accommodates strollers, and the gentle outdoor ambient noise naturally masks any baby sounds.

Espadin Restaurant

Perched directly above Carrizalillo Bay, this venue offers the best sunset views in town. It features a breezy terrace, highchairs, and an exceptionally welcoming staff that caters beautifully to young families.

Lychee Thai Food

A gorgeous, lantern-lit garden restaurant in La Punta serving exceptional upscale Thai cuisine. The open-air layout is incredibly spacious, making it very easy to park a stroller right next to your table.

$250-400/night

$300-400/night

An architectural masterpiece offering luxury suites with striking dome designs and private plunge pools. The tranquil, high-end atmosphere is perfect for families seeking quiet luxury, and the master suites offer generous space for a crib.

An eco-luxury design hotel where each villa features its own private pool and open-air shower. It offers a highly serene environment, blackout curtains for baby's naptime, and top-tier service that caters to every family need.
