What to do

Morning 9:30-11:30am

Basin Head Provincial Park

Famous for its singing sands, this beach offers a gentle, shallow ocean channel perfect for letting Mary Claire dip her toes. The boardwalk is highly stroller-accessible, allowing Kathryn and Will to walk comfortably along the coast.

Late Morning 10:00am-12:00pm

Greenwich Dunes Trail

Located inside PEI National Park, this trail features an exceptionally smooth, wide, floating wooden boardwalk. It is incredibly stroller-friendly, allowing you to wheel baby Mary Claire right over the coastal lagoons with ease.

Afternoon 2:00-4:00pm

Green Gables Heritage Place

Walk the historic, shaded paths of Lover's Lane and the beautiful, quiet gardens. The smooth pathways are perfect for a stroller nap while Will and Kathryn admire the historic architecture and pristine grounds.

Late Afternoon 4:00-5:30pm

Victoria Park Promenade

A gorgeous, wide waterfront boardwalk in Charlottetown. Completely flat and paved, it is perfect for an afternoon stroll to catch the harbor breeze while baby Mary Claire relaxes in her stroller.

Midday 12:00-1:30pm

The Dunes Studio Gallery and Cafe

Explore stunning gardens filled with vibrant flowers, sculptures, and water features. The paved garden paths are highly stroller-accessible, and the quiet environment provides a peaceful sensory experience for Mary Claire.

Morning 10:00-11:30am

East Point Lighthouse

The meeting point of the tides offers a dramatic but peaceful vista. The spacious, manicured grassy lawns surrounding the lighthouse are perfect for laying down a picnic blanket for Mary Claire's tummy time.

Afternoon 1:30-3:00pm

Lennox Island Cultural Centre

Engage in Mi'kmaq culture with an intimate, private indoor exhibit tour. The facility is fully air-conditioned, stroller-friendly, and offers a quiet, educational atmosphere perfect for escaping the midday sun.

Late Morning 11:00am-12:30pm

Point Prim Lighthouse

PEI's oldest lighthouse features expansive, ocean-facing grassy grounds. While climbing the tower is not baby-friendly, the sea-level views and fresh ocean air are ideal for a quiet family walk with a baby carrier.

Afternoon 3:00-4:30pm

Confederation Centre of the Arts

Stroll through the indoor, temperature-controlled art galleries in downtown Charlottetown. The wide, quiet corridors are highly accessible for strollers and provide a serene sanctuary during baby's naptime.

🍽️ Where to eat

The Inn at Bay Fortune

Enjoy the world-renowned Firework Feast. Book an early outdoor farm-tour seating; the staff is exceptionally welcoming to upscale families, and the open-air culinary theater keeps Mary Claire happily distracted.

New Glasgow Preserve Company

Dine on upscale local comfort food on a stunning outdoor terrace overlooking the River Clyde. They offer excellent wooden highchairs, early seating options, and a quiet, breezy atmosphere perfect for an early family dinner.

Merchantman Fresh Seafood & Oyster Bar

Feast on premium local oysters and fresh lobster on their beautiful, spacious outdoor courtyard patio. The natural outdoor ambiance easily absorbs baby noises, and premium highchairs are readily available.

Blue Mussel Cafe

An iconic harborfront spot in North Rustico specializing in fresh, local seafood. Opt for an early evening seating on the deck; the coastal breeze and ambient harbor sounds are great for keeping Mary Claire calm and cozy.

Water Prince Corner Shop

A historic, beloved seafood institution in Charlottetown. Extremely family-friendly despite its high culinary reputation, offering rapid service, early dining hours, and comfortable highchairs for Mary Claire.
