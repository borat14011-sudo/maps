Volcanic wonders and sunny family adventures in Tenerife.

Tenerife

← back to travel board
With its dramatic volcanic landscapes, warm year-round sun, and gentle ocean breezes, Tenerife is an island playground designed for family memories. From scaling the heights of Mount Teide to splashing in calm, golden-sand tide pools, this Canary Island treasure blends natural wonders with effortless, kid-friendly comfort.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Teide National Park Cable Car

Ride the cable car up Spain’s highest peak for otherworldly volcanic views that will leave kids spellbound. Toddler note: Stick to the flat Roques de García trail at the base to avoid altitude sickness.

Full Day / 5-6h

Siam Park Water Kingdom

This massive, Thai-themed water park offers adrenaline-pumping slides for older kids and the sprawling Lost City splash zone for little ones. Toddler note: The gentle Coco Beach wave pool is perfect for a calm family dip.

Morning to Afternoon / 4-5h

Loro Parque

A lush, world-renowned animal conservation park featuring playful penguins in a real snow biome and majestic killer whales. Toddler note: Stroller rentals are available at the entrance and paths are incredibly smooth.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Playa de Las Teresitas

A stunning golden-sand beach protected by an artificial reef, creating calm, wave-free waters perfect for splashing. Toddler note: Rent a sun lounger under the palm trees for built-in shade during midday naps.

Morning / 2h

Anaga Rural Park

Wander through an enchanted, mist-shrouded laurel forest that feels straight out of a fairy tale. Toddler note: The Sendero de los Sentidos path has a fully boardwalked flat section perfect for strollers.

Morning / 1-2h

Mercado Nuestra Señora de África

A vibrant, colorful market housed in a beautiful Moorish-style building filled with fresh tropical fruits and local treats. Toddler note: There is a fun outdoor playground right in the central courtyard to keep tots happy.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Garachico Natural Rock Pools

Swim in safe, unique ocean pools formed by historic lava flows, offering crystal-clear water and small fish to spot. Toddler note: Stick to the shallowest, walled-off pools during low tide to avoid strong currents.

Morning / 2h

Palmetum of Santa Cruz

A spectacular botanical garden transformed from a former landfill, now home to Europe's largest collection of palm trees and cascading waterfalls. Toddler note: Wide, paved pathways make this a peaceful, stroller-friendly morning stroll.

Morning / 2-3h

Whale Watching from Los Gigantes

Set sail from the towering cliffs of Los Gigantes to spot resident pilot whales and bottlenose dolphins jumping in the wild. Toddler note: Choose a large catamaran tour rather than a small zodiac boat for a smoother, safer ride.

Where to eat

  • Papas Arrugadas con Mojo — These salty, baby potatoes boiled in sea salt are served with mild, flavorful red and green dipping sauces that kids love.
  • Guachinche El Primero — Experience a traditional, family-run rustic eatery serving homemade Canarian wine and hearty, kid-friendly grilled meats.
  • Churros de Pescado — Fluffy, lightly battered fresh fish bites that are crispy on the outside and tender inside, perfect for picky young eaters.
  • Cafeteria Imperial — Famous for their towering, multi-layered Leche y Leche sweet espresso for parents and giant, fresh fruit smoothies for the kids.
