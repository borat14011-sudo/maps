West Palm Beach

← back to travel board
Where gentle ocean breezes meet lush tropical gardens, West Palm Beach offers a vibrant Florida escape tailored for little wanderers. From close-up wildlife encounters to splash-ready waterfront parks, this coastal gem balances laid-back relaxation with active family fun. Pack your sunscreen and prepare for stress-free days under the palms.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

Cox Science Center and Aquarium

Explore hands-on science exhibits, a beautiful backyard play space, and a colorful aquarium filled with native Florida marine life. Toddler note: The dedicated sensory play zone is perfect for toddlers to safely crawl and explore.

Afternoon / 2h

McCarthy's Wildlife Sanctuary

Get up close with rescued panthers, lions, and birds of prey on an intimate guided tour. Toddler note: The guided nature keeps little ones engaged in strollers, and they will love seeing the bright scarlet macaws.

Morning / 3-4h

Peanut Island Park

Take a short, scenic water taxi to this island paradise featuring shallow, crystal-clear snorkeling lagoons and paved walking paths. Toddler note: The shallow, wave-free sandbar area is ideal for toddlers to safely splash and build sandcastles.

Saturday Morning / 2h

West Palm Beach GreenMarket

Voted one of the best outdoor markets in the US, this lively waterfront spot features fresh local pastries, tropical fruits, and live music. Toddler note: There is plenty of open grassy space along the waterfront for toddlers to stretch their legs between food stalls.

Morning / 3h

Palm Beach Zoo & Conservation Society

Wander through shaded, jungle-like pathways to see koalas, tigers, and flamingos in an intimate, easy-to-navigate setting. Toddler note: Bring a change of clothes so your toddler can cool off in the popular interactive play fountain.

Morning / 1-2h

Mounts Botanical Garden

Discover West Palm Beach's oldest and largest public garden, showcasing colorful tropical flowers, a tranquil koi pond, and hidden pathways. Toddler note: Toddlers will love feeding the giant koi fish from the bridge and exploring the whimsical children's maze.

Evening / 2h

The Square

This vibrant, European-inspired shopping and dining district features beautiful fountains, family-friendly events, and lush landscaping. Toddler note: The central wishing tree lawn is a safe, vehicle-free space where toddlers can run around while parents grab a coffee.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Manatee Lagoon

Learn about Florida's gentle giants at this free, eco-discovery center featuring hands-on exhibits and a waterfront observation deck. Toddler note: Visit during the winter months to spot wild manatees warming up in the clean water outflows.

Afternoon / 2h

Okeeheelee Park

A massive county park offering quiet nature trails, a nature center, and lovely lakeside picnic spots. Toddler note: The gentle, paved walking paths are stroller-friendly, and the nature center has touch tanks that fascinate little eyes.

Where to eat

  • Sloan's Ice Cream — This whimsical, neon-pink wonderland features massive scoops of homemade ice cream and a famous glass-walled bathroom that toddlers find magical.
  • Howley's Restaurant — A retro 1950s diner serving up comfort food classics like banana pancakes and milkshakes in a highly casual, loud, and family-friendly atmosphere.
  • Grandview Public Market — A trendy food hall offering everything from gourmet tacos to ramen, featuring a spacious outdoor patio area perfect for wiggly toddlers.
  • Pizza Al Fresco — Enjoy delicious wood-fired pizzas in a beautiful, breezy outdoor courtyard where kids are welcomed with open arms and plenty of space.
👨‍👩‍👧 Utilize the free, stroller-friendly Circuit shuttle to easily navigate downtown West Palm Beach without having to constantly load and unload car seats. Always pack a dry change of clothes and towels in your day bag, as many parks and outdoor plazas feature unexpected splash pads or water fountains.
