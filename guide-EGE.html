Alpine magic and mountain memories for all ages

Vail

← back to travel board
Nestled in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, Vail offers a picture-perfect European-style pedestrian village paired with breathtaking alpine adventures. Whether splashing in gentle mountain creeks or riding the scenic gondola, families will find a wonderland of year-round outdoor magic. It is a stress-free mountain escape where cobblestone streets are entirely car-free and stroller-friendly.

What to do

Morning / 1-2h

Betty Ford Alpine Gardens

The world's highest botanical garden features paved paths, beautiful waterfalls, and a dedicated Children's Garden with a sandbox and stream. Toddlers will love the stepping stones and searching for hidden fairy houses.

Late Morning / 2h

Vail Scenic Gondola

Ride Gondola One up to Mid-Vail for panoramic views of the Gore Range and access to easy mountain walking trails. Strollers easily fold up to fit inside, and little ones will be thrilled by the smooth, floating ride.

Sunday Morning / 2-3h

Vail Farmers' Market & Art Show

This vibrant summer market is packed with local food vendors, live music, and colorful Colorado crafts. It is highly stroller-friendly, though going early helps beat the crowds with fussy toddlers.

Afternoon / 3-4h

Epic Discovery Marmot Mini Kids Play Area

Located at the top of the gondola, this mountain adventure zone offers a giant sandbox, animal tracks to follow, and gentle tubing lanes. Toddlers can safely play here while older siblings tackle the mountain coaster.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Pirate Ship Park

Located right in Vail Village, this iconic wooden pirate ship playground is surrounded by picnic tables and mountain views. It's the perfect spot for toddlers to climb, slide, and burn off energy safely.

Morning / 1h

Gore Creek Path

Walk along this paved, stroller-accessible path that winds alongside the rushing mountain river and through Vail Village. Toddlers will love tossing small pebbles into the shallow creek edges near the covered bridge.

Morning / 3-4h

Piney River Ranch

This stunning high-alpine lake offers canoeing, easy flat trails, and frequent moose sightings against a dramatic mountain backdrop. Toddlers will love petting the friendly horses and exploring the lakeside dirt paths.

Afternoon / 1h

Vail Public Library

This cozy, light-filled library features a dedicated children's room with toys, puzzles, and a large selection of books. It’s a perfect, quiet rainy-day retreat or a peaceful spot for toddlers to wind down.

Morning / 2h

Imagination Station

Located in the Lionshead parking structure, this indoor play space offers interactive science exhibits and a soft play area for toddlers. It is a fantastic, climate-controlled option for energetic toddlers on a snowy or rainy day.

Where to eat

  • Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory — Treat the kids to giant, gourmet caramel apples and fresh fudge made right before your eyes in a sweet-scented shop.
  • The Little Diner — This cozy, family-run Lionshead staple serves up giant Dutch Baby pancakes and classic breakfast food that toddlers love.
  • Blue Moose Pizza — A casual, lively spot in Lionshead serving delicious New York-style slices where kids get dough to play with while waiting.
  • Alpenrose Vail — Indulge in cozy Austrian-German comfort food like giant pretzels and schnitzel in a whimsical, fairytale-like chalet setting.
