Lake Constance (Bodensee) Region

← back to travel board
The Lake Constance region exudes an understated, tri-country sophistication where the gentle waters of the Rhine meet the snow-capped Alps. Will and Kathryn can unwind along the stroller-ready harbor promenade of Lindau or the manicured estate pathways of Mainau Island. The calm, tide-free waters of the Bodensee offer a serene environment, ensuring peaceful afternoons and quiet sailboat viewing for baby Mary Claire. This temperate microclimate seamlessly blends Austrian alpine charm, Swiss precision, and German hospitality into one premium, baby-friendly escape.
🚕 Getting there: Zürich Airport (ZRH) to Konstanz is a scenic 60-minute drive; we recommend a private Mercedes V-Class transfer with a pre-installed luxury infant car seat and ample space for your stroller.

What to do

Morning 9-11am / 2h

Mainau Island (Blumeninsel Mainau)

A breathtaking botanical paradise featuring stroller-accessible paved paths through vibrant rose gardens. The peaceful butterfly house offers a warm, quiet environment perfect for a calm walk with Mary Claire.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Pfänderbahn Cable Car

Ascend Mount Pfänder in Bregenz in a spacious, modern cabin that easily accommodates a stroller. At the summit, enjoy panoramic alpine views and a flat, stroller-friendly alpine wildlife park.

Afternoon 2-4pm / 2h

Affenberg Salem

Walk through a shaded forest sanctuary where Barbary macaques roam freely. The wide, flat gravel paths are excellent for stroller navigation, and the dense canopy provides cooling shade.

Late afternoon 4-6pm / 2h

Lindau Harbor Promenade

Stroll along the iconic Bavarian harbor entrance featuring the stone lion and lighthouse. The entire promenade is completely flat, paved, and lined with high-end cafes for easy nursing stops.

Morning 10-12pm / 2h

Zeppelin Museum Friedrichshafen

A spacious, climate-controlled indoor museum celebrating aviation history. The wide corridors are perfect for strollers, and the quiet ambient noise makes it an ideal spot for baby's afternoon nap.

Afternoon 3-4:30pm / 1.5h

Seebühne Bregenz

Visit the spectacular floating opera stage during the quiet daytime hours. The surrounding lakefront park is completely flat, beautifully paved, and perfect for a peaceful stroller walk.

Morning 9:30-11:30am / 2h

Reichenau Island (Insel Reichenau)

A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its ancient Romanesque churches and flat, quiet roads. Perfect for a leisurely bike ride with a baby trailer or a peaceful, stroller-guided walk through vegetable fields.

Morning 10am-12pm / 2h

Lake Dwelling Museum Unteruhldingen

Explore the open-air archaeological museum featuring reconstructed Bronze Age stilt houses. The outdoor boardwalks are fully stroller-accessible, offering fresh, cool breezes directly off the water.

Late afternoon 4-5:30pm / 1.5h

Meersburg Castle Gardens

While the ancient castle interior has stairs, the surrounding palace gardens and terrace are stroller-friendly. They offer magnificent elevated views of the lake and a quiet space to rest.

🍽️ Where to eat

Seerestaurant at Steigenberger Inselhotel

Savor fresh, locally caught Bodensee whitefish on a secluded lakeside terrace. The restaurant offers generous table spacing, highchairs, and an early dinner seating starting at 6:00 PM.

Restaurant Villino

An elegant, Michelin-starred Italian-Mediterranean venue in Lindau. Book an early evening table on their tranquil, shaded garden terrace where Mary Claire can sleep comfortably in her stroller.

Restaurant Strandhaus Lindau

An upscale, lakeside BBQ restaurant with a relaxed, sophisticated vibe. Features a spacious outdoor lawn, direct lake views, highchairs, and a highly welcoming atmosphere for families.

Gasthof zum Becher

A historic Meersburg institution serving refined Swabian cuisine like Zwiebelrostbraten. They offer highchairs, a warm and welcoming staff, and cozy, quiet alcoves perfect for families.

Wirtshaus zum Gucken

Located on the island of Lindau, this refined tavern serves premium regional specialties including gourmet Kässpätzle. Features quiet outdoor street-side seating with plenty of room for a stroller.
