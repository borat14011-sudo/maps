Lake Constance (Bodensee) Region

← back to travel board
Nestled where Germany, Austria, and Switzerland meet, Lake Constance offers a fairytale landscape of shimmering blue water, medieval castles, and alpine backdrops. Families can cycle along flat shorelines, explore flower-filled islands, and hop on scenic ferries connecting charming lakeside towns. It is an outdoor playground where storybook adventures and relaxed lakeside living seamlessly blend for all generations.
🚕 Getting there: ~35 min train ride from Friedrichshafen Airport (FDH) directly to Friedrichshafen Stadt station, or ~1 hour drive/taxi to main hub hotels in Lindau

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Mainau Island (Flower Island)

Wander through spectacular botanical gardens, massive adventure playgrounds, and a butterfly house that kids will adore. Toddlers will love the petting zoo and the water-play areas, so bring a change of clothes.

Morning / 2-3h

Pfänder Cable Car & Alpine Wildlife Park

Ride the cable car up Pfänder mountain for panoramic lake views and a free, stroller-friendly alpine wildlife trail. Toddlers can easily spot deer, ibex, and wild boars along the gentle path.

Full Day / 5-6h

Ravensburger Spieleland

A massive theme park dedicated to classic Ravensburger games with gentle rides, puzzle zones, and character meet-and-greets. Ideal for toddlers who can drive mini-tractors and splash in the water playgrounds.

Afternoon / 2h

Lake Dwelling Museum (Pfahlbauten)

Step back into the Stone Age at this open-air museum featuring reconstructed houses on stilts over the water. Toddlers will be fascinated by the hands-on sensory trail and the lakeside grassy areas.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Lindau Old Town & Harbor

Stroll the car-free island streets of Lindau to see the iconic Bavarian lion statue and lighthouse guarding the harbor entrance. Toddlers will love watching the boats and eating gelato along the lakeside promenade.

Morning / 2h

Affenberg Salem (Monkey Mountain)

Walk among over 200 free-roaming Barbary macaques in a lush forest where you can watch them feed on specially provided popcorn. Toddlers are safely kept in strollers or carriers while enjoying close-up animal encounters.

Afternoon / 2h

Zeppelin Museum

Explore the history of giant airships and walk inside a massive, life-sized reconstruction of the Hindenburg's passenger quarters. There is a fantastic kid-friendly workshop area with building blocks perfect for toddlers.

Morning / 2-3h

Rheinfall (Rhine Falls)

Witness Europe's most powerful waterfall up close with thrilling but safe boat rides that take you right near the thundering water. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the spray and can enjoy the stroller-accessible viewing platforms.

Morning / 1-2h

Konstanz Weekly Market (Wochenmarkt)

Visit this vibrant, sensory-filled market to sample fresh local cheeses, seasonal berries, and freshly baked soft pretzels. Toddlers can snack on pastries while you soak in the lively local atmosphere near the Stephansplatz.

🍽️ Where to eat

Gasthaus zum Becher

Order the traditional Swabian Maultaschen (stuffed pasta pockets) in a rich broth or pan-fried with onions.

Wirtshaus zum Gucken

Try the freshly caught Bodensee Kretzer (perch fillets) served with buttered potatoes in a family-friendly courtyard.

Milchpilz

Grab a classic strawberry milkshake and fresh pastries from this iconic, quirky mushroom-shaped retro kiosk that kids adore.

Restaurant Seegarten

Enjoy a Swiss cheese fondue or child-friendly schnitzel on the lovely outdoor terrace right next to the lake.

Hotei

Head here for delicious, family-friendly ramen bowls and handmade dumplings that are perfect for little hands to share.
