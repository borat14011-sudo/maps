What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Insel Mainau

Stroll through millions of blooms on this car-free paradise island featuring a massive tropical butterfly house. The paved, flat pathways are flawless for premium strollers, and there is a dedicated toddler water-play area.

Morning / 2-3h

Pfänderbahn

Ride the panoramic cable car up to the Pfänder mountain peak for sweeping views across the entire lake. At the top, a flat, stroller-friendly alpine wildlife park lets your toddler spot deer and alpine marmots.

Afternoon / 2h

Affenberg Salem

Walk through a 20-hectare forest where over 200 Barbary macaques roam completely free. The gravel paths are wide enough for sturdy strollers, and toddlers will be mesmerized by the close-up animal encounters.

Late Afternoon / 1-2h

Lindau Harbor and Lighthouse

Walk the historic harbor entrance flanked by the Bavarian Lion statue and the medieval stone lighthouse. The entire island harbor is flat, pedestrian-only, and perfect for an easy afternoon stroller nap.

Morning / 2h

SEA LIFE Konstanz

An immersive indoor aquarium featuring a walk-through ocean tunnel with sharks and green sea turtles. It is fully stroller-accessible, climate-controlled, and perfect for keeping a toddler entertained on a rainy afternoon.

Morning / 4-5h

Ravensburger Spieleland

An amusement park designed specifically for children aged two and up, featuring gentle rides and hands-on play zones. It offers extensive stroller rentals, clean baby-changing stations, and shaded rest areas.

Morning / 1-2h

Konstanz Wochenmarkt

This vibrant twice-weekly outdoor market at Stefansplatz is packed with local cheeses, fresh fruits, and pastries. It is highly stroller-accessible, allowing parents to sample local delicacies while baby enjoys the sights.

Morning / 3h

Säntis Schwebebahn

Take a breathtaking cable car ride to the Säntis summit for a six-country view. The base station in Schwägalp features a gentle, stroller-accessible sensory nature trail through the alpine pastures.

Afternoon / 2h

BSB Ferry Cruise

Board a historic white motorboat for a relaxing, breezy cruise across the tranquil lake waters. The ships have spacious indoor cabins for stroller parking, clean restrooms, and quiet decks perfect for nursing.

🍽️ Where to eat

Strandhaus Lindau

Order the slow-smoked beef brisket or local lake trout while sitting in the relaxed, spacious beer garden. It's incredibly welcoming for families, featuring high chairs and plenty of lawn space for a toddler to toddle.

Hafenhalle

Savor the signature crispy 'Kretzer' (local perch fillets) in a spacious, lively harbor-side setting. The bustling atmosphere naturally drowns out any toddler fussiness, and there is ample room to park a large stroller.

Wirtshaus am See

Enjoy a world-class Wiener Schnitzel on the massive outdoor terrace directly overlooking the floating opera stage. The outdoor seating provides a low-stress environment for dining with an infant right by the water.

Seerestaurant Bellevue

Try the fresh Swiss whitefish 'Egli' fillets while enjoying panoramic views of the lake's southern bend. The restaurant is exceptionally baby-friendly, with spacious dining rooms and quick, accommodating service.

Gasthof zum Becher

Indulge in traditional Swabian 'Maultaschen' at this historic, cozy establishment. Arrive early for dinner at 5:30 PM to secure a spacious corner booth that easily fits a baby carrier.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

Hotel Bad Schachen

A grand, historic lakeside resort featuring a stunning outdoor heated pool and private parklands. They provide premium wooden cribs, spacious family suites, and pristine lawns ideal for stroller strolls.

Steigenberger Inselhotel

A luxurious former Dominican monastery on its own private island, offering quiet lakeside gardens. It is highly family-friendly, providing top-tier baby cots, babysitting services, and easy, steps-away access to the old town.

Seehotel Am Kaiserstrand

A contemporary lakeside hotel with a beautiful indoor pool and immediate access to the flat Austrian shoreline promenade. The rooms are incredibly spacious, making it easy to set up travel cribs and play areas.
