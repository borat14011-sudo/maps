Alsace

← back to travel board
Alsace’s Wine Route blends storybook medieval charm with high-end comfort, making it a dream for a discerning family. Strolling through cobblestone villages like Riquewihr and Colmar is surprisingly easy with a sturdy stroller, while the region’s legendary white wines keep the parents pampered. Here, Michelin-starred hospitality meets an incredibly welcoming, family-first French-Germanic culture.
🚕 Getting there: Fly into EuroAirport Basel-Mulhouse-Freiburg (BSL) and take a ~45-minute premium rental car drive north along the scenic A35 directly to Colmar or your Wine Route hotel.

What to do

Morning / 2-3h

NaturOparC

Walk among native storks and otters in a beautifully landscaped, flat park. The wide, unpaved gravel paths are perfectly suited for all-terrain strollers, and the gentle aquatic feeding shows will captivate your toddler.

Morning / 1-2h

La Montagne des Singes

Walk through a lush forest where over 200 Barbary macaques roam completely free. The paths are stroller-accessible, and your toddler will be mesmerized watching the baby monkeys interact at arm's length.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Jardin des Papillons

Step into a warm, tropical greenhouse filled with hundreds of vibrant, exotic butterflies flying freely around you. The flat, paved pathway is highly stroller-friendly, and the colorful environment is a sensory wonderland for a young child.

Morning / 3-4h

Ecomusée d'Alsace

Explore France's largest open-air museum, featuring a recreated 20th-century Alsatian village with real farm animals, artisans, and boat rides. The wide dirt roads are excellent for strollers, and the free-roaming geese and sheep are a massive hit with toddlers.

Morning / 3-4h

Parc Cigoland

A gentle, vintage-style amusement park specifically designed for families with children under six, centered around the region's famous storks. With flat walkways, a monorail shaped like giant stork nests, and plenty of shade, it's incredibly stress-free for parents.

Afternoon / 1h

Little Venice Boat Tour

Glide down the quiet Lauch River in a flat-bottomed wooden boat to view Colmar's half-timbered houses from the water. Fold your stroller at the dock (the staff will store it) and enjoy a peaceful, sensory-friendly 30-minute ride that easily holds a toddler on your lap.

Morning / 2-3h

Château du Haut-Koenigsbourg

Experience a fully restored medieval fortress perched high in the Vosges mountains with breathtaking valley views. Since strollers are not permitted inside due to steep spiral stairs, pack a sturdy baby carrier to comfortably explore the drawbridges and armory.

Lunchtime / 1-2h

Marché Couvert de Colmar

Visit this historic permanent indoor market hall to sample local cheeses, fresh pretzels, and seasonal berries. The aisles are wide enough for a stroller, and it's the perfect spot to pick up an easy, high-quality picnic lunch before a drive.

Morning / 1-2h

Champ de Mars Park and Playground

Let your toddler run wild at this elegant green park featuring a fantastic, modern playground and a beautiful vintage carousel. It's surrounded by shaded, paved paths, making it the perfect spot for a morning coffee stroll while your daughter burns off some energy.

🍽️ Where to eat

Wistub Brenner

Order the classic Tarte Flambée (Alsatian flatbread) and a glass of local Grand Cru Riesling. The spacious canal-side terrace in Little Venice easily accommodates strollers, and the bustling outdoor ambient noise perfectly masks any toddler fussiness.

La Stub

Savor refined Alsatian classics like slow-cooked pork cheeks in a chic, upscale rustic setting inside Hotel Parc Spa. It is highly accommodating to families, offering high chairs, wide table spacing, and an early dinner service starting at 6:30 PM.

Wistub du Sommelier

Try the Choucroute Garnie (traditional sauerkraut platters) paired with exceptional local wines. The quiet, enclosed courtyard terrace offers a safe, spacious, and relaxed environment to dine early with a baby.

Restaurant Le Grognard

Indulge in artisanal Munster cheese fondues and sweet blueberry tarts in a laid-back, tavern-style atmosphere. The staff are incredibly welcoming to young children, and the service is fast enough to beat any toddler bedtime meltdown.

Brasserie de la Maison des Têtes

Dine on upscale, modern French-Alsatian cuisine inside a historic 17th-century monument. The elegant inner courtyard is stroller-accessible, spacious, and provides a quiet, upscale setting where well-behaved infants are warmly welcomed.

🏨 Family-friendly hotels

L'Esquisse Hotel & Spa Colmar - MGallery

This modern 5-star hotel sits directly inside the Champ de Mars park, offering immediate playground access, spacious suites, baby cots, and a gorgeous indoor pool perfect for family swim time.

Hotel Le Parc Spa & Wellness

A luxury family-friendly resort along the wine route featuring massive indoor and outdoor pools, expansive family suites with separate living areas, and beautiful gardens for stroller walks.

Resort Barrière Ribeauvillé

Located right on the vineyards, this resort offers exceptionally spacious modern rooms, high-quality cribs, and access to a brilliant balneotherapy pool complex with dedicated family swim hours.

👨‍👩‍👧 Invest in a high-quality all-terrain stroller like a UPPAbaby Ridge or Babyzen YOYO with robust wheels to handle the historic, uneven cobblestones of the medieval wine villages.
