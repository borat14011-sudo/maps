For Will, Kathryn, and baby Mary Claire, the beautifully manicured, stroller-friendly paths of the Michael-Ende-Kurpark offer a peaceful, shaded retreat right in the center of town. Stroll along manicured paved pathways in these peaceful historic gardens, featuring quiet shaded lawns perfect for an impromptu blanket picnic and infant tummy time.
