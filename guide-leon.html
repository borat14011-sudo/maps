León

León welcomes families with its dynamic blend of industrial heritage, massive green spaces, and friendly local culture. From feeding giraffes in a sprawling safari park to exploring bustling markets, this safe and walkable city is a hidden gem for multi-generational travel. Parents will love the unbeatable leather shopping while toddlers delight in the interactive museums and quiet plazas.
🚕 Getting there: ~30 min taxi or Uber from Del Bajío International Airport (BJX) to downtown León hotels

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Zooleón (León Zoo)

Feed majestic giraffes from a custom safari truck and explore a massive, shaded animal sanctuary. Toddlers will love the petting zoo and the mini-train ride that loops around the park.

Late Afternoon / 2-3h

Parque Metropolitano de León

Rent a family quad-cycle to ride around the scenic reservoir or let kids run free in the expansive playgrounds. It is the perfect spot for toddlers to watch ducks and stretch their legs in nature.

Morning / 3h

Explora Science Center (Centro de Ciencias Explora)

A highly hands-on science museum featuring an outdoor park with real airplanes and locomotives. The dedicated early-childhood pavilion is perfect for toddlers to touch and play safely.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Templo Expiatorio del Sagrado Corazón de Jesús

Marvel at the stunning neo-Gothic stained glass and explore the cool, mysterious underground crypts. The car-free plaza outside is a safe space for toddlers to watch pigeons while parents admire the architecture.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Plaza Fundadores & León Cathedral

Experience the heart of the city with its famous bronze lion fountain, street performers, and nearby ice cream shops. Toddlers will love chasing bubbles in the wide-open, pedestrian-only square.

Midday / 1-2h

Mercado de San Juan de Dios

Dive into a sensory wonderland of colors and aromas to sample local street food and fresh fruit juices. Toddlers will enjoy traditional, creamy snow-cone style ice creams from the local vendors.

Afternoon / 1-2h

Acuario del Bajío

Walk through immersive tunnels surrounded by sharks, stingrays, and colorful jellyfish inside a fully air-conditioned mall space. It is highly stroller-accessible and the perfect size to keep toddlers engaged without tiring them out.

Morning / 1-2h

Parque de los Cárcamos

A serene, shaded park filled with tranquil duck ponds, wooden bridges, and families feeding the resident geese. It's a quieter alternative to Metropolitano, ideal for a peaceful toddler stroller nap under the trees.

Morning / 2h

Zona Piel

Navigate hundreds of colorful stalls selling incredibly cheap, high-quality leather shoes, jackets, and cute toddler-sized boots. Keep toddlers secure in a stroller or carrier as the bustling sidewalks here can get very crowded.

🍽️ Where to eat

Panteón Taurino

Try the sizzling arrachera beef platters and handmade corn tortillas in a vibrant, museum-like bullfighting themed setting that kids find fascinating.

Frascati

Get the classic margherita wood-fired pizza and fresh handmade pasta that are guaranteed crowd-pleasers in a beautiful garden setting.

La Casona del Arco

Order the colorful duck tacos or pork belly, and make sure to save room for their artisanal Mexican chocolate desserts.

Rincón de Gaucho

Order the tender kid-friendly beef empanadas and premium grilled ribeye perfect for sharing with the whole family.

Guacamayas Javier

Grab the iconic local 'guacamaya' pork rind sandwich with avocado, and ask for a mild, deconstructed version for the little ones.

👨‍👩‍👧 Opt for Uber over local buses for cheap, convenient, and safe door-to-door transit with toddlers. Bring a lightweight stroller for the large parks, but pack a baby carrier for navigating the uneven, crowded sidewalks of Zona Piel.
