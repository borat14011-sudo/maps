León

← back to travel board
León bursts with vibrant energy, blending its rich shoemaking heritage with expansive green parks perfect for little explorers. Families can transition seamlessly from feeding giraffes at the city zoo to cycling along a sparkling reservoir under the warm Mexican sun. It is an approachable, active destination where colorful street life meets spacious, stroller-friendly attractions.
🚕 Getting there: ~30 min authorized airport taxi or Uber from Del Bajío International Airport (BJX) to central hotels.

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Parque Metropolitano de León

Rent a family surrey bike to pedal along the massive reservoir or ride the mini-train. Toddlers will love the spacious playgrounds and watching the geese by the water.

Morning / 3h

Zooleón (León Zoo)

Take an open-air safari truck to see free-roaming African animals and hand-feed friendly giraffes. The paved pathways are highly stroller-friendly with plenty of shaded resting spots.

Afternoon / 2-3h

Centro de Ciencias Explora

An interactive science museum with hands-on pavilions designed to engage young minds. The outdoor plaza features real, retired airplanes that toddlers can marvel at up close.

Late Afternoon / 1h

Templo Expiatorio del Sagrado Corazón de Jesús

Marvel at this striking neo-Gothic cathedral with magnificent stained glass windows. The wide, car-free stone plaza outside is perfect for toddlers to run around safely.

Evening / 1-2h

Plaza Principal (Centro Histórico)

Soak up the local culture under manicured trees, listen to live music, and chase bubbles near the fountains. Toddlers will love grabbing a sweet treat while you enjoy the architecture.

Afternoon / 2h

Acuario del Bajío

Located inside the Altacia mall, this indoor aquarium offers a cool, air-conditioned escape filled with sharks, jellyfish, and rays. Toddlers will be mesmerized by the glass viewing tunnels.

Morning / 1-2h

Mercado Aldama

Dive into a sensory explosion of colorful fruits, local toys, and sizzling food stalls. It is best to use a baby carrier here as the narrow aisles can get busy.

Morning / 1-2h

Parque Los Cárcamos

A serene, lush park featuring a beautiful lake filled with ducks, turtles, and fish. It is an ideal, quiet spot for a peaceful toddler nap walk under the weeping willows.

Morning / 2h

Shopping in Zona Piel

Explore the world's leather capital, where you can find adorable, high-quality handmade toddler leather shoes and boots. Toddlers will love looking at the colorful mini-footwear.

🍽️ Where to eat

Panteón Taurino

Order the juicy arrachera steak or crispy tacos in a lively, museum-like bullfighting themed setting that kids find fascinating.

Frascati

Get the wood-fired artisanal pizzas and fresh handmade pasta in a spacious, highly welcoming family-friendly atmosphere.

Guacamayas Javier

Try the famous local guacamaya street food—a crusty bread roll stuffed with crispy pork chicharron, fresh pico de gallo salsa, lime, and avocado.

Cebadina Portal Deluz

Grab a glass of refreshing Cebadina, a bubbly crimson barley and hibiscus drink fermented with a pinch of baking soda that kids love to watch fizz.

Ma Come No

Enjoy upscale yet incredibly child-friendly Italian-Mediterranean dining, featuring delicious risottos and customized kid-friendly pasta on a beautiful garden terrace.

👨‍👩‍👧 Use official airport taxis (Taxitel) for a safe, hassle-free ride to your hotel, and choose baby carriers over bulky strollers for the busy markets. Keep small change handy for park restrooms and for buying birdseed to feed the ducks.
