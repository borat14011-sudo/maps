Punta Cana welcomes sophisticated families with its mesmerizing blend of swaying coconut palms, powdery white sands, and calm turquoise waters. What to do

Morning 8:30 AM - 11:30 AM / 3h

Bávaro Beach

Enjoy the iconic soft white sands and exceptionally calm, shallow waters that are perfect for dipping baby Mary Claire's toes. Abundant natural shade from the fringing palm trees provides a cool canopy for midday outdoor naps.

Morning 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 3h

Altos de Chavón

Explore this stunning replica of a 16th-century Mediterranean village perched above the Chavón River. While the cobblestones require a baby carrier rather than a stroller, the peaceful morning air and historic stone architecture make for breathtaking family photos.

Afternoon 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM / 2h

BlueMall Punta Cana

Take a cool, air-conditioned break in this upscale shopping center. It features wide, highly stroller-friendly corridors, premium family changing rooms, and a selection of luxury boutiques.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 2.5h

Playa Blanca

Spend a quiet morning at this pristine beach located within the secure Puntacana Resort area. The exceptionally flat, shallow turquoise lagoon is entirely sheltered from rough waves, making it incredibly safe for an infant's gentle swim.

Morning 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM / 2h

Indigenous Eyes Ecological Reserve

Stroll through shaded, lush forest trails protecting natural freshwater lagoons. Utilize a high-quality baby carrier to comfortably navigate the natural dirt paths and enjoy the tranquil, quiet sounds of local birdlife.

Early morning 8:00 AM - 9:30 AM / 1.5h

Playa Macao

Visit this stunning, wide beach to enjoy the dramatic coastal vistas. The expansive, hard-packed shoreline near the cliffside is ideal for a smooth morning stroll with an all-terrain stroller before the midday heat sets in.

Morning 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 4h

Corales Golf Course Clubhouse

While Will enjoys playing a round on the breathtaking oceanfront 18-hole PGA course, Kathryn and baby Mary Claire can relax in the breezy, upscale clubhouse overlooking the manicured greens and dramatic cliffs.

Morning 9:30 AM - 10:30 AM / 1h

Punta Cana Resort & Club Equestrian Center

Introduce baby Mary Claire to the gentle horses in a beautifully maintained, breezy stable environment. The shaded paddock paths are flat and easy to navigate with a stroller, offering a quiet sensory experience.

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 2h

Scape Park Sensory Walk

Take a tranquil walk through the botanical gardens and animal exhibits at this eco-adventure park. Stick to the flat, paved walkways near the entrance to view colorful orchids and parrots while avoiding the rugged cave trails.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Yola Restaurant

Dine on Mediterranean-inspired fresh seafood at this spectacular open-air, thatch-roof restaurant built directly over the water. Booking an early 6:00 PM seating allows you to catch the sunset while enjoying a gentle sea breeze that naturally masks baby noise.

Brassa Grill & Bar

An elegant beachfront grill serving exceptional steaks and fresh catches. The spacious outdoor beach terrace easily accommodates luxury strollers, and the rhythmic sound of crashing waves provides the perfect white noise for a sleeping infant.

Citrus Restaurant

Located in El Cortecito, this vibrant local favorite offers an extensive menu of sushi, seafood, and pasta. Request a table on the covered, breezy outdoor terrace which is highly welcoming to young families and provides sturdy highchairs.

La Palapa by Eden Roc

Experience world-class oceanfront dining featuring fresh ceviche and Italian-Caribbean fusion. The pristine, luxury setting offers exceptionally spaced tables, an incredibly accommodating staff who anticipate baby needs, and breathtaking coastal views.

Jellyfish Beach Restaurant

A stunning, architectural beachside venue offering a relaxed yet luxury atmosphere. The spacious layout allows you to roll your stroller directly to the table on the outdoor wooden deck, where you can savor grilled lobster with your toes near the sand.
