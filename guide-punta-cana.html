Punta Cana

Punta Cana welcomes families with its powdery white-sand beaches, swaying palm trees, and warm Caribbean breezes. From calm, shallow lagoons perfect for tiny splashing feet to exciting eco-adventures, this tropical haven offers a relaxed pace for parents and endless wonder for kids. It is a postcard-perfect escape where memories are made under the golden Dominican sun.
🚕 Getting there: ~15 to 30 minute private minivan transfer or pre-arranged resort shuttle to most major beach hotels

What to do

Morning / 3-4h

Playa Bávaro

This iconic beach features incredibly soft white sand and calm, shallow waters protected by a natural reef. It is perfect for toddlers to splash safely at the water's edge while parents relax under the palms.

Morning / 2-3h

Hoyo Azul at Scape Park

A breathtaking natural cenote filled with crystal-clear, deep blue water hidden at the base of a cliff. The rainforest trail has some stairs, so use a baby carrier, but toddlers will be mesmerized by the vibrant color of the water.

Morning / 2h

Indigenous Eyes Ecological Reserve

A lush nature reserve featuring shaded, stroller-friendly dirt paths that wind past freshwater lagoons. Toddlers will love spotting the turtles and colorful fish swimming in the clear water.

Morning / 3-4h

Monkeyland Punta Cana

An interactive nature spot where gentle squirrel monkeys climb right onto your shoulders. It is a magical experience for young kids, though toddlers should be held close so they don't get startled by the quick movements.

Afternoon / 3h

Playa Macao

A beautiful, undeveloped public beach known for its dramatic cliffs and local energy. While the waves can be active, the wide sandy shoreline is ideal for building sandcastles and shallow wading with toddlers.

Afternoon / 1-2h

ChocoMuseo Punta Cana

A sweet, hands-on museum where families can learn how chocolate is made from bean to bar. Toddlers will enjoy the interactive exhibits and, of course, the delicious chocolate tastings.

Morning / 3h

Bávaro Adventure Park Dino World

An outdoor adventure park featuring a dedicated Dino World trail with life-sized animatronic dinosaurs. The shaded, flat pathway is completely stroller-friendly and highly engaging for dinosaur-loving toddlers.

Late Afternoon / 2h

Los Corales Beach

A relaxed beach area lined with coconut palms and cozy beach cafes. It offers gentle waves and plenty of shade, making it a stress-free spot for a toddler nap under an umbrella.

Afternoon / 2h

Galerías Punta Cana

An open-air shopping village near the airport featuring wide pedestrian pathways and a colorful children's playground. It is a safe, car-free environment where toddlers can freely stretch their legs and burn off energy.

🍽️ Where to eat

Jellyfish Beach Restaurant

Order the fresh grilled lobster or seafood paella while the kids play safely in the soft sand right next to your beachfront table.

La Yola

Savor the Dominican-style braised red snapper on an open-air wooden deck built right over the sparkling marina waters.

Wacamole

Grab the fresh tableside-prepared guacamole and mild chicken tacos in a colorful, casual setting that is highly welcoming to loud toddlers.

Pastrata Dominican Restaurant

Try the classic 'La Bandera Dominicana' (a traditional plate of seasoned rice, red beans, and stewed chicken) for an authentic local lunch.

Noah Restaurant & Lounge

Order a wood-fired gourmet pizza for the kids and fresh, creative sushi rolls for the adults in this stylish yet family-friendly venue.

👨‍👩‍👧 Always use bottled water for mixing formula and brushing your toddler's teeth to avoid stomach upsets. Pack plenty of reef-safe sunscreen and a lightweight UV beach tent, as the Caribbean sun is exceptionally strong even in the shade.
