Punta Cana welcomes young families with its powdery white sands and exceptionally calm, reef-protected waters. The exclusive enclave of Cap Cana offers a stroller-ready marina boardwalk and tranquil lagoons perfect for baby Mary Claire’s first ocean dips. Will and Kathryn can unwind under private beach cabanas while enjoying world-class service just steps from their suite. The destination seamlessly pairs tropical relaxation with high-end convenience, making it an effortless getaway for a finance couple seeking respite.

What to do

Morning 8:30-10:30am / 2h

Playa Juanillo

This pristine beach offers shallow, crystal-clear waters sheltered by a natural reef, making it incredibly safe for Mary Claire to splash. The wide, flat sandy shores are easy to navigate, and the nearby palm trees provide ample natural shade.

Early morning 8:00-10:00am / 2h

Indigenous Eyes Ecological Reserve

This private reserve features lush tropical forests and twelve freshwater lagoons. While the trails are too uneven for strollers, wearing Mary Claire in a baby carrier allows you to comfortably explore the shaded pathways and view turtle-filled waters.

Mid-morning 10:00am-12:00pm / 2h

Playa Blanca

An exclusive beachfront area with exceptionally calm and shallow waters, perfect for an infant's gentle introduction to the ocean. Will and Kathryn can rent a shaded daybed and enjoy refreshing coconut water right at the shoreline.

Late afternoon 5:00-6:30pm / 1.5h

Cap Cana Marina

A beautifully paved, upscale marina promenade that is completely stroller-friendly and ideal for a sunset walk. You can admire the luxury yachts while Mary Claire naps soundly in her stroller, cooled by the gentle ocean breeze.

Midday 1:00-2:30pm / 1.5h

Chocomuseo Punta Cana

This air-conditioned indoor venue offers a delightful sensory experience with chocolate tastings and hands-on workshops. It provides a perfect escape from the midday Caribbean heat, featuring smooth floors that are fully stroller-accessible.

Afternoon 2:00-4:00pm / 2h

BlueMall Puntacana

A modern, high-end shopping center featuring luxury boutiques, excellent dining, and spacious baby-changing facilities. The wide, air-conditioned corridors offer a cool, sensory-friendly environment to walk Mary Claire during the hottest hours of the day.

Morning 9:00-11:30am / 2.5h

Hoyo Azul at Scape Park

A stunning cenote filled with vibrant turquoise water located at the base of a massive cliff. The journey requires a guided walk through a lush rainforest trail, so you must use a baby carrier instead of a stroller to safely navigate the wooden stairs.

Morning 9:00-11:00am / 2h

Cabeza de Toro Beach

A peaceful beach protected by an offshore reef, ensuring the water remains as calm as a swimming pool. The soft, white sand is perfect for spreading out a blanket so Mary Claire can play safely under the shade of a rental umbrella.

Morning 8:00-9:30am / 1.5h

Playa Bávaro

One of the most famous beaches in the region, offering a wide, flat shoreline that is perfect for a scenic morning walk with a baby carrier. The majestic palm canopy offers pockets of deep shade where the family can relax together.

🍽️ Where to eat

La Yola Restaurant

Savor fresh Mediterranean-influenced seafood while dining on an open-air deck styled after a fishing boat. An early 6:00 PM seating ensures a quiet atmosphere, beautiful sunset views, and plenty of space for your stroller beside the table.

La Palapa by Eden Roc

This elite oceanfront venue offers breathtaking views and world-class Italian-Caribbean fusion. The gentle sea breeze keeps the terrace cool, and the staff is remarkably attentive to families, readily providing highchairs and accommodating early dining times.

Little John

Located right on the sands of Playa Juanillo, this vibrant yet relaxed spot serves delicious tropical cuisine and fresh ceviche. The casual, open-air beachfront setting is highly forgiving of baby noises, and highchairs are readily available.

Bachata Rosa by SBG

A visually stunning restaurant in BlueMall designed with musical memorabilia, serving creative Dominican and international dishes. The indoor space is fully air-conditioned, stroller-friendly, and offers spacious booths that make managing an infant very comfortable.

Jellyfish Beach Restaurant

An iconic beachfront restaurant famous for its fresh lobster and distinctive architecture. The spacious outdoor wooden deck allows for easy stroller parking, and the soothing sound of the waves provides excellent natural white noise for an evening nap.
