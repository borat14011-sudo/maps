Nestled at the base of Vail Mountain, this alpine paradise offers a European-style refuge perfect for refined family bonding. Will and Kathryn can navigate the entirely pedestrian-only, heated cobblestone paths of Vail Village with baby Mary Claire in complete comfort. With world-class mountain concierges and pristine high-altitude air, Vail effortlessly blends rugged Colorado beauty with five-star convenience for your infant's first mountain retreat.

What to do

Morning 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM / 2h

Vail Village Promenade

Stroll along the entirely pedestrianized, heated cobblestone paths of Vail Village. It is completely stroller-accessible, offering a gentle, quiet environment with gorgeous mountain backdrops and plenty of benches for nursing stops.

Late Morning 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM / 1.5h

Betty Ford Alpine Gardens

Explore the world's highest botanical gardens via beautifully paved, stroller-friendly pathways. The peaceful atmosphere, shade from towering evergreens, and soothing waterfalls provide a serene sensory experience for baby Mary Claire.

Midday 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM / 1.5h

Gondola One

Enjoy a scenic, fully enclosed cabin ride up the mountain. It is exceptionally quiet and smooth, making it safe and comfortable for an infant, with panoramic valley views that will captivate Will and Kathryn.

Early morning 8:00 AM - 9:30 AM / 1.5h

Gore Creek Path

A paved, multi-use trail winding alongside the tranquil Gore Creek. Perfect for a morning jog or stroller walk under the shade of mature aspen trees, with the rushing water serving as a natural white noise machine for baby's naptime.

Sunday morning 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM / 2h

Vail Farmers' Market & Art Show

An upscale Sunday market on Meadow Drive. Stroll through the artisan stalls with a premium coffee in hand while baby Mary Claire enjoys the vibrant colors, gentle acoustic live music, and fresh alpine breeze.

Afternoon 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM / 1.5h

Ford Park Playground

A beautifully manicured park featuring a dedicated soft-surface playground with infant-safe bucket swings. Will and Kathryn can relax on the lush grass while Mary Claire enjoys gentle outdoor tummy time under the shade of mature pine trees.

Afternoon 1:30 PM - 3:00 PM / 1.5h

Eagle Bahn Gondola

Board this enclosed, spacious gondola from Lionshead Village for a smooth ride to Eagle's Nest. The cabin easily accommodates a folded luxury stroller, offering a breezy, quiet, and breathtaking ascent suitable for family photos.

Morning 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM / 1.5h

Vail Nature Center

A peaceful, seven-acre historic yurt oasis situated along Gore Creek. The gentle meadows and stroller-accessible loop trails offer a quiet, shaded respite from the busier resort areas, perfect for a calm family walk.

Mid-afternoon 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM / 1.5h

Donovan Park

A sprawling public park featuring beautiful manicured lawns, paved flat trails, and spectacular views of the Gore Range. The quiet pavilion area is perfect for an outdoor picnic lunch with baby Mary Claire.

🍽️ Where to eat

Sweet Basil

Indulge in modern American cuisine with a signature dish of Hot Sticky Toffee Pudding. Request an early 5:00 PM seating on the glass-enclosed terrace overlooking Gore Creek, where the moderate ambient noise easily masks baby babbles.

Mountain Standard

Savor wood-fired classics like the Rocky Mountain Trout in a rustic-chic tavern setting. This vibrant spot features spacious booths, accommodating staff who gladly provide premium highchairs, and an energetic atmosphere perfect for families.

The Little Diner

Vail's premier breakfast joint serving up legendary Dutch Baby pancakes. Arrive early to secure a spacious booth where the family-friendly, bustling atmosphere keeps Mary Claire entertained without disturbing other patrons.

Swiss Chalet Restaurant

Enjoy authentic alpine fondue in a cozy, Bavarian-themed dining room. The early dinner seatings are highly accommodating to families, offering comfortable highchairs and a warm, welcoming environment for parents traveling with infants.

Matsuhisa Vail

Feast on world-class yellowtail sashimi with jalapeño. This ultra-luxury dining space features an expansive outdoor heated terrace during warmer months, offering ample space for a stroller beside your table and highly attentive service.
