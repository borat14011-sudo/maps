The heated cobblestone streets of Vail Village ensure a smooth, ice-free stroll with your stroller even during the crisp mountain mornings. Will and Kathryn can savor high-end boutique shopping and gourmet dining, while baby Mary Claire relaxes in the pristine alpine air.
