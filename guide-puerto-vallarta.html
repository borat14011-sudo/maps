Framed by the dramatic Sierra Madre mountains and the warm waters of Banderas Bay, Puerto Vallarta offers a sophisticated coastal escape where Mexican heritage meets five-star comfort. The exclusive Marina Vallarta district serves as a quiet, yacht-filled sanctuary for discerning travelers seeking refuge from the busier tourist corridors. For Will, Kathryn, and baby Mary Claire, the flat, stroller-friendly Malecon boardwalk provides a seamless beachfront promenade perfect for sunset strolls without navigating steep cobblestones. Here, private oceanfront cabanas and gentle ocean breezes make for an effortless tropical escape tailored to sophisticated parents and their little one.
🚕 Getting there: 15-minute drive. The premier family transport option is a pre-arranged private luxury SUV transfer through VIP Vallarta Transportation, equipped with a sanitized rear-facing infant car seat and ample luggage space for your upscale stroller.

What to do

Early morning 8:30-10:00am / 1.5h

El Malecón Boardwalk

This wide, flat, pedestrian-only esplanade is completely stroller-accessible, featuring stunning ocean views, whimsical bronze sculptures, and plenty of shaded spots for Mary Claire to rest during a morning walk.

Morning 9:00am-12:00pm / 3h

Vallarta Botanical Garden (Jardín Botánico de Vallarta)

A lush mountain sanctuary featuring paved main pathways easy for robust strollers, offering a peaceful, shaded canopy of tropical orchids and wildflowers perfect for keeping baby cool.

Thursday evening 6:00-8:00pm / 2h

Marina Vallarta Art & Market

A vibrant evening market along the flat, yacht-lined marina boardwalk where parents can browse local crafts with a stroller while enjoying the cool, gentle ocean breeze.

Morning 9:00-10:00am / 1h

Playa Los Muertos Pier

An iconic, sail-shaped modern pier offering beautiful sunset views; the flat concrete walkway allows for easy stroller access, though visiting during the quieter morning hours avoids crowds and loud music.

Morning 9:00am-1:00pm / 4h

Private Yacht Charter via Mike's Fishing & Yacht Charters

Experience Banderas Bay privately on an infant-safe luxury yacht with air-conditioned cabins, shaded decks, and customized slow-speed cruising to spot dolphins while Mary Claire naps.

Morning 10:00am-12:00pm / 2h

Isla Cuale Flea Market and Cultural Center

A shaded oasis river island in the heart of PV with flat dirt paths under massive fig trees, offering a tranquil, quiet escape from the city heat with soft murmuring river sounds.

Early morning 8:30-11:00am / 2.5h

Playa Las Gemelas

Located south of the city, this pair of gentle, white-sand beaches offers remarkably calm, clear turquoise waters perfect for dipping baby Mary Claire's toes under a rented beach umbrella.

Morning 10:00-10:45am / 45m

Parroquia de Nuestra Señora de Guadalupe

The iconic crown-topped cathedral in Centro; parents can admire the stunning architecture from the flat, stroller-accessible plaza outside to avoid the indoor echoing acoustics that might startle baby.

Morning 8:00-9:30am / 1.5h

Gringo Gulch Historic Walk

A scenic, picturesque residential area above the river; while steep in parts, parents using a baby carrier can marvel at the beautiful white-washed villas, including Elizabeth Taylor's former home, in the cool early hours.

🍽️ Where to eat

Cafe des Artistes

Renowned for French-Mexican fusion; reserve an early 6:00 PM seating in the lush, tiered garden terrace where the natural waterfall white noise easily masks any baby babble.

La Palapa Restaurant

Gourmet beachfront dining on Playa de los Muertos; request a table directly on the sand so Mary Claire can play quietly while Will and Kathryn enjoy signature coconut shrimp and fresh ceviche.

Trio Restaurant

Mediterranean cuisine in a refined multi-level courtyard with a soothing fountain; the open-air layout and welcoming staff make early dinners highly comfortable with a stroller.

Barcelona Tapas

Upscale Spanish tapas overlooking the bay; features elevator access to the rooftop terrace, spacious tables with room for highchairs, and spectacular sunset views.

The Iguana Restaurant & Tequila Bar

Elegant fine dining overlooking the city; offers an open-air terrace with spacious seating, quiet classical background music, and a sophisticated atmosphere where early-seating families are graciously welcomed.
